1 Koriŋ.
8.
Ndwɛ, nɔ pa maoŋ jɨ ŋa pi fɛ̄ʼi pa nnwi nɔ ghɔ nɛ, pigi ji ŋa pichəɨ ŋgwa shɨna pəɨ nchhu ŋa, “ŋguoŋ yia fāʼo lɨna.” Lɨna ghà nchwīe ŋoŋ fāʼo nōoŋ noŋ, ndɔ khwǎ ghà māʼaŋ ghrɨ́. 
S̈hesheŋoŋ mbīʼi ŋa a ji nnu, ŋgaŋ vi lɔ naa nji nɔ haʼaŋ a fāʼo nɔ ji nu nɛ. 
Ndɔ shesheŋoŋ ŋa a khwā Minnwi nɛ, Minnwi ji vi. 
Nthɛ yie ghɔ, nɔ jɨ nu maoŋ ŋa pi fɛ̄ʼi pa nnwi nɔ ghɔ nɛ, pigi ji yinɛ nɔ haʼaŋ pichəɨ shɨna pəɨ chhu ŋa, “yichəɨ yaoŋ lɔ mbɔ fɔ nɔ nnwi moŋ mbhi” pugu pa ŋa “a wuʼɔ taʼa Minnwi ŋa a pɔ.” 
Ä ndɔ mbɔ pi ŋa yaoŋ pɔ ŋa pi mɛ̄iŋ ni pa nnwi, ki a pɔ pi po ki shhɛ, wuʼɔ nɔ haʼaŋ shishini ntou pa “nnwi” pugu pa “taathɔ” pɔ nɛ, 
ndɔ ni pia a wuʼɔ mbɔ taʼa Minnwi, mbɔ Tǎa ŋa ŋguoŋ pa yaoŋ llɔ pi mbhɔ yu, pia chɔmbhi nthɛ pi ju, ni taʼa Taathɔ mbɔ Jishɔ Krishto ŋa pi tɔgɔ pi mbɨŋ yu nchwīe ŋguoŋ pa yaoŋ, pia fāʼo chɔmbhi ntɔgɔ pi mbhɔ yu nɛ. 
Ndɔ a lɔ mfāʼo ŋguoŋ ŋgwa mfāʼo lɨna yei. Pichəɨ pa ŋgwa ni ŋkwo we ni pa nnwi ti nchəɨŋ ndwɛ, nnɛ ŋa ndɨɨ ŋa pugu jɨ maoŋ ŋa pi fɛ̄ʼi pa nnwi nɔ ghɔ nɛ, pugu pīʼi ŋa a pɔ pi ghaʼo nu ya nnwi shini. Nthɛ ŋa ŋgaŋthɔ yugu lɔ ntɨnɨ nɛ, pugu pīʼi ŋa pugu chwīe phɨ. 
Mbaʼa a yiʼi nchwīe maoŋ jɨ shɨ̀na yia pia pa Minnwi pɔ shiʼi. Mbaʼa pia yiʼi mbhɛ yichəɨ yaoŋ nɔ haʼaŋ p̈ia mieŋ ki jɨ, ki nshei yichəɨ yaoŋ nɔ haʼaŋ p̈ia njɨ. 
P̂əɨ lɨna nu ŋa kiʼi pɔ nu ŋa pəɨ fāʼo njɨ nɔ chwīe nu sheshe nnu pɔ kɨʼɨŋgɨnɨ ni ŋgwa ŋa pugu lɔ njiʼi ntɨnɨ moŋ piŋ nɛ. 
Nthɛ ŋa s̈hesheŋoŋ njəɨ ghɔ ŋa ɔ fāʼo lɨna nɛ, ɔ thɔ njɨ yaoŋ nda pa nnwi, paʼa yie ghɔ lɔ nchwīe vi juju ŋkaa yu, ŋa ŋgaŋthɔ yi lɔ njiʼi ntɨnɨ nɛ, a jɨ maoŋ ŋa pi fɛ̄ʼi pa nnwi nɔ ghɔ nɛ? 
Nɛnnɛ ntɔgɔ pi ŋkuoŋ pɔ nu ŋa ɔ ji, ɔ pɨrɨ ŋoŋ vei ŋa njùʼɔ yi lɔ nja ntɨnɨ nɛ, mbɔ ŋoŋ ŋa Krishto ni ŋkhu nthɛ vi nɛ. 
Pəɨ kie nɛnnɛ mfāaŋ mbɨŋ pa lǐŋ pəɨ, nchhɔ mfɛ fɨ́ ni ŋgaŋthɔ yugu ŋa a lɔ njiʼi ntɨnɨ nɛ, nɛnnɛ mfāaŋ mbɨŋ Krishto. 
Nthɛ yie ghɔ, m̈aoŋ jɨ nchwīe ndǐŋ a moŋ piŋ chwīe phɨ, paʼa ǹdɔ ma nuʼuŋ ŋkru frɛ̀i, nnɛ ŋa kiʼi ndǐŋ a gū moŋ phɨ.
