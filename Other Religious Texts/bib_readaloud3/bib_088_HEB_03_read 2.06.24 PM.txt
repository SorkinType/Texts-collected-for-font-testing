Ebree gʊɔɔ.
3.
Naa tɔ ma bɩ, m danyɩnɔ wosogʊɔɔ, Woso y'awɔɔ bir sɔ k'awɔɔ n yɩ a gʊɔɔ. K'a a hɔɔn da Yeezuu ma mɩŋŋa, bala, Woso y'a nyɔɔ k'an bɩ yɩ sɩrakarɛ kʊ wɔɔ n'a pa dam bɩ mannɩbər jɩtaarɛ. 
Sɩra m, Yeezuu zi kʊ Woso a bɔ ʊ k'an a ba bɩ ba taalɛra nɔ ʊ, amba Moyiisi nyɩnta an a ba bɩ m, n n a da Wosoci ʊ, n ʊ: «Moyiisi yɩ taalɛrazaa zibəə kʊ Woso a ka ʊ haay a gʊɔɔ wɔɔ barla bɩ nɔ ʊ.» 
Gʊaa k'a cɛ dɔ b'ɩ daʊrɛ yɩm an lɛ da cɛ bɩ mɩŋŋa m. Ncɩnaaʊ bɩ, Yeezuu a ga lɛbɔʊrɛ gʊta m an lɛ da Moyiisi lɛbɔʊrɛ ra. 
Cɛ k'an ta ʊ haay, gʊɔɔ yɩ ŋ dɔ, an bɔkarɛ, Woso ɩ hɔ woo… da. 
Kʊ Moyiisi nɩ, zibəənyɩ m, ta a ʊ a lɛ ra zi kʊ Woso a ka ʊ haay a gʊɔɔ wɔɔ barla bɩ nɔ ʊ, a zibəə bɩ, hɔ kʊ Woso ʊ k'a hɩ bɩ kasɛtɩ a a ka. 
Bɩ, kʊ Krista nɩ, a m Woso Nyɩ an nɩ dɩgarɛ a Zɩ bɩ har bɩ nyasʊm ma, taalɛra nɔ ʊ. A har bɩ, wɔɔ m, kʊ wɔɔ gɔɔta sɩrakarɛ nɔ ʊ, ʊ n'ʊ heer nyɔɔm hɔ kʊ wɔɔ ta ʊ heer lɛ bɩ nɔ ʊ. 
Naa tɔ ma m, amba Sɛnt-Ɛspri n'a hɩm bɩ, a ʊ: K'awɔɔ Woso leer ma her dɔmɩm naa ʊ, 
a b'a gu ma, amba awɔɔ nyɩnta a bɩsɩ ka ma biisi bɩ nɩ y, amba hinni k'awɔɔ nyɩnta a makra ba poo ʊ bɩ nɩ y. 
Ncɩnaaʊ m Woso n a hɩ, a ʊ: Poo ʊ b'ʊ bɩ, awɔɔ yaabɔɔ wɔɔ mɔɔ kɔ k'a dɩga hɔ kʊ mɔɔ n'a bam bɩ ma, bɩ, ŋ hɔ kʊ mɔɔ ba bɩ yɩ, dɔɔraa bam busi. 
Naa y'a ka mɔɔ nɔ n ma ʊ kan gʊɔɔ nɔɔn duro kɩ, m a hɩ, m ʊ: A nyʊnnyɔɔ ŋ nawʊm a hɔɔndarɛ nɔ ʊ tee…, ŋ n hɔ kʊ mɔɔ lɛ n taa ŋ gʊr zi bɩ jɩ dɔ y. 
Naa y'a ka mɔɔ nɔ n ma ʊ, mɔɔ n m wer si, m ʊ: Ŋ bɩ hoonsire kʊ mɔɔ ʊ kʊ m a ka ŋ ʊ bɩ yɩm fɩɩga y. 
M danyɩnɔ, k'a dundo a zi, k'awɔɔ gɔsɩ bɩ nyɩnta heer bʊnyaa m a n a gu sɩrakarɛ ma, a n a bɔ Woso k'an mɩsɩr b'ʊ y. 
Bɩ, an ta ʊ bɩ, k'a bɩr kʊ heer yom dɔmɩm biyəə, a b'a hɩ a ʊ k'a dɔ booti ma y. K'a bɩr a barɛ ncɩnaaʊ, kʊ gɔsɩ b'a mɩŋŋa to kʊ mimbʊnyaa n bra ba nɩ y. 
Bala, Krista hɔjirlezannɔ n wɔɔ m, bɩ, kʊ wɔɔ gɔɔta sɩra kʊ wɔɔ ka a sɩŋŋɩda ʊ bɩ m kɔɔkɔɔ, an ta an a tɛkka ku. 
Hɔ kʊ Wosoci bɩ n'a hɩm bɩ, mɔɔ m ya m nɩ som la: K'awɔɔ Woso leer ma her, a b'a gu ma, amba awɔɔ nyɩnta biisi a bɩsɩ ka ma, a a gu ma bɩ nɩ y. 
Nkɔɔ ɩ Woso leer bɩ ma, ŋnɩ bɔkarɛ ŋn'a gu ma? Gʊɔɔ kʊ Moyiisi ŋ bɔ Eziptɩ ʊ haay rɔ wɔɔ m. 
Nkɔɔ Woso nɔ ma ʊ kan ŋ kɩ dɔɔraa bam busi bɩ? Gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ mimbʊnyaa ba, ŋnɩ gɩnga poo ʊ b'ʊ rɔ wɔɔ m. 
Woso k'a wer si, a ʊ: «Ŋ bɩ hoonsire kʊ mɔɔ ʊ kʊ m a ka ŋ ʊ bɩ yɩm bɩ.» Nkɔɔ a a hɩ ŋ ma? Gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ bɩsɩ ka ma rɔ wɔɔ a a hɩ ŋ ma. 
Wɔɔ y'a yɩm sɔ denter, kʊ ŋ n sɩra ka bɩ y'a ka, ŋ n a da ma, ŋnɩ hoonsire kʊ Woso ʊ k'a ka ŋ ʊ bɩ yɩ y.
