Hɩnkarɛ Ci.
15.
Mɔɔ m ya, m makra vanta yɩ brama gandaa ʊ, a dɩgama sʊra n ta ʊ. Malɛɛkarɔ saanhɩnarɔ nɩ kʊsɩrɔ saanhɩna m a wɔ ʊ. Kʊsɩrɔ jɛzannɔ m, bala, brɔɔ nɩ Woso heerbusu bɩ zibəə nyambʊrlɛ. 
Mɔɔ m higʊta hi k'a nɩ yɔm pɛkʊ-pɛkʊ an bɔ kʊ ʊ kan kristal kɩ yɩ, an a war kʊ ma kan sɛ kɩ. Mɔɔ yɩ sɔ gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ bʊr hɔturma bɩ ra kan a bɔkʊrɛ bɩ kɩ, kan a tɔ bɩ lɛsarɛnyɩ bɩ kɩ ra rɔ wɔɔ n jɩm higʊta hi k'a nɩ yɔm pɛkʊ-pɛkʊ am kristal hɔ bɩ m bɩ jɩr ma, ŋn'ɩ Woso kɔnnɩrɔ m. 
Ŋ n'ɩ laa kʊ Moyiisi, Woso zibəənyɩ a lɔ bɩ lɔrɛ, sinnyɛɛm bɩ hɔ kɩ, ŋ ʊ: «Zuuba Woso paŋŋa k'a jire ba ʊ zaa, ɩbɩɩ zibəəro wɔɔ gʊta, ŋnɩ mɩŋŋa paan…, ŋn'a ga dɩgama m. Tararɔ haay cir, ɩbɩɩ n'a bam haay bɩ tɩrga, sɩra m sɔ, Zuuba! 
Nka ɩ bɩ nyi barɛ ɩbɩɩ tɔ m, an a lɛ bɔ ʊ? Bala, ɩbɩɩ y'ɩ deem n mɩŋŋa. Tararɔ haay zɛrɛ ŋnɩ b'ɩbɩɩ da ʊ. Bala, ɩbɩɩ dʊdɔkarɛ b'a hɩnka.» 
Bɩ jɛ ʊ bɩ, mɔɔ yɩ Wosocɛ kʊ gʊaasɩbabaa kasɛtɩ pɛɛracɛ bɩ n ta ʊ bɩ n a gʊ brama. 
Malɛɛka saanhɩnarɔ kʊ ŋ nɩ ta kʊsɩ saanhɩna rɔ wɔɔ n bɔ Wosocɛ ʊ b'ʊ. Ŋ n huu furo kʊ ŋ nɩ yɔm nɔ wɔɔ du a ma, ŋnɩ sarma hɔsoaʊrɔ so a cɩ ʊ. 
Hɔɔmɩsɩɩrɔ siro wɔɔ deem n sarma laaga saanhɩnarɔ ka malɛɛkarɔ saanhɩnarɔ wɔɔ ʊ, Woso k'an mɩsɩr dɔɔraa k'a nyarɛ ba ʊ bɩ nɔmaa nɩ ŋ hɩnhan. 
Siŋŋə n Wosocɛ bɩ han, Woso lɛbɔʊrɛ kan a paŋŋa gʊta bɩ kɩ minto. Bɩ gʊaa b'a dam ma, an gasʊ Wosocɛ b'ʊ kʊ kʊsɩ saanhɩnarɔ kʊ malɛɛkarɔ saanhɩnarɔ wɔɔ bʊr ŋ nɩ rɔ wɔɔ n nya y.
