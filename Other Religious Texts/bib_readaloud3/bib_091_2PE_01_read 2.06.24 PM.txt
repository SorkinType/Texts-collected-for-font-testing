Ci hɩɩya hɔ kʊ  PƖYƐƐR  a ba.
1.
Mɔɔ mɩŋŋa Simɔn Pɩyɛɛr, mɔɔ nɩ ci bɩ bam, Yeezuu Krista zibəənyɩ m mɔɔ m, a zibəəzaa m mɔɔ m sɔ. Ci bɩ ba mɔɔ nawʊm awɔɔ ma, awɔɔ k'a sɩrakarɛ n hɔdeem kan wɔɔ hɔ kɩ rɔ wɔɔ. Yeezuu Krista m wɔɔ Woso, a m wɔɔ Bʊmbɔrɛzaa sɔ, a tɩrgabaa y'a ka, awɔɔ n sɩra ka. 
A yɛ mɔɔ nawʊm k'awɔɔ n bɩr Woso dɔm, kan wɔɔ Zuuba Yeezuu kɩ, k'a a da ma a barka kan laafɩɩ kɩ yɩ gʊta. 
Woso zi ba a paŋŋa bɩ m, an a ka wɔɔ nɩ hɔ haay kʊ wɔɔ ʊ ga m ʊ a yɩ, k'ʊ ʊ da ma, ʊ mɩsɩrbaa ba a zuure nɔ ʊ mɩŋŋa bɩ m. A ka k'a ʊ wɔɔ n Krista dɔ bɩ nɔ ʊ. Bɩ mɩŋŋa ɩ wɔɔ bir a lɛbɔʊrɛ kan a paŋŋa kɩ m. 
Yɩ a ʊ, ncɩnaaʊ, an hɔɔ mɩŋŋɔɔ kʊ ŋ nɩ gʊtɔɔ Woso n ŋ ler bɔ rɔ wɔɔ ka wɔɔ ʊ, ŋ ka a ʊ, k'awɔɔ n a da ma a a yɔ lɛtaamarɔ bʊnyaarɔ kʊ ŋ nɩ durnya ʊ, ŋn'ɩ gʊɔɔ zaar bam nɔ wɔɔ m. K'a wɔ ʊ, a Woso mɩŋŋa mɩsɩrbaa ba. 
Naa tɔ ma bɩ, k'a a tɔ ʊ paan… a mɩsɩrbaa zore na a sɩrakarɛ bɩ ma, a Woso dɔrɛ na mɩsɩrbaa zore bɩ ma. 
A a tɔ ʊ gʊta, a a da a mɩŋŋa ma, k'a tʊntɔʊrɛ na daamɩŋŋama bɩ ma, k'a mɩsɩrbaa k'a Woso nɔ yɩ na tʊntɔʊrɛ bɩ ma, 
k'a a tɔ ʊ sɔ, a bakʊmmɩŋŋa na mɩsɩrbaa k'a Woso nɔ yɩ bɩ ma, k'a ŋʊarɛ na bakʊmmɩŋŋa bɩ ma. 
K'awɔɔ ɩ hɔɔ nɔɔn duro bam tee…, ŋ y'a karɛ awɔɔ n ziro ba gʊta a bɩr wɔɔ Zuuba Yeezuu Krista dɔm tee…. 
Bala, gʊaa kʊ hɔɔ nɔɔn duro ba zi bɩ, bʊʊ n a zaa m, an n'a wɔ dɩndam, a tɔ ba a nɔ ʊ dɔ, a ʊ, n y'a mimbʊnyaarɔ gʊngʊ a ma biisi y. 
Naa tɔ ma bɩ, m danyɩnɔ, Woso y'awɔɔ bɔ ʊ, k'a a tɔ ʊ, a gɔɔta a zuure nɔ ʊ kɔɔkɔɔ. K'awɔɔ ba ncɩnaaʊ, awɔɔ b'a dom fɩɩga y. 
Yɩ a nawʊrɛ ncɩnaaʊ, Woso n zaa ka awɔɔ ʊ fasɩ, awɔɔ n gasʊ wɔɔ Zuuba Yeezuu Krista kʊ wɔɔ Bʊmbɔrɛzaa m bɩ cirbəə kʊ k'a nyarɛ ba ʊ b'ʊ. 
Naa tɔ ma m, mɔɔ n'a ŋʊam kʊ m bɩr hɔɔ nɔɔn duro tɔ darɛ awɔɔ nɔ ʊ, baa k'awɔɔ ŋ dɔ kara, an nɩ zum sɩra naa do ʊ kɔɔkɔɔ bɩ kɩ. 
Bɩ, mɔɔ y'a gɩngam m ʊ, a a ga m kʊ m bɩr hɔɔ nɔɔn duro tɔ darɛ awɔɔ nɔ ʊ tee…, kʊ mɔɔ gɔɔta m m mɩsɩr naa dɔmɩm ʊ, k'awɔɔ b'a zu a ʊ y. 
Mɔɔ dɔ mɔɔ ʊ, mɔɔ hʊrlɛ a zɔ durnya naa ʊ, amba wɔɔ Zuuba Yeezuu Krista nyɩnta an a hɩ mɔɔ m bɩ m. 
Bɩ, mɔɔ yɩ m tɔŋ ʊ, k'an bɩr yɩrɛ ɩ ʊ, mɔɔ baʊrɛ jɛ ʊ, awɔɔ a da ma dɔmɩm haay nɔ ʊ a hɔdɩndarɛrɔ nɔɔn duro tɔ da a nɔ ʊ. 
Bala, yo bɛɛ wɔɔ ʊ ŋʊaar dɔrɛ noonno ma, ʊ wɔɔ Zuuba Yeezuu Krista k'a nɩ zɛm kan paŋŋa gʊta kɩ bɩ dɩnda awɔɔ nɩ y. A mɩŋŋa yɩ wɔɔ ʊ ʊ zim a lɛbɔʊrɛ gʊta nɔ ʊ. 
Wakatɩ kʊ Zɩ Woso daʊrɛ kan lɛbɔʊrɛ kɩ ka ʊ bɩ, wɔɔ nyɩnta b'ʊ. Woso mɩŋŋa nyɩnta a lɛbɔʊrɛ gʊta nɔ ʊ, an meer ba kan kɩ, a ʊ: «Mɔɔ Nyɩ m naa do, mɔɔ n'a ŋʊam gʊta.» 
Nyɩnta kʊ wɔɔ ʊ kan kɩ ci mɩŋŋa bɩ burə ʊ bɩ, wɔɔ leer naa do k'a a to brama bɩ ma ʊ zim. 
Ncɩnaaʊ bɩ, wɔɔ sɩra ka gʊta, ʊ ʊ, lemim kʊ wosolɛsinnɩsorazannɔ wɔɔ a hɩ bɩ, sɩra m. A a ga m, k'awɔɔ n lemim bɩ si mɩŋŋa, bala, fɩtɩrɛ m, an nɩ yɔm monsigə ʊ, k'a gɔɔta a lemim bɩ si kʊ lɛɛgure dɔmɩm n a ku, kʊ Krista k'a bɔ kʊ ʊ kan dɔgta monyaa kɩ bɩ n bʊr, an lɛɛ gu awɔɔ heerlɔ wɔɔ ʊ. 
A n cem haay bɩ, k'a dɔ a ʊ, gɔsɩ b'a dam ma an wosolɛsinnɩsorameerbaarɔ kʊ ŋ nɩ Wosoci b'ʊ rɔ wɔɔ jɩ dɩnda a zim nɩ y. 
Bala, gʊaa bɛɛ hɔɔn bɔ ʊ a zim, k'a wosolɛsinnɩsora meer ba y, fɩɩga. Bɩ, *Sɛnt-Ɛspri y'a ka gʊɔɔ n wɔ ʊ, ŋnɩ Woso lɛ si, ŋnɩ so ra.
