Ebree gʊɔɔ.
8.
Hɔ kʊ wɔɔ ʊ k'ʊ a hɩ bɩ ba n nɩ: Mannɩbər jɩtaarɛ kʊ wɔɔ mim hɩ bɩ, wɔɔ n ta m denter, a ɩ nyɩntam cirbəə nɔ ʊ, Woso k'a a so hɔ haay ʊ bɩ jɩr ʊ arzana ʊ. 
A mannɩbər jɩtaarɛ zi an n'a bam b'ʊ Woso taʊrɛ nɔ ʊ, pɛɛracɛ paan… kʊ Woso a dɔ, gʊɔɔ bɛɛ y'a dɔ bɩ nɔ ʊ. 
Mannɩbər haay zibəə n ganwʊrɛrɔ kan mannɩrɔ kɩ ka Woso ʊ m, a a ga m sɔ, kʊ wɔɔ mannɩbər jɩtaarɛ bɩ n bɩr hɔ m k'a ka Woso ʊ sɔ. 
Kʊ durnya naa do gʊaa nɩ, a b'a dam ma an yɩ mannɩbər y. Bala, mannɩbənno ta ʊ kara, ŋn'ɩ mannɩrɔ kam Woso ʊ amba Moyiisi lɛrɔ a hɩ bɩ m. 
Bɩ, mannɩbənno wɔɔ duro zibəə bɩ, mannɩbər zibəə paan… k'a nɩ arzana ʊ bɩ bɔkʊrɛ m, a makra m. A do m ba Moyiisi nyɩnta k'a pɛɛracɛ dɔ a ka Woso ʊ bɩ m. Woso a hɩ m, a ʊ: Ɩ tɔ ʊ k'ɩ pɛɛracɛ bɩ dɔ k'an yɩ hɔdeem kan mɔɔ n a hɩnka ɩbɩɩ m ci bɩ burə ʊ bɩ kɩ. 
Bɩ kɛɛrɛ naa, mannɩbər zibəə k'a lɛ da brɔɔ hɔ bɩ ra gʊta bɩ Yeezuu n n'a bam. Bala, am gʊaasɩbabaa k'an mɩŋŋa an lɛ da bɩ zɛkʊrazaa, gʊaasɩbabaa bɩ do jɩr bɩ, lerbɔrɛrɔ mɩŋŋɔɔ kʊ ŋ lɛ da a yo ma. 
Kʊ nyɩnta lɛɛkarɛ gʊaasɩbabaa b'ʊ zore, a bɩ yɩm pakra kʊ gʊaasɩbabaa k'an mɩŋŋa an lɛ da n sompi a yaa ʊ y. 
Bɩ, Woso nyaa ba kan a gʊɔɔ wɔɔ kɩ, ŋ kʊrɔmarɔ tɔ ma, a ʊ: Dɔmɩnnɔ ɩ zɛm, mɔɔ m bɩ gʊaasɩbabaa daa dam Israyɛl gʊɔɔ wɔɔ ma, kan Ziida gʊɔɔ wɔɔ kɩ. 
Gʊaasɩbabaa bɩ do bɩ bɩr yɩrɛ, am gʊaasɩbabaa kʊ mɔɔ da ŋ zɩrɔ ma bɩ nɩ y, hinni kʊ mɔɔ ŋ gaa ŋ wɔ ma, m bɔ ŋ nɩ Eziptɩ ʊ bɩ, ŋ n ta a lɛ ra gʊaasɩbabaa bɩ do nɔ ʊ y, bɩ tɔ ma m, mɔɔ nɩ m bɔ ŋ ʊ. Mɔɔ m Zuuba, mɔɔ ɩ meer bɩ ba. 
Dɔmɩnnɔ nɔɔn duro jɛ ʊ, gʊaasɩbabaa kʊ mɔɔ n n'a dam Israyɛl gʊɔɔ wɔɔ ma bɩ, ba n nɩ: Mɔɔ yɩ m lɛrɔ wɔɔ darɛ ŋ hɔɔn ʊ, m ŋ gʊrsɩra ba ŋ heer ʊ. Mɔɔ yɩrɛ ŋ Woso, ŋnɩ yɩ mɔɔ gʊɔɔ. 
Ŋ gɔsɩ bɩ nyɩntaŋ ʊ k'a a ka ma k'a hɔ dɩnda a mim nɩ y, gɛɛ a danyɩ m an bɩr a hɩrɛ a ʊ, a ka k'ɩ Zuuba dɔ. Bala, ŋ haay mɔɔ dɔm, an a sa ŋ poore ma an ta m ŋ gʊtɔɔ ma. 
Mɔɔ yɩ ŋ kʊrɔmarɔ sugur karɛ ŋ ʊ, mɔɔ bɩ ŋ mimbʊnyaarɔ tɔ darɛ m nɔ ʊ y. 
Woso k'a bʊr an nɩ gʊaasɩbabaa daa mim dɩndam bɩ, naa ʊ k'a hɩ a ʊ, lɛɛkarɛ gʊaasɩbabaa bɩ ba a ʊ hɔgusire. A n bɔkarɛ hɔ k'a gusi, b'a zibəə ba ʊ bɩ, a nyaanɩm zɔɔ.
