Ebree gʊɔɔ.
2.
K'a a yo Woso n meer ba kan wɔɔ kɩ a Nyɩ bɩ lɛ m bɩ, ʊ hɔ k'a hɩ wɔɔ m bɩ nyasʊ kɔɔkɔɔ gʊta, kʊ n bɛɛ m, wɔɔ nyaanɩm. 
Lemim kʊ malɛɛkarɔ nyɩnta ŋnɩ b'a pa da bɩ, a a zi ba fasɩ, gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ n yɛ ma y, ŋnɩ bɩsɩ ka ma rɔ wɔɔ, brɔɔ a bɩsɩ bɩ karɛ fɩr yɩ amba a a ga m bɩ m. 
Bɩ, wɔɔ sɔ, kʊ wɔɔ n nyasʊ bʊmbɔrɛ gʊta maam-maam naa do nɩ, Woso wɔɔ dʊdɔ kam sɔ, wɔɔ bɩ bɔm lɛ nɩ y. Zuuba mɩŋŋa ɩ lɛɛ ka an bʊmbɔrɛ bɩ pa da, bɩ jɛ ʊ, gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ y'a ma rɔ wɔɔ n a mɩŋŋabaa k'an ta ʊ hɩnka wɔɔ m. 
Woso mɩŋŋa nɩ ŋ kasɛtɩ bɩ karɛ gore da ʊ, hoserlo paŋŋazannɔ barɛ nɔ ʊ, kan makra do y'a hʊnhɔ kɩ m. A *Sɛnt-Ɛspri ganwʊrɛrɔ jir amba a lɛ n taa bɩ m. 
Durnya daa kʊ wɔɔ n'a mim dɩndam bɩ, malɛɛkarɔ bɛɛ Woso ŋ da ʊ, kʊ ŋ n'a so ʊ y, a Nyɩ b'a da ʊ. 
Bɩ, Wosoci bɩ lɛɛ niŋŋə ʊ, n y'a hɩ, n ʊ: Woso ra, bɔ m gʊaa m, ɩbɩɩ n'ɩ hɔɔn dam ma? Gʊaa nyɔɔdʊ n bɔ m, ɩbɩɩ n nɩ nyasʊm nɩ? 
Ɩbɩɩ ka an nɩ malɛɛkarɔ taa dɔmɩm poore nɔ ʊ, ɩ bɔkarɛ ɩ b'a gore da ʊ, ɩ lɛbɔʊrɛ ka ʊ gʊta. 
Ɩbɩɩ ka an a so hɔ haay ʊ, an nɩ hɔ haay burə ʊ. Kʊ n y'a hɩ, n ʊ: ‹Woso a ka gʊaa nɩ hɔ haay burə ʊ bɩ, naa k'a hɩ, a ʊ, a y'a so hɔ haay ʊ, hɔsɩ n gɔɔta k'a bɛɛ y'a so ʊ y.› A n bɔkarɛ, dɩɩtaa naa, dɩgarɛ nɔ ʊ, gʊaa bɛɛ y'a so hɔ haay ʊ y. 
Bɩ hɔ kʊ wɔɔ n'a yɩm bɩ n naa do: Woso a ka Yeezuu n a ba poore malɛɛkarɔ taa dɔmɩm poore nɔ ʊ, k'an bɩr yɩrɛ ɩ ʊ, Woso barka bɩ tɔ ma, a ga gʊɔɔ haay minto. Bɩ wɔɔ y'a yɩm kɛɛrɛ an nɩ lɛbɔʊrɛ kan daʊrɛ gʊta kɩ m, fɩr k'a yɩ a zɛ bɩ nɔ ʊ bɩ tɔ ma. 
Woso ɩ hɔ haay da, ŋn'a bɩncɛrɔ ʊ, a a ŋʊa kʊ gʊɔɔ gʊta n yɩ a nyɩnɔ, ŋnɩ nyɩnta a lɛbɔʊrɛ nɔ ʊ. A ga a ʊ m denter, k'an a ka kʊ Yeezuu n fɩr yɩ, k'an wɔ ʊ an yɩ gʊɔɔ bʊmbɔrɛzaa k'a a ga m. Bala, Yeezuu nɩ karɛ ŋ lɛɛ kʊ ŋ nɩ bʊmbɔrɛ bɩ yɩ. 
Yeezuu nɩ gʊɔɔ mimbʊnyaarɔ gʊrɛ ŋ ma, a mɩŋŋa kan gʊɔɔ k'a nɩ ŋ mimbʊnyaarɔ gʊrɛ ŋ ma rɔ wɔɔ kɩ, ŋ Zɩ deem. Bɩ y'a ka, Yeezuu nɩ ŋ birle a danyɩnɔ b'a nyannɩ ba nɩ y. 
A a hɩ denter, a ʊ: Woso ra, mɔɔ y'ɩ mim dɩndarɛ m danyɩnɔ m, m m lɛ bɔ ɩ ʊ, sokʊmagʊɔɔ haay lɛɛ. 
A a hɩ sɔ, a ʊ: Mɔɔ yom Woso ma. A n a ya, an a hɩ sɔ, a ʊ: Mɔɔ ba n nɩ kan nyɩnɔ kʊ Woso ŋ ka mɔɔ ʊ rɔ wɔɔ kɩ. 
Nyɩnɔ k'a n'a hɩrɛ ŋ ma rɔ wɔɔ, gʊɔɔ m, ŋnɩ yɩ mɛsim kan ma kɩ nɔ ʊ, Woso Nyɩ bɩ mɩŋŋa a ba am ŋ hɔ bɩ m an nyɩnta gʊaa sɔ am ŋ hɔ bɩ m. A ba a ʊ, ncɩnaaʊ, k'a Sʊtaana zaar ba, bɩ k'a a so zɛ paŋŋa ʊ bɩ tɔ ma. 
K'a a da ma, a gʊɔɔ kʊ lɔnnɔ nɩ ŋ mɩ, nyi kʊ ŋ n'a bam zɛ m a mɩsɩrbaa tɛkka ma rɔ wɔɔ yɔ. 
Bala, Yeezuu bɩ, malɛɛkarɔ bɛɛ an n'a darɛ ŋ han y, Abraham nyɩnɔ wɔɔ an n'a darɛ ŋ han. 
Bɩ y'a ka, an yɩ pakra k'an a ba am a danyɩnɔ wɔɔ m hɔ haay nɔ ʊ, k'a wɔ ʊ, a yɩ ŋ mannɩbər jɩtaarɛ kʊ taalɛrazaa m, a m cicirzɛrɛzaa, a zibəə bɩ nɔ ʊ Woso lɛɛ. K'a gʊɔɔ mimbʊnyaarɔ sugur yɩ, a ka ŋ ʊ. 
Bɩ, kɛɛrɛ naa, a a da ma an a da gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ nɩ makra lɛɛ rɔ wɔɔ han, bala, a mɩŋŋa makra yɩ, an fɩr yɩ.
