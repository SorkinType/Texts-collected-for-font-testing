1 TESALONIKAFƆ.
3.
Nya se ɛnlɛ bo bulǝ se boensinyǝ mi tɔ esoo, bóse nya uyi boǝ bo inuǝ lǝ bɔtsyimɛ lǝ Atene. 
Nya bótsyese Timoteo lǝ ubǝ okpe mi nkɛ alɛ, nya lǝ ɔwunsǝ ko mi lǝ mi bofokanɔ ǝsuǝ. Uyifo bo ɔyimisami wǝ laawunsǝko bo lǝ kanantoyifo ku Kristo eto Ditiki Sɛɛ eto bɔtɛyi ǝsuǝ. 
Bo nsusu ni bǝnkǝ boanla bǝ bulǝ ku kanyi ka bɛnlɛ bunyǝ lǝ elo mi kuutsyuǝ saa ani. Mi kosate fe biyi koko biǝ bulǝ ku kanyibunyǝ ni nnya leetu nwǝ ke ntǝkǝko Kristo. 
Biokloma biǝ lefe nǝ bɔkɛ mi lǝ ɔflɔɔ, bútǝ mi bote katɔnsǝ boǝnkǝ bulǝ kpǝ ebutu mi. Nya fe kase binyii, íbǝ itǝ nko sitinti. 
Nyamfo eso míntsyese Timoteo minkǝ ubǝ obe lǝ bisi bińyǝ keklǝkǝ lǝ mi bofokanɔ ǝsuǝ. Itsyise mmuǝ utu nnɔ. Nya mɔ sikpitete ni bǝnkǝ ǝtsyuǝ oboale ǝmǝ ébubǝ óbiasǝ mi nsusu itǝ bǝ bo bokplatsya kenke ǝbǝ íyifo bimba. 
Nya se Timoteo dísinkli ubǝǝ, ɔ́tɛyi bo ku disuǝyuǝ nkǝ bisi bińyǝ kekleke lǝ mi bofokanɔ ku mi lelabi itǝ mi bǝtsyuǝ insi enso. Ɔ́tɛyi bo tsya nkǝ biakloma bo tinti nya bɛnlɛ kusu bobe biǝ biǝnyǝ bo fe kase bo tsya nlɛ bola boǝ boǝnyǝ mi. 
Lǝ nyamfo esoo, mɔ bayimi, ite bǝ bɔlɛ kanyi bunyǝ nya asa nyamfo lekle bo tsyaa, mi bofokanɔ aakpe bo disuǝyuǝ kǝyuǝsǝ bo ketu. 
Se bɔ́nɔ kase bińyǝ kekleke lǝ Bonamute eto bofokanɔ esuǝǝ, mfe fɛ nkpǝ fɔfɔ dídufǝ bo lǝ ǝsuǝ nɛ. 
Boǝmbofo Onanto ani bɔsɔ yooyo disuǝyuǝ nǝ bɔnlɛ bunyǝ lǝ wǝ ǝnǝmi lǝ mi disi eso. 
Bɔlɛ Boanto Onanto lekpakpa bɔlɛ ketsye ku kakɔmi boǝ utǝ lǝ busi botsya lǝ bokpe mi nkɛale lǝ mi bofokanɔ ǝsuǝ. 
Nyaso mɔmɔɔ, Boanto Onanto kosate ku Bonamute Yesu lǝ basini bo kusu bǝ lǝ bofo mi ɔflɔ bubǝ. 
Nya Bonamute lǝ utǝ kusu bǝ mi lelabi itǝ mi bǝtsyuǝ ku betidi bamba lǝ difui lekpe fe kase boala mi ke. Nya lǝ bela betidi bamba tsya fe kase boala mi. 
Nyamfo luutǝ Boanto Onanto lookpe mi ǝsuǝale, ulǝkǝ mi utsyiko lǝ Obonsam eto esu kenke ǝsuǝ nya lǝ uyifo mi kɛnkɛ lǝ wǝ ǝnǝmi isu ese diyi nǝ Bonamute Yesu luusinkli ubǝ kawunsiǝ kamfo ku wǝ mba kenke diyifo kɛnkɛ.
