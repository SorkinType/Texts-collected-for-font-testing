Fǔoŋ nnu kiʼɛ pɔ ŋa, ǹchhɔ ndɨ̄gəɨ vəɨ ŋa p̂əɨ fɛ pa píe, pa lúoŋ, yūa nu chà ni tōo nu ni Minnwi nɔ ligi ŋguoŋ ŋgwa ghao. 
Pəɨ luoŋ Minnwi mfɛ ni pa fùoŋ pugu pa ŋguoŋ ŋgwa ŋa pugu pɔ moŋ pa mɛmmɛ nthɨŋkwo nɛ, nnɛ ŋa minthɛ pia chhɔ chɔmbhi ghɨghrɨ pugu pa juju, ŋa a fāʼo pɔ́gɔ Minnwi ndɔ nchɨɨ moŋ ŋguoŋ puoŋ shɛndaoŋ nɛ. 
Yei nnu pwa ndɔ mfɛ pwatua ni Minnwi mbɔ Ŋkwe via, 
ŋa a khwā ŋa ŋguoŋ ŋgwa lūgu ndɔ nji nnu shini nɛ. 
Nthɛ ŋa a wuʼɔ taʼa Minnwi ndɔ a wuʼɔ mbɔ taʼa ŋoŋ ŋa a thi shɨna Minnwi pugu pa ŋgwamishua, mbɔ ŋoŋ ŋa pi mɛ̄iŋ vi ni Krishto Jishɔ nɛ. 
Krishto Jishɔ ghɔ ni mfɛ chɔmbhi yi ŋkhu nɔ nchuoŋ thɔ ŋguoŋ ŋgwa. Yie ghɔ ni mbɔ lì moŋ ndɨɨ kaʼokaʼo nɔ nōoŋ nu ŋa Minnwi tāʼa nu ŋa shesheŋoŋ lūgu. 
Nthɛ ŋa yei nnu ŋa pi ni nchuʼɔ a nɔ ghɔ ŋa m̀bɔ ŋgaŋ chīi chrà ndɔ mbɨnɨ mbɔ ŋgaŋ ntaoŋ Jishɔ, ǹthɔ nchhu pi nnu shini, ǹdɔ njiʼi ndɛʼi pi ŋgiɛŋ, mbɔ pi masha pa ŋgwa ŋa pugu lɔ mbɔ pa Juu moŋ piŋ pugu pa nnu shini nɛ. 
Nthɛ yie ghɔ, ǹtāʼa nu ŋa pa pimbia moŋ ŋguoŋ lɨʼɨ ghao luoŋ Minnwi, mbɨʼɨ mbhɔ taoŋtaoŋ tɔthɨ ki lɔ njiʼi mfāʼo pɨgɨtua ki mindoundou. 
Wuʼɔ nɛnnɛ, p̂iŋgɛ chwīe noŋ yugu shiʼi ni pa ndhwí ŋa a kaʼokaʼo ndɔ nchɨɨ nɛ, ki lɔ mbɔ pi ni chwīe nu ntou ŋgɨ̀nɔ thɔ pugu, pugu pa shɨ́ga laoŋgo pugu pa pichəɨ pa shɨ́ga tɨnɨnchuoŋ ki pa mɛmmɛ ndhwí tɨnɨnchuoŋ. 
A pie ŋa pugu chwīe noŋ yugu pi ni fàʼa shiʼi, mbɔ yaoŋ ŋa a kāʼo ni piŋgɛ ŋa pugu ghà ŋgaʼo Minnwi nɛ. 
P̂iŋgɛ lɛrɛ laŋ, njɛʼi yaoŋ ni ŋguoŋ yaʼo nchò ghao. 
Ǹdɔ njiʼi mbiŋ ŋa miŋgwɛ yɛʼi ki ŋa a fāʼo njɨ mbɨŋ mimbia, a ka pie pi ŋa a lɛrɛ laŋ. 
Nthɛ ŋa Minnwi ni fǔoŋ mbuoŋ pi Adaŋ ŋkaoŋ maa mbra ni Ive. 
Ndɔ pi shini ndɔ mfɨ̄ɨ pi Adaŋ, pi ni mfɨ̄ɨ pi miŋgwɛ ghɔ a gū ŋgaŋ pəʼɨ gɨ́. 
Ndɔ miŋgwɛ shi ndūgu ntɔgɔ pi ŋkuoŋ phi nu muuŋ nɔ haʼaŋ pugu ntīi shii moŋ piŋ pugu pa khwǎ, ni chɔmbhi taoŋtaoŋ pugu pa wɛ̄iŋ nu noŋ.
