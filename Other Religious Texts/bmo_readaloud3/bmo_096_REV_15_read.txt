Nnu Nōoŋ.
15.
Nnɛ, ǹjəɨ yichəɨ lì po, a pɔ mmɛ yi ndɔ mbɔ yi ghraoghrao, khwachəɨ pa ghaŋ ntaoŋ Minnwi ni khwachəɨ pa ŋkwaŋ ŋgəʼɨŋgəʼɨ, mbɔ pa ndugwi pi nthɛ ŋa a ndugwi njiŋ nōoŋ nu pɨgɨtua Minnwi. 
Ǹjəɨ yichəɨ yaoŋ ŋa a pɔ nɔ mmɛ ŋkhǐ ŋgrashi nyaŋ ni móŋoŋ. Mbɨnɨ nuʼuŋ njəɨ ŋgwa pɛ ŋa pugu ghaʼa minyieŋ ghɔ pugu yaoŋ ŋa pi chwīe a pɔ nɔ ju nɛ, ni ndigi yaoŋ ŋa a thi nɔ ligi yi nɛ a thi yəɨ ŋkhǐ ŋgrashi ghɔ, ni yaoŋ paʼo lóu Minnwi mbhɔ pugu. 
Pugu yrāo ŋkhǐ Mushi, mbɔ muuŋ fàʼa Minnwi pugu pa ŋkhǐ Muuŋ Minjɨɨ ghɔ, nchhu nu ŋa, “Fàʼa pɔ ghaʼo ndɔ mbɔ yi ghraoghrao, ma Minnwi Taathɔ, ŋa a chaa ŋguoŋ njɨ nɛ. Ɔ nōoŋ fàʼa yɔ yi ndɨndɨ ndɔ mbɔ yi shishini, ma Fùoŋ pa taoŋ taoŋ! 
Yo Taathɔ, a gɔ̌ ŋa a shi mieŋ ki pɔgɔ ndɔ ŋgaʼo ligi yɔ? A gɔ̀ təʼɨ ghɔ mbɔ yi taoŋtaoŋ. Ŋguoŋ pa laʼataoŋ shi nthɔ ŋgaʼo ligi yɔ nthɛ ŋa ɔ ni nōoŋ pa mbha fàʼa ndɨndɨ pɔ.” 
Nnu pei ni ŋga ntɔgɔ, m̀bɛʼi, Nda Minnwi po pɔ yi wo, mbɔ ntǎa ŋa Minnwi ni nūʼɔŋ gɨ́ kɨ̀na yi moŋ ghɔ nɛ. 
Llɔ moŋ Nda Minnwi ghɔ, khwachəɨ pa ghaŋ ntaoŋ Minnwi ni khwachəɨ pa ŋgəʼɨŋgəʼɨ taoŋ moŋ ghɔ, mbɔ moŋ ya ŋkiɛŋ ndhwí fhu chiachia ni khwachəɨ pa ŋgwara laoŋgo ŋkuoŋ njùʼɔ yugu. 
Yichəɨ taʼa yi shɨna khwɛ miyaoŋ maoŋ pighɔ ni mfɛ khwachəɨ kumbu ŋa pi chwīe ni laoŋgo ni khwachəɨ ghaŋ ntaoŋ Minnwi pighɔ, pɨgɨtua lɨnaoŋ moŋ ghɔ mbɔ pɨgɨtua Minnwi ŋa a chɔmbhi ki ndugwi yi nɛ. 
Nda Minnwi ni ndɨnaoŋ ni ndindhi ndighaʼo Minnwi pugu pa ghrɨ́ yi, ti a kaŋ ni nthɛ paʼa yichəɨ ŋoŋ lɔ nii moŋ Nda Minnwi ghɔ ti khwachəɨ pa ŋgəʼɨŋgəʼɨ khwachəɨ pa ghaŋ ntaoŋ Minnwi pighɔ nuʼuŋ mmɛ.
