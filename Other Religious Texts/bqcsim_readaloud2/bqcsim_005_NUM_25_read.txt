Naokɛa.
25.
Isailiↄ ku Sitiũ, ↄ̃ aa nà gbãsĩkɛawa ń Mↄabu nↄɛↄ. 
Aa Isailiↄ sìsi ń tã́a sa'o guu, ↄ̃ aa pↄblè aa kùlɛ ń tã́aↄnɛ. 
Màa Isailiↄ nàkↄ̃wa ń Baali Peooo, ↄ̃ Dii pↄ pàńzi. 
A ò Mↄiziɛ: Isaili kíaↄ kãaa píi, ní ń loo ma aɛ ĩatɛ̃ gbãai, kɛ́ ma pↄ e wee Isailiↄwa. 
Mↄizi ò Isaili dↄaanaↄnɛ: Á baade a gbɛ̃́ pↄ́ aa nàkↄ̃wa ń Baali Peoooↄ dɛdɛ. 
Isailie Madiã nↄɛ sɛ̀ mↄ̀ò a bɛ Mↄizi ń Isailiↄ píi wáa, gↄↄ pↄ́ aalɛ ↄ́ↄlↄ kpaaũkpɛ kpɛɛlɛ. 
Kɛ́ Ɛleazaa nɛ́ Finɛasi, Aalona tↄũna è màa, à fɛ̀lɛ ń guu à sↄ̃na sɛ̀, 
à gɛ̃̀ gↄ̃ɛpizi a kpɛ́u, ↄ̃ à Isaili gↄ̃ɛpi zↄ̃̀ à ń naaa ḿpla ḿpii, gↄ̃ɛpi ń nↄɛpio, ↄ̃ gagyã pↄ́ kà Isailiↄgu zɛ̀. 
Gbɛ̃́ pↄ́ gàga ń gyãpioↄ gbɛ̃ↄn ↄ̀aasↄsↄo lɛɛ baasↄo mɛ̀ndosaiɛ. (24.000) 
Dii yã'ò Mↄiziɛ à mɛ̀: 
Finɛasi mɛ́ tò ma pↄ wèe Isailiↄwa. À zɛ̀ gì ma yã́ musu, ↄ̃ a tò ma pↄ wèemá. A yã́i mi Isailiↄ midɛ ma pↄfɛ̃a guuo. 
A yã́i tò oɛ̀ ma bàa aↄ kuaànↄ, aà kua iↄ na. 
Aàpi ń a buiↄ aaↄ dɛ sa'onaↄ ũ gↄↄpiiɛ, kɛ́ à zɛ̀ gì ma yã́ musu yã́i, ↄ̃ bee tò ma Isailiↄ kɛ̃̀. 
Isaili pↄ́ wa dɛ̀ ń Madiã nↄɛopi tↄ́n Zimli, Simɛↄ buiↄ uabele Salu nɛ́ɛ. 
Madiã nↄɛ pↄ́ wa dɛ̀pi tↄ́n Kↄzɛbi, Madiãↄ uabele Zuu nɛ́ɛ. 
Dii yã'ò Mↄiziɛ à mɛ̀: 
À fɛlɛ ń Madiãↄ, í ń dɛdɛ, 
asa aa íbɛlɛsɛ̀ánↄ ń kↄ̃nikɛaɛ́oɛ. Aa kↄ̃nikɛ̀ɛ́ Peoo yã́ musu, Madiãↄ kíae nɛnↄɛ Kↄzɛbi pↄ́ wa dɛ̀ gↄↄ pↄ́ gagyã kàágu Peoo yã́ musuɛ.
