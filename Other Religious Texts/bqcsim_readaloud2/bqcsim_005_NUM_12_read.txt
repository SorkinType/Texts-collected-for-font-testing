12.
Miliaũ ń Aalonao Mↄizi tàae è Etiopi nↄ pↄ́ a sɛ̀ yã́ musu, asa à Etiopi nↄ sɛ̀ɛ. 
Aa mɛ̀: Mↄizi ado ↄ̃ Dii dìlɛ à a yã'òa? A tò wá ò sↄ̃o lé? Dii yã́pi mà. 
Mↄizi sↄ̃ nↄ̀sɛdoũdeɛ, aà sáa kú dṹnia guuo. 
Wegↄ̃ↄ Dii ò Mↄizi ń Aalonao ń Miliaũoɛ: À bↄlɛ gɛ́ kpaaũkpɛu á gbɛ̃ↄn àaↄ̃. Ɔ̃ aa gɛ̀ ń gbɛ̃ↄn àaↄ̃ ḿpii. 
Dii pìla tɛlu guu à zɛ̀ kpɛ́pi kpɛɛlɛ. À Aalona ń Miliaũo sìsi, ↄ̃ aa sↄ̃̀aàzi ḿpla ḿpii. 
A ònɛ́: À ma yãma. Tó ma ãnabie kú á guu, mi bↄ mↄwà wɛ́pungu'ea guu, mi yã'oɛ̀ nana guuɛ. 
Kási a dɛ màa ń ma zↄ̀blena Mↄizioo. Zĩkɛn náaideɛ ma ua. 
Wi yãbↄ kↄ̃ lɛ́u. Mili yã'oɛ̀ ma a bakũaanakɛ̀ɛ̀o. Ì wɛsi ma taalɛ. Àkɛa i ma zↄ̀blena Mↄizi tàae'ea vĩakɛo ni? 
Ɔ̃ Dii pↄ pàńzi à ɛ̀a tà. 
Kɛ́ tɛlupi gò kpɛ́pia, kusu lì Miliaũla gↄ̃̀ↄ, à puakũ̀ lán buawa. Kɛ́ Aalona gwà, a è kusu lìaàla, 
ↄ̃ a ò Mↄiziɛ: N yã́ na Baa! Ńsu to wà mìsaikɛ tàae pↄ́ wá kɛ̀ gbɛ'eo. 
Ńsu to aàↄ dɛ lán nɛ́ pↄ́ wa ì gɛ̀, aà mɛgueↄ yaikɛ̀wao. 
Mↄizi wiilɛ̀ Diiwa à mɛ̀: Lua, n yã́ na! Ǹ aà gbã́gbã. 
Ɔ̃ Dii òɛ̀: Tó aà mae lɛ́'ikà aà oawa, aↄ dɛɛ̀ wíyã ũ gↄↄ soplao lé? Wà aà ya bↄ bòo kpɛ gↄↄ sopla gĩa, wi gbasa wà aà sí. 
Miliaũ ku bòo kpɛ e gↄↄ sopla. Isailiↄ i dazɛuo e wà ɛ̀a wà aà sì. 
Bee gbɛa aa fɛ̀lɛ Azelo aa gɛ̀ bòokpà Palana guwaiwaiu.
