Isailiↄ fɛ̀lɛ Sini guwaiwaiu ḿpii, aalɛ bòokpakpa lá Dii ònɛ́wa, ↄ̃ aa gɛ̀ bòokpà Lefidiũ. Aai í e we wa mìo, 
ↄ̃ aa zↄadↄ̀ Mↄiziwa aa mɛ̀: Wá gba í wà mi. A ònɛ́: Bↄ́yãi álɛ zↄadↄai? Àkɛa álɛ Dii lɛ́wɛi? 
Kɛ́ imi lɛ́ ń dɛ, ↄ̃ aa zↄadↄ̀ Mↄiziwa aa mɛ̀: Bↄ́yãi n wá bↄ́lɛ Egipii? Ń ye ǹ wá dɛdɛ ń imio ń wá nɛ́ↄ ń wá pↄtuoↄ yã̀? 
Ɔ̃ Mↄizi wiilɛ̀ Diiwa à mɛ̀: Kpelewa má kɛ ń gbɛ̃́piↄi? À gↄ̃̀ yↄↄ aa ma pápa ń gbɛo wà ma dɛɛ. 
Dii òɛ̀: Isaili gbɛ̃zↄ̃ↄↄ sɛ́lɛ, í dↄaa gbɛ̃́piↄnɛ. Ǹ n go pↄ́ n Niili lɛ̀ò sɛ lↄ. 
Máↄ zɛa n aɛ Olebu gbɛwa we. Gbɛpi lɛ́, í i bↄ, aai mi. Ɔ̃ Mↄizĩkɛ̀ màa Isaili gbɛ̃zↄ̃ↄↄ wáa. 
Ɔ̃ wà tↄkpà gupiɛ Masa, asa we aa Dii lɛ́wɛ̀u aa mɛ̀: Dii kúwanↄɛ wee? Wà tↄkpàɛ̀ Mɛliba lↄ, kɛ́ Isailiↄ zↄakà we yã́i. 
Amalɛkiↄ mↄ̀ zĩkai ń Isailiↄ Lefidiũ. 
Ɔ̃ Mↄizi ò Yozueeɛ: Gbɛ̃́ↄ plɛwɛ̃ɛ, í gɛ zĩka ń Amalɛkiↄ. Zia máↄ zɛa sĩ̀sĩɛ bee misonawa ń Lua goo kũa. 
Yozuee kɛ̀ lá Mↄizi òɛwa, à gɛ̀ zĩkai ń Amalɛkiↄ. Ɔ̃ Mↄizi ń Aalonao ń Huluo dɛ̀dɛ sĩ̀sĩpi misonawa. 
Tó Mↄizi a ↄↄ sɛ̀, Isailiↄ ìↄ Amalɛkiↄ fuɛ, ãma tó à a ↄↄ sìilɛ sↄ̃, Amalɛkiↄ ìↄ Isailiↄ fuɛ. 
Kɛ́ Mↄizi ↄↄ wã̀, ↄ̃ wà gbɛ sɛ dìlɛ aà zíɛ, à zↄ̃̀lɛwà, ↄ̃ Aalona ń Huluo aa aà ↄↄ kṹkũa, gbɛ̃do ↄplaai, gbɛ̃do ↄzɛɛi, ↄ̃ aà ↄↄ gↄ̃̀ zɛa e ĩatɛ̃ gɛ̀ gɛ̃̀ kpɛ́u. 
Ɔ̃ Yozuee zĩblè Amalɛkiↄwa, à ń dɛdɛ ń fɛ̃ndao. 
Dii ò Mↄiziɛ: Bee kɛ̃ lá guu àↄ dɛ dↄngubↄ ũ, ní o Yozueeɛ, asa má Amalɛkiↄ midɛ dṹnia guu píiɛ. 
Mↄizi gbagbakĩi bò, ↄ̃ à tↄkpàɛ̀ Diimadalapoo 
à mɛ̀: À Dii dàlapoo dↄ musu, Dii iↄ zĩka ń Amalɛkiↄ gↄↄpiiɛ.
