Dii yã'ò Mↄiziɛ à mɛ̀: 
Ma! Ma Uli nɛ́ Bezalɛli, Hulu tↄũna, Yuda bui sɛ̀. 
Má tò ma Nisĩna dìɛ̀, ↄ̃ à ↄ̃nↄ ń gↄ̃io ń dↄ̃ao è ↄzĩ píi yã́ musu. 
A vua zĩ dↄ̃ ń ã́nusu zĩo ń mↄgotɛ̃ zĩo ń gↄ̃inakɛa pↄ́wao. 
A gbɛ̀ bɛɛɛde ãa ń a daa pↄ́uo dↄ̃ ń lí'ãao ń ↄzĩ píio. 
Bee gbɛa ma Aisama nɛ́ Ɔↄliabu, Dã bui dìlɛ aà dↄnlɛde ũ. Ma ↄzĩkɛnaↄ gbà ↄ̃nↄ píi, kɛ́ aa e zĩ pↄ́ má dànɛ́ↄ kɛ: 
Kpaaũkpɛ, ikoyã kpagolo ń a nɛtaɛo ń kpɛ́pi pↄkeleↄ píi, 
táabũnu ń a pↄkeleↄ, vua tɛ̃ɛtɛ̃ɛ dau ń a pↄkeleↄ píi, tulaletikatɛakĩi, 
sa pↄ́ wì a pↄ́ kátɛu à tɛ́kũ okĩi ń a pↄkeleↄ píi, dàa ń a dibↄo, 
pↄkasa pↄ́ wa tã̀ↄ, sa'ona Aalona pↄkasa pↄ́ ali sↄ̃omaziↄ ń aà nɛ́ↄ sa'on pↄkasaↄ píi, 
nísi pↄ́ wì pↄ́ dilɛò à gↄ̃ ma pↄ́ ũ, zwã̀akpɛ tulaleti gĩnana. Aa pↄ́piↄ kɛ, lá má dìlɛnɛwa píi. 
Dii yã'ò Mↄiziɛ à mɛ̀: 
O Isailiↄnɛ aa kã́mabogↄↄ pↄ́ má dìlɛ yãda, asa a dɛ seela ũ mapi ń ápiↄ zãnguo e á buiwa, kɛ́ à e dↄ̃ ma Dii ma á sɛ́ ma gbɛ̃́ↄ ũɛ. 
Àli kã́mabogↄↄpi yãda, asa gↄↄpi adoaɛ. Gbɛ̃́ pↄ́ i gↄↄpi yãdao, à gàɛ. Gbɛ̃́ pↄ́ zĩkɛ̀ gↄↄpi zĩ́, wà ade bↄ a gbɛ̃́ↄ guu wà dɛ. 
Àli zĩkɛ gↄↄ soolo, a gↄↄ soplade íli kã́mabo, asa ma Dii ma pↄ́ɛ. Gbɛ̃́ pↄ́ zĩkɛ̀ kã́mabogↄↄzĩ, à gàɛ. 
Isailiↄ lí kã́mabogↄↄpi yãda, aa dilɛ gbia e ń buiwa ma bàakuańnↄ yã́ ũ. 
Ìↄ dɛ seela ũ mapi ń Isailiↄ zãnguo gↄↄpii, asa ma Dii ma musu ń dṹniao kɛ̀ gↄↄ sooloɛ, ↄ̃ má làa, ↄ̃ ma kã́mabò a gↄↄ soplade zĩ́. 
Kɛ́ Dii yã'ò Mↄiziɛ Sinai gbɛ musu a làa, ↄ̃ à ikoyã kɛ̃̀ gbɛ̀ pɛ̀pɛɛↄwa mɛ̀n pla ń azĩa ↄtonao, a kpàwà.
