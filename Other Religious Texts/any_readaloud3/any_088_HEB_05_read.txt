EBELE'N-MƆ.
5.
Nyanmɩan tɛɛyɩfʋɛ kpain biala mɔ bɛ kɔ nɩan menian'n-mɔ anun bɛ kɔ yɩ yɩ́'n, ɔ di asafian. Ɛtɛ nɩn anwʋn tɛɛ ɛyɩɛ'n nʋn acɛdɩɛ'n, yɩ́ yɛ̂ ɔ fa man Nyanmɩan ɔ. 
Kɛmɔ tɛɛyɩfʋɛ sɔ'n muonun tɩ kulo sʋanlan'n, ɔ kʋala kɛ ɔ nwun bɛ́ mɔ bɛ nzɩ man Nyanmɩan mala'n nʋn bɛ́ mɔ b'a fʋn'n-mɔ anwʋn anwunnvoe. Ɔ sanlɩn kɛ, yɩ́ muonun ɔ kʋala kɛ ɔ fʋn. 
Ɛhɩka ati, ɔ di kɛ ɔ yɩ yɩ́ muonun yɩ́ ti tɛɛ kɛ ɔ yɩ bagua nɩn ati tɛɛ'n. 
Sɛ Nyanmɩan muonun a nvɛlɛ man sʋanlan kʋn kɛ ɔ fɛlɛlɩ Moyizɩ anianman Aalʋn ɔ sielɩ yɩ́ tɛɛyɩfʋɛ kpain nɩn an, sʋanlan fɩ́ɩ́ ngʋala njasʋ man ɔ nzie man yɩ́ nwʋn tɛɛyɩfʋɛ kpain. 
Kilisi muonun a njasʋ man ɔ'a nzie man yɩ́ nwʋn tɛɛyɩfʋɛ kpain. Nan Nyanmɩan muonun a han a hele yɩ́ kɛ: Ɛnɛ m'ɔ kɔ'n, ɛ tɩ mɩ́n Wa, mɩ́n kʋsʋ n dɩ wɔ́ Sɩ. 
Ɛsɛ ɔ'a han yɩ́ lɩka fʋfɔlɛ biekun kɛ: Ɛ tɩ tɛɛyɩfʋɛ titi kɛ Mɛlɛkɩsedɛkɩ tɩ yɩ́'n. 
Mɔ anɩn Zozi wɔ asɩɛ nɩn asʋ ɛwa'n, ɔ yɔlɩ asɔnɩn ɔ tɩanlɩn sɛlɛ kpa ɔ sunlin fɛlɛlɩ Nyanmɩan m'ɔ kʋala yɩ́ de fi ewue nun'n. Kɛmɔ Zozi manlɩn Nyanmɩan anyɩnnzɔ nɩn ati, Nyanmɩan kʋsʋ tielɩ yɩ́. 
Zozi tɩ Nyanmɩan Awa, kʋsʋ yɩ́ amannɩnhunlun nɩn anun, ɔ manlɩn Nyanmɩan anyɩnnzɔ. 
Mɔ Nyanmɩan man Zozi fʋlɩ hɔlɩ nyanmɩansʋ yuelɩ'n, Zozi a kaci bɛ́ kʋalaa mɔ bɛ man yɩ́ anyɩnnzɔ'n, bɛ́ nyanmɩansʋ ngʋan baka. 
Ɛhɩka ati, Nyanmɩan a sie yɩ́ tɛɛyɩfʋɛ kpain kɛ ɔ sielɩ Mɛlɛkɩsedɛkɩ'n. 
Yɛ le ɛjɔlɛ dɔʋn yɛ kan yɛ kele ɛmɔ ɛhɩ anwʋn, nan kʋsʋ, yɩ́ bʋ etue yɔ sɩ. Ɔ sanlɩn kɛ ɛmɔ ndɩ man ɛjɔlɛ bʋ ndɛ. 
Ɛmɔ mɔ ahan ɛmɔ lɩlɩ y'ɔ di kɛ ɛmɔ kekele menian'n-mɔ Nyanmɩan anwʋn ɛjɔlɛ'n, ɛmɔ kulo kɛ bɛ kekele ɛmɔ Nyanmɩan anwʋn ɛjɔlɛ fɔtɩlɩ'n-mɔ biekun. Alɩɛ mɔ ɛmɔ 'kpʋnndɛ bɛ di'n, ɛhɩ nɔfanlan yɛ̂ ɛmɔ tɛ nʋn ɔ. 
Sʋanlan biala m'ɔ tɛ nʋn nɔfanlan'n, anɩn ɔ tɩ batʋnman. Ɔ nzɩ man amanmuo sɛsɛ'n mɔ Nyanmɩan lɛ kpʋnndɛ'n. 
Nan bɛ́ mɔ b'a tin'n, bɛ́ yɛ̂ bɛ di alɩɛ ketee nɩn ɔ, afɩ bɛ́ dɩɛ bɛ́ ti a tin. Bɛ sɩ like m'ɔ tɩ kpa nɩn angʋnmɩn. Bɛ sɩ m'ɔ tɩ man kpa'n kʋsʋ angʋnmɩn.
