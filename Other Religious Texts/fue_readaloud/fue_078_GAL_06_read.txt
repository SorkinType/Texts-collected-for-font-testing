Galaatiya.
6.
Banniraaɓe, to on nanŋii goɗɗo e waɗa boofol, onon wooduɓe Ruuhu, bittinee joomum e ɗiggere. Amma, onon bo kakkilee gam to on naatee der jarbeeji. 
Ballontiree roonaago doolle mooɗon, hande non kebbinirton ko Almasiihu resani on. 
To goɗɗo e miili e laatii goɗɗum, nani bo o laataaki goɗɗum, o jammike hoore makko. 
Moy fu daara golle muuɗum. To ɗe potii ko o juroro, o juro gam hoore makko, amma to o jura dow golle goɗɗo. 
Gam moy fu roonoto e hoore mum doongal mum. 
Janŋinteeɗo haala Alla, e haani seennira ko woodi e janŋinoowo ɗum. 
To on majjinee ko'e mooɗon, Alla nootataako goɗɗo yawoo ɗum. Ko moy fu aawi, kanjum ruggata. 
Aawuɗo ko suuno mum yiɗi, ruggan ko suuno rimata, waato maayde. Aawuɗo ko Ruuhu Ceniiɗo yiɗi, ruggan ko Ruuhu rimata, waato yonki tabitiiki. 
To en comee waɗugo geetum. Gam to en tampay, en duggan to wakkati mum warii. 
To non, de gooduɗen wakkati, en gaɗanee himɓe fu geetum, accu dun banniraaɓe meeɗen goonɗinɓe. 
Daaree binniiji mawɗi ɗi binnanmi on e junŋo am. 
Dartooɓe hollitingo hoore mum yeeso himɓe, kamɓe doolata on juulnaago. Ɓee gaɗa ɗum gam ɓe jiɗaa tooreego sabbu leggal Almasiihu palaaŋal. 
Baa kamɓe juulnaaɓe, ɓe tokkaaki tawreeta. Ɓe jiɗu nun juulnoɗon gam ɓe keɓa juroraago on. 
Min kan, mi jurortaako goɗɗum to hinaa leggal Joomiraawo meeɗen Yeesu Almasiihu palaaŋal. Diga e maggal duuniyaaru tiggaa gam am, min bo tiggaami gam mayru. 
Gam juulnaago e dullere juulnaago fu laataaki goɗɗum, sey laataago neɗɗo keso, tagitaaɗo. 
Tokkuɓe laawol ŋol fu, Alla hokku ɗum jam e enɗam, kamɓe e Isira'iilaaɓe ɓe Alla. 
Diga jooni, to goɗɗo tooranmi kade, gam ɓannu am e woodi maane ɓattarɗe Yeesu. 
Banniraaɓe, moƴƴere Joomiraawo meeɗen Yeesu Almasiihu wonda e mooɗon! Aami.
