Apokalis.
18.
"Bɛ -sru ɛn an Bali -le 'pasiazan pee 'yɩ, te e ya 'sinan laji. E ya 'plɛblɛ 'kpa, ɛn -yee 'tɛ 'san 'trɛda 'si "fo. 
E paan 'kpa 'plɛblɛ -a vɩdɩ -a 'nan: «Babilɔn -nan -nyan. Babilɔn 'fla -dan bɛ, -a -nan -nyan. E drɛ -yʋ -wlidɩnun fla -a. -Yʋ -wlidɩ 'tɔ 'tʋdʋ pɛɛnɔn -nyɛanla 'nan nun. "Loman 'tɔ 'tʋdʋ pɛɛnɔn nɛn o 'ka 'saun Bali 'lɛ dɩ, ɛn minnun 'ka o ye "yi "dɩɛ, o ya -nan nun. -Wi -tʋdʋ pɛɛnɔn nɛn o 'ka 'saun Bali 'lɛ dɩ, ɛn minnun 'ka o ye "yi "dɩɛ, o ya -nan nun. 
'Lɛglɔn pɛɛnɔn 'ji minnun Babilɔn 'fla 'le -wɛn mlin 'e 'ciɩla "da. -Yɛɛ cɩ 'nan e o pɛɛnɔn wɛɛ 'kɔnnɛn -a. Ɛn "nyian bɛ 'trɛda mingɔnnɛnnun -fɔ -a -sru, ɛn o 'wɩ 'wlidɩ "tʋwli zɩɛ -a drɛ. Ɛn 'flɛ 'tannɔn nɛn 'trɛda bɛ, o drɛ fɛnɔn 'a, Babilɔn 'fla yɩfɛ a "kaga "le "wɛan.» 
"Bɛ -sru ɛn an wei pee 'man laji. Wei zɩɛ yaa 'vɩ 'nan: «'Mɛn minnun, 'ka 'si Babilɔn 'fla! 'Ka 'si 'nan nun, -e -yee 'wɩ 'wlidɩ 'e vɩlɛ e baa 'ka man dɩ, "tɔgɔ 'wɩ nɛn e -taa "bɔlɛ "man "bɛ, e 'bɔ 'ka man. 
'Fla zɩɛ -yee 'wɩ 'wlidɩ a "kaga, e bɔa labli man. -Yee 'wɩ 'wlidɩnun 'bɔ zɩɛ -a -cin -trɔa Bali ji. 
'Wɩnun nɛn yaa drɛ minnun lɛ bɛ, 'mɛn 'pasianɔn "o "dra "nyian -yee "lɛ. Waa 'lɛji bɔala 'e 'pa -a "fli. 'Kulenɛn nɛn e 'wɩ 'wlidɩ 'fa 'a ɛn yaa -nɔn minnun lɛ bɛ, waa faa 'e 'pa -a "fli -e 'o -nɔn -yee "lɛ. 
'Fla zɩɛ, zɩ 'e 'tɔ 'wɩdɩ 'yɩ "yi ɛn e fɛ yɩdɩ 'yɩ "yi "bɛ, -a da nɛn o 'tɛ "paa "da, ɛn -a da nɛn waa ta dra -trɔdrɔ. Yaa 'vɩ 'e 'fli man 'nan, e ya mingɔnnɛn -blɩnan, ɛn -kɔnnɛn 'ka 'kalɛ 'e da dɩ, ɛn 'e 'ta 'ka 'li 'dra -trɔdrɔ "fo "dɩ. 
'Wɩ nɛn yaa 'vɩ zɩɛ -yee "wɛan bɛ, 'wɩnun nɛn o -taa "bɔlɛ "man yi -tʋwli "cɛ 'wlu bɛ -nyrɛn 'gʋ, -cɛ bɔdɩ a -tʋ, ɛn ta drɛdɩ -trɔdrɔ a -tʋ, ɛn dra -tɛdɩ a -tʋ. Ɛn 'tɛ 'bɛ -taa -a -nan -nyanlɛ. Kɔɔ Minsan Bali nɛn e tin -tɛ "da "bɛ, e ya 'plɛblɛ.» 
'Trɛda mingɔnnɛnnun nɛn o -fɔ -a -sru, ɛn -yee 'wɩ 'wlidɩ zɩɛ "o drɛ, ɛn "o fɛ yɩdɩ 'yɩ "yi "bɛ, 'wɩ -taa 'nanlɛ o man. Tʋ nɛn o 'fla zɩɛ -a -yee 'tɛ 'blɩnan bɛ, -a -nan nɛn 'wɩ 'nandɩ "siala o man. 
'Wɩ nɛn e -taa "bɔlɛ 'fla zɩɛ -a man bɛ, e o -tɛa "klan -a, -yee "wɛan wa'a -kɔlaman -e 'o -pli "man "kogo dɩ. 'Pian o -fo -kɔɔbli te o -paanman. Waa ve 'nan: «Babilɔn 'fla -dan, 'wɩ 'bɔ 'i man, 'wɩ 'bɔ 'i man. Tʋ "wɛnnɛn "cɛ 'wlu nɛn Bali cɛɛn 'wɩ "nɛn -kɔɔndɩ man 'yiɛ.» 
'Wɩ nɛn e -taa "bɔlɛ 'fla zɩɛ -a man bɛ, -yee "wɛan 'flɛ 'tannɔn 'wuɔdɩ "siala, kɔɔ min 'ka "nyian -wee 'flɛ 'lɔa dɩ. 
-Wee 'flɛnun 'bɔ nɛn 'gʋ: -siga fɛnun 'lee "lala fɛnun, 'lee -kɔlɛnun nɛn o 'flɛ "mlian -siga da bɛ, 'lee mingɔnnɛnnun da sɔnun, 'lee yiba "nɛn -a 'flɛ cɩ bɛ, 'lee -fiɩ 'shɛ fɛnun, 'lee -bulalɛ fɛnun, 'lee -kɔlɛ 'kpagba, 
'lee -laziglɔ 'tɔ 'tʋdʋ pɛɛnɔn, 'lee rɛzɛn -wɛn, 'lee 'nyrɔn, 'lee -fari puu, 'lee fei fɛ 'wlɛnun, 'lee trinun, 'lee 'blanun, 'lee -sʋnun, 'lee odronun, 'lee nɔannun. 
Yiba "bɛ 'wlɛ "trandɩ pɛɛnɔn nɛn yaa 'yɩ "yi "bɛ, -a -nan -nyan. Ɛn -a yɩfɛnun pɛɛnɔn bɛ, o 'san -yrɔ, wa'a "nyian taa "fo "dɩ. 
'Flɛ 'tannɔn nɛn o drɛ fɛnɔn 'a Babilɔn 'fla 'le "wɛan bɛ, 'wɩ nɛn e -taa "bɔlɛ 'fla zɩɛ -a man bɛ, e o -tɛa "klan -a, -yee "wɛan wa'a 'kɔlaman -e 'o -pli "man "kogo dɩ. 'Pian o -fo -kɔɔbli te o -wua. 
Waa 've 'nan: «Babilɔn 'fla -dan, 'wɩ 'bɔ 'i man, 'wɩ 'bɔ 'i man. 'I man a 'e 'padɩ "nɛn mingɔnnɛnnun da sɔnun -a 'lee -siga fɛnun 'lee -kɔlɛnun nɛn o 'flɛ "mlian -siga da bɛ -a. 
Ɛn tʋ "wɛnnɛn 'wlu nɛn Bali -a pɛɛnɔn zɩɛ -a -nan -nyan. E bɔa 'lɛri -tʋ.» 'Wɩ nɛn e -taa "bɔlɛ 'fla zɩɛ -a man bɛ, -yee "wɛan -klʋ -dandan -fɔnɔn 'lee minnun nɛn o "ta 'ta wo ɛn o ya -klʋ 'bɔ 'ji bɛ, 'lee minnun nɛn o 'nyranman "paa -klʋ 'bɔ 'ji bɛ, 'lee minnun nɛn o 'wee -blɩfɛ ye 'yi yiɛ da bɛ, o pɛɛnɔn 'fʋ -kɔɔbli. 
Zɩ 'fla zɩɛ e ya -blɩnan, ɛn waa 'tɛ -goei 'yɩ bɛ, ɛn o pɛɛnɔn -a vɩdɩ 'sia 'wɩ 'nanmandɩ -a 'nan: «Babilɔn 'fla -dan, 'i yra "nɛn 'fla 'ka yɩlɛ 'trɛda 'li "fo "dɩ.» 
O 'trɛ 'wi 'o da, te o -wua 'wɩ 'nanmandɩ -a. Waa ve 'nan: «Babilɔn 'fla -dan, 'wɩ 'bɔ 'i man, 'wɩ 'bɔ 'i man. Minnun pɛɛnɔn nɛn 'wee -klʋ -dandannun a 'yi yiɛ da bɛ o drɛ fɛnɔn 'a, 'i yɩfɛ a "kaga "le "wɛan. Tʋ "wɛnnɛn "cɛ 'wlu nɛn Bali cɛɛn 'wɩ "nɛn -kɔɔndɩ man 'yiɛ. 
'Pian kaa nɛn ka cɩ laji bɛ, 'lee kaa nɛn ka cɩ Bali -le minnun -a bɛ, 'lee kaa nɛn ka cɩ Zozi -le 'pasianɔn 'a bɛ, 'lee kaa nɛn ka cɩ Bali 'lewei vɩnɔn 'a bɛ, 'ka 'ci 'e "nran! Kɔɔ Bali 'wɩ "nɛn kɔɔn Babilɔn 'fla -dan lɛ, 'wɩ nɛn yaa drɛ 'cɛɛ bɛ -yee "wɛan.» 
-A -nan nɛn Bali -le 'pasiazan -tʋ nɛn e ya 'plɛblɛ 'kpa bɛ, e -kɔlɛbu -dan 'kpa 'sia ɛn yaa tuʋ jemie -va, ɛn yaa 'vɩ 'nan: «Zɩ Babilɔn 'fla -dan bɛ o -taa -a tuʋlɛ zɩɛ, te wa'a "nyian -a ye 'li "fo "dɩ. 
Wa'a "nyian "dre "fɔ "da "fɛnun -cin 'wɩdɩ maan -nan 'li "fo "dɩ, ɛn wa'a "nyian "dre maan -nan 'li "fo "dɩ. Wa'a bla bʋnɔn 'ye 'nan 'li "fo "dɩ, ɛn wa'a "nyian fɛ -sɔndɩ -cin 'wɩdɩ maan -nan 'li "fo "dɩ. 
Wa'a "nyian -kannɛn 'san ye -nan 'li "fo "dɩ, ɛn lɩ 'pa "fɛdinun 'ka "nyian -kɔan -nan 'li "fo "dɩ. Fɛ -le "wɛan nɛn 'wɩ zɩɛ e 'bɔ 'i man bɛ, -nyrɛn 'nan: 'Yie 'flɛ 'tannɔn 'tɔ bɛ wɩɩ 'trɛ 'gʋɛ -a da. 'Yie -dravia wɩnun drɛdɩ -a nɛn i 'trɛda minnun pɛɛnɔn see paa. 
Babilɔn 'fla -dan, yiɛ Bali 'lewei vɩnɔn tɛɛ, ɛn yiɛ Bali -le minnun tɛɛ. Ɛn "nyian bɛ minnun pɛɛnɔn nɛn minnun o tɛɛ bɛ, yiɛ -maan ɛn e drɛ. 'Wɩ zɩɛ -yee "wɛan nɛn Bali 'wɩ "nɛn kɔɔn 'yiɛ.»
