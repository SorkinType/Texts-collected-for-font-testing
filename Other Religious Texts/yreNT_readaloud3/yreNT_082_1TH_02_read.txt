'Kʋ "bʋɩ Zozinɔn, kaa -tɔa 'ka 'fli -a 'nan 'kʋe 'lʋdɩ 'ka 'va bɛ -a "nɛn 'ka 'fʋlɛ 'e "tun "dɩ. 
Ɛn kaa -tɔa "nyian 'nan, minnun 'kʋ srɔn, ɛn o 'tɛ 'pa 'kʋ da Filipʋ fla 'vaa ɛn kʋ 'bɔla 'ka 'va. Ɛn 'ka 'va "nyian bɛ, 'kʋ 'man 'ka 'fʋlɛa flɩɩ dɩ. 'Pian kʋ yi -tɛra Bali da, ɛn kʋ -yee 'wɩ 'nɔnnɔn 'vɩ 'cɛɛ. 
Kʋ "koe 'sia, ɛn kʋ 'wɩ zɩɛ -a drɛ. Kɔɔ Bali -le 'wɩ nɛn kʋa ve minnun lɛ bɛ, kʋ'a ve 'wlu 'a dɩ, ɛn kʋ'a ve 'ci "nrɔndɩ -wlidɩ "a dɩ, ɛn kʋ'a -dawli dra dɩ. 
'Pian 'wɩ nɛn Bali "e 'nan 'kʋ vɩ bɛ, -nyrɛn kʋa ve. E 'kʋ 'nanjɛɛn, ɛn e 'kʋ 'yɩ tɩglɩ. -A -nan nɛn e 'yee 'wɩ 'nɔnnɔn vɩdɩ -pɛba 'wʋ 'kʋ 'lɔ. -Yee "wɛan kʋ 'ka -a -wɛɛnan 'nan 'kʋ 'sɔ minnun lɛ dɩ, 'pian kʋa -wɛɛman 'nan, 'kʋ 'sɔ Bali nɛn e min ci wɩ -tɔa "bɛ -yrɛ. 
Kaa -tɔa 'wein 'nan, kʋ'a 'wɩ vɩlɛ 'cɛɛ see "paa wei -a dɩ, ɛn kʋ'a 'wɩ vɩlɛ 'cɛɛ 'nan -e 'kʋ 'ka 'lɔ fɛ yɩ dɩ. Bali a 'kʋe 'wɩ 'nan yɩzan -a. 
Kʋ'a -wɛɛlɛ 'nan min 'e 'kʋ 'tɔ "yi "vɩ dɩ. -Te e ya 'ka 'bɔ 'a oo, ɛn -te e ya min peenun -a oo, kʋ'a -wɛɛlɛ 'nan 'o 'kʋ 'tɔ "yi "vɩ "fo "dɩ. 
Kʋ -kɔlaman paan -e 'kʋ drɛ 'ka 'win -ji -trɔ -a, kʋ ya Crizi -le 'pasianɔn 'a -le "wɛan. 'Pian kʋ -fʋ 'ka 'va 'trɔɔ "le zɩ 'nɛn "bʋ 'e yiɛ -tɔa 'yee 'nɛn 'va bɛ -yee 'wɩ 'zʋ. 
Ɛn 'ka yɩdɩ "yi "bʋʋ "le "wɛan, Bali -le 'wɩ 'nɔnnɔn 'saza "cɛɛ kʋa 'vɩ 'cɛɛ dɩ, 'pian 'kʋ 'fli -nɔn 'cɛɛ "fo. 'Wɩ 'kpa, 'ka 'nan wɩ a 'kʋ man "bʋʋ, -yee "wɛan kʋa drɛ zɩ. 
'Kʋ "bʋɩ Zozinɔn, 'nyranman nɛn, kʋa 'pa 'ka 'va, 'lee 'kʋ "koe "nɛn e -nyan bɛ, -a -cin a 'ka 'ji. Bodrun funnin, te kʋ ya 'nyranman da, 'nan -e 'kʋe -blɩfɛ 'e vɩlɛ 'e drɛ 'ka 'tʋ 'win -ji -trɔ -a dɩ. 'Wɩ zɩɛ -a 'bɔ nɛn kʋa drɛ, te kʋ -ko Bali -le 'wɩ 'nɔnnɔn vɩdɩ -a 'cɛɛ. 
Kaa nɛn ka yi -tɛra Bali da bɛ, tʋ nɛn kʋa drɛ 'ka 'va bɛ, kaa 'yɩ 'nan, 'kʋe 'ta wʋ cɛin a 'saun, ɛn e ya tɩglɩ, ɛn 'tɔ 'ka 'e 'padɩ "man "dɩ. 'Wɩ 'kpa, ka ya 'wɩ zɩɛ -a -nan yɩnɔn 'a, ɛn Bali "e "ya wo "nyian -a -nan yɩzan -a. 
Ɛn "nyian bɛ, 'wɩ nɛn kʋa drɛ 'ka -tʋdʋ pɛɛnɔn lɛ bɛ, -a -cin a 'ka 'ji. 'Wɩ nɛn 'nɛn "tɩ -a dra 'yee 'nɛnnun lɛ bɛ, -a 'bɔ nɛn kʋa drɛ 'cɛɛ. 
Kɔɔ kʋ 'wɩ 'pla 'cɛɛ, ɛn kʋ 'ka "koe "tɔ 'ka da. Ɛn kʋ 'ka trʋ 'ba 'nan 'ka -wɛɛ 'nan 'cee 'ta wʋ cɛin 'e 'sɔ Bali lɛ. -Yɛɛ 'ka laabʋ 'nan 'ka mingɔnnɛn -blɩ 'e 'va, ɛn -yee 'tɔ -dan 'e drɛ 'cee vɛ -a. 
-Yee "wɛan kʋ Bali muo -fɔa "tʋ pɛɛnɔn man, 'nan Bali -le 'wɩ 'nɔnnɔn nɛn kʋa 'vɩ 'cɛɛ bɛ, 'ka "trɔɛn "tɔ -yrɛ, ɛn ka wɩɩ "man. Ka'a yɩlɛ "le min 'lewei "tun 'zʋ dɩ. 'Pian kaa 'sia fɛ nɛn e cɩ -a bɛ -a, Bali wei nɛn. -Yɛɛ cɩ 'nyranman 'panan kaa nɛn ka yi -tɛra Zozi da bɛ, 'ka man. 
'Kʋ "bʋɩ Zozinɔn, fɛ nɛn kʋa -tɔa 'nan, 'wɩ zɩɛ e ya 'wɩ 'kpa -a bɛ, Zozinɔn nɛn Zude 'lɛglɔn 'ji bɛ, ka sɔɔnla o "pa "da. 'Tɛ nɛn Zuif 'nɔn 'a 'pa o da bɛ, -a 'tɛ 'tʋwli zɩɛ, -a 'bɔ nɛn 'ka flanɔn cɩ -a 'panan "cee "da. 
Zuif 'nɔn zɩɛ, -wɛɛ Minsan Zozi 'tɛ, ɛn -wɛɛ Bali 'lewei vɩnɔn tɛɛ. Ɛn -wɛɛ 'tɛ 'pa 'kʋ da "nyian. Bali 'ka 'o drɛ wɩ ye "yi "fo "dɩ. Ɛn -wɛɛ "naan min -tʋdʋ pɛɛnɔn man. 
O -si -tɔa 'kʋ 'lɔ 'nan, te 'kʋ 'kʋ minnun nɛn o 'ka Zuif 'nɔn 'a dɩɛ, o va dɩ. Wa'a yɩlɛ "yi 'nan, 'kʋ Zozi 'le 'wɩ 'nɔnnɔn vɩ -wlɛ -e Bali 'e o 'si 'wɩ 'ji dɩ. 'Wɩ zɩɛ -a drɛdɩ bɛ, e ya -wee 'wɩ 'wlidɩ 'bɔ 'padɩ -a "da. 'Pian 'bɛ nɛn 'gʋɛ Bali a nyran -blɩnan o va. 
'Kʋ "bʋɩ Zozinɔn, -a -nan yi "fli 'bɔ te -ka'a cin ye dɩ. 'Pian 'kʋe ci "nrɔndɩ a 'ka da tʋ pɛɛnɔn man. 'Ka dra 'kʋ -tɛa tɩglɩ 'le "wɛan, kʋ ya "vale 'kʋ "lo 'ka yɩlɛ. 
Kʋa wɛɛ 'nan, kʋ "lo 'ka 'va. 'An 'bɔ Pɔl, maan ve 'cɛɛ 'nan 'an 'lʋdɩ 'ka 'va yi 'pa 'e 'pa -a "fli, 'pian Satan -si -tɔ 'kʋ 'lɔ. 
Fɛ -le "wɛan nɛn kʋa wɛɛ 'nan, kʋ "lo 'ka 'va bɛ, kɔɔ 'ka 'bɔ "nyian, 'ka da nɛn kʋ yi -tɛala, ɛn 'cɛɛ cɩ 'kʋe ci "nran wɩ -a, ɛn 'cɛɛ 'kʋ 'blɔ -tɔa "da, tʋ nɛn -kaa san Zozi Crizi -taa "bɛ -a man. 
'Wɩ 'kpa, 'cee "wɛan Bali 'tɔ -dan -nɔan 'kʋɛ, ɛn 'cee "wɛan 'kʋ 'ci "nranman.
