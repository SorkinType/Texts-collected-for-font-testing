Širawt ta n sanatat iktab Yaxya.
1.
Awa nak amɣar a din-ihulan Ǝlkǝnisat ta tǝnnǝfranat ǝd bararan-net. Areq-qawan ǝs tidǝt, amaran wǝrgeɣ nak ɣas a kawan-iran, aytedan kul win ǝssannen tidǝt aran-kawan. 
Nar-iwan fǝlas tidǝt a tǝhat iman-nana, wǝr tan-tǝgǝmmǝd har faw. 
Ǝgmaya daɣ Mǝššina di n Abba ǝd Barar-net Ɣaysa Ǝlmǝsix a danaɣ-ikfan arraxmat ǝd tǝhanint, d alxer, attaddin daɣ-na ǝs tidǝt ǝd tara. 
Ǝkneɣ tǝfalawist wǝllen as ǝsleɣ as wiyyad daɣ bararan-nam ǝzilalaɣan daɣ tarrayt ta n tidǝt s ǝmmǝk was dana-omar Abba-nana. 
Ǝmǝrǝdda, Ǝlkǝnisat, ǝnta da arat wa daɣ-am gammaya: ad nǝnǝmǝru gar-ena. Wǝrgeɣ alwajib izrayan a dam-in-ǝktabaɣ da kalar alwajib awa nǝgraw harwa ɣur ǝnǝtti. 
Tara almaɣna-net ad nǝzǝlulǝɣ daɣ alwajiban ǝn Mǝššina. Ǝnta da da alwajib was za tǝlkǝmam s ǝnta as tǝslam harwa ɣur ǝnǝtti. 
Imǝssǝxrak aggotnen ǝwizawazan daɣ ǝddǝnet. Ma imosan za ǝmǝssexrǝk n aytedan imos deɣ azangaw n Ǝlmǝsix? Adi za wa inkuran as Ɣaysa Ǝlmǝsix osâ-du ǝddǝnet imos aggadǝm. 
Daɣ adi ǝnkǝdat ǝd man-nawan fǝl ad wǝr tǝsǝbbǝnǝnam ǝššǝɣǝl-nawan. As tǝgam ǝnǝkud wa ad tǝgrǝwam aroz-nawan indân. 
Awedan kul wa inamaggagan d ǝsǝssǝɣri n Ǝlmǝsix wǝdi wǝr irtay ǝd Mǝššina. Amaran wa iglan ittâf ǝsǝssǝɣri wa, wǝdi irtây d Abba ǝd Barar-net. 
As kawan-d-osa awedan d ǝsǝssǝɣri iyyan wǝr nǝmos wa, a tu-wǝr-tǝqbǝlam, amaran a das-wǝr-taɣim assalam fadda. 
Fǝlas i das-oɣan assalam wǝdi inimannak dǝr-ǝs daɣ arak mazalan-net. 
Ǝllan-tu aratan aggotnen as areɣ a dawan-tan-in-annaɣ mišan s a wǝr nǝmos akatab ǝn širawt ǝs taddawat. Ǝssofaɣ a kawan-in-asaɣ iman-in annaɣ-awan-tan s ǝmi d ǝmi fǝl ad tǝkmǝl tǝfalawist-nana. 
Ǝhulan-kawan-in bararan ǝn tǝmidit-nawan ta tǝnnǝfranat.
