Sɔ̃ɔsiru.
24.
Goo ù n kurɔ suɑ mɑ u kurɔ wi ɡɑ̃ɑnu wɑɑsi ye yɑ ǹ kɑ nùn nɑɑwɛ, mɑ u kurɔ wi yinɑnɑɑn tireru yoruɑ u nùn nɔmu bɛriɑ, mɑ u nùn ɡirɑ win yɛnun di u doonɑ, 
mɑ yen biru, kurɔ wi, u dɑ u mɔre durɔ ɡoon mi, 
durɔ dɑ̃ɑko win tii ù n nùn yinɑbun tireru yoruɑ, mɑ u nùn yinɑ, u nùn yɑrɑ win yɛnun di ǹ kun mɛ ù n ɡu, 
win durɔ ɡbiikoo wi u rɑɑ nùn yinɑ mi, u ku mɑɑ nùn kɑsu bu mɛnnɑ. Domi kurɔ wi, u kuɑ disiɡii. Yeyɑ Yinni Gusunɔ u tusɑ. Ì n kuɑ mɛ, i tem mɛ Gusunɔ bɛsɛn Yinni u bɛɛ wɛ̃ bɛɛrɛ sɑri kookoosu koosiwɑ mi. 
Goo ù n kurɔ kpɑo suɑ, u ku tɑbu dɑ. Mɛyɑ bu ku mɑɑ nùn tilɑsi ɡɑɑ ko. Bu de u n tii mɔ sere wɔ̃ɔ tiɑ, kpɑ u wɑ u win kurɔ wi nukuru dorɑsiɑ. 
N ǹ weenɛ i ɡoon nɛɛru tɔrubɑ mwɑ bɑɑ ten biɑ. Domi ì n kuɑ mɛ, i mwɑwɑ mi ye n ko n de u n wɑ̃ɑ. 
Bɛɛn ɡoo ù n win Isirelisi mwɑ u ɡɔsiɑ yoo ǹ kun mɛ u nùn dɔrɑ, i ko i yɛ̃ro wi ɡowɑ. Nɡe mɛyɑ i ko i kɑ kɔ̃sɑ yen bweseru wunɑ bɛɛn suunu sɔɔn di. 
I de i be bɑ bɑrɑ disiɡiru mɔ lɑɑkɑri ko ɡem ɡem kpɑ i ko nɡe mɛ yɑ̃ku kowobu Lefibɑ bɑ koo bɛɛ sɔ̃ɔsi. Nɡe mɛ nɑ yiire i ko, nɡe mɛyɑ i ko i ko mɑm mɑm. 
I yɑɑyo ye Gusunɔ bɛsɛn Yinni u Mɑriɑmu kuɑ sɑɑ ye sɑ wee Eɡibitin di sɑ wɑ̃ɑ swɑɑɔ. 
Ì n bɛɛn Isirelisi ɡoo ɡɑ̃ɑnu bɔkurɑ, i ku du win dirɔ i kɑ tɔrubɑ suɑ. 
I yɔ̃ro tɔɔwɔ kpɑ wi i ɡɑ̃ɑnu bɔkurɑ mi u yɑrimɑ u bɛɛ tɔrubɑ wɛ̃. 
Ì n yɑ̃ɑro ɡɑ̃ɑnu bɔkurɑ mɑ u bɛɛ win yɑberu tɔrubɑ wɛ̃, i ku de tɑ n wɑ̃ɑ bɛɛn mi sere yɑm mu kɑ tĩrɑ. Bɑɑ ǹ n kuɑ ɑmɔ, sɔ̃ɔ ù n duɑ, i ko i nùn win yɑbe te wesiɑwɑ kpɑ u wɑ u tu wukiri u kɑ kpunɑ. Kpɑ u bɛɛ domɑru kuɑ kpɑ Gusunɔ bɛsɛn Yinni u mɑɑ bɛɛ siɑrɑ. 
Ì n yɑ̃ɑro ɡoo mɔ sɔm kowo, ù n sɑ̃ɑ Isireli ǹ kun mɛ sɔɔ, i ku nùn tɑki di. 
I nùn win sɔmburun ɡobi wɛ̃ɛyɔ bɑɑdommɑ sɔ̃ɔ u sere du. Domi u sɑ̃ɑwɑ yɑ̃ɑro, u win ɡobin bukɑtɑ mɔ ɡeɑ. Ì kun kue mɛ, mɑ u Yinni Gusunɔ nɔɔɡiru sue bɛɛn sɔ̃, i ko i tɑɑrɛ wɑ. 
Bɑ ǹ tɔnu ɡoomɔ win biin torɑrun sɔ̃, mɛyɑ bɑ ǹ mɑɑ bii ɡoomɔ win mɔwɔbun torɑrun sɔ̃. Bɑ ǹ ɡoo ɡoomɔ ɡoon torɑrun sɔ̃. 
I ku rɑ ko i sɔɔ ǹ kun mɛ ɡobeku ɡem birɑ siribu sɔɔ. Mɛyɑ i ku rɑ mɑɑ ɡɔmini ɡoon bekuru tɔrubɑ mwɑ. 
Adɑmɑ i yɑɑyo mɑ sɑ rɑɑ yoru di Eɡibitiɔ mɑ Gusunɔ bɛsɛn Yinni u sun yɑkiɑ. Yen sɔ̃nɑ nɑ bɛɛ yiiremɔ i n dɑ woodɑ yeni swĩi. 
Sɑɑ yè sɔɔ i bɛɛn dĩɑnu ɡɛ̃ɛmɔ, mɑ i yen ɡɛsɛ tiɑ duɑri ɡbee suunu sɔɔ, i ku mɑɑ wurɑ i ye suɑ. I de yu ko sɔbu kɑ ɡobekubɑ kɑ ɡɔminibuɡiɑ, kpɑ Gusunɔ bɛɛn Yinni u wɑ u bɛɛn nɔmɑn sɔmɑ kpuro domɑru kuɑ. 
Ì n yɔɔwɑ bɛɛn dɑ̃ɑ ye bɑ mɔ̀ olifin wɔllɔ i ye sɔrɑ, i ku rɑ mɑɑ wure i yɔ i ye i duɑri sɔri. I de ye, yu ko sɔbu kɑ ɡobekubɑ kɑ ɡɔminibuɡiɑ. 
Sɑnɑm mɛ i bɛɛn resɛm binu sɔrimɔ, i ku rɑ mɑɑ ɡɔsirɑ i wurɑ biruɔ. Ye i deri mi, yeyɑ yɑ koo ko sɔbu kɑ ɡobekubɑ kɑ ɡɔminibuɡiɑ. 
Bɛɛn tii i yɑɑyo mɑ i yoru di Eɡibitiɔ. Yen sɔ̃nɑ nɑ bɛɛ yiiremɔ i n dɑ yeni swĩi.
