Gbɑburu.
25.
Sɑɑ ye Isirelibɑ bɑ wɑ̃ɑ Sitimuɔ, yerɑ bɑ kɑ Mɔɑbubɑn wɔndiɑbɑ sɑkɑrɑru toruɑ. 
Mɑ wɔndiɑ be, bɑ derɑ Isireli be, bɑ ben bũu sɑ̃ɑru wɔri, bɑ kɑ bu di, bɑ kɑ bu nɔrɑ. Mɑ bɑ ben bũnu yiirɑ bɑ sɑ̃wɑ. 
Bɑ tii bũu wi bɑ mɔ̀ Bɑɑli Peoriɡii wɛ̃. Mɑ Yinni Gusunɔ u kɑ bu mɔru kuɑ. 
U Mɔwisi sɔ̃ɔwɑ u nɛɛ, ɑ Isirelibɑn wiruɡibu kpuro mɛnnɔ, kpɑ ɑ de bu be bɑ tɑɑrɛ mɔ ɡo sɔ̃ɔ sɔɔ nɛn wuswɑɑɔ. Sɑɑ yerɑ nɛn mɔru yɑ koo sure. 
Mɑ Mɔwisi u Isirelibɑn wiruɡibu sɔ̃ɔwɑ u nɛɛ, ben bɑɑwure u mɛɛrio win tɔmbu sɔɔ be bɑ Mɔɑbubɑn bũu wi bɑ mɔ̀ Bɑɑli Peoriɡii sɑ̃ɑmɔ, kpɑ u bu ɡo. 
Sɑɑ yè sɔɔ Isirelibɑ bɑ wuri mɔ̀ Yinni Gusunɔn kurun kɔnnɔwɔ, sɑɑ yerɑ ben turo u kɑ kurɔ Mɑdiɑni ɡoo nɑ wiɡibun mi, Isirelibɑ kpuron wuswɑɑɔ kɑ Mɔwisin wuswɑɑɔ. 
Ye Finɛɛsi, Eleɑsɑɑn bii, Aronin debubu u wɑ mɛ, yerɑ u seewɑ tɔn ben suunu sɔɔn di u yɑɑsɑ suɑ. 
Mɑ u Isireli wi swĩi sere win kurɔ mɑ u bu yɑɑsɑ sɔkurɑ ben bɔsɔnɔ. Sɑɑ yerɑ wɑhɑlɑ yɑ yɔ̃rɑ Isirelibɑ sɔɔ. 
Yen dɔmɑ te, tɔmbu nɔrɔbun subɑ yɛndɑ nnɛwɑ (24.000) bɑ ɡu. 
Yinni Gusunɔ u mɑɑ Mɔwisi sɔ̃ɔwɑ u nɛɛ, 
wee yɑ̃ku kowo Finɛɛsi, Eleɑsɑɑn bii, Aronin debubu, u nɛn mɔru suresiɑ ye u kɑ mɑn sɑnnɑ, Isirelibɑn suunu sɔɔ. Yen sɔ̃nɑ nɑǹ bu kpeerɑsie. 
Yen sɔ̃, ɑ nùn sɔ̃ɔwɔ ɑ nɛɛ, wee nɑ kɑ nùn ɑrukɑwɑni bɔkumɔ ye yɑ sɑ̃ɑ bɔri yɛnduɡiɑ. 
Arukɑwɑni ye, yɑ koo de wi kɑ win bibun bweseru bɑ n sɑ̃ɑ yɑ̃ku kowobu sere kɑ bɑɑdommɑɔ. Domi u nɛ, Gusunɔ win Yinni sɑnnɑ. Mɑ yɑ derɑ Isirelibɑ bɑ ben torɑrun suuru wɑ. 
Isireli wi bɑ ɡo kɑ kurɔ Mɑdiɑni wi sɑnnu, win yĩsirɑ Simiri, Sɑlun bii. U sɑ̃ɑwɑ Simɛɔn bwese kɛrɑ tiɑn wiruɡii. 
Kurɔ Mɑdiɑni win yĩsirɑ mɑɑ Kosibi. Win tundon yĩsirɑ Suri. U sɑ̃ɑwɑ Mɑdiɑnibɑn bwese kɛrɑ tiɑn wiruɡii. 
Yinni Gusunɔ u mɑɑ Mɔwisi sɔ̃ɔwɑ u nɛɛ, 
i Mɑdiɑnibɑ wɔrio, kpɑ i bu ɡo. 
Domi bɑ sɑ̃ɑwɑ bɛɛn yibɛrɛbɑ be bɑ bɛɛ torɑsiɑ ben bũu Bɑɑli Peoriɡiin sɔ̃ kɑ Kosibi, ben wiruɡii turon bii wɔndiɑn sɔ̃, wi bɑ ɡo sɑnɑm mɛ wɑhɑlɑ yɑ seewɑ Peoriɔ.
