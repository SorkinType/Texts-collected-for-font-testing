Abimɛlɛki gaa gbɛa Pua nɛ́ Tola, Isakaa bui Dodo tↄũna fɛ̀lɛ Isailiↄ suabà. A ku Samii, Ɛflaiũ buiↄ bùsu sĩ̀sĩdeuɛ. 
À dↄ̀aa Isailiↄnɛ wɛ̃̀ bao ń àaↄ̃oɛ, ↄ̃ à gbàsa gà, ↄ̃ wà aà vĩ̀ Samii. 
Aà gaa gbɛa Galada bùsu gbɛ̃́ Yaii dↄ̀aa Isailiↄnɛ wɛ̃̀ bao ń plao. 
A nɛgↄ̃ɛ vĩ baakwi, an baade ń a zàa'ĩnao ń a wɛ̃́lɛo Galada bùsuu, ↄ̃ wì mɛ wɛ̃́lɛpiↄnɛ Yaii zↄ̃ɛwiaↄ e ń a gbão. 
Kɛ́ Yaii gà, ↄ̃ wà aà vĩ̀ Kamↄ. 
Isailiↄ ɛ̀a yã́ pↄ́ Dii yeio kɛ̀ lↄ, aalɛ zↄble Baaliɛ ń Asetaatio ń Siliↄ diiↄ ń Sidↄ̃deↄ diiↄ ń Mↄabuↄ diiↄ ń Amↄniↄ diiↄ ń Filitɛ̃ↄ diiↄ. Aa pãkpà Diizi, aalɛ zↄbleɛ̀ lↄo, 
ↄ̃ Dii pↄ pàńzi à ń nà Filitɛ̃ↄ ń Amↄniↄnɛ ń ↄzĩ. 
Ɔ̃ Amↄniↄ ĩadà Isaili pↄ́ aa ku Yuudɛ̃ baale, Amↄleↄ bùsu pↄ́ wì mɛ Galada guuↄwa. Aa wɛ́tɛ̃amↄ̀nɛ́ e wɛ̃̀ bao plasai. 
Amↄniↄ bùa Yuudɛ̃wa aa gɛ̀ zĩkai ń Yuda buiↄ ń Bɛ̃yãmɛɛ buiↄ ń Ɛflaiũ buiↄ, ↄ̃ Isailipiↄ gↄ̃̀ yooyea. 
Ɔ̃ aa wiilɛ̀ Diiwa aa mɛ̀: Wa duunakɛ̀nɛ, wa pãkpànzi wá Lua, wa zↄblè Baaliɛ. 
Ɔ̃ Dii ònɛ́: Kɛ́ Egipiↄ ń Amↄleↄ ń Amↄniↄ ń Filitɛ̃ↄ 
ń Sidↄ̃deↄ ń Amalɛki buiↄ ń Madiãↄ gbãamↄ̀ɛ́, a wiilɛ̀a, ↄ̃ ma á bↄ́ ń ↄzĩ. 
Ɔ̃ a pãkpàmazi, a zↄblè tã́aↄnɛ. A yã́i tò má ɛa mà á suaba lↄo. 
À gɛ wiilɛ tã́a pↄ́ a zɛńnↄↄwa aa á suaba ń á taasio mɛ! 
Ɔ̃ aa ò Diiɛ: Wa duunakɛ̀. Kɛwɛ̃ɛ lá à kɛ̀nɛ maawa, ãma wá bↄ gbã. Wálɛ wabikɛmaɛ. 
Ɔ̃ aa dii zĩ̀loↄ bↄ̀ ń guu aa zↄblè Diiɛ, ↄ̃ an taasipi tò Dii i fↄ̃ wɛvĩ̀ńla lↄo. 
Amↄni zĩgↄ̃ↄ kↄ̃ kã̀aa aa bòokpà Galada, ↄ̃ Isailiↄ kã̀aa aa bòokpà Mizipa. 
Ɔ̃ Isaili pↄ́ kú Galadaↄ dↄaanaↄ òkↄ̃ɛ aa mɛ̀: Gbɛ̃́ pↄ́ dↄ̀aa gɛ̀ zĩkai ń Amↄniↄ ↄ̃mɛ a gↄ̃ wá Isaili pↄ́ kú Galadaↄ píi swana ũ.
