Yozuee.
5.
Kɛ́ Amↄleↄ kía pↄ́ kú Yuudɛ̃ bɛ'aɛ oiↄ ń Kanaaↄ kía pↄ́ kú ísia saɛↄ mà lá Dii Yuudɛ̃ í zↄ̃̀ɛ Isailiↄnɛ aa bùawà, ↄ̃ sↄ̃ kɛ̃̀ńgu, an kã́ gà ḿpii Isailiↄ yã́i. 
Gↄↄ bee Dii ò Yozueeɛ: Gbɛ̀ fɛ̃naↄ kɛ, ní tↄ̃zↄ̃ Isailiↄnɛ. 
Ɔ̃ Yozuee ò wà gbɛ̀ fɛ̃naↄ kɛ, ↄ̃ wà tↄ̃zↄ̃̀ò Isailiↄnɛ gu pↄ́ wì mɛ tia Gyafↄↄsĩsĩ. 
Yã́ pↄ́ tò à tↄ̃zↄ̃̀nɛ́n kɛ: Isailiↄ bↄlɛa Egipi gbɛa, an tá guu gↄ̃gbãaↄ gàga guwaiwaiu píiɛ. 
Gbɛ̃́ pↄ́ bↄ̀lɛ Egipiↄ tↄ̃zↄ̃a ḿpiiɛ. Gbɛ̃́ pↄ́ wà ń í guwaiwaiu zɛ́ guu bↄa Egipi gbɛaↄ, aamɛ aai tↄ̃zↄ̃ gĩao. 
Isailiↄ bɛ guwaiwaiu wɛ̃̀ bla e gbɛ̃́ pↄ́ dɛ gↄ̃gbãaↄ ũ gↄↄ pↄ́ aa bↄ̀lɛ Egipiↄ gɛ̀ gàgaò píi, asa aai Dii yãmao. Dii ònɛ́ aa wɛsi bùsu pↄ́ a a lɛgbɛ̃̀ ń deziↄnɛ à kpámálɛ bauo, bùsu pↄ́ vĩ ń zↄ́'io diupi. 
An nɛ́ pↄ́ Dii tò aa fɛ̀lɛ ń gbɛaↄ ↄ̃ Yozuee tↄ̃zↄ̃̀nɛ́, asa gyafↄↄdeↄnɛ, wi tↄ̃zↄ̃nɛ́ zɛ́uo. 
Kɛ́ Isailiↄ tↄ̃zↄ̃̀ ḿpii aa làa, aa gↄ̃̀ gu pↄ́ aa bòokpàu we e a bↄ gɛ̀ làaò. 
Ɔ̃ Dii ò Yozueeɛ: Ma wí pↄ́ Egipiↄ dàwá bↄ̀ɛ́ gbãn we. Ɔ̃ wì o gupiɛ Giligali e ń a gbão. 
Isailiↄ bòokpaa Giligali ↄ̃ aa Gɛ̃amusu dikpɛ kɛ̀ Yeliko sɛ̃u we mↄpi gↄↄ gɛ̃o mɛ̀ndosaide zĩ́ oosi. 
Dikpɛpi kaa'isizĩ ↄ̃ aa bùsupi buapↄ́ↄ blè, kàaↄ ń pↄ́wɛn kpasaao. 
An bùsupi buapↄ́ↄ blea gbɛa, kɛ́ gu dↄ̀, manakwɛa zɛ̀, i kwɛ́nɛ́ lↄo, ↄ̃ aa Kanaa bùsu blɛ blè wɛ̃ bee sa. 
Kɛ́ Yozuee kãikũ̀ ń Yelikoo, à wɛsɛ̀ gugwà, ↄ̃ à gↄ̃ɛe è zɛa a aɛ, à a fɛ̃nda wò kũa. Ɔ̃ Yozuee sↄ̃̀aàzi à aà là à mɛ̀: Ń kú wá kpɛ yã̀, ge wá ibɛɛↄ kpɛɛ? 
A wèwà à mɛ̀: Màa no. Dii zĩgↄ̃ↄ dↄaanan ma ũ, ma kaa lan kɛ. Ɔ̃ Yozuee kùlɛɛ̀ à wùlɛ a gbɛɛu, ↄ̃ à aà là à mɛ̀: Yã́ kpele ma Dii a o mapi a zↄ̀blenaɛi? 
Dii zĩgↄ̃ↄ dↄaanapi wèwà à mɛ̀: Ǹ n kyale bↄbↄ, asa gu pↄ́ ń zɛu kua adoaɛ. Ɔ̃ Yozuee kɛ̀ màa.
