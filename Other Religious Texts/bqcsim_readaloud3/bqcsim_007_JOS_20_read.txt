Yozuee.
20.
Ɔ̃ Dii ò Yozueeɛ à mɛ̀: 
O Isailiↄnɛ aa ń wɛ̃́lɛeↄ dilɛ ń misiwɛ̃lɛↄ ũ, lá má dà Mↄiziɛ a òwa, 
kɛ́ gbɛ̃́ pↄ́ kai mɛ́ mↄ̀ à gbɛ̃dɛ̀ lí e bàasi tau azĩa misikĩi ũ ade daɛ pↄ́ asu tↄsiwà yã́i. 
Tó gbɛ̃́ bàasi tà wɛ̃́lɛpiↄ doe guu, aàli zɛ wɛ̃́lɛpi bↄlɛu, i a yã'o wɛ̃́lɛpi gbɛ̃zↄ̃ↄↄnɛ. Aali aà sí ń wɛ̃́lɛ guu, aai kpɛ́ kpawà aàↄ kúńnↄ. 
Tó tↄ̀sina pɛ̀lɛaàzi, aasu gbɛ̃dɛnapi naɛ̀ a ↄzĩo, asa kai mɛ́ mↄ̀ ↄ̃ à a gbɛ̃́dee dɛ̀, ĩ́ie kú ń zãnguo za zio. 
Iↄ ku wɛ̃́lɛpiu e wà yã́kpalɛkɛaànↄ, e sa'onkia pↄ́ kú gↄↄ bee gɛ gaò, i gbasa ta a bɛ wɛ̃́lɛ pↄ́ à bↄ̀u. 
Ɔ̃ Isailiↄ Kɛdɛsi dìlɛ ado ũ Galile bùsuu Nɛfatali buiↄ bùsu sĩ̀sĩdeu ń Sikɛũo lↄ Ɛflaiũ buiↄ bùsu sĩ̀sĩdeu ń Kiliataaba pↄ́ wì mɛ lↄ Hɛblↄ̃o Yuda buiↄ bùsu sĩ̀sĩdeu. 
Yuudɛ̃ baa gukpɛ aa Bɛzɛɛ pↄ́ kú gusalala wáiwaiu dìlɛ Lubɛni buiↄ bùsuu ń Gada buiↄ wɛ̃́lɛ Lamↄtuo Galada bùsuu ń Manase buiↄ wɛ̃́lɛ Golanao Basana bùsuu. 
Gbɛ̃́ pↄ́ kai mɛ́ mↄ̀ à gbɛ̃dɛ̀, Isailie ge bↄ̀mↄ pↄ́ kú ń guun nò, aàli bàasi ta wɛ̃́lɛ pↄ́ wa dìlɛpiↄ dou. Beewa gbɛ̃́ pↄ́ gà daɛ pↄ́ dɛ aà tↄsiwà a e gbɛ̃́pi dɛo e wà yã́kpalɛkɛaànↄ gĩa.
