MA HEBRISE.
5.
Sɔrɛdzekpakpa biara kɛkɛ ne, ɔ̃ manyii ndɛ̃ masɛ maɖi wũ mabɔrɛgu sɔ ɔsɔrɛ Ɣaa i ma kanya, ana ne, ɔsɛ ɔsu ma karatã gu ara fɛ̃a ɔtã Ɣaa i ma akpi iti. 
Gɔ ɔ̃ mɔmɔ ba aɣɛrɛ i siri agbãagbã iso ɔso ne, ɔsɛ ɔna ɔtu ɔtã mma loiɣe nnɛ maabara gu mma nto mayu ɔri. 
Fiɛ gɔ ɔ̃ mɔmɔ ba akpi ɔso ne, iiɖe maturi iti ɔnɔwɛ̃ ɔsɛ ɔfɛ̃ ara, ɣɛɛ ku ɔ̃ mɔmɔ akpi iti. 
Kuwɛ̃ iisɛ ɔɖi so, ɔkɔlɛ so ɔkpesera sɔrɛdzekpakpa pelepele. Ɣaa ɔnɔwɛ̃ nsɛ ɔɖi ɔturi lɛ kumɛgɔ ɔɖi Aaron. 
Ngbɔ ame i Kristo iikɔlɛ so kato ɔkpesera so sɔrɛdzekpakpa pelepele ne. Ɣɛɛ Ɣaa ɔɣere wũ sɔ, “Fɔ nɖe wũ Ɔbi. Nɔme ne, loɔkpese fɔ Ɔse.” 
Ɔɣɛ ana i kakɔi mama sɔ, “Aaɖe sɔrɛdze ɔwi ɖaa lɛ Melkisedek awe.” 
Ɔwi gɔ i Yesu ɔwe i karɔ̃ iso ngbe ne, ɔkparama kayi ɔkarɛ ara ku kulu gu sikpabo teteree gu nnɔmɛ ɔtã Ɣaa gɔ loawo wũ ɔɖi ɔbɔrɛgu i kukpi ame. Gɔ ɔbɔ so karɔ̃ fiɛ ɔsu so ɔtã ɔso ne, Ɣaa ɔnɔ wũ. 
Atoa sɔ Ɣaa ɔbi ɔɖe ne, iki i ɔ̃ inyɛwe ame ne, ɔsuã sɔ ɔ̃akã Ɣaa atsue. 
Gɔ Ɣaa ɔɔbara wũ ɔkote i ɔri biara iso ɔro ne, ɔba ɔ̃akpese ngɔ iso i mma nto makã Ɣaa atsue aaki maana iɖi. 
Fiɛ Ɣaa ɔɔsia ita ɔsɛ sɔ ɔ̃aɖe sɔrɛdzekpakpa pelepele lɛ Melkisedek awe. 
Atɔ̃me gbodzoo pia tee boaɣere mi i ara wagbe iso, ɣɛɛ wã iɣɛ ba ɔle alasɔ miito minɔ ara karɔ̃ malamala. 
Gbaã ne, ikote sɔ tee ka miakpese maratedze, ɣɛɛ ne, mikpese miɣiã ɔturi mama gɔ loate mi ara ɖeakatɔ̃ i Ɣaa ara ame. Gɔ tee ka miaɖe aɖera wa mba ɔle ne, mikpese mitogu inyɛ imɛmɛ. 
Ɔbiara gɔ lokpese ɔto ɔmeme inyɛ ne, ɔbiɛtɛ gɔ nna iwola i isɛɛ gu ikpi ndɛ̃ ɔɖe. 
Aɖera wa mba ɔle ne, makpakpa aɖera aɖe, mma iki i ma ara isuã ɔso ne, ka maba iwola i isɛɛ gu ikpi ndɛ̃.
