MA HEBRISE.
8.
Itɔ̃me nɛ boto boɣɛ ɔdziri kere nɖe sɔ, boba Sɔrɛdzekpakpa gɔgbe. Ɔsi i Ɔlesate sigaraiyara kuɖearɔ̃ iso i kato. 
Sɔrɛdzekpakpa pelepele gɔgbe sɛ ɔbara ɔ̃ kasumu karabara i kasekelekɔ̃ ga nɖe sikati ikpayo mɔmɔ ame. Bosate mɔmɔ lopɛ ikpayo nɛgbe ne iiɖe maturi. 
Masɛ masɛra sɔrɛdzekpakpa pelepele biara sɔ ɔ̃asu karatã gu ara fɛ̃a, ɔtã Ɣaa. Ne ɔso ikote sɔ sɔrɛdze gɔgbe ana ne, ɔ̃awe nnɛ ɔ̃asu ɔtã Ɣaa. 
Si karɔ̃ iso ngbe iɖe ɔpia ne, ɔ̃ibana ɔri ɔbara sɔrɛdze karabara, alasɔ masɔrɛdze ma nsɛ masu karatã mabɔ i mmara kanya pia koko. 
Masɔrɛdze karabara ga masɛ mabara ne, kare nga mpia i kato i ɔri ɔwɛ̃ iso fiɛ iɖe ira nɛ mato masore. Alasɔ gɔ Mose to ɔba ɔ̃apɛ sikati ikpayo ne, Ɣaa ɔɣere wũ sɔ, “Nyɔ sɔ aabara ira biara i kumɛgɔ maɖi mate-ɔ i kube iso pɛpɛɛpɛ.” 
Ɣɛɛ masɔrɛdze karabara ga i Ɣaa ɔsu ɔpia Yesu nrɔɔ̃ ame lɛ kaɖo kakuere kokooko lɛ kumɛgɔ ame i ndamu ɣɛtɛ mɛ ɔkã i Ɣaa gu ɔ̃ maturi ndɛ̃ lɛ miɖo mikuere alasɔ matsue kã masia i kaɣɛkasɛ ga nlɛ kaɖo iso. 
Si kuira iinina i ndamu ɖeakatɔ̃ iso ne, tee iisibanya sɔ maakã minyɔare. 
Ɣɛɛ Ɣaa ɔnya iɣɛrɛ i ɔ̃ maturi iso gɔ ɔɣɛ sɔ, “Bosate sɔ, ɔwi to ɔba gɔ loanyi kanya ɣɛtɛ ku Israel kaɖe gu Yuda kaɖe. 
Fiɛ kaibase lɛ kanya inyi ɖeakatɔ̃ ga lonyigu ma mawa ku ɔwi gɔ lomɔɛ̃ ma i kɔrɔ̃ lokɔ ma lobɔrɛgu i Egipte karɔ̃ iso. Gɔ maibara i kanya ga lonyigu ma iso ɔso ne, mme wũ loikpa ma. 
Bosate ɔledza iɣɛ sɔ kanya ɣɛtɛ ga loanyigu Israel kaɖe i ngbe ne, ɔwi gɔmɔ ame ne, Loasu wũ mmara lopia i ma adzuni ame fiɛ loatsɛrɛ me losia i ma situ iso. loaɖe ma Ɣaa fiɛ ma wũ maaɖe wũ maturi. 
Kuwɛ̃ iisibate ɔ̃ ɔlaa ɣee ɔte ɔ̃ ɔnyiibi sɔ ɔtsue Ɣaa. Alasɔ ma ɔɖuɖu to maatsue me ita i ɔbiɛtɛ kosoi tsɔra isɛ iabo makpakpa pelepele iso. 
Loanyɔ ma nnya losu ma akpi lotsɛ ma fiɛ loisibanyigi wã lonya.” 
Gɔ Ɣaa ɔɖe ika i kanya inyi ɣɛtɛ iso ne, ite sɔ kaɖeakatɔ̃ ɔɔkpese kakuere, fiɛ ira biara nɛ nsɛ ikuere ne, isɛ inina iwɔ pelepele.
