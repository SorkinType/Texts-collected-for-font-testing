2 Yohanes.
1.
Ɔko gɔgbe ɔbɔrɛ i mme gɔ mme nɖe mafɔɖedze ɔkpakpa kɔrɛ nto losese ɔrɔ̃go gɔ nɖe Ɣaa ɖɔɛse gu ɔ̃ mabi ma nto loɖɔɛ i ɔnukuare ame. Iiɖe wũ ɔnɔwɛ̃ me nsɛ loɖɔɛ mi, ɣɛɛ mma ɔɖuɖu nɣe ɔnukuare. 
Alasɔ ɔnukuare pia i bo ame fiɛ ɔto ɔ̃awe ɔwi biara. 
Bo Ɔse Ɣaa gu Yesu Kristo gɔ nɖe Ɣaa Ɔbi si matã bo ma nto boɖɔɛ manyii i ɔnukuare ame abualɛra gu nnyainyɔ gu isobuɛ. 
So ɔɣɔ me gbaã gɔ lonya sɔ fɔ mabi mawɛ̃ si ngbã i ɔnukuare ame lɛ kumɛgɔ i Bo Ɔse ɔte bo. 
Ne ɔso loto lotsɛrɛ ɔko gɔgbe losese mi Bokɔnyi ɖɔɛse sɔ mitã boɖɔɛ so. Iiɖe mmara ɣɛtɛ loto lotã mi, ɣɛɛ mmɛ bofɔ ta karɔ̃kasɛkɔ̃ miɖe. 
Kuɖɔɛ gɔ itɔ̃me boto boɣere mi nɖe sɔ boabara Ɣaa kuɖɔɛ. Mmara mɛ minɔ ta karɔ̃kasɛkɔ̃ nɖe sɔ, mi ɔɖuɖu si misɛgu so i kuɖɔɛ ame. 
Maturisɔsɔladze gbodzoo ka mapia i kayi ame. Mato mate sɔ Yesu Kristo iiba kayi lɛ ɔturi awune awe. Maturisɔsɔladze nɖe mmagbe igbã fiɛ maɖe Kristo matsirise. 
Minyɔ so kukaakɔ sɔ nnɛ iti mito mikpɛ̃ iibayu, ɣɛɛ miafɔ mi kuso ɔɖuɖu pɔpɔɔpɔ. 
Ɔbiara gɔ loisɛ ɔmɔɛ̃ Kristo ara ite ɔla fiɛ ɔsɛ ɔte ara mama ne, ɔna i Ɣaa ame. Ɣɛɛ ɔbiara gɔ lomɔɛ̃ wã ɔto ne, Ɔse gu Ɔbi ɔɖuɖu pia i ɔ̃ ame. 
Si ɔrere ɔba mi kɔrɛ fiɛ iiɖe Kristo ara ite iɖe ɔsu ɔbɔ ne, midaafɔ ɔnyiiɔ, midaatofora wũ kɔra. 
Alasɔ ngɔ lowarɛ nrɔɔ̃ ɔfɔ ngɔmɔ ne, ɔbaragu wũ mawɛ̃ i ɔ̃ ikpi ame. 
Atoa sɔ ara gbodzoo pia gɔ lobie sɔ loatsɛrɛ losese mi ne, loito lobie sɔ loatsɛrɛ wã i ɔko ame. Ɣɛɛ i ne kanya ne, nto lonyɔ ɔri sɔ mme mɔmɔ loaba mi kɔrɛ loba loanya mi ku anɔ fiɛ ka loaɣere mi fiɛ bo isoɣɔ aayi. 
Fɔ ɔnyiibi rɔ̃go gɔ nɖe Ɣaa ɔturi mabi sɔ maya-ɔ kukaakɔ.
