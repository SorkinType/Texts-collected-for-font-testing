15.
O wi'i ɓe kadi: «Ko min woni daƴƴere tigi-tigiire nden, ko Baaba an woni Tutuɗo on. 
O taƴay kala salndu wonndu e an ndu rimataa, o feppita rimooji ɗin fow haa ɗi laaɓa fii yo ɗi ɓurtu rimude. 
Ɗun-le wuren on wonii laaɓuɓe sabu kongol ngol mi feɲɲinani on ngol. 
Awa duumee e humagol e an, wano min kadi mi duumori e humagol e mon non. Ko fii salndu tun waawataa rimude, hara ndu siŋaaki e daƴƴere nden. Onon kadi wano non, si on humaaki e an, haray on waawataa rimude. 
«Ko min woni daƴƴere nden, ko onon woni calɗi ɗin. Kala on humondirɗo e an, wano min kadi mi humondiri e makko non, o rimay buy. Ko fii on waawataa waɗude hay huunde e ɓaawo an. 
Kala mo humaaki e an, ko bugorteeɗo wa salndu taƴaandu yoori, sifa ɗin calɗi ko mooɓeteeɗi, bugee ka yiite, sunnee. 
Si on humike e an, konguɗi an ɗin kadi wonii e mon, haray toree kala ko faalaɗon, ɗun newnante on. 
E hino no Baaba an mawninirtee: ko yo on rimu ko ɗuuɗi, wonon taalibaaɓe an. 
«Wano Baabaajo on yiɗirimmi non, ko non min kadi mi yiɗiri on. Tabitee e giggol an ngol. 
Si on ɗoftike yamirooje an ɗen, wano mi ɗoftori yamirooje Ben an ɗen non, haray on tabitay e giggol an ngol, wano mi tabitiri e giggol makko ngol non. 
Ko mi yewtirani on nii ko fii yo weltaare an nden wonu e mon, e fii yo weltaare mon nden onon kadi wonu timmunde. 
E hino yamiroore nde mi yamiri on nden: Yiɗindiree wano mi yiɗiri on non. 
Hay gooto alaa giggol ɓurngol mawnude wa okkitirgol ngurndan mun ɗan fii yiɓɓe mu'un ɓen. 
Si on waɗii ko mi yamiri on kon, haray ko on yiɓɓe an. 
Mi innataa on hande kadi kurkaaɗi, ko fii kurkaadu andaa ko jom mun waɗata. Mi wi'ii on yiɓɓe, ko fii ko mi andiri Ben an kon fow mi andinii on. 
Awa kadi hinaa onon suɓii lan, kono ko min suɓii on, toɗɗii on kadi, fii yo on yahu rimoyon, kon ko rimuɗon lutta, e yo Baabaajo on yeɗu on kala ko toriɗon mo e innde an. 
Ko mi yamiri on, ko yo on yiɗindir.» 
«Si aduna on non aɲii on, andee o aɲiino lan ko adii on. 
Si tawiino ko on ɓe oo aduna, aduna on yiɗayno on, ɓay ko on ɓe makko, kono nde tawnoo hinaa on ɓe oo aduna, awa kadi mi suɓike on e hakkunde oo aduna, ko ɗun waɗi si aduna on no joganii on ngayngu. 
Anditee ngol kongol ngol mi wi'unoo on wonde kurkaadu ɓuraa jom mun teddude. Si ɓe cukkike lan, ɓe cukkoyto on onon kadi. Si ɓe ɗoftike kongol an ngol, ɓe ɗoftoyto konguɗi mon ɗin kadi. 
Kono ɓe waɗay on ɗun fow sabu innde an nden, ɓay ɓe andaa Nuluɗo lan on. 
Si tawno mi araali, maa mi wowlanaano ɓe, harayno ɓe felnaaki. Kono jooni non ɓe alaa lamitorgal fii junuubaaji maɓɓe ɗin. 
Ko fii kala aɲuɗo lan, haray aɲii Ben an. 
Si tawno mi waɗaali e hakkunde maɓɓe ɗee kuuɗe ɗe hay gooto waɗaali, harayno ɓe felnaaki, kono jooni non ɓe yi'ii, ɓe aɲii lan, ɓe aɲii Ben an. 
Kono bee ngol kongol windiingol ka Sariya maɓɓe laato, ɗun ko ngol wi'ungol: ‹Ɓe aɲii lan e ɓaawo hujja.› 
«Kono tuma Ballo on ari, ɗun ko on mo mi nulata e mon immorde e Baabaajo on, ɗun ko Ruuhu Wonduɗo e Goonga, on Ruuhu mo tawata ko iwruɗo ka Baabaajo on, o seeditanto lan. 
Awa onon kadi, on seeditanoyto lan, ko fii hiɗon wondunoo e an gila ka fuɗɗoode.»
