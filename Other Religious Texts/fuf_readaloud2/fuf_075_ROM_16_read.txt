16.
Mi halfinii on oo bandiraawo men wi'eteeɗo Fibii, wallitotooɗo ka *moftal wongal Kenkere, 
fii no jaɓɓoron mo e innde Joomiraaɗo on, e noone no haanirani gomɗinɓe ɓen. Wattitoɗon e juuɗe makko fii kala huunde nde haaju makko yanoyi e mon, ko fii o wallitike ɗuuɗuɓe, min kadi men o wallitike lan. 
Hiwranee lan Piriskiila e Akiila, ɓen wondiɓɓe an e golle e nder humondiral e Iisaa Almasiihu on, 
ɓen weeɓituɓe ngurndan mun fii hiwugol ngurndan an ɗan. Hinaa min tun non weltanii ɓe, kono ko mofte ɓe wonaa Yahuudiyanke ɓen fow kadi. 
Salminanee lan kadi moftal wongal ka suudu maɓɓe ngal. Salminanon mi kadi oo giɗo an wi'eteeɗo Apayniiti, on adiiɗo gomɗinde *Almasiihu on e hakkunde ɓe *Aazii ɓen. 
Salminanon mi Mariyama, on gollannooɗo on fota. 
Salminanon mi kadi Andoroniiki e Yuniyaasi, ɗun ko ɓee musiɓɓe an e wondiɓɓe an ka kaso, ɗun ko ɓen ɓe *sahaabaaɓe ɓen hoolii fota, adiiɓe lan jeyeede e Almasiihu on. 
Salminanon mi kadi Ampiliyaasi, on giɗo an e nder humondiral e Joomiraaɗo on. 
Salminanon mi kadi Urbaanu, ɗun ko oo wondiɗɗo men e golle e nder humondiral e Almasiihu on, e oo giɗo an wi'eteeɗo Astakiisi. 
Salminanon mi kadi Apeliisi, on mo gomɗinal mun e Almasiihu on seeditaa fii mun. Salminanon mi ɓee jeyaaɓe e nduu suudu oo wi'eteeɗo Aristobuulu. 
Salminanon mi oo musiɗɗo an wi'eteeɗo Herodiyuuna. Salminanon mi kadi ɓee jeyaaɓe e nduu suudu oo wi'eteeɗo Narkiisu, humondirɓe e Joomiraaɗo on. 
Salminanon mi kadi Tarifayna e Tarifuusa, ɗun ko ɓee rewɓe gollanooɓe Joomiraaɗo on. Salminanon mi kadi Persiisa, giɗo an on, on kadi gollanii Joomiraaɗo on fota. 
Salminanon mi kadi Rufuusa, oo suɓaaɗo Joomiraaɗo on. Salminanon mi kadi yumma makko, on mo tawata kadi ko yumma an. 
Salminanon mi kadi Asinkiriiti e Filiguuna e Hermiisi e Patrobaasi e Hermasa e musiɓɓe wonduɓe e maɓɓe ɓen fow. 
Salminanon mi kadi Filolooge e Yuliyaa e Niriyuusa, wondude e bandiraawo makko on, e Olimpaasi e wondiɓɓe maɓɓe gomɗinɓe ɓen fow. 
Salmondiree, hirbondiron hirbondiral laaɓungal. Mofte* Almasiihu on ɗen fow salminii on. 
Mi wakkilinii on, yo musiɓɓe an, e reenagol ko wonata sabu sendondire e pegge ko luutondiri e jannde nde hendiɗon nden. Woɗɗitee ɓen wonayɓe sabu ɗun. 
Ko fii sifa ɓen yimɓe kurkantaako Almasiihu on, on Joomi men, kono ko deedi maɓɓe ɗin tun ɓe kurkantoo. Ɓe faljinira ɓerɗe ɓen ɓe bonnaa, konguɗi maɓɓe welɗi e waccooje. 
Onon non, ɗoftaare mon nden ko nde yimɓe ɓen fow andi. Awa miɗo weltori fii mon on, e miɗo yiɗi yo on wonu faamuɓe e telen-ma ko moƴƴi kon, e ɗayɓe e telen-ma ko boni kon. 
Alla Jom ɓuttu on muncay Ibuliisa ka ley koyɗe mon ko neeɓaa. Yo moƴƴere Iisaa Joomi men on wonu e mon. 
Timotee, oo wondiɗɗo an e golle, salminii on, wondude e Lukiyuusi e Yaasuunu e Sosipaate, ɓee musiɓɓe an. 
E innde Joomiraaɗo on, min oo wi'eteeɗo Tertiyuusi, mi salminii on, ɗun ko min oo winduɗo nduu ɓatakuru. 
Gayuusi wernuɗo lan on, werni moftal ngal fow kadi, salminii on. Arastuusa kadi, ɗun ko halfinaaɗo jawdi saare nden, salminii on, wondude e oo musiɗɗo wi'eteeɗo Kawartuusu. 
Yo moƴƴere Iisaa Almasiihu Joomi men on wonu e mon on fow. Aamiina! 
Mangu wonanii Alla, on Waawuɗo tabintinde on, immorde e Kibaaru Moƴƴo an on e waajuuji fii Iisaa Almasiihu on, ko yaadi e feɲɲinannde gundoo suuɗinooɗo on gila ka fuɗɗoode saa'iiji ɗin, 
kono o feɲɲiniraama jooni defte annabaaɓe ɓen, wano Alla poomayankeejo on yamiri non, o feɲɲinanaama leƴƴi ɗin fow fii yo ɓe gomɗin mo, ɓe ɗoftoo mo. 
Mangu wonanii on Alla Bajjo, Jom Faamu, immorde e Iisaa Almasiihu on haa poomaa. Aamiina!
