Zibəəzannɔ Ci.
11.
Kristazibəəzannɔ kan danyɩnɔ wɔɔ kɩ kʊ ŋ nyɩnta Zidee ʊ rɔ wɔɔ a ma ŋ ʊ, do vantɔɔ gʊɔɔ wɔɔ Woso lemim bɩ si kɛɛrɛ. 
Kʊ Pɩyɛɛr a bɔ an ta Zerizalɛm ʊ bɩ, zɩɩfʊrɔ sɩrakarɛzannɔ wɔɔ a sɩŋŋɩda sa, ŋn'ɩ bɩsɩ kam kan kɩ, ŋ ʊ: 
«Ɩbɩɩ gasʊ gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ n gasʊ baŋŋʊ ʊ rɔ wɔɔ zi, ɩ hɔbɩrɛ bɩ kan ŋ kɩ!» 
Pɩyɛɛr n a bɔ, an mim bɩ sa a jɩ ma deem-deem, an n'a hɩrɛ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: 
«Nyɩnta mɔɔ ʊ Zoppee kʊ nɔ ʊ, m nɩ yaa dam, mɔɔ m hɔhɩnkarɛ yɩ. Hɔ niŋŋə y'a to brama, an nɩ zerm, an bɔ kʊ ʊ kan pɛɛra gʊta kɩ, n n a bɔ kʊ ʊ, an bɩ lee ku mɔɔ zi. 
Kʊ mɔɔ nɩ dɩgam ma sɩɩ… bɩ, mɔɔ tara burə hɔɔ yɩ, ŋ gam n si-si, poohɔɔ, bɔɔ n'a gaatam nɔ, kan bɔɔ nɩ werm brama rɔ kɩ. 
Mɔɔ leer ma, an n'a hɩm mɔɔ m, a ʊ: Pɩyɛɛr, wuti, ɩ ŋ zɩnzɛ, ɩ ŋ sʊ. 
Mɔɔ n a hɩ, mɔɔ ʊ: Fɩɩgaa Zuuba, mɔɔ mɩsɩrbaa nɔ ʊ, hɔ bʊnyaa gɛɛ dintim hɔ n gasʊ mɔɔ lɛ ʊ y. 
Leer bɩ n a ya, an a to brama, an n'a hɩm mɔɔ m a hɩɩya hɔ ma, a ʊ: Hɔ kʊ Woso dintim gʊ ma bɩ, ɩ b'a bir dintim hɔ y. 
Naa do ba gallɛm kaakʊ, a haay n a bɔ, an ta brama. 
Lɛɛm bɩ, gʊaa kaakʊ a hɩnka har kʊ wɔɔ n ta ʊ b'ʊ. Ŋ bɔ ŋ y'a ʊ Sezaree ʊ, ŋnɩ ŋ nyɔɔ mɔɔ ma. 
Sɛnt-Ɛspri n a hɩ mɔɔ m, a ʊ, m ta kan ŋ kɩ, bɩ, m bɩ bɩsɩ ka y. Danyɩnɔ sɔrdɩrɔ nɔɔn duro mɔɔ da zaa ʊ, wɔɔ n aa gasʊ gʊaa kʊ n y'a hɩ ma bɩ har ʊ. 
A n malɛɛka bɩ k'a a hɩnka hɔ niŋŋə a har ʊ bɩ sa, an a dɩnda wɔɔ m, an n'a hɩrɛ, a ʊ: Gʊaa nyɔɔ Zoppee ʊ k'an a ka kʊ Sɩmɔɔn kʊ n so ra, n n a tɔ ka Pɩyɛɛr bɩ n bʊr. 
A minno kʊ ŋ n'a kam ɩbɩɩ n bʊmbɔrɛ yɩ kan ɩ har gʊɔɔ kɩ bɩ dɩndarɛ ɩbɩɩ m. 
Kʊ mɔɔ meerbaa bɩ sa bɩ, Sɛnt-Ɛspri zer ŋ la, amb'a a ba wɔɔ m a sɩŋŋɩda ʊ bɩ m. 
Mɔɔ hɔ kʊ Zuuba a hɩ bɩ tɔ da m nɔ ʊ, a ʊ: Zaan hi batɛm ka denter, b'awɔɔ, awɔɔ batɛm sim Sɛnt-Ɛspri nɔ ʊ. 
Kʊ Woso ganwʊrɛ deem ka gʊɔɔ nɔɔn duro ʊ ncɩnaaʊ amb'a a ka wɔɔ ʊ, dɔmɩm kʊ wɔɔ sɩra ka Zuuba Yeezuu Krista ʊ bɩ, nka m mɔɔ m kʊ m m ta Woso m tolle k'a b'a zi ba?» 
Naa k'a a hɩ bɩ, gʊɔɔ wɔɔ heer a da, ŋn'a lɛ bɔm Woso ʊ, ŋ ʊ: «Woso heerlʊrlɛ k'a nɩ mɩsɩrbaa kam bɩ ka do vantɔɔ haay ʊ sɔ.» 
Gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ y'a si, ŋnɩ hɩsɩga da fɩr kʊ ŋ y'a yɩ Etɩyɛnnɩ garɛ kur bɩ, ŋ ta Fenisii ʊ kan Siipɩr kɩ, Antɩyɔs kɩ ʊ. Ŋ bɩr Woso lemim bɩ pa dam gɔsɩ vanta m, kʊ zɩɩfʊrɔ wɔɔ bɛɛ bala y. 
Bɩ, Siipɩr zannɔ kan Sirɛnnɩ zannɔ kɩ gɔsɩnnɔ yɩ ŋ barla, kʊ ŋ lee ku Antɩyɔs ʊ, ŋnɩ Zuuba Yeezuu lemim nyɩnta bɩ pa da grɛkkɩ zannɔ wɔɔ m sɔ. 
Zuuba paŋŋa nyɩnta an nɩ kan ŋ kɩ, hallɩ gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ sɩra ka ŋn'a bɔ ŋnɩ zu Zuuba ʊ rɔ wɔɔ lɛ n'a dam la gʊta. 
Zerizalɛm sɩrakarɛzannɔ wɔɔ mim naa do dɩndarɛ ma, n n Barnabas nyɔɔ Antɩyɔs ʊ. 
K'a lee ku, an Woso barka bɩ yɩ, an nɩ zi bam bɩ, a heer yɩ nyɩnta, an ŋ dʊndɔ haay, kʊ ŋ heer n gɔɔta an bɩr kan Zuuba kɩ kɔɔkɔɔ. 
Nyɩnta a ʊ gʊaa tɩrga, Sɛnt-Ɛspri n a han kan sɩrakarɛ kɩ. Gʊɔɔ gʊta n zu Zuuba zaa ʊ. 
Barnabas ta Tarsɩ ʊ k'a yaa a ka Sol ma. 
A n aa a yɩ, an a gaa, an bʊr m Antɩyɔs ʊ. Ŋ nɩ dɔɔraa deem ba kan kʊ kɩ, ŋn'ɩ zi bam sokʊma bɩ do ʊ, ŋn'ɩ hɔ dɩndam gʊɔɔ gʊta m. Antɩyɔs ʊ n lɛɛ ka ʊ, n nɩ karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ bir kristagʊɔɔ. 
Dɔmɩnnɔ wɔɔ duro ʊ bɩ, wosolɛsinnɩsorazannɔ a to Zerizalɛm ʊ, ŋnɩ ta Antɩyɔs ʊ. 
Ŋ gʊaa deem kʊ n n'a birm Agabus bɩ wuti, an a hɩ Sɛnt-Ɛspri paŋŋa m, a ʊ: nɔ gʊta n'a dam tara burə ʊ jilli. A nyɩnta ʊ denter, cir Kloodɩ dɔmɩm ʊ. 
Karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ n a cɩnta kan kʊ kɩ, gʊaa biyəə kan a wɔhɔ kɩ, k'a nyɔɔ a danyɩnɔ kʊ ŋ nɩ Zidee ʊ rɔ wɔɔ daahan minto. 
Bɩ ŋ y'a ba, ŋnɩ Barnabas kan Sol kɩ bɔ ʊ, ŋnɩ ŋ nyɔɔ gʊɔɔgusinno wɔɔ ma kan daahan bɩ kɩ.
