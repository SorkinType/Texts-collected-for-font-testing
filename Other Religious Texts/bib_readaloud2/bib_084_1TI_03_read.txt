1 Timote.
3.
Sɩra m, meerbaa kʊ mɔɔ ʊ kʊ m a ba naa m, kʊ gʊaa lɛ taa k'a ka wosogʊɔɔ lɛɛ, zibəə mɩŋŋa a ʊ k'a ba. 
Bɩ, kristagʊɔɔ lɛɛzaa a ga m, an bɩr gʊaa kʊ n bɩ mim yɩrɛ n a hɩ ma y, a lʊ m bɩr deem, an bɩr gʊaa k'a a da a mɩŋŋa ma zaa, hɔɔndamimmazaa, nyasʊ a mɩŋŋa m zaa, an bɩr saanɔɔ sim mɩŋŋa a har ʊ, an n'a dam ma an hɔ dɩnda gʊɔɔ m. 
A bɩ yɩ bɛɛmirezaa waasɩ y, a bɩ yɩ wɔkaarɛzaa nyaa bɩncɛ ʊ y, bɩ k'an yɩ kʊmarɛzaa, laafɩɩkamazaa, a bɩ yɩ busooŋʊarɛzaa y. 
K'an dɩga a har mim ma mɩŋŋa, an a da a nyɩnɔ ma, ŋnɩ bɩr zuŋ ʊ, ŋnɩ bɩr a daŋ ʊ hɔ haay nɔ ʊ. 
Bala, kʊ gʊaa n a da ma an nyasʊ a har mim m mɩŋŋa, a y'a bam lɔ k'a a da ma, a nyasʊ Woso gʊɔɔ sokʊma nɩ? 
Kʊ gʊaa k'a yɩ kristagʊɔɔ lɛɛzaa, a n a ga m, an yɩ sɩrakarɛzaa daa y, kʊ m bɛɛ m, a y'a mɩŋŋa busum, an dʊdɔyarlɛ kʊ Sʊtaana a yɩ bɩ yɩ. 
A a ga m sɔ, k'a kasɛtɩ m bɩr mɩŋŋa gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ bɩr zum Krista ʊ rɔ wɔɔ zi, kʊ n b'a da ma, n a gore bɔ ʊ y, k'a b'a da Sʊtaana baŋŋa ʊ y. 
Daahanzannɔ wɔɔ a ga m sɔ, ŋnɩ yɩ mɩsɩrbaa zore zannɔ, sɩrazannɔ, ŋ bɩ yɩ bɛɛmirezannɔ waasɩ y, ŋ bɩ yɩ zambʊ hɔkamazannɔ y. 
Ŋ y'a ga m, ŋnɩ jɩm kɔɔkɔɔ sɩra kʊ sɩrakarɛ a hɩnka bɩ nɔ ʊ, hɔɔndarɛ mɩŋŋa m. 
K'a lɛɛ ka a ŋ ka lɛɛ, a dɩga ŋ ma, kʊ mim ba ŋ gʊr zi, k'a bɔkarɛ a ŋ da daahanbaa zibəə b'ʊ. 
Daahanzannɔ kʊ lannɔ m nɔ wɔɔ a ga m sɔ, ŋnɩ bɩr mɩsɩrbaa mɩŋŋa m, ŋ bɩ yɩ meerbaa bʊnyaa ba gʊɔɔ ma zannɔ y, ŋn'a da a mɩŋŋɔɔ ma, ŋnɩ yɩ jɩnalɛrazannɔ hɔ haay nɔ ʊ. 
Daahanzaa biyəə lʊ a ga m an bɩr deem, an a da a nyɩnɔ ma mɩŋŋa, an dɩga a har mim ma mɩŋŋa. 
Bala, gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ nɩ daahanbaa zi bam mɩŋŋa rɔ wɔɔ, ŋ gore daʊrɛ yɩrɛ ŋnɩ heerkoodəbaa gʊta yɩ sɩra ka Krista Yeezuu ʊ zɛkʊra nɔ ʊ. 
Mɔɔ y'a gɩngam kʊ m bʊr ɩbɩɩ zi zɔɔ, bɩ naa haay gʊrsɩra mɔɔ ba ɩbɩɩ ma, k'an bɩr yɩrɛ ɩ ʊ, 
kʊ mɔɔ bʊr m bɩr zɛm zɔɔ, k'ɩbɩɩ m mɩsɩrbaa k'a a ga m, an bɩr bam Woso gʊɔɔ gʊr zi bɩ dɔ. Wosogʊɔɔ wɔɔ duro, kristagʊɔɔ sokʊma bɩ m, Woso k'an mɩsɩr bɩ y'a so ʊ, sokʊma bɩ do n ta hɔdɩndarɛ k'a m sɩra bɩ m, an n'a kam gʊɔɔ n'a dɔrɛ. 
Wɔɔ sɩrakarɛ hɔ gʊta k'a nyaakʊm da Woso n a hɩnka bɩ, gɔsɩ b'a dam ma an bɩsɩ ka ma y. A a hɩnka mɛsim nɔ ʊ, Sɛnt-Ɛspri n a hɩnka gʊaa tɩrga, malɛɛkarɔ m dɩga a. N n'a mim pa da do vantɔɔ zi. Gʊɔɔ sɩra ka ʊ durnya nɔ ʊ jilli. Woso n a busu an a nyɩnta lɛbɔʊrɛ nɔ ʊ arzana ʊ.
