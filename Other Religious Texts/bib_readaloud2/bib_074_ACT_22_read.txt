Zibəəzannɔ Ci.
22.
«M danyɩnɔ, kan m zɩrɔ kɩ, k'a tʊr ka, kʊ m m gumə mim dɩnda awɔɔ m kɛɛrɛ.» 
Kʊ ŋ Pol ma an ebree lemim sa k'a hɩ bɩ, ŋ bʊgʊr bɩ to haay. 
«Zɩɩfʊ n mɔɔ m, yɩ mɔɔ Silisii kʊ Tarsɩ ʊ, bɩ tʊr mɔɔ ʊ naa ʊ, tara naa do nɔ ʊ. Gamalɩyɛl ɩ wɔɔ zɩrɔ wɔɔ lɛrɔ wɔɔ dɩnda mɔɔ m zʊr-zʊr. Mɔɔ nyɩnta m Woso zuure nyasʊ kɔɔkɔɔ, amba awɔɔ haay m her naa m. 
Mɔɔ nyɩnta, m fɩr ka gʊɔɔ ma karɩnda naa do zuure ma, m gɔsɩnnɔ zɩnzɛ, m gɔsɩnnɔ sur, m ŋ du lɔnnɔcɛ ʊ, lannɔ kan yaarɔ kɩ. 
Wosocɛmannɩbər jɩtaarɛ kan do bɩ jɩtannɔ wɔɔ kɩ a da ma, ŋn'a kasɛtɩ ka. Ciro mɔɔ ŋ si ŋ han wɔɔ danyɩnɔ zɩɩfʊrɔ kʊ ŋ nɩ Damas ʊ rɔ wɔɔ minto. Bɩ mɔɔ ɩ tam Damas ʊ, kʊ m aa sɩrakarɛzannɔ kʊ ŋ nyɩnta b'ʊ rɔ wɔɔ sur, m bʊr ŋ nɩ Zerizalɛm ʊ, kʊ ŋ nɩ bɩ fɩr ka ŋ ma. 
Mɔɔ m zaa sa, m nɩ tarɛ, m m zɔ Damas ma. Lɛɛm bɩ, sinuu zeezi, lɛɛgure gʊta n a to brama an b'a ku mɔɔ ra. 
Mɔɔ n m do tara ʊ, mɔɔ nɩ leer marɛ, an n'a hɩrɛ, a ʊ: ‹Sol, Sol, bɔ minto ɩ nɩ fɩr kam mɔɔ ma?› 
Mɔɔ n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, mɔɔ ʊ: ‹Nka n'ɩbɩɩ nɩ, Zuuba?› Leer bɩ n a ya, an a hɩ, a ʊ: ‹Nazarɛtɩ Yeezuu m mɔɔ m, mɔɔ ɩbɩɩ nɩ fɩr karɛ m ma.› 
Mɔɔ zukuuzannɔ wɔɔ lɛɛgure bɩ yɩ mɩŋŋa, bɩ ŋ bɩr leer k'a nɩ meer bam kan mɔɔ kɩ bɩ marɛ y. 
Mɔɔ n laaka da: ‹Bɔ mɔɔ m ga m, m a ba Zuuba?› Zuuba n a nyɩ bɔ mɔɔ nʊ, a ʊ: ‹Wuti, ɩ ta Damas ʊ. K'ɩ lee ku b'ʊ, hɔ k'ɩbɩɩ ɩ ga m, ɩ ba bɩ, n y'a hɩrɛ ɩbɩɩ m deem-deem.› 
Bɩ, kʊ lɛɛgure naa do mɔɔ mɩm bɩ ta bɩ, mɔɔ zukuuzannɔ wɔɔ a wɔ dɔ mɔɔ rɛ, mɔɔ n lee ku Damas ʊ. 
Gʊaa niŋŋə n nɩ b'ʊ, a tɔ m Ananɩyas. A nyɩnta gʊaa mɩŋŋa, an nɩ lɛrɔ wɔɔ sim mɩŋŋa, zɩɩfʊrɔ kʊ ŋ nɩ b'ʊ rɔ wɔɔ n'a lɛ bɔŋ ʊ. 
A n bʊr, an bɩ mɔɔ yɩ, an a hɩ mɔɔ m, a ʊ: ‹M danyɩ Sol, k'ɩ mɩm n lɛɛ yɩ!› Lɛɛm bɩ, mɔɔ mɩm a gʊ, m a yɩ. 
A n a hɩ mɔɔ m, a ʊ: ‹Wɔɔ zɩrɔ Woso ɩ bɔ ʊ, k'ɩ hɔɔnhɔ dɔ, k'ɩ Gʊaa tɩrga bɩ yɩ, k'ɩ mɩŋŋa lɛ meerbaa ma. 
Sɩra m, ɩbɩɩ ɩ ga m, ɩ bɩr kasɛtɩzaa a minto gʊaa haay taa kan hɔ haay k'ɩbɩɩ yɩ, ɩ ma sɔ bɩ kɩ. 
Bɔ minto, ɩ nɩ sɩnsɩm karɛ? Aya! Wuti ɩ batɛm si, k'ɩ tɔ bir, ɩ ɩ mimbʊnyaarɔ dintim gʊ ɩ ma.› 
Kʊ mɔɔ m wusigə Zerizalɛm ʊ bɩ, hindeem ʊ, mɔɔ nɩ yaa dam Wosocɛ ʊ, 
mɔɔ n Zuuba yɩ hɔhɩnkarɛ nɔ ʊ, an n'a hɩrɛ, a ʊ: ‹A ba zɔɔ, ɩ ɩ hʊr Zerizalɛm ʊ, bala, ŋ bɩ mɔɔ kasɛtɩ k'ɩbɩɩ n'a kam bɩ sire y.› 
Mɔɔ n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, mɔɔ ʊ: ‹Zuuba, ŋ y'a dɔ mɩŋŋa, ŋ ʊ, mɔɔ ɩ nyɩnta, m nɩ tam sokʊmacɛrɔ ʊ, m nɩ gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ sɩra ka ɩbɩɩ ʊ rɔ wɔɔ gʊrm, m a ka, ŋn'ɩ ŋ dum lɔnnɔcɛ ʊ, ŋn'ɩ ŋ zɛm madɩɩrɔ m. 
Ɩbɩɩ kasɛtɩzaa Etɩyɛnnɩ ma k'a bra tara ra bɩ, mɔɔ mɩŋŋa nyɩnta b'ʊ sɔ, m m zɛ kʊ ra kan gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ n'a zɛm nɔ wɔɔ kɩ, m dɔ ŋ huuro ma.› 
A n a hɩ mɔɔ m, a ʊ: ‹Ta, ɩbɩɩ nyɔɔ mɔɔ nawʊm do vantɔɔ kʊrɔ ʊ.› » 
Zɩɩfʊrɔ wɔɔ kʊ ŋ y'a tʊr ka Pol ma, an ta an meerbaarɔ nɔɔn duro ku bɩ, ŋ wuti, ŋn'ɩ zee dam, ŋ ʊ: «K'a gʊaa ncɩnaaʊ bɩ bɔ durnya ʊ! A n a ga m, an bɩr mɩsɩr y.» 
Kʊ ŋ nɩ zee dam bɩ, ŋ y'a huuro bɔ a ra, ŋnɩ taa gʊr, ŋn'a nyɔɔm brama, 
sʊrdaa tisi lɛɛzaa bɩ n lɛ ka, kʊ ŋ nɩ gasʊ Pol m, sʊrdaarɔ wʊtajɩ b'ʊ, ŋn'a zɛ, k'a laaka da han a dɔ, kʊ bɔ minto m zɩɩfʊrɔ wɔɔ nɩ zee darɛ awɔɔ ma gɛ. 
Kʊ ŋ n'a bankam k'a Pol wʊta k'a zɛ bɩ, a a hɩ sʊrdaa zɔrɔ lɛɛzaa k'a nɩ zibəə ʊ b'ʊ bɩ m, a ʊ: «Rɔm zannɔ gʊaa kʊ ŋ n'a dʊdɔ ka bɩ, awɔɔ a ga m a zɛ madɩr m na?» 
Sʊrdaa zɔrɔ lɛɛzaa k'a naa ma bɩ, a ta an aa hɩ sʊrdaa tisi lɛɛzaa bɩ m, a ʊ: «Bɔ ɩbɩɩ k'ɩ ba naa? Gʊaa bɩ, Rɔm zannɔ gʊaa m!» 
Sʊrdaa tisi lɛɛzaa bɩ n a bɔ, an bɩ Pol lar, a ʊ: «Rɔm zannɔ gʊaa n'ɩbɩɩ m paan… ra, k'ɩ hɩ m m?» Pol n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «Ɩɩn…» 
Sʊrdaa tisi lɛɛzaa bɩ n a hɩ m, a ʊ: «Mɔɔ busoo bɔ gʊta kʊ m bɔkarɛ, m m ba Rɔm zannɔ gʊaa.» Pol n a hɩ m, a ʊ: «Mɔɔ naa, yɩ mɔɔ a nɔ ʊ.» 
Lɛɛm bɩ, gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ nyɩnta k'a Pol zɛ rɔ wɔɔ a to. Sʊrdaa tisi lɛɛzaa bɩ k'a a dɔ a ʊ, Rɔm zannɔ gʊaa m Pol m, awɔɔ n a sur bɩ, dabɔʊ a nyasʊ. 
Dɔ k'a mɛ bɩ, sʊrdaa tisi lɛɛzaa bɩ k'a lɛ n taa k'a a dɔ paan… kʊ bɔ minto m, zɩɩfʊrɔ wɔɔ nɩ mim kam Pol ra bɩ, a a ka, ŋnɩ baŋŋarɔ wɔɔ bʊmbɔ ma. A n a ka, ŋnɩ wosocɛmannɩbənno jɩtannɔ wɔɔ bir kan dʊdɔkənno wɔɔ kɩ, an ŋ so kʊ ma, an a ka Pol n bʊr ŋ taa.
