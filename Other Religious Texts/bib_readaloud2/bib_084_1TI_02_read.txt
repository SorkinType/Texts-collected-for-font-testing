Hɔ kʊ mɔɔ lɛɛ ka m n'a yɛm bɩ, a yɛ mɔɔ nawʊrɛ, kʊ gʊɔɔ n yaa da Woso m gʊɔɔ haay tɔ ma, a ŋ mim haay da Woso wɔ ʊ, a yaa da m barka ŋ tɔ ma. 
K'a yaa da cinno wɔɔ tɔ ma, kan lɛɛzannɔ haay kɩ tɔ ma sɔ, k'ʊ nyɩnta laafɩɩ nɔ ʊ, ʊ bɩr zum Woso ʊ zenyy…, mɩsɩrbaa zore nɔ ʊ kan daʊrɛ kɩ. 
Naa m mɩŋŋa, an Woso kʊ wɔɔ bʊmbɔrɛzaa m bɩ nɔ yɩ, 
a lɛ taa kʊ gʊɔɔ haay n bʊmbɔrɛ yɩ, kʊ ŋ n'a da ma, ŋnɩ sɩra dɔ. 
Bala, Woso deem n ta ʊ, gʊaa deem taan… nɩ gʊɔɔ zɛm kʊ ra kan Woso kɩ, Krista Yeezuu m. 
A ɩ yɛ an a mɩŋŋa ka zɛ ma, kʊ gʊɔɔ haay n bʊmbɔrɛ yɩ. Dɔmɩm kʊ Woso a cɩnta bɩ k'a b'a ku an naa ba bɩ, a yɩ kasɛtɩ, an a hɩnkarɛ a ʊ, Woso lɛ taa k'a gʊaa haay bʊmbɔ. 
Naa mɩŋŋa tɔ ma m Woso n mɔɔ bɔ ʊ kʊ m yɩ a lemim padər, an mɔɔ ba kristazibəəzaa, sɩra mɔɔ n'a hɩm, mɔɔ bɩr ŋʊaar barɛ y, mɔɔ cɩnta a ʊ, kʊ m sɩrakarɛ kan sɩra kɩ dɩnda do vantɔɔ gʊɔɔ wɔɔ m. 
Naa minto bɩ, mɔɔ lɛ taa kʊ gʊɔɔ yaarɔ wɔɔ n yaa da Woso m, lɛɛ haay ʊ, ŋn'a wɔrɔ busu brama kʊ mimbʊnyaa bɩ nyɩnta ŋ gʊr zi y, ŋ bɩ nɔmaa kan nyaa kɩ to a heer ʊ ŋnɩ yaa bɩ da y. 
Kʊ lannɔ wɔɔ nɩ, mɔɔ lɛ taa, kʊ ŋ nɩ huu k'a ga kʊ ʊ ba du a ma, ŋnɩ bɩr sappɩ, mɩŋŋahɩnkarɛ ba ŋ zi y, ŋ b'a ka mim kʊsɩrɛ waasɩ ma, gɛɛ sarma nyɛɛnnɔ ma y, gɛɛ busoo gʊta huu ma, gɛɛ mɩŋŋabankarɛ waasɩ ma y. 
Bɩ, kʊ ŋ n'a ka zi mɩŋŋa barɛ ma, bala, lannɔ kʊ ŋ ʊ, a ɩ zum Woso ʊ rɔ wɔɔ, a ga a wʊm kʊ ŋ n'a ba ncɩnaaʊ. 
Hɔdɩndarɛ wakatɩ ʊ, lannɔ wɔɔ a ga m, ŋnɩ zɛ sɩɩ…, ŋnɩ yɛ hɔ haay ma. 
Mɔɔ bɩ yɛm kʊ lannɔ n hɔ dɩnda gʊɔɔ yaarɔ m, ŋn'a so ŋ ʊ y, a a ga m, ŋnɩ zɛ sɩɩ…. 
Bala, Adam Woso lɛɛ ka an a da, Ɛɛvɩ n bɔkarɛ an a nya ʊ. 
Adam bɛɛ Sʊtaana bra ba nɩ y, Ɛɛvɩ a bra ba m, an yɛ, an bɩsɩ ka Woso lɛ ma. 
Bɩ, k'a yɛ nyɩdazaambaa ma an a tʊntɔ ʊ sɩrakarɛ nɔ ʊ, kan ŋʊarɛ kɩ, mɩŋŋabaa kɩ, mɩŋŋazerle kɩ, a bʊmbɔrɛ yɩm.
