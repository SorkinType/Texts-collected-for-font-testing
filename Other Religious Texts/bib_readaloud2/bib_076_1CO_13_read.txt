1 Korɛntɩ zannɔ.
13.
Baa kʊ mɔɔ ɩ leminno haay hɩm, gʊɔɔ hɔ kan malɛɛkarɔ hɔ kɩ, kʊ mɔɔ ba ŋʊarɛ m, hɔsɩ bɛɛ m mɔɔ nɩ y. Bɔ mɔɔ ʊ kʊ ʊ kan lɔmbɩr k'a nyi nɩ bɔm kɩ, gɛɛ bɔ mɔɔ ʊ kʊ ʊ, kan sɩsaka k'a nyi nɩ bɔm kɩ. 
A a da ma mɔɔ m wosolɛsinnɩsora meerbaa ganwʊrɛ yɩ, m bɩr hɔdɔrɛ m haay, m hɔ k'a a nyaakʊm da haay dɔ, a a da ma mɔɔ m bɩr sɩrakarɛ m haay, m m dam ma m n'a kam ciro n'a gaarɛ a bɩncɛrɔ ʊ, lɛ meerbaa tɔkkɛ ma, kʊ mɔɔ ba ŋʊarɛ m hɔsɩ bɛɛ m mɔɔ nɩ y. 
Kʊ mɔɔ bɩ to m m hɔnyɩ haay jir cicirzannɔ m, m m da ma sɔ m yɛ fɩr ma, n m da sɛ rɛ, kʊ mɔɔ ba ŋʊarɛ m, naa woo… naa, vam m. 
Kʊ gʊaa ɩ ŋʊarɛ m, a ɩ bʊrlɛ a ma, a ɩ hɔ mɩŋŋa bam gʊɔɔ m, a bɩr jɩm barɛ y, a bɩr a lɛ bɔrɛ a mɩŋŋa ʊ y, a bɩr a mɩŋŋa busure y. 
Kʊ gʊaa ɩ ŋʊarɛ m, a bɩr zi daŋŋada barɛ y, a bɩr a karɛ a mɩŋŋa daahan ma y, a nɔ bɩr maŋ ʊ zɔɔ-zɔɔ y, a bɩr mim cɩntarɛ a heer ʊ kan gʊaa kɩ y, 
kʊ gʊaa ɩ ŋʊarɛ m, a bɩr a heer nyɔɔm zibʊnyaa barɛ ma y, a heer a n'a nyɔɔm sɩra ma. 
Kʊ gʊaa ɩ ŋʊarɛ m, a ɩ sugur bam hɔ haay nɔ ʊ, a ɩ sɩra kam dɔmɩm haay, a ɩ taaheerlɛ m tee…, a ɩ yɛm kɔrɛ do haay ma. 
Ŋʊarɛ nyarɛ ba ʊ y. Wosolɛsinnɩsora meerbaarɔ ganwʊrɛ nyarɛ, leminno y'a hʊnhɔ rɔ wɔɔ hɩrɛ nyarɛ, hɔdɔrɛ nyam sɔ. 
Bala, wɔɔ n hɔ haay dɔ y, wosolɛsinnɩsora zibəə kʊ wɔɔ n'a bam sɔ bɩ, wɔɔ bɩr a barɛ an n'a gam kʊ ʊ haay y. 
Bɩ, kʊ hɔ k'an mɩŋŋa paan… bɩ bɩ bʊr, hɔ k'a jirə n ta ʊ bɩ nyarɛ. 
Nyɩnta kʊ mɔɔ ʊ nyɩ bɩ, nyɩnzɛɛmbaa meer mɔɔ n'a barɛ, m nɩ m hɔɔn darɛ am nyɩ hɔ bɩ m, m nɩ nyɩbaa hɔɔn m. Bɩ kʊ mɔɔ bɩ m ba gʊaa burgu bɩ, mɔɔ nyɩbaa zibəəro wɔɔ to. 
Her-her naa, bɔ a ʊ kʊ ʊ, ɩ ʊ, dɩga wɔɔ nawʊm jɛtɩga ʊ, hɔ kʊ wɔɔ nɩ dɩgam ma bɩ bɩr yɩm fasɩ y, bɩ dɔmɩm ɩ zɛm wɔɔ m b'a yɩm garga ra haay. Her-her naa, mɔɔ hɔdɔrɛ jirə ta ʊ, bɩ hinni bɩ do ʊ, mɔɔ hɔ haay dɔm, amba Woso mɔɔ dɔ mɩŋŋa bɩ m. 
Kɛɛrɛ naa, hɔɔ kaakʊrɔ nɔɔn duro n ta ʊ, bɩ ŋ nyarɛ ba ʊ y: Sɩrakarɛ m, bɩ taaheerlɛ m kan ŋʊarɛ kɩ, bɩ ŋʊarɛ ɩ lɛ da ŋ nɩ haay.
