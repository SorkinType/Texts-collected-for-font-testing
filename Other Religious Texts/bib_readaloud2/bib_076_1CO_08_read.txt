1 Korɛntɩ zannɔ.
8.
Ʊ wosobaawɔnnɔ mannɩrɔ sinno wɔɔ mim dɩnda kɛɛrɛ. Awɔɔ y'a hɩrɛ, a ʊ: «Ʊ haay ʊ ta hɔdɔrɛ m.» Denter m, bɩ hɔdɔrɛ ɩ gʊaa dam mɩŋŋabusure ma. A n bɔkarɛ ŋʊarɛ y'a kam gʊɔɔ sɩrakarɛ n'a dam la. 
Kʊ gʊaa y'a hɔɔn darɛ a ʊ, a hɔ dɔ, k'an a dɔ a ʊ, a n hɔ dɔ amba a a ga m bɩ nɩ y. 
Bɩ, kʊ gʊaa Woso ŋʊa, Woso bɩ zaa dɔ a gʊaa. 
Kɛɛrɛ naa, wɔɔ ʊ ga m ʊ wosobaawɔnnɔ mannɩrɔ wɔɔ sim sʊ gɛɛ? Wɔɔ dɔ ʊ ʊ, wosobaawɔm deem taan… ba ʊ an mɩsɩr durnya ʊ y, bɩ Woso vanta ba ʊ, kʊ Woso k'an ta ʊ deem bɩ bɛɛ y. 
Bala, baa kʊ gʊɔɔ ɩ hɔɔ niŋŋoo kʊ ŋ nɩ brama gɛɛ tara ʊ birm wosoro, ŋnɩ ŋ ba denter sɔ wosoro kan zuubɔɔ kɩ, 
a bɩ ncɩnaaʊ wɔɔ zi y, wɔɔ n a dɔ bɩ, Woso deem taan…, Zɩ bɩ m, a to hɔ haay ʊ a mɩŋŋa zi, wɔɔ n ta ʊ a tɔ ma. Bɩ Zuuba bɩ deem sɔ, Yeezuu Krista m, a mɩŋŋa tɔ ma m hɔ haay woo… n ta ʊ, a tɔ ma m sɔ wɔɔ n ta ʊ. 
Bɩ, kristagʊɔɔ haay bɛɛ naa dɔ y. Gɔsɩnnɔ kʊ ŋ mim ka wosobaawɔnnɔ mannɩrɔ sinno wɔɔ m bɩ, ŋ gɔɔta ŋn'ɩ ŋ sʊm, ŋn'a hɔɔn darɛ ŋ ʊ, mannɩrɔ wɔɔ ka n nawʊm wosoro paan…rɔ ʊ. Naa minto bɩ, kʊ ŋ sɩrakarɛ paŋŋa ba ʊ bɩ, ŋ y'a hɔɔn darɛ ŋ ʊ, a kʊrɔ ma Woso mɩm ʊ, mannɩrɔ wɔɔ sinno wɔɔ sʊrɛ tɔ ma. 
Wɔɔ dɔ, ʊ ʊ, hɔbɩrɛ bɛɛ nɩ wɔɔ zɔm Woso ma y, kʊ wɔɔ n a bɩ, a b'a dam ma an wɔɔ ba laatʊ Woso ma y, kʊ wɔɔ bɩ sɔ a bɛɛ n n'a dam ma an wɔɔ zɔ Woso ma sɔ y. 
Bɩ, k'a dundo a zi, awɔɔ ɩ soamɩŋŋaʊ m k'a a hɔɔnhɔ bɩ, b'a ba ka k'awɔɔ soamɩŋŋaʊ bɩ n a ka kʊ gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ ba paŋŋa m cɩna Woso zuure bɩ nɔ ʊ rɔ wɔɔ n a do mimbʊnyaa nɔ ʊ y. 
Bala, ɩbɩɩ k'ɩ hɔ dɔ bɩ, kʊ gʊaa kʊ paŋŋawarzaa m Woso zuure nɔ ʊ b'ɩbɩɩ yɩ ɩ nɩ nyɩntam wosobaawɔm cɛ ʊ, ɩ n'a mannɩ sim sʊrɛ, naa y'a dam han denter la? A bɩ mannɩrɔ wɔɔ sim sʊm, bɩ n a heer kɔ ra? 
Yɩ a nawʊrɛ ncɩnaaʊ, ɩbɩɩ k'ɩ hɔ dɔ bɩ minto, paŋŋawarzaa n a dom Woso ma, b'ɩbɩɩ danyɩ m, Krista mɩŋŋa n ga a tɔ ma. 
K'awɔɔ ɩ naa bam, a bʊnyaa awɔɔ n'a barɛ a danyɩnɔ paŋŋawarzannɔ wɔɔ m, an nɩ ŋ heer warm kʊ ma, bɩ mimbʊnyaa naa do ba awɔɔ nawʊm Krista m sɔ. 
Naa minto bɩ, kʊ mɔɔ dɔ mɔɔ ʊ, mɔɔ danyɩ y'a dom mimbʊnyaa nɔ ʊ, hɔ kʊ mɔɔ n'a bɩm tɔ ma, k'a maam mɔɔ bɩ n yarɛ m sim sʊ fɩɩga y, kʊ m danyɩ b'a do y.
