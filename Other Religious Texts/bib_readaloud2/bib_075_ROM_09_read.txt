Rɔm zannɔ.
9.
Sɩra mɔɔ n'a hɩm, Krista zɛkʊra nɔ ʊ, mɔɔ bɩr ŋʊaar barɛ y, Sɛnt-Ɛspri a zɛ kʊ ra kan mɔɔ heer kɩ, ŋnɩ mɔɔ sɩra bɩ hɩrɛ kasɛtɩ karɛ. 
Mɔɔ ɩ heerzaarbarɛ gʊta m, m heer nɩ busum dɔmɩm haay. 
Mɔɔ y'a ŋʊam k'an bɩr yɩrɛ ɩ ʊ, Woso kaanm mɔɔ m, m bɩr laatʊ Krista ma, m danyɩnɔ, m zɩrɔ wɔɔ, mɔɔ do gʊɔɔ wɔɔ tɔ ma, k'a ɩ yɩrɛ, ɩ ʊ, naa yɩm ŋ bʊmbɔrɛ. 
Ŋ mɩŋŋɔɔ m Israyɛl zannɔ, Woso n ŋ bɔ ʊ k'a ŋ ba a nyɩnɔ, an a lɛbɔʊrɛ ka ŋ ʊ kan a gʊaasɩbabaa zaarɔ kɩ, a lɛrɔ kɩ, a Woso daʊrɛ zaa kɩ, lerbɔrɛrɔ kɩ. 
Ŋ yaabɔɔ m Abraham m, Izakkɩ kan Zakɔɔb kɩ m. Kʊ yɩrɛ nɔ ʊ nɩ, Krista bɩ, ŋ do gʊaa m. Krista mɩŋŋa nɩ hɔ haay burə ʊ. A m Woso, a tɔ m lɛbɔʊrɛ yɩ dɔmɩm haay nɔ ʊ. Amina! 
A bɩr yɩrɛ ɩ ʊ, jɩra Woso ʊ ler k'a bɔ bɩ ra wa, bɩ, Israyɛl yaŋŋɔɔ wɔɔ haay bɛɛ m Israyɛl do gʊɔɔ paan…rɔ y, 
Abraham yaŋŋɔɔ haay bɛɛ n a nyɩnɔ paan…rɔ y, bala, Woso a hɩ Abraham m, a ʊ: ‹Izakkɩ n n'ɩ nyɩnɔ kʊ mɔɔ ŋ ler bɔ ɩbɩɩ m nɔ wɔɔ yɩrɛ.› 
Naa k'a hɩ, a ʊ: nyɩnɔ kʊ gʊaa nɩ ŋ yɩrɛ, am gʊaa haay hɔ bɩ m bɩ, brɔɔ bɛɛ m Woso nyɩnɔ y, bɩ nyɩnɔ kʊ n yɩ ŋ ler bɔ rɔ wɔɔ, brɔɔ n nɩ dɩgarɛ ŋ ma yaŋŋɔɔ paan…rɔ. 
Ler kʊ Woso a bɔ bɩ ba n nɩ: ‹Kʊ dɔmɩm kʊ mɔɔ cɩnta b'a ku, mɔɔ yɩ m yarɛ m bʊr, Saara m nyɩyar yɩ.› 
Naa bɛɛ n a deem nɩ y, cirgərenyɩnɔ kʊ Rebeka ŋ yɩ rɔ wɔɔ zɩ deem, wɔɔ yaaba Izakkɩ m. 
A n bɔkarɛ, Woso y'a mɩŋŋa zaa m, an n'a gʊaa bɔŋ ʊ a hɔɔnhɔ ma, a bɩr dɩgam zi kʊ gʊɔɔ a ba bɩ ma y, dɩga a nawʊm a mɩŋŋa lɛtaama ma, an nɩ gʊɔɔ birm. Woso k'a lɛ n taa kʊ zaa naa do n gɔɔta an bɩr ta ʊ bɩ, an a to nyɩnɔ wɔɔ m yɩ y. Maam bɩ, ŋ n a da ma ŋnɩ hɔ mɩŋŋa, gɛɛ hɔ bʊnyaa ba y. 
A n lɛɛ ka nyɩnɔ wɔɔ yɩrɛ ma bɩ, a a hɩ Rebeka m, a ʊ: ‹Nyɩnɔ wɔɔ poore bɩ n n'a som jɩtaarɛ b'ʊ.› 
Amba n y'a gʊrsɩra ba Wosoci ʊ, n ʊ: ‹Mɔɔ Zakɔɔb ŋʊa, m Eso bɔ m jɛ m› bɩ m. 
Bɔ wɔɔ kʊ a hɩ? Kʊ a hɩ, ʊ ʊ, Woso bɩr a zi bam tɩrga ra? Ayy…, fɩɩga! 
Bala, Woso a hɩ Moyiisi m, a ʊ: ‹Gʊaa kʊ mɔɔ lɛ n taa bɩ, mɔɔ nɩ m ŋʊarɛ zi bam kan kɩ, gʊaa kʊ mɔɔ lɛ n taa bɩ, mɔɔ n'a cicir zɛ m.› 
Woso mɩŋŋabaa bɩ bɩr zum kʊ ʊ kan gʊaa hɔɔnhɔ kɩ y, b'a ba a zibəə ma sɔ y. Woso deem an ta ma, kʊ nka a lɛ n taa b'a bɩ zaa cicir zɛ. 
Naa minto m Wosoci bɩ n'a hɩ Eziptɩ cir m, a ʊ: ‹Mɔɔ y'ɩbɩɩ cɩnta cir kʊ m m paŋŋa bɩ hɩnka, bɩsɩ k'ɩ n'a kam mɔɔ ma bɩ nɔ ʊ, kʊ mɔɔ tɔ m bɔ durnya lɛɛ haay ʊ.› 
Ncɩnaaʊ bɩ, kʊ nka Woso lɛ n taa, b'a cicir zɛ, kʊ nka a lɛ n taa sɔ, b'a ka sɔ a zaa n a ba bɩsɩkər heerkarɛ nɔ ʊ. 
Gʊaa a da ma an a hɩ, a ʊ: «K'a ɩ yɩrɛ ncɩnaaʊ, bɔ m Woso n a ya, an nɩ həən sim gʊɔɔ ma? Bala, nka n'a dam ma an bɩsɩ ka a hɔɔnhɔ ma?» 
Bɩ, ɩbɩɩ k'ɩ m gʊaa, bɔ zaa n'ɩbɩɩ m k'ɩ bɩsɩ ka kan Woso kɩ? Taanm y'a hɩm a dɔrɛ zaa bɩ m, a ʊ: «Bɔ m, ɩbɩɩ m mɔɔ dɔ ncɩnaaʊ gɛ?» 
Mɔrdor y'a so ji k'a nɩ zi bam nɩ b'ʊ, a a da ma, an ji deem-deem bɩ yɔɔ an mɔr dɔ m cibsa tɔ ma, an mɔr dɔ m sɔ, dɔmɩm haay zibəə tɔ ma. 
Woso a ŋʊa k'a a nɔmaa hɩnka, a a ŋʊa sɔ kʊ n n'a paŋŋa dɔ, an bɔkarɛ, gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ y'a ga dʊdɔyarlɛ m Woso nɔ k'a ma ʊ kan ŋ kɩ bɩ minto rɔ wɔɔ, Woso a gu an bʊr a mɩŋŋa ma, an ŋ dʊdɔ yar y. 
K'a lɛbɔʊrɛ gʊtabaa hɩnka gʊɔɔ k'a nɩ ŋ cicir zɛm nɔ wɔɔ m. Lɛɛ a ka an brɔɔ banka k'a lɛbɔʊrɛ ka ŋ ʊ. 
Bɩ, gʊɔɔ wɔɔ duro m wɔɔ m, a n wɔɔ bir zɩɩfʊrɔ barla bala y, wɔɔ bir a ʊ sɔ doro vantɔɔ gʊɔɔ barla. 
Naa Woso a hɩ wosolɛsinnɩsorazaa Ozee ci ʊ bɩ, a a hɩ, a ʊ: Do kʊ mɔɔ do bɛɛ m bɩ, mɔɔ y'a hɩm ma, m do, do kʊ mɔɔ bɩr a ŋʊam biisi bɩ, mɔɔ y'a hɩm ma, m ŋʊarɛ do. 
Lɛɛ kʊ n y'a hɩm nɩ hɔɔ, n ʊ: ‹Mɔɔ do bɛɛ n'awɔɔ m bɩ,› lɛɛ bɩ do n ŋ birŋ ʊ, n ʊ: ‹Woso k'an mɩsɩr bɩ nyɩnɔ.› 
Ezayii Israyɛl do bɩ mim hɩ sɔ, a ʊ: ‹Baa kʊ Israyɛl gʊɔɔ wɔɔ pasɩ, an higʊta lɛ ra nyɩntaayaa m bɩ, gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ nɩ bʊmbɔrɛ yɩrɛ ŋ barla rɔ wɔɔ, bɩ yɩm gʊta y. 
Bala, Zuuba hɔ k'a nyɩnta an a hɩ haay bɩ bam tara burə ʊ zɔɔ-zɔɔ.› 
Amba Ezayii a ya, an a hɩ, a ʊ: Kʊ hɔ haay Zuuba bɩ bɛɛ yaŋŋɔɔ to wɔɔ m poore y, bɔ wɔɔ nawʊm kʊ ʊ kan Sodɔm kʊ kɩ, ʊ ʊ ba am Gomɔɔr kʊ hɔ bɩ m. 
Naa jɩ m bɔ nɩ? Do vantɔɔ gʊɔɔ wɔɔ kʊ ŋ n nyɩnta ŋ n'a ka tɩrgabaa ma rɔ wɔɔ, ŋ tɩrgabaa yɩ, tɩrgabaa k'a a to sɩrakarɛ nɔ ʊ m. 
A n bɔkarɛ, Israyɛl gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ n'a kam lɛro kʊ ŋ nɩ tɩrgabaa karɛ ŋ ʊ rɔ wɔɔ ma bɩ, ŋ n a da ma, ŋ n zu lɛrɔ wɔɔ duro ʊ y. 
Bɔ minto nɩ? A ɩ yɩrɛ ɩ ʊ, Israyɛl n a ka ma k'a yɩ mɩŋŋa Woso mɩm ʊ sɩrakarɛ nɔ ʊ y, a ka a ʊ ma, k'a yɩ mɩŋŋa zibəəro mɩŋŋɔɔ barɛ nɔ ʊ. Ncɩnaaʊ nɩ, ŋn'a gəm zɛ, gənzɛrɛ jaa bɩ ma, 
amba n y'a gʊrsɩra ba Wosoci ʊ bɩ m, n ʊ: K'a dɩga a, mɔɔ ɩ gənzɛrɛ jaa cɩntam *Siyɔn ʊ, jaa m, an nɩ gʊaa dom. Bɩ kʊ gʊaa sɩra ka ʊ, a bɩ nyannɩ bɩrɛ y.
