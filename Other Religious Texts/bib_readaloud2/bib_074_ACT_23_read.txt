Zibəəzannɔ Ci.
23.
Pol n dɩga dʊdɔkənno wɔɔ ma sɩɩ…, an a hɩ, a ʊ: «M danyɩnɔ, mɔɔ mɩsɩrbaa ba kan noom fu kɩ Woso mɩm ʊ, an ta, an her naa ku.» 
Bɩ, wosocɛmannɩbər jɩtaarɛ Ananɩyas lɛ ka a gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ n'a jɩr ʊ rɔ wɔɔ ʊ, a ʊ, ŋn'a zɛ a lɛ ma. Pol n a hɩ m, a ʊ: 
«Ɩbɩɩ Woso n n'ɩ zɛm, noonkundəzaa ra! Nyɩnta ɩbɩɩ ʊ naa ʊ, ɩ zu lɛrɔ ʊ, k'ɩ mɔɔ dʊdɔ ka, ɩ bɔkarɛ ɩ kaanm lɛrɔ wɔɔ m, ɩ lɛ ka kʊ ŋ nɩ mɔɔ zɛ ra?» 
Gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ nɩ b'ʊ rɔ wɔɔ n həən si ma, ŋ ʊ: «Wosocɛmannɩbər jɩtaarɛ b'ɩbɩɩ n'a sʊnsɔ wʊm naa ra?» Pol n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: 
«Mɔɔ n a dɔ mɔɔ ʊ wosocɛmannɩbər jɩtaarɛ nɩ y, m danyɩnɔ. Kʊ sɩra nɩ, n y'a gʊrsɩra ba, n ʊ: ‹Ɩ b'ɩ do cir sʊnsɔ wʊ y.› » 
Pol k'a b'a dɔ, a ʊ, gʊɔɔ wɔɔ sadisɛɛnrɔ m kan farizɛɛnrɔ kɩ bɩ, a a leer busu brama dʊdɔkənno wɔɔ bire ʊ, a ʊ: «Farizɛn m mɔɔ m, m danyɩnɔ, farizɛɛnrɔ nyɩ m mɔɔ m. Kʊ mɔɔ si mɔɔ ʊ gəəno y'a mɩm bɔŋ ʊ bɩ y'a ka ŋn'ɩ mɔɔ dʊdɔ kam.» 
K'a meer naa do ba an a nya bɩ, nyaa wuti farizɛɛnrɔ wɔɔ bire ʊ kan sadisɛɛnrɔ wɔɔ kɩ. Zamaa bɩ n a gʊngʊ. 
Sadisɛɛnrɔ wɔɔ ʊ, gəəno mɩmbɔʊrɛ ba ʊ y, malɛɛka ba ʊ, nyi ba ʊ sɔ y. Farizɛɛnrɔ n bɔkarɛ, ŋn'a sim, ŋ ʊ, ŋ ta ʊ. 
Bʊgʊr paan… nyɩnta ʊ. Wosocikarɩnsaambɔɔ gɔsɩnnɔ wuti farizɛɛnrɔ wɔɔ bire ʊ ŋnɩ meer ba kan heerbusu kɩ, ŋ ʊ: «Wɔɔ n gʊaa naa do kʊrɔ ma yɩ y. Kʊ nyi ɩ meer ba kan kɩ, gɛɛ malɛɛka, wɔɔ n a dɔ y.» 
Kʊ nyaa bɩ nɩ paŋŋa yɩm bɩ, sʊrdaa tisi lɛɛzaa b'ɩ nyi bam, ŋ y'a Pol kɩnkaansʊ. A lɛ ka kʊ gʊɔɔ n a bɔ bire ʊ, ŋnɩ ta m sʊrdaarɔ wʊtajɩ ʊ b'ʊ. 
Gunuu bɩ do ʊ, Zuuba a hɩnka Pol m, a ʊ: «Ɩ da bɩ bɔ ʊ, amba ɩbɩɩ mɔɔ kasɛtɩ ka Zerizalɛm ʊ bɩ, ɩbɩɩ mɔɔ kasɛtɩ kam Rɔm ʊ maam sɔ.» 
Dɔ k'a mɛ bɩ, zɩɩfʊrɔ wɔɔ a dɩnda ŋn'a zɛ kan kʊ kɩ, ŋn'a wer si k'a bɩ hɔ bɩ, a hɔ mi k'a n Pol zɛ gər y. 
Kutim-kutim naa do nɔ ʊ, gʊɔɔ wɔɔ lɛ lɛ da busi ra, ŋn'a wer bɩ si. 
Ŋ n ta, ŋn'aa wosocɛmannɩbənno jɩtannɔ wɔɔ yɩ kan jɩtannɔ wɔɔ kɩ, ŋn'a hɩ ŋ nɩ, ŋ ʊ: «Wɔɔ ʊ wer si, ʊ ʊ, kʊ wɔɔ n Pol zɛ gər y, wɔɔ bɩ hɔ bɩrɛ y. 
Kɛɛrɛ bɩ, awɔɔ kan dʊdɔkənno wɔɔ kɩ, k'a kʊ yɩ, k'a hɩ sʊrdaa tisi lɛɛzaa bɩ m k'an a gaa, an a ka a ʊ, ɩ ʊ, awɔɔ lɛ n taa k'a b'a banka a lar. Kʊ wɔɔ nɩ, hɔ kʊ wɔɔ n n'a bam, ʊ a ku, ʊ a zɛ b'a n lee ku bɩ, wɔɔ ɩ bɩ dɔ.» 
Pol danlʊ nyɩ n minno nɔɔn duro ma. A n ta sʊrdaarɔ wʊtajɩ ʊ, an aa gasʊ, an a hɩ Pol m. 
Pol n sʊrdaa zɔrɔ lɛɛzaa deem bir, an a hɩ m, a ʊ: «Nyukumbueere naa gaa, ɩ ta m sʊrdaa tisi lɛɛzaa bɩ zi, a lɛ n taa k'a meer ba kan kɩ.» 
Sʊrdaa zɔrɔ lɛɛzaa bɩ n a gaa, an ta m sʊrdaa tisi lɛɛzaa bɩ zi, an a hɩ m, a ʊ: «Lɔ Pol b'ɩ mɔɔ bir, an a yɛ mɔɔ han, kʊ m nyukumbueere naa do gaa, m bʊr m ɩbɩɩ zi. A lɛ n taa k'a meer ba kan ɩbɩɩ kɩ.» 
Sʊrdaa tisi lɛɛzaa bɩ n a gaa a wɔ ma, an bɔ m toore ma, an a lar, a ʊ: «Bɔ ɩbɩɩ ʊ k'ɩ hɩ mɔɔ nɩ?» Nyukumbueere bɩ n a hɩ m, a ʊ: 
«Zɩɩfʊrɔ wɔɔ a dɩnda ŋn'a zɛ kan kʊ kɩ, k'a yɛ ɩbɩɩ han, k'ɩ Pol gaa ɩ bʊrm booti m dʊdɔkənno wɔɔ taa, ɩ ʊ, ŋ lɛ n taa k'a banka a lar a mim bɩ jɩ ma mɩŋŋa k'a a dɔ. 
Ɩ bɩ ŋ meerbaa bɩ si y. Ŋ lɛ dam gʊaa busi ra, ŋnɩ kunku ma. Ŋ y'a wer si k'a bɩ hɔsɩ bɩ, a bɩ hi mi k'awɔɔ n Pol zɛ gər y. Ŋ y'a banka, ŋnɩ nya, ŋn'ɩ dɔrɛ ɩbɩɩ zaakarɛ ma.» 
Sʊrdaa tisi lɛɛzaa bɩ n nyukumbueere bɩ da zaa ra, an a hɩ m, a ʊ: «Ɩ b'a hɩ gɔsɩ nɩ, ɩ ʊ, ɩ hɔ naa do hɩ mɔɔ nɩ y.» 
Sʊrdaa tisi lɛɛzaa bɩ n sʊrdaa zɔrɔ lɛɛzannɔ bir hɩɩya, an a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «K'a a banka, k'a ta Sezaree ʊ yirbəə wakatɩ nɛɛhʊ ma, sʊrdaarɔ zɔɔhɩra, siirozannɔ busaanhɩna, kannɩzannɔ zɔɔhɩra. 
K'a siiro banka, kʊ Pol n da ŋ ʊ an lee ku kan laafɩɩ kɩ tara lɛɛzaa Feligsi zi.» 
A n ci ba, an a hɩ ncɩnaaʊ: 
«Mɔɔ Klodiyus Liziyas nɩ yaa dam tara lɛɛzaa mɩŋŋa Feligsi m. 
Zɩɩfʊrɔ ɩ gʊaa kʊ mɔɔ n'a nyɔɔrɛ ɩbɩɩ zi naa nyasʊ ŋn'a ŋʊam k'a zɛ. Kʊ mɔɔ b'a ma ŋ ʊ Rɔm gʊaa m bɩ, mɔɔ ta kan m sʊrdaarɔ kɩ, m aa yɔ. 
Kʊ mɔɔ lɛ n taa kʊ m mim kʊ ŋ y'a ka ra bɩ dɔ bɩ, mɔɔ ka ŋnɩ ta m ŋ dʊdɔkənno taa. 
Mɔɔ bɩ dɩga a, bɩsɩ ŋ n'a kam, a lɛrɔ wɔɔ ma, ŋnɩ mim ka ra. B'a n hɔsɩ ba, an a ga zɛ m, gɛɛ ŋn'a sur baŋŋarɔ nɩ y. 
Bɩ, kʊ mɔɔ ma ŋ ʊ, zɩɩfʊrɔ wɔɔ ɩ dundom zi k'a zɛ bɩ y'a ka, mɔɔ n'a nyɔɔrɛ ɩbɩɩ zi zɔɔ-zɔɔ. Mɔɔ hɩ gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ mim ka ra rɔ wɔɔ m sɔ, kʊ ŋ nɩ bʊr ɩbɩɩ zi, ŋnɩ b'a gumə lemim hɩ.» 
Sʊrdaarɔ wɔɔ n lɛ kʊ n y'a ka ŋ ʊ bɩ si, ŋnɩ Pol gaa, ŋnɩ ta m gunuu ʊ Antipatris ʊ. 
Dɔ k'a mɛ bɩ, kannɩzannɔ wɔɔ m Pol to siirozannɔ wɔɔ m, ŋn'a wusigə, ŋnɩ bʊr sʊrdaarɔ wʊtajɩ ʊ b'ʊ. 
Kʊ ŋ yaa lee ku Sezaree ʊ bɩ, siirozannɔ wɔɔ ci bɩ ka tara lɛɛzaa b'ʊ, ŋnɩ Pol hɩnka m sɔ. 
Tara lɛɛzaa bɩ n ci bɩ karɩnda ba, an laaka da kʊ Pol bɩ, a to a ʊ gʊrga kara ʊ gɛ. K'a a dɔ a ʊ, a to a ʊ Silisii ʊ bɩ, a a hɩ m, a ʊ: 
«Gʊɔɔ k'ʊ ŋ mim ka ɩbɩɩ ra bɩ, kʊ ŋ bʊr, mɔɔ yɩ m tʊr karɛ ɩbɩɩ meerbaa ma.» A n lɛ ka, a ʊ, ŋnɩ dɔ ma naa ʊ, hargʊta kʊ Hɩrɔɔdɩ a dɔ b'ʊ.
