1 Tesaloniki zannɔ.
3.
Yɩ a ʊ ncɩnaaʊ, kʊ wɔɔ n'a ŋʊam kʊ awɔɔ yɩ, bɩ wɔɔ bɩr ʊ dam ma bɩ, wɔɔ n kɔ ʊ lɛ sa, ʊ ʊ k'ʊ gɔɔta ʊ minto Atɛnnɩ ʊ. 
A m bɩ, wɔɔ n'ʊ danyɩ Timote nyɔɔ, wɔɔ zibəəkɔɔn m an nɩ Woso zi bam Krista lemim nyɩnta bɩ padarɛ nɔ ʊ. A nyɔɔ wɔɔ ʊ awɔɔ ma k'an awɔɔ paŋŋa da ra, an awɔɔ heer yo a sɩrakarɛ nɔ ʊ, 
k'an bɩr yɩrɛ ɩ ʊ, awɔɔ gɔsɩ b'a bɔ a jɛ m fɩr k'awɔɔ n'a yɩm naa minto y. Bala, awɔɔ dɔ a ʊ, Woso zaa bɩ nɔ ʊ, pakra m kʊ wɔɔ n fɩr yɩ. 
Nyɩnta kʊ wɔɔ ʊ awɔɔ zi b'ʊ bɩ, wɔɔ ser ka ʊ a hɩ awɔɔ m, ʊ ʊ, pakra m kʊ wɔɔ n fɩr yɩ, b'awɔɔ dɔ, a ʊ, hɔ kʊ wɔɔ hɩ b'ɩ ba bɩ. 
Naa tɔ ma m, kʊ mɔɔ n m da ma dɔ, m bʊr m ma kʊ m awɔɔ mim dɔ bɩ, mɔɔ n Timote nyɔɔ, k'an awɔɔ sɩrakarɛ mim dɔ, k'an b'a hɩ m m. Bala, mɔɔ da bɔ ʊ, mɔɔ ʊ, ɩ b'a ka Sʊtaana n awɔɔ makra ba, awɔɔ m sɩrakarɛ to, zi kʊ wɔɔ ba awɔɔ zi bɩ n bɔ vam y. 
Bɩ kɛɛrɛ naa, Timote a to awɔɔ zi b'ʊ an bʊr, a lemim nyɩnta hɩ wɔɔ m, awɔɔ sɩrakarɛ kan awɔɔ ŋʊarɛ kɩ mim bɩncɛ ʊ, a a hɩ, a ʊ, wɔɔ tɔ gɔɔta an awɔɔ nɔ ʊ nyɩnta, bɩ amba wɔɔ lɛ n taa k'ʊ ʊ ya, ʊ awɔɔ yɩ bɩ, awɔɔ lɛ taa k'a a ya, awɔɔ yɩ sɔ. 
Ʊ danyɩnɔ, yɩ a ʊ ncɩnaaʊ awɔɔ sɩrakarɛ minto bɩ, wɔɔ n heerkoodəbaa yɩ, ʊ fɩryɩrɛ kan ʊ makrarɔ kɩ nɔ ʊ. 
Bala, wɔɔ ɩ heernyɔɔ m kɛɛrɛ ɩ ʊ, mɩsɩrbaa daa wɔɔ yɩ, k'awɔɔ nɩ jɩnɩm kɔɔkɔɔ Zuuba zuure nɔ ʊ bɩ minto. 
Awɔɔ tɔ ma, wɔɔ b'ʊ dam ma ʊ Woso barka da, ʊ a nya y, heernyɔɔ gʊta k'awɔɔ n'a kam wɔɔ ʊ a taa bɩ minto. 
Sinuu ʊ kan gunuu ʊ kɩ wɔɔ y'a yɛm han gʊta, k'an a ka k'ʊ ʊ da ma, ʊ awɔɔ yɩ k'ʊ hɔ k'a jɩra awɔɔ sɩrakarɛ ma bɩ na awɔɔ m ma. 
Wɔɔ gɔɔta ʊ n'a yɛm wɔɔ Zɩ Woso mɩŋŋa kan wɔɔ Zuuba Yeezuu kɩ han, kʊ ŋ nɩ wɔɔ zaa banka k'ʊ ʊ da ma, ʊ bʊr awɔɔ zi. 
Wɔɔ y'a yɛm kʊ Zuuba n a ka k'awɔɔ ŋʊarɛ kan kʊ kɩ kan gʊɔɔ haay kɩ bɩ n a da ra an bɩr tam lɛɛ tee…, amba wɔɔ n'awɔɔ ŋʊam bɩ m. 
K'an a ba maam, an awɔɔ heer yo, k'awɔɔ m bɩr mɩŋŋa, mim ba awɔɔ gʊr zi wɔɔ Zɩ Woso taa y, hinni kʊ wɔɔ Zuuba Yeezuu n nɩ zɛŋ ʊ kan a gʊɔɔ kɩ haay bɩ tɔ ma.
