Zibəəzannɔ Ci.
12.
Bɩ ma bɩ, cir *Hɩrɔɔdɩ a ka, ŋnɩ gʊɔɔ gɔsɩnnɔ kʊ ŋ nɩ zum Yeezuu ʊ rɔ wɔɔ gʊr, k'a fɩr ka ŋ ma. 
A a ka, n n Zaan danyɩ Zakkɩ bɩ zɛ kargʊ m. 
K'a dɩga a zɩɩfʊrɔ wɔɔ heer nyɔɔ mim naa do ma bɩ, a a ya, an a ka, ŋnɩ Pɩyɛɛr nyasʊ. Nyɩnta a ʊ, bur kʊ n n nyʊnyɔɔ da ma *cibsa dɔmɩm ʊ. 
K'a a ka, ŋn'a nyasʊ, ŋn'a du lɔnnɔcɛ ʊ bɩ, a a ka sʊrdaarɔ n dɔ ma gʊaa bueeresɔrdɩ, ŋn'a sinsim kʊ m si-si, k'a b'a dʊdɔ ka pakkɩ cenle kur gʊɔɔ lɛɛ. 
Dɔmɩm kʊ Pɩyɛɛr nɩ lɔnnɔcɛ ʊ bɩ, sokʊma bɩ y'a tɔŋ ʊ kan Woso yaadarɛ kɩ kʊsɩ-kʊsɩ a minto. 
N Pɩyɛɛr kʊsɩ baŋŋarɔ m hɩɩya. A dɔ k'a nɩ mɛm kʊ Hɩrɔɔdɩ n a bɔ bɩ, gum bɩ do ʊ, Pɩyɛɛr ɩ hinceem bam sʊrdaa hɩɩya bire ʊ, sʊrdaarɔ nɩ dɔm lɔnnɔcɛ bɩ lɛ ma. 
Lɛɛm bɩ, Zuuba malɛɛka sʊr zɛ, lɛɛgure n cɛ bɩ han haay. Malɛɛka bɩ n Pɩyɛɛr zɛ pa-pa a boon ma, an a gu, a ʊ: «Wuti zɔɔ-zɔɔ!» Baŋŋarɔ wɔɔ n a hʊnhʊr Pɩyɛɛr wɔrɔ wɔɔ ma. 
Malɛɛka bɩ n a ya, an a hɩ m, a ʊ: «Ɩ hɔsoaʊ bɩ sa, ɩ so ɩ ʊ, k'ɩ ɩ kʊsɔrɔ wɔɔ da ʊ!» A n a ba maam. Malɛɛka bɩ n a ya, an a hɩ, a ʊ: «Ɩ zaba bɩ sa, ɩ da ɩ ra, k'ɩ zu mɔɔ ʊ!» 
Pɩyɛɛr n bɔ, an zu a jɛ ʊ. B'a n a dɔ a ʊ, hɔ kʊ malɛɛka b'a ba bɩ, sɩra nɩ y, a ɩ dɩgam ma a ʊ makra awɔɔ yɩ. 
Ŋ n lɛɛ ka, ŋnɩ cem lɛɛkarɛ bɩncɛ bɩ han kan a dɔmazannɔ kɩ, ŋn'a ya, ŋnɩ cem a hɩɩya hɔ bɩ han, ŋn'a lee ku pɔyaa cɛlɛ k'a nya kʊ bɩ rɛ bɩ ma. A n a gʊ a zim ŋ lɛɛ. Kʊ ŋ bɔ garga ra bɩ, ŋnɩ ta, ŋn'aa zada bɩ ku, lɛɛm bɩ, malɛɛka b'a gʊ Pɩyɛɛr ma. 
Pɩyɛɛr hɔɔn n zer kɛɛrɛ. A n a hɩ, a ʊ: «Naa ma bɩ, mɔɔ dɔ kɛɛrɛ. Sɩra m, Zuuba a malɛɛka nyɔɔ, an bɩ mɔɔ bɔ Hɩrɔɔdɩ wɔ ʊ kan zɩɩfʊrɔ kɩ, kan hɔ haay kʊ zɩɩfʊrɔ wɔɔ a cɩnta k'a ba mɔɔ m bɩ kɩ.» 
K'a hɔɔn bɩ zer bɩ, a a hʊr, an ta Zaan kʊ n so ra, n n a tɔ ka Markɩ bɩ da Maarii har ʊ. B'ʊ bɩ, gʊɔɔ gʊta nyɩnta b'ʊ, ŋn'ɩ yaa dam. 
K'a cɛlɛ bɩ zɛ bɩ, Zibəənyɩlʊ k'a tɔ m Rode bɩ bʊr k'a bɩ dɩga a. 
A Pɩyɛɛr leer dɔ, kan heernyɔɔ kɩ, cɛlɛ bɩ gʊrɛ tɔ bɔ a nɔ ʊ, an a wusigə an bra si, k'a yaa hɩ a ʊ, Pɩyɛɛr bʊr, an nɩ cɛlɛ ma. 
Ŋ n a hɩ m, ŋ ʊ: «Ɩbɩɩ mim y'a wusigə ra?» A n gɔɔta, an n'a hɔ bɩ ra, a ʊ sɩra m. Ŋ n a hɩ, ŋ ʊ: A malɛɛka bɛɛ ra? 
Pɩyɛɛr n gɔɔta, an nɩ cɛlɛ bɩ zɛm. Ŋ n kɔ ŋn'a gʊ, kʊ ŋ y'a yɩ bɩ, ŋ n zɛ kədə. 
A n meer ba a wɔ m, a ʊ, ŋnɩ zɛ pi. Hɔ kʊ Zuuba a ba, an a bɔ lɔnnɔcɛ ʊ bɩ, a bɩ jɩsa an n'a dɩndarɛ ŋ nɩ, an b'a nyambʊr, a ʊ: «K'a ta, a yaa hɩ *Zakkɩ m kan danyɩnɔ wɔɔ kɩ.» A n bɔ, an ta bɩncɛ vanta ʊ. 
Dɔmɩrɛ ma bɩ, tatʊr gʊta nyɩnta ʊ sʊrdaarɔ wɔɔ bire ʊ. Ŋ n a dɔ kʊ Pɩyɛɛr a ba lɔ y? 
Hɩrɔɔdɩ n a ka, ŋn'a ka ma, bɩ ŋ n a yɩ y. A n a ka, ŋnɩ sʊrdaarɔ wɔɔ lar, an lɛ ka a ʊ, ŋnɩ ŋ zɩnzɛ. Bɩ jɛ ʊ bɩ, Pɩyɛɛr a to Zidee ʊ, an ta Sezaree ʊ, an aa gɔɔta b'ʊ dɔmɩm poore nɔ ʊ. 
Hɩrɔɔdɩ heer wuti kan Tiir zannɔ kɩ, Sidɔn zannɔ kɩ. Brɔɔ n a lɛ ba deem, ŋnɩ bʊr k'a b'a yɩ. Ŋ n gʊaasɩbabaa da cirhar dɩgamazaa Blastus ma, ŋn'a zɛ kʊ ra, ŋnɩ bʊr k'a bɩ kʊ ma. Bala, ŋ kʊ bɩ hɔbɩrɛ bɩ, bɔ a nawʊm cir bɩ kʊ b'ʊ. 
Dɔmɩm kʊ ŋ y'a bɔ ʊ bɩ k'a a ku bɩ, Hɩrɔɔdɩ a cirbəəhuu da a ra, an nyɩnta duu burə ʊ, an nɩ gʊtabaa meer bam kan gʊɔɔ wɔɔ kɩ, 
ŋn'ɩ zee dam Hɩrɔɔdɩ mim ʊ, ŋ ʊ: «Woso leer n naa, gʊaa hɔ bɛɛ y!» 
Lɛɛm bɩ, Zuuba malɛɛka Hɩrɔɔdɩ zɛ, k'a n a lɛ bɔ Woso ʊ bɩ minto. Mukuno n a bɩ, an ga. 
Dɔmɩm bɩ do ʊ, Woso lemim y'a dam la, karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ lɛ n n'a zum la. 
Barnabas kan Sol kɩ kʊ ŋ y'a dɔ, ŋ ʊ, a zi bɩ ba an nya Zerizalɛm ʊ bɩ, ŋ y'a bɔ ŋn'ɩ tam Antɩyɔs ʊ. Ŋ Zaan kʊ n so ra ŋn'a tɔ ka Markɩ bɩ gaa kan a kɩ, ŋnɩ ta m.
