Musiɓɓe, si goɗɗo e mon aru e waɗugol bonnere, haray onon, ɓe Ruuhu on ardii ɓen, wurtiree mo hakkil newiiki. Onon tigi reenoɗon, fii wota onon kadi on jarribe. 
Rondondiranee anndeeji mon ɗin. Ko non timminirton Sariya *Almasiihu on. 
On mo no sikki ko o goɗɗun, hara-le hinaa o huunde, haray o ɗaynitike kanko tigi. 
Mo kala yo tasko kuuɗe mun ɗen tigi, o yi'anay hoore makko hara hinaa e ɓeya, ɗun o anday no o mantora, 
ko fii mo kala no haani rondaade dongal mun ngal. 
Yo on wonaaɗo janneede daaluyee on sendodu kala piiji moƴƴi e wonɗo mo jannude on. 
Wota on ɗaynito, ko fii Alla yawitetaake, ko fii kala ko goɗɗo sanki ko ɗun o wa'ata. 
Mo sankii mbeleeɗe ɓandu wa'ay e mayru halakuyee, kono on sankuɗo faale Ruuhu on, o wa'ay e makko *ngurndan poomayankejan. 
Wota en haaɓu waɗugol ko moƴƴi, ko fii en wa'oyay e saa'i haanuɗo si maayu ɓernde heɓaali en. 
Ko ɗun waɗi, fewndo ka weeɓani en ɗon, waɗanen yimɓe ɓen fow ko moƴƴi, tentinii musiɓɓe men ɓen ka gomɗinal. 
On yi'ii ittiri bindi njani ɗi mi windirani on jungo an ngon ɗin. 
Kala ɓen faalaaɓe waɗude ko welata yimɓe ɓen kon, ɓe karhay on fii *sunninegol, hara ko fii tun wota ɓe tampine sabu *leggal altindiraangal Almasiihu on. 
Ko fii hay kamɓe sunninaaɓe ɓen tigi ɓe jokkataa Sariya on, kono ko ɓe faalirtee yo on sunnine, ko fii no ɓe mantora e hoore mon. 
Min non hinaa non! Ko fii mi mantortaa huunde si hinaa leggal altindiraangal Iisaa Almasiihu Joomi men on. Sabu maggal aduna on maayii telen-ma an, min kadi mi maayii telen-ma aduna on. 
Awa hinaa sunninegol maa angal sunninegol hitti, kono ko ko Alla heyɗintini en kon hitti. 
Yo ɓuttu e yurmeende wonan kala jokkuɗo ndee yamiroore, hay e *Isra'iila mo Alla. 
Ka heddii ɗoo, wota hay gooto tampinan, ko fii ɓattaate Iisaa ɗen no ka ɓandu an. 
Yo moƴƴere Iisaa Almasiihu Joomi men on wonu e ɓerɗe mon, yo musiɓɓe. Aamiina!
