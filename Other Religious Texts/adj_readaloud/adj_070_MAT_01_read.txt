Jesu Krist lagŋɛl Abraham ɛm, lele David ɛm ab e mew. 
Abraham ecʼalaw Isak e lʼew ab ɛm ecʼen ecʼes ↄsŋm: Jakↄb, 
Juda lele lisijimɛl ab; Perɛs lele Zera ab (Tamar el ɛl lis), Ɛsrↄm, Aram, 
Aminadab, Nasↄn, Salmↄ, 
Boas (Rahab el lis), Obɛd (Rut el lis) 
Jese, lele jim ɛb ebu David ab. Salomↄ (lis el Uri e yↄw a); 
Roboam, Abia, Asaf, 
Josafat, Joram, Osias, 
Joatam, Akas, Esekias, 
Manase, Amↄ, Josias, 
Yekonia lele lisijimɛl ab. Lɛgŋ ab ɛm anake wɛl ot Israɛl ecʼey im madu ↄkr ɛm Babilↄn wus af a. 
Ca eke wɛl ot Israɛl ecʼey im Babilↄn a, ow e jam a, ow el Salatiɛl, Sorobabɛl, 
Abiud, Eliakim, Asↄr, 
Sadↄk, Akim, Eliud, 
Eleasar, Matan, Jakↄb, 
ɛtŋ Jakↄb ew Josɛf, Mari ɛc; Mari anake ew Jesu eke wɛl bʼɛsŋ Mesi a. 
Sica kʼigŋ Abraham toŋ David a, ow ij alaw sakp lɛw yar; David toŋ ke kʼok lɛgŋ ab ɛm a eke wɛl ot Israɛl ecʼey im Babilↄn wus af a, ow ij alaw sakp lɛw yar; sica aŋa toŋ ke ok Mesi e mew e lɛgŋ a, ow ij ɛmɛny alaw sakp lɛw yar. 
Kin Jesu Krist eci mew owi ecʼelel eke ɛcr. Josɛf ɛsu Jesu lis Mari; gbɛkↄ gbuŋ ke -lʼuwarir any a, Abŋ Lala ecʼabusu ɛm, Mari anŋ nimamn. 
Ɛc Josɛf el ɛgŋ eke bʼɛlum Nyam ɛtŋ lʼerurm eke li kʼɛlu ow ↄkm dedeku fɛŋ ecʼany af; ow sosiɛm lʼɛgŋ lʼeb eke ew ew ɛm, li kʼɛlu ow es. 
Sica eke nʼam tutr low na a, Ɛs Kↄtↄkↄ ecʼafr ɛrm ɛs nyam ow mɛmn ɛm ɛŋnʼn ɛtŋ dadʼr ninɛ: «Josɛf, David eci lilaw, ɛrmij kʼókʼŋ eke kʼoc Mari ɛsɛ ŋ e yↄw af, aŋke Abŋ Lala ecʼabusu ɛm anake nʼanŋ nimamn a. 
Li bʼow lʼew iy, ŋ bʼow ɛsŋ iy a Jesu, aŋke in anake li bʼow lʼeb in ecʼagŋ a nimum lʼɛlu es ɛl e sikpl ɛm a.» 
Low na ɛy yecʼɛtŋ ke odad a eke Ɛs Kↄtↄkↄ dad kokoba ↄb ɛs ab ɛm a íy nɛny. 
Kin odad a: Dedyↄwli a eke anm igŋ ab a bʼow anŋ lokab ɛtŋ bʼow ew iy igŋ eke wɛl bʼow ɛsŋ Emanuɛl. (Nin a e nyandrɛ el: “Nyam anŋ ɛy ab.”) 
Eke Josɛf ufŋm es a, li kok ɛsɛ elel eke Ɛs Kↄtↄkↄ ecʼɛrm ɛs a dadʼr ab af, ɛtŋ lʼoc Mari ɛsɛ in e yↄw af. 
Gbɛkↄ nʼanm ow ab toŋ batŋ Mari ew iy igŋ eke Josɛf ɛw nin Jesu.
