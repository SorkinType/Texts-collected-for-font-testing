Lankɛnɛmayana.
21.
Na xanbi ra, n yi kore xɔri nɛnɛn nun bɔxɔ xɔri nɛnɛn to. Amasɔtɔ kore xɔri singen nun bɔxɔ xɔri singen bata yi tunun, fɔxɔ igen mi yi fa na sɔnɔn. 
Awa, n yi Alaa Taa Sariɲanxin to godɛ keli ariyanna yi Ala fɛma, Yerusalɛn nɛnɛna, a yitɔnxi alo ɲaxalandin naxan a yitɔnxi siga a xɛmɛn nalandeni. 
N yi fala xui gbeen mɛ manga gbɛdɛni, a naxa, “Ala dɔxɔden bata lu adamadiine tagi! A luma nɛn e fɛma, e findi a yamaan na. Ala yɛtɛɛn luma nɛn e fɛma, a findi e Ala ra. 
A yi e yɛɛgen birin fitan e yɛɛ ra yi, sayaan mi fa luma na sɔnɔn hanma sununa hanma wugana hanma xɔlɛna. Amasɔtɔ fe fonne bata ɲan.” 
Na xanbi ra, naxan yi dɔxi manga gbɛdɛni, na yi a fala, a naxa, “N bata feen birin findi a nɛnɛn na!” A mɔn yi a fala, a naxa, “Ito sɛbɛ, amasɔtɔ fe ragidixin nun ɲɔndin nan falani itoe ra.” 
A mɔn yi a fala n xa, a naxa, “N tan nan Alifa nun Omega ra. Na nan na ra, a fɔlɔn nun a raɲanna. Xa min xɔnla naxan ma, n siimaya tigi igena nde soma nɛn a yii, a sare mi na. 
Na nan findima nɔ sɔtɔ muxun kɛɛn na. N findima nɛn a Ala ra, a yi findi n ma diin na. 
Koni nafigine nun dɛnkɛlɛyatarene nun muxu xɔsixine nun faxa tiine nun yanga suxune nun kɔɛramuxune nun suxure batune nun wule falane birin, ne tan gbeen tɛɛ daraan nan na naxan dɛgɛma dole tɛɛn na. Sayaan firinden nan na ra.” 
Lenge soloferen yi suxi maleka soloferen naxanye yii, naxanye yi rafexi fitina fe dɔnxɛ soloferene ra, na nde keden yi fa n fɛma, a naxa, “Fa be, n xa ɲaxalandin yita i ra, Yɛxɛɛ Diina ɲaxanla.” 
Alaa Nii Sariɲanxin yi so n yi, malekan yi siga n na geya gbeen fari pon, a sa Taa Sariɲanxin yita n na, Yerusalɛn naxan godo keli ariyanna yi Ala fɛma. 
Alaa nɔrɔn yi a ma, a mayilenma alo yasipi gɛmɛna alo gɛmɛ fixɛna. 
Yinna yi taan nabilinxi, a mate, a gbo. So dɛ fu nun firin yi a ma. Maleka fu nun firin tixi na dɛɛne ra. Isirayila bɔnsɔn fu nun firinne xinle sɛbɛxi dɛɛne ma. 
Sogeteden binni dɛ saxan, kɔmɛnna ma dɛ saxan, yiifanna ma dɛ saxan, sogegododen binni dɛ saxan. 
Bɛtɛn sade fu nun firin nan yi taan yinna bun, Yɛxɛɛ Diina xɛra fu nun firinne xinle sɛbɛxi e ma. 
Malekan naxan yi falan tima n xa, se maliga tamin yi suxi na yii naxan yi rafalaxi xɛmaan na, alogo a xa taan maliga e nun a dɛɛne nun a yinna. 
Tongon naanin nan yi taan ma. A kuyan nun a yigboon yi lan. Malekan yi taan maliga se maliga tamin na: kilo wuli firin. A kuyan nun a yigboon nun a yiteen birin yi lan. 
Malekan yi yinna maliga adamadiine se maliga kiini, nɔngɔnna yɛ kɛmɛ tonge naanin e nun naanin. 
Na yinna yi tixi yasipi gɛmɛn nan na, taan yi tixi xɛma faɲin nan na. A fixa alo kilasina. 
Taan nabilinna yinna bɛtɛn sadene yi rayabuxi gɛmɛ faɲin sifan birin na: yasipi gɛmɛn nan yi a bɛtɛn sade singen ma, a firindena safiri gɛmɛna, a saxandena agati gɛmɛna, a naanindena emerodi gɛmɛna, 
a suulundena onixi gɛmɛna, a sennindena karinɛliya gɛmɛna, a soloferedena kirisoliti gɛmɛna, a solomasɛxɛdena berili gɛmɛna, a solomanaanindena topasi gɛmɛna, a fudena kirisopirasi gɛmɛna, a fu nun kedendena yasinti gɛmɛna, a fu nun firindena ametisi gɛmɛna. 
A so dɛ fu nun firinne birin yi rafalaxi gɛmɛ faɲine nan na. Dɛ keden kedenna birin yi rafalaxi pɛrili gɛmɛ tofaɲi keden nan na. Taan kira gbeen yi rafalaxi xɛma faɲin nan na, a fixa alo kilasina. 
Koni n mi Ala Batu Banxin se to na taani, amasɔtɔ Marigina, Ala Sɛnbɛn Birin Kanna nun Yɛxɛɛ Diin nan findixi Ala Batu Banxin na. 
Taan mako mi yi sogen nun kike dɛgɛn ma, amasɔtɔ Alaa nɔrɔn yi a yiyalanma. Yɛxɛɛ Diin nan a lɛnpun na. 
Dunuɲa siyane sigan tima a dɛgɛn nan ma, dunuɲa mangane fama nɛn e binyen na a xɔn. 
Taan dɛɛne mi ragalin mumɛ, amasɔtɔ kɔɛɛn mi soma na yi mumɛ! 
Siyane birin fama nɛn e binyene nun e xunnayerenne ra a xɔn. 
Koni se sariɲantare yo mi soɛ na taani, hanma fe xɔsixi rabana hanma wule falana, koni fɔ naxanye xili sɛbɛxi Yɛxɛɛ Diina Nii Rakisin Kitabun kui.
