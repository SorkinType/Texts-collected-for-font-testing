Lankɛnɛmayana.
4.
Na xanbi ra, n yi fe gbɛtɛ to, n yi dɛna nde to rabixi ariyanna yi. N fala xui singen naxan mɛ, naxan yi gbo alo xɔta xuina, na yi a fala, a naxa, “Te be, feen naxanye fama ligadeni to xanbi ra, n xa ne yita i ra.” 
Alaa Nii Sariɲanxin yi so n yi mafurɛn! N yi muxuna nde to dɔxi manga gbɛdɛni ariyanna yi. 
A yɛtagin yi mayilenma alo yasipi gɛmɛn nun karinɛliya gɛmɛna. Manga gbɛdɛn yi rabilinxi sengunna ra naxan yi mayilenma alo emerodi gɛmɛna. 
Manga gbɛdɛ mɔxɔɲɛn nun naanin gbɛtɛye yi manga gbɛdɛn nabilinni, fori muxu mɔxɔɲɛn nun naanin yi dɔxi naxanye ma e maxidixi dugi fixɛne yi, xɛma mangaya kɔmɔti taxamasenne soxi e xun na. 
Kuyen ɲin masɔxɔnna nun xuine nun galan xuiin yi minima manga gbɛdɛni. Lɛnpu dɛgɛ gbeen solofere yi dɛgɛma mangaya gbɛdɛn yɛtagi. Alaa Nii soloferene nan yi ne ra. 
Lonna nde yi mangaya gbɛdɛn yɛtagi naxan gbo alo fɔxɔ igena, a fixa alo mato kikena alo kilasina. Dalise naanin yi mangaya gbɛdɛn yireni, e nun a rabilinni, naxanye yɛɛye yi e yɛtagi nun e xun xanbin ma. 
Dalise singen yi maliga yatan nan na. A firinden tan yi luxi alo turana. Muxun yɛtagin yi a saxanden ma. A naaninden yi luxi alo singbinna tuganmatɔna. 
Gubugubu sennin nan yi na daliseene keden kedenna birin ma, e yɛɛne yi e fatin yiren birin ma, e yi bɛtin bama kɔɛɛn nun yanyin birin na, e naxa, “Marigina Ala Sɛnbɛn Birin Kanna sariɲan, a sariɲan, a sariɲan, naxan yi na, naxan na, e nun naxan fama.” 
Naxan dɔxi mangaya gbɛdɛni naxan luma habadan, dalise naaninne nɛma yi binyen nun xunna kenla nun barikan birɛ na xa waxatin naxan yi, 
na fori muxu mɔxɔɲɛn nun naaninne yi e xinbi sinma nɛn a yɛtagi, e yi a batu, e yi e manga kɔmɔtine woli mangaya gbɛdɛn yɛtagi, e yi a fala na kanna xa naxan dɔxi manga gbɛdɛn ma naxan luma habadan, e naxa, 
“Nxu Marigina, nxɔ Ala, binyen nun xunna kenla nun sɛnbɛn daxa a fi i ma, amasɔtɔ i tan nan seen birin daxi, e taranxi na i sagoon nin, e daxi i sagoon nin.”
