Lankɛnɛmayana.
10.
N yi maleka sɛnbɛma gbɛtɛ to godɛ keli ariyanna yi, kundaan filinxi a ma alo dugina, sengunna rabilinxi a xunna ma. A yɛtagin mayilenma alo sogena, a sanne yi tɛɛn yiyani alo tɛɛ sɛnbɛtɛnne. 
Kɛdi mafilinxin xuridin nabixin yi suxi a yii. A yi a yiifari ma sanna ti fɔxɔ igeni, a kɔmɛnna xaren na. 
A sɔnxɔ xuini te alo yatan wurundun xuina. A to a xuini te, kuye sarin soloferene yi e xui ramini. 
E yi naxan falama, n yi nɔɛ na sɛbɛ nɛn nun, koni n yi fala xuina nde mɛ keli kore, a naxa, “Kuye sarin soloferene naxan falama, na ramara wundoni, i nama a sɛbɛ!” 
Na xanbi ra, n malekan naxan to tixi fɔxɔ igen nun xaren ma, na yi a yiifanni te kore xɔnna ma. 
A yi a kɔlɔ habadan Ala xinli, naxan kore xɔnna nun bɔxɔ xɔnna nun fɔxɔ igen daxi e nun e yi seene birin. Malekan yi a fala, a naxa, “A mi fa buma sɔnɔn! 
Koni maleka solofereden na a xɔtaan fe waxatin naxan yi, Alaa wundo fe ragidixin kamalima nɛn nayi alo a rali a nabine ma kii naxan yi, a walikɛne.” 
Nba, n fala xuiin naxan mɛ a singeni keli kore, na mɔn yi falan ti n xa, a naxa, “Siga, i sa kɛdi rabixin tongo na malekan yii naxan tixi fɔxɔ igen nun xaren ma.” 
N yi n maso malekan na, n yi a fala a xa, a xa kɛdi mafilinxi xuridin so n yii. A yi a fala n xa, a naxa, “A tongo, i yi a ragerun, a ɲaxunma ayi nɛn i dɛ alo kumina, koni a i kuiin nafema nɛn xɔlɛn na.” 
N yi kɛdi mafilinxi xuridin tongo malekan yii, n to a raso n dɛɛ, a yi ɲaxun n dɛɛ alo kumina, koni n to a ragodo n kuini, n kuiin yi rafe xɔlɛn na. 
Na xanbi ra, e yi a fala n xa, a n mɔn xa nabiya falane ti lan yamane nun siyane nun xuine nun mangane ma.
