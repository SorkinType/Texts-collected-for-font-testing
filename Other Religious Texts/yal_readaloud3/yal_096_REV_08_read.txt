Lankɛnɛmayana.
8.
Yɛxɛɛ Diin to taxamasenna solofereden nabi, kore xɔnna yi madundu han waxatidi. 
N yi maleka solofere to tixi Ala yɛtagi, xɔta solofere yi so ne yii. 
Maleka gbɛtɛ yi fa saraxa ganden yɛtagi, wusulan gan se xɛma daxin suxi a yii. E wusulan gbegbe so a yii a gan daxin na, sa yama sariɲanxine birin ma maxandi xuine fari, a yi ne gan saraxa ganden xɛma daxin fari manga gbɛdɛn yɛtagi. 
Wusulanna tutin nun yama sariɲanxina Ala maxandi xuine yi te, keli malekan yii siga Ala yɛtagi. 
Na xanbi ra, malekan yi wusulan gan seen tongo, a yi a rafe saraxa ganden tɛɛn na, a yi a woli bɔxɔni. Galan xuiin nun xuine nun kuyen ɲin masɔxɔnna yi mini, bɔxɔn yi xuruxurun. 
Nayi, xɔta soloferene yi maleka soloferen naxanye yii, ne yi keli xɔtane fedeni. 
Maleka singen yi a gbee xɔtaan fe. Balabalan kɛsɛn nun tɛɛn nun wunla basanxin yi fa bɔxɔ xɔnna fari. Bɔxɔni taxunxin dɔxɔde saxanna ra, na kedenna yi gan, e nun a wudine nun a sɛxɛ xindene birin. 
Malekan firinden yi a xɔtaan fe. Gɛmɛ gbeena nde yi sin fɔxɔ igeni naxan yi gbo alo geyaan ganmatɔna. Fɔxɔ igene yitaxunxin dɔxɔde saxanna ra, na kedenna yi findi wunla ra, 
daliseen naxanye birin yi ɲɛɲɛ na fɔxɔ igeni, ne yi faxa, mɛnna kunkine fan yi kala. 
Malekan saxanden yi a gbee xɔtaan fe. Sare gbeen ganmatɔɔn yi keli kore, a ganma alo xaye raxidi dɛgɛna. Baane nun tigine birin yitaxunxin dɔxɔde saxanna ra, saren yi bira na keden yi. 
Na saren yi xili nɛn, “Se Xɔlɛna.” Na igene yi xɔlɔ ayi, muxu wuyaxi yi faxa na ige xɔlɛn mindeni. 
Malekan naaninden yi a gbee xɔtaan fe. Sogen nun kiken nun sarene yitaxunxin dɔxɔde saxanna ra, na kedenna yi yidimi, alogo na yatɛn ɲɔxɔn xa ba e dɛgɛn na. Na kɛnɛnna ɲɔxɔn yi ba yanyin nun kɔɛɛn kɛnɛnna ra. 
Nba, n yi fe gbɛtɛ to, n singbinna nde fala ti xuiin mɛ, a yi foyeni kore, a naxa, “Gbalona, gbalona, gbalon bɔxɔ xɔnna muxune xa, maleka saxanna bonne na e xɔtane fe waxatin naxan yi.”
