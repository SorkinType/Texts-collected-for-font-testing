Jan e lɛl nyahan ɛm ekʼawŋ.
1.
Ɛm Nyamel ecʼƐgŋ Likpekp, ɛm lawl Gayus eke mʼerur nawrɛ ɛm, mʼam awŋ lɛl. 
Erur ɛm e lawl mamn, mʼam erur eke ów fɛŋ kʼakpalʼŋ, ke sos írmʼŋ es, ɛsɛ elel eke abŋ ɛm yɛji ↄny lɛc ab af. 
Sos ɛm am iŋnʼm nↄnↄ, lɛgŋ a ekʼagŋ lisijimɛl ow, ke ij daŋku eke ŋ bi kok cɛcɛ Nyam e low fɛŋ ɛm a. 
Eke mʼiri eke ɛm ecʼey am an es low nawrɛ ɛm a, sos ɛm am iŋnʼm ligbɛl akm low ɛjeci fɛŋ. 
Erur ɛm e lawl mamn, bi kok ów amamn ↄmn ɛm ↄŋ agŋ lisijimɛl ekʼel etu a yɛji. 
-Nʼam ij ŋ ecʼɛrm mamn e daŋku Nyamel ecʼany af. Mʼam ŋↄŋnʼŋ, kòk ɛl ecʼejagb us e low ɛsɛ elel eke Nyam erur ab af, 
aŋke Jesu Krist e nin a sosiɛm anake -nʼimn, ke -lʼebm ob kaka Nyam e low any uwm ɛsɛl ecʼabu ɛm a. 
Sʼↄtu eke si kʼɛlum agŋ amua abu, yecʼɛtŋ ke ɛl ɛrŋ ɛm sʼókr af sʼel low nawrɛ e juma kok ɛsɛl. 
Mi nↄn ów bɛb mʼↄŋ Nyamel; kↄ Diotrɛf eke bʼerur e kʼel ɛl ɛm e krɛkrɛ a ebm ɛy ecʼodad a. 
Na sosiɛm, eke mʼam ow mʼɛkn ↄny a, mʼow mi dad ↄny ów eke nʼam kok a. Nʼam dad ów eŋuŋ lʼok ɛy af. Na yɛji ow ɛ́yrmʼn ɛrm es, lʼébm agŋ lisijimɛl, ɛtŋ agŋ eke bʼerur eke kʼeb ɛl a yɛji, li bʼɛdŋn wɛl, ɛtŋ li bʼogŋ wɛl Nyamel ɛm. 
Erur ɛm e lawl mamn Gayus, kì wewrm ów eŋuŋ kok ɛsɛl ów, kↄ kòk ów amamn. Ɛgŋ eke bi kok low mamn a, el Nyam eci; ɛgŋ eke bi kok low ŋuŋ a, lʼɛ́knm Nyam. 
Demetrius ecʼa gbɛ, agŋ a fɛŋ am ij in e daŋku, in ecʼes an mamn a sosiɛm. Ɛy obi yɛji sʼam ij in e daŋku. Ɛtŋ ŋ obi yɛji uw any eke ɛy e daŋku ij a el nawrɛ. 
Mʼↄny ów nↄnↄ eke mi ki nↄn mʼawŋʼŋ, kↄ mʼerurm eke mi kʼoc lɛl mij lele lɛl likŋ ab mi nↄn. 
Mʼam tutr eke mi kʼow mʼɛknʼŋ lɛgŋ tɛl ɛm, ke si dád ów sʼokarir. 
Ke ɛrm es ɛy ánŋ ŋ ab! Awlŋɛl a fɛŋ am ibrmʼŋ. Sʼam ɛkn ɛy lawlɛl a fɛŋ ecʼɛgŋ nyam nyam.
