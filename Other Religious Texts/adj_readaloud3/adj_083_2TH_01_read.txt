Pↄl e lɛl yony ɛm ekʼawŋ Tesaloni Eyŋ.
1.
Pↄl, Silas lele Timote ab e lɛl ekʼam awŋ Nyamel ecʼagŋ ekʼanŋ Tesaloni baŋn, ke el Nyam ɛy Ɛs lele Ɛs Kↄtↄkↄ Jesu Krist ab ecʼagŋ: 
Ke Nyam Ɛs lele Ɛs Kↄtↄkↄ Jesu Krist ab ɛ́dŋ ↄny ↄwrↄ, ke ↄ́ŋ ↄny ɛrm es ɛy. 
Agŋ lisijimɛl, ow akpl eke sɛgŋ fɛŋ, si kʼɛlu Nyam bia ↄny sosiɛm. Ow it eke si ki kok ɛbɛn, aŋke ↄny ecʼↄmn ekʼↄnyn a am bobn ɛtŋ ↄny ecʼerur eke ↄnyn ↄny ↄfr ɛm a, am agb imn anym eci sɛgŋ fɛŋ. 
Na sosiɛm, si bi dad ↄny e lís e low, sʼaw ↄny Sinyamel ecʼagŋ a fɛŋ eke bʼas luku igŋ Nyam nuŋ a ecʼany af, aŋke ↄny bʼinymn es kikri ↄmn ɛm, gbre ijr lele sos es ↄw fɛŋ eke bʼↄbar ab ab ɛm. 
Gbre ijr amua am yɛgm eke Nyam e jɛj ok a el nyim nyim. Tasi ɛm a, gbre ijr amua sosiɛm anake bʼow eel Nyam e gbreŋgbi usu a ekʼam ijr ow e gbre a ecʼagŋ tasi a. 
Nyam el nɛnyɛmbri nyam Ɛs: ow sosiɛm agŋ ekʼam ijr ↄny gbre a, ɛl yɛji li bʼow lʼijr wɛl gbre. 
Ɛtŋ ↄny ekʼam ijr gbre a, li bʼow lʼɛyr ↄny ɛrm es, ↄny lele ɛy ab, lɛgŋ eke Ɛs Kↄtↄkↄ Jesu bʼow anŋ afr ow in lele ɛrm ɛsɛl abusu ɛsɛl ab ab a. 
Lɛgŋ a, li bʼow nʼanŋ al ekʼam iri ecʼaraŋn lʼɛy ↄkm, yecʼɛtŋ ke li kók agŋ eke uwm Nyam any, ke ɛ́luam ɛy Ɛs Kↄtↄkↄ Jesu ecʼAmani Mamn a low ŋuŋ. 
-Li bʼow -nʼɛŋn gbre eke kʼow ↄnym uwr, ɛtŋ gbre ij akpasu na, bʼow ↄny kpab ɛl es Ɛs Kↄtↄkↄ e lís lele in ecʼanygbɛl e lɛc ab ab e lís. 
Low a bʼow ɛy lɛgŋ a ekʼƐs Kↄtↄkↄ bʼow ow a. Li bʼow lʼow yecʼɛtŋ kʼagŋ a ekʼel in eci ke ubʼr nawrɛ a, úsrʼr sos, kↄ́klmʼn. Ɔny yɛji bʼow anŋn ɛl ɛm, aŋke ubr amani a eke sʼↄŋ ↄny a nawrɛ. 
Na sosiɛm, sɛgŋ fɛŋ sʼam ŋↄŋn Nyam sʼↄŋ ↄny yecʼɛtŋ ke ɛy e Nyam a ɛ́kn ↄny ɛsɛ agŋ eke it af, in ecʼɛsŋ a eke lʼɛsŋ ↄny ab ɛm. Ke in ecʼabusu ab ɛm, lʼɛ́lum ↄny abu kokr low nimamn fɛŋ ekʼam erurir a, ke nʼↄ́ŋ ↄny ecʼↄmn a ɛw gbagba! 
Na ɛm a wɛl bʼow dad ɛy Ɛs Kↄtↄkↄ Jesu e nin mamn, ɛtŋ ↄny yɛji -li bʼow -lʼaw ↄny. Ow bʼow el ɛy e Nyam lele Ɛs Kↄtↄkↄ Jesu Krist ab ecʼↄwrↄ ekʼɛŋan.
