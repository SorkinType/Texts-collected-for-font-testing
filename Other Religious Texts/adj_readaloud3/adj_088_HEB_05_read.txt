EBRE.
5.
Egb waw ɛs ligbɛl fɛŋ, agŋ ecʼaraŋn wɛl bi sↄsumʼn ocʼr. In e juma el ke lʼɛ́lum agŋ abu ir ɛb Nyam ab, li kok ob ap lele egb waw ab agŋ e sikpl a ecʼoc ap eci. 
Egb waw ɛs ligbɛl a, in obi yɛji lʼel ɛgŋ ekʼↄny sikpl. Ow sosiɛm, lʼↄtu eke li kʼiri agŋ a ekʼuwm low any lel ɛl a ekʼam irm ab ab e ses babur a. 
In e sikpl e nʼↄny a sosiɛm, ow it eke li ki waw egb dedeku a e sikpl a eci, ɛtŋ ow elm dedeku a e sikpl a cɛ eci, kↄ in obi in obi ecʼa yɛjʼeci. 
Ɛtŋ ɛmɛny, ɛgŋ kaka áwm egb waw ɛs ligbɛl e juma a ebm in obi ɛm. Kↄ Nyam anake bʼɛsŋ ɛgŋ ow ecʼel ɛm, ɛsɛ elel eke lʼɛsŋ Arↄn el ab af a. 
Krist ecʼa yɛji, ɛbɛn ow ɛcr. Ow elm in obi lʼawm egb waw ɛs ligbɛl ecʼel a lʼebm. Nyam eke dadʼr odad a ogŋ ow anŋ ow. Kin odad a: Ŋ el ɛm ecʼIy, yɛfɛnyna, ɛm mʼewʼŋ. 
Ɛmɛny Nyam dad ninɛ: Ŋ el egb waw ɛs sɛgŋ fɛŋ eci, ɛsɛ elel eke ow ↄb nuŋ Mɛlkisedɛk ɛm ab af. 
Krist, in e wus af aŋa ecʼɛb ir ab ɛm, ŋↄŋn Nyam ɛtŋ kok sↄrŋ ↄŋ Nyam, babarm ɛm lele ↄŋn ikŋ ɛm ab. Ni ŋↄŋn Nyam ekʼↄtu eke kʼↄny ocʼr es luw ɛm a. Ɛtŋ Nyam iriʼr lebl, aŋke lʼɛlum ow es is ɛm. 
Krist el Nyam ecʼIy, na low nawrɛ! Gbɛkↄ gbre ijr amua fɛŋ ɛm eke lʼɛcr a, li wewr ɛlum nawrɛ tasi. 
Ɛtŋ sica eke lʼiy nɛny lʼigŋ nuŋ a, lʼel sel ɛŋn e nuŋ agŋ fɛŋ eke bʼɛlumʼn a eci. 
Ɛtŋ ɛbɛn a, Nyam ɛwʼr egb waw ɛs ligbɛl ɛsɛ Mɛlkisedɛk ecʼab af. 
Low na af a, sʼↄny ów nↄnↄ eke si ki dad, kↄ ɛl e dad a ↄtu nↄnↄ, aŋke ↄny e low iri a ɛwl am ↄtu. 
Ɔny, ↄtur eke wanci na af a kʼeel low yɛgm ɛsɛl tasi gↄŋ! Kↄ kin eke ow am ibrm eke wɛl ki yɛgmn ↄny nuŋ ↄb ɛm e Nyam odad ecʼów ab ɛm. Ɔny, ↄnymn tɛri eke kʼijr ob ij leg leg, mɛny mij ow it eke wɛl kʼↄŋ ↄny. 
Ɛgŋ a eke bʼanŋ am ɛgŋ mɛny mij a, el iyli ↄmn ɛm. Li kʼↄtum lʼuwm yecʼa ekʼel low nyim nyim ab any. 
Gbɛkↄ ob ij leg leg el agŋ ɛgbɛl ↄmn ɛm eci. Ɛl e low es ɛkn ɛw a, ↄŋ ɛl e nuŋ ɛm ↄny a, bʼuw any ok ereb low mamn lele low ŋuŋ ab ecʼaraŋn.
