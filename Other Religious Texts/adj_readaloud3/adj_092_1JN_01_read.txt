Jan e lɛl krɛkrɛ eke awŋ.
1.
Ɛgŋ a ekʼin sosiɛm sʼam nↄn lɛl na a, lɛgŋ eke wɛl ki kokm wus na yɛjʼa, ke in nʼanŋ ŋ̂. Low yecʼeke sʼuw any in e lís a el: ɛy sʼiri in ecʼodad, sʼɛknʼn ɛy ecʼanyamn ɛm, sʼɛpr mɛny es gbiy sʼɛknʼn, ɛtŋ sʼitŋn in abu. In lʼel Odad eke bʼↄŋ agŋ sel. 
Odad a ekʼel sel a ɛy ↄkm, sʼɛknʼn ɛtŋ sʼij in e daŋku, ɛtŋ sʼam dad ↄny sel ɛŋn ekʼanŋ sɛgŋ fɛŋ a ecʼodad. Godʼɛtŋ nʼanŋ Nyam ab, ɛtŋ sica lʼɛy ↄkm sʼɛknʼn. 
Yecʼeke sʼiri ke sʼɛkn a, sʼam dad sʼok ↄny, yecʼɛtŋ ke ↄny yɛji ókar af ɛy ab, ke ↄny lel ɛy ab sʼókr af si lool sʼel nyam, Ɛs lele Jim Jesu Krist ab ɛm. 
Sʼam nↄn ów amua sʼↄŋ ↄny, yecʼɛtŋ ke ɛy e sos ɛm iŋn a él sos ɛm iŋn tasi. 
Amani e sʼiri in ogŋ ke sʼam dad ↄny a el: Nyam el usuayl, ncebn kaka anm in ɛm. 
Eke si bi dad sʼɛsɛ sʼokr af in ab, ɛtŋ si bʼan es ncebn ɛm a, ke sʼel ayadŋu ɛtŋ low nɛnyɛmbri nyam anm ɛy ɛm. 
Kↄ eke si bʼan es usuayl ɛm, ɛsɛ elel eke Nyam in obi el usuayl ab af a, ke ɛy fɛŋ sʼokr af ŋ̂, ɛtŋ Jim Jesu Krist e mebl a am yↄyↄg ɛy e sikpl fɛŋ es. 
Eke si bi dad sʼɛsɛ: sʼam kokm sikpl, ke ɛy ↄfr sʼam ɛjurir, ɛtŋ low nɛnyɛmbri nyam anm ɛy ɛm. 
Eke si bʼɛgŋ ɛy e sikpl sʼeb Nyam ecʼany af a, li bʼow lʼoc lʼap ɛy ke li yↄ́yↄg ɛy eci ŋgɛsi fɛŋ es, aŋke lʼel cɛcɛ, lʼel nɛny nyam ɛs. 
Eke si bi dad sʼɛsɛ: sʼam kokm sikpl, ke sʼɛw Nyam yadŋu, ɛtŋ in ecʼodad ebl a anm ɛy ɛm.
