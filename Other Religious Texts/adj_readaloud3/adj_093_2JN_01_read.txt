Jan e lɛl yony ɛm ekʼawŋ.
1.
Ɛm Nyamel ecʼƐgŋ Likpekp, mʼam awŋ Yↄw gbɛl lele in ecʼey ab eke Nyam sↄsum ot, ke mʼerur a lɛl. Ɛtŋ ow elm ɛm sↄny mʼerur ↄny, kↄ ɛl a fɛŋ ekʼuw low nawrɛ ab any a yɛji, 
low nawrɛ ekʼanŋ ɛy ɛm, ke bʼow anŋ ɛy ɛm godo godo fɛŋ a sosiɛm. 
Ke Nyam Ɛs, lele Jesu Krist Ɛs Iyŋ ecʼↄwrↄ, sↄrŋ ikŋ lele ɛrm es ɛy ab, ánŋ ɛy ab nawrɛ ɛm lele erur ɛm ab. 
Sos ɛm am iŋnʼm nↄnↄ eke mʼɛkn ŋ ecʼey bɛb am an es low nawrɛ ɛm ɛsɛ ol eke sʼeb Ɛs ogŋ ab af. 
Sica Yↄw gbɛl eke Nyam sↄsum oc, low eke mʼam ibrmʼŋ a el: sʼérurir ɛy ↄfr. Ke na ow elm ol owr mʼɛnym mʼↄↄmʼŋ, kↄ ow eke sʼeb kpↄkpↄ ɛm ɛtŋ ab ana. 
Ɛtŋ erur a el: ke sʼán es Nyam e sol ab af. Ɛtŋ ol a el: ke kókr erur e low ɛsɛ elel eke irir kpↄkpↄ ɛm ɛtŋ ab af. 
Ayadŋu nↄnↄ ow wus na af ekʼɛ́gŋm Jesu Krist ekʼow sos megl ɛm a e low ebm. Ɛgŋ eke bi kok ɛsɛ ɛl af a, lʼel yadŋu, lʼel Krist ecʼifnu. 
Ɔ̀sur sos gↄŋ yecʼɛtŋ ke kʼírmnmn ↄny e juma eke kokr a eci labm sↄg, kↄ ke ɛ́ŋan ow ecʼob ap ligbɛl a. 
Ɛgŋ fɛŋ eke bi digŋ Krist eci low yɛgm a ɛlu es a, okrm af Nyam ab; kↄ ɛgŋ eke bʼucu low yɛgm na ↄny es a, lʼokr af Ɛs lele Jim ab. 
Ekʼɛgŋ fɛŋ am ow ↄny ogŋ ɛtŋ lʼisam low yɛgm na ab a, kʼèbr ir kʼɛlul il akŋ, kʼↄ̀ŋan in akpa bↄbↄ yɛji; 
ɛgŋ eke bʼↄŋʼn akpa a, ke ow e juma eŋuŋ a anake in yɛji nʼam kok a. 
Mʼↄny ów nↄnↄ eke mi ki nↄn mʼawŋ ↄny, kↄ mʼerurm eke mi kʼoc lɛl lele lɛl mij ab; gbɛkↄ mʼam tutr eke mi kʼow mʼɛkn ↄny, ke si dád sʼokarir yecʼɛtŋ ke ɛy e sos ɛm iŋn a él tasi. 
Esŋ jↄw eke Nyam sↄsum oc a ecʼey a am ibrmʼŋ.
