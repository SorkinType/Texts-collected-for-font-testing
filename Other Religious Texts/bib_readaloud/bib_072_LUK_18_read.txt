Likki.
18.
Yeezuu hɔbɔkʊrɛnoom da ŋ nɩ k'a hɩnka a ʊ, a a ga m kʊ ŋ nɩ yaa da dɔmɩm haay, bɩ ŋ bɩ kɔ kan yaadarɛ bɩ kɩ y. 
A a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «Dʊdɔkər ɩ nyɩnta tara deem nɔ ʊ, a bɩr nyi bam Woso nɩ y, a bɩr gʊɔɔ daŋ ʊ sɔ y. 
Zoro nɩ tara bɩ do nɔ ʊ, an nɩ zɛm tee… an n'a hɩrɛ, a ʊ: Gumə ka m ʊ mim kʊ mɔɔ jɩm bɩ n ta m kan mɔɔ kɩ bɩ nɔ ʊ. 
Dʊdɔkər bɩ n a gu, gallɛm hɔka. Bɩ kur bɩ, a n n'a hɩrɛ a mɩŋŋa m, a ʊ: Baa kʊ mɔɔ bɩr nyi bam Woso m, bɩ mɔɔ bɩr gʊɔɔ daŋ ʊ sɔ, 
lʊ naa do k'a bʊr an nɩ mɔɔ kɔm bɩ, mɔɔ y'a dʊdɔ bɩ karɛ, m gumə ka ʊ, k'a bɩ bʊr mɔɔ zi dɔ dɔmɩm haay, an bɩr mɔɔ kɔrɛ y.» 
Zuuba n a ya, an a hɩ, a ʊ: «K'a tʊr ka, hɔ kʊ dʊdɔkər bʊnyaa b'a hɩ bɩ ma mɩŋŋa. 
Bɩ, Woso b'a darɛ a gʊɔɔ k'a ŋ nyʊkɔ ʊ ŋn'ɩ zee dam zi sinuu ʊ kan a gunuu ʊ kɩ rɔ wɔɔ han ra? A maasɩrɛ bam, an bɔkarɛ an a da ŋ han ra? 
A hɩ mɔɔ nawʊrɛ awɔɔ m, a y'a darɛ ŋ han zɔɔ-zɔɔ. Bɩ kʊ Gʊaanyɩ bɩ bɩ bʊr, a zɛm bɩ sɩrakarɛ yɩ tara burə ʊ ra?» 
Yeezuu hɔbɔkʊrɛnoom da gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ n'a hɔɔn darɛ ŋ ʊ, gʊɔɔ mɩŋŋɔɔ n'a mɩ, ŋnɩ bɔkarɛ, ŋn'a muno bɔŋ ʊ rɔ wɔɔ m, a ʊ: 
«Gʊɔɔ hɩɩyɔɔ ɩ gasʊ Wosocɛ ʊ k'a yaa da. Gʊaa deem bɩ farizɛn m, deem bɩ yawʊrsirezaa m. 
Farizɛn bɩ n jɩm brama an nɩ yaa darɛ a heer ʊ ncɩnaaʊ: Woso, mɔɔ ɩ yaa darɛ ɩbɩɩ m barka kʊ mɔɔ n bɔ kʊ ʊ kan gʊɔɔ gɔsɩnnɔ kʊ ŋ nɩ konno, mimbʊnyaabənno, jaandənno, gɛɛ yawʊrsir naa do kɩ minto. 
Mɔɔ yɩ m lɛ kʊsɩm gallɛm hɩɩya dɔmɩm saanhɩna nɔ ʊ, mɔɔ hɔnyɩ haay nɔ ʊ mɔɔ y'a bu hɔ karɛ ɩbɩɩ ʊ. 
Yawʊrsir bɩ n jɩm laatʊ, a lɛ baa k'a a mɩm busu brama y, an n'a cɩ zɛrɛ k'a a heerzar hɩnka, a ʊ: Woso, mɔɔ cicir zɛ, bala, mimbʊnyaabər m mɔɔ m. 
A hɩ mɔɔ nawʊrɛ awɔɔ m, a tɩrgabaa yɩ Woso mɩm ʊ, an a bɔ an doo, deem bɩ n a yɩ y. Bala, gʊaa k'a n'a busum bɩ, n y'a zerm tara ʊ, bɩ gʊaa k'a a mɩŋŋa zer bɩ n y'a busum.» 
Gʊɔɔ bʊr nyɩnɔ poorero m Yeezuu zi k'an a wɔ ka ŋ mim la. Karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ kʊ ŋ naa yɩ bɩ, ŋ nyaa ba kan ŋ kɩ. 
Yeezuu n a ka ŋnɩ bʊr nyɩnɔ wɔɔ m, an n'a hɩrɛ, a ʊ: «K'a nyɩnɔ wɔɔ to ŋnɩ bʊr mɔɔ zi, a b'a ta ŋ nɩ toore y, bala, gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ bɔ kʊ ʊ kan ŋ kɩ rɔ wɔɔ y'a so Woso cirbəə b'ʊ. 
Sɩra mɔɔ n'a hɩrɛ awɔɔ m, gʊaa k'a n Woso cirbəə bɩ si am nyɩ poore hɔ bɩ m bɩ, a bɩ gasʊŋ ʊ y.» 
Cir deem Yeezuu lar, a ʊ: «Karɩnsaamba mɩŋŋa, bɔ mɔɔ n'a barɛ kʊ m mɩsɩrbaa k'a nyarɛ ba ʊ bɩ yɩ?» 
Yeezuu n a hɩ m, a ʊ: «Bɔ m, ɩbɩɩ nɩ mɔɔ birm mɩŋŋa? Gʊaa mɩŋŋa ba ʊ kʊ Woso y'a deem bɛɛ y. 
Ɩbɩɩ lɛrɔ wɔɔ dɔ ra? Ɩ bɩ jaan da y, ɩ bɩ gər zɛ y, ɩ bɩ koor ba y, ɩ bɩ ŋʊaar kasɛtɩ ka y, ɩ zɩ kan ɩ da kɩ da ʊ.» 
Gʊaa bɩ n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «Mɔɔ naa haay ba m nyɩbaa ma tee….» 
Yeezuu k'a naa ma bɩ, a a hɩ m, a ʊ: «Hɔ deem ɩ gɔɔta ɩbɩɩ ma k'ɩ ba: Hɔ k'a n'ɩbɩɩ ʊ bɩ hɛɛ haay ɩ jinjir ŋʊazannɔ m, k'ɩ bʊr ɩ zu mɔɔ ʊ, ɩbɩɩ arzaka yɩm arzana ʊ.» 
Kʊ gʊaa bɩ naa ma bɩ, a heer a zar, bala, a arzaka bɩ gʊta. 
Yeezuu k'a a heerzarlɛ bɩ yɩ bɩ, a a hɩ, a ʊ: «Arzakazannɔ Woso cirbəə gasʊʊrɛ yɩm kɔɔkɔɔ. 
Sɩra m, yoonde huuka yaa gasʊʊrɛ nyɩntam naanaa, an lɛ da arzakazaa Woso cirbəə gasʊʊrɛ m.» 
Gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ n'a tʊr kam ma rɔ wɔɔ a hɩ, ŋ ʊ: «Ncɩnaaʊ bɩ, nka m nɩ bʊmbɔrɛ yɩrɛ?» 
Yeezuu n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «Hɔ kʊ gʊaa b'a dam ma an a ba bɩ, a naanaa Woso ma.» 
Pɩyɛɛr n a hɩ, a ʊ: «Dɩga, wɔɔ hɔ haay to, ʊ zu ɩbɩɩ ʊ.» 
A n a nyɩ bɔ ŋ nʊ, a ʊ: «Sɩra mɔɔ n'a hɩrɛ awɔɔ m, gʊaa k'a a har to, kan a lʊ kɩ, a danyɩnɔ kɩ, a zɩrɔ kɩ, a nyɩnɔ kɩ Woso cirbəə minto, 
a naa haay yɩm gʊta an lɛ da durnya naa do nɔ ʊ, b'a mɩsɩrbaa k'a nyarɛ ba ʊ bɩ yɩm durnya k'a nɩ zɛm bɩ nɔ ʊ sɔ.» 
Yeezuu bueerehɩɩyɔɔ wɔɔ bir, an a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «Ba wɔɔ nɩ tam Zerizalɛm ʊ na, bɩ, hɔ haay kʊ wosolɛsinnɩsorazannɔ wɔɔ a gʊrsɩra ba Gʊaanyɩ bɩ minto bɩ kʊ yɩm. 
N y'a nyasʊm, n n'a ka kɩfɩɩrɔ ʊ kʊ ŋ nɩ yaa wʊ ma, kʊ ŋ n'a sʊnsɔ wʊ, kʊ ŋ nɩ lihi dɩnda ma, 
kʊ ŋ n'a zɛ madɩɩrɔ m, kʊ ŋ nɩ bɔkarɛ ŋn'a zɛ gər, b'a y'a mɩm bɔŋ ʊ dɔmɩm kaakʊ hinni ʊ.» 
Bɩ, karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ n hɔsɩ jɩ ma y. Mim naa do a nyaakʊm da ŋ nɩ, ŋ n hɔ kʊ Yeezuu a hɩ bɩ jɩ dɔ y. 
K'a ta, an n'a zɔm Zeriko ma bɩ, bʊʊ deem ɩ nyɩnta zaa lezi b'ʊ, an nɩ hɔ yɩnyɛm. 
Zamaa k'a bʊr an nɩ cenim an a ma bɩ, a laaka da kʊ bɔ m gɛ. 
N n a hɩ m, n ʊ: «Nazarɛtɩ Yeezuu nɩ cenim.» 
A n zee da, a ʊ: «*Davɩd Nyɩ, Yeezuu, m cicir zɛ!» 
Gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ nyɩnta lɛɛ rɔ wɔɔ nɩ nyaa bam kan kɩ ŋ ʊ, an zɛ pi. A n a banka an zee da an lɛ da biisi ra, a ʊ: «Davɩd Nyɩ, mɔɔ cicir zɛ!» 
Yeezuu n jɩm, an lɛ ka kʊ ŋ n'a gaa, ŋn'ɩ bʊr m. K'a a zɔ bɩ, Yeezuu a lar, a ʊ: 
«Bɔ ɩbɩɩ ʊ kʊ mɔɔ n a ba ɩbɩɩ minto?» A n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «Zuuba, a ka kʊ m mɩm n a ya an lɛɛ yɩ.» 
Yeezuu n a hɩ m, a ʊ: «K'ɩ mɩm n a ya an lɛɛ yɩ! Ɩ sɩrakarɛ ɩ bʊmbɔ.» 
Lɛɛm bɩ, a mɩm a gʊ, an nɩ lɛɛ yɩm, an zu Yeezuu jɛ ʊ, an n'a lɛ bɔm Woso ʊ. Zamaa bɩ kʊ ŋ naa yɩ bɩ, ŋn'a lɛ bɔm Woso ʊ sɔ.
