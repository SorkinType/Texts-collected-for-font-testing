Matiyee.
3.
Dɔmɩnnɔ wɔɔ duro ʊ bɩ, Zaan-Batiisi bʊr Zidee poo nɔ ʊ, an nɩ Woso lemim pa dam. 
A n n'a hɩrɛ, a ʊ: «K'a a bɔ a taam, k'a sugur yɛ, bala, Woso cirbəə b'a zɔ.» 
A mɩŋŋa wosolɛsinnɩsorazaa Ezayii a hɩ ma, a ʊ: Gʊaa leer nɩ bɔm poo nɔ ʊ: k'a Zuuba zaa banka, k'a zaa fɔɔrɛrɔ wɔɔ bɩmbanka kʊ ŋ nɩ yɩ tɩrga. 
Zaan bɩ, yoonde ka huu a a du a ma, an kʊmbar sa an a so a ʊ, bɩsɩnɔ kan zɔɔn kɩ n a hɔbɩrɛ. 
A m naa, Zerizalɛm, Zidee kʊ bɩ haay, kɔ k'a tɔ m Zʊrdɛn kʊrɔ wɔɔ haay nɩ tam zi, 
ŋ n'a mimbʊnyaarɔ hɩrɛ, Zaan nɩ batɛm karɛ ŋ ʊ Zʊrdɛn kɔ ʊ. 
K'a farizɛɛnrɔ kan sadisɛɛnrɔ kɩ yɩ ŋn'ɩ zɛm gʊta batɛm sire bɩncɛ ʊ bɩ, a a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «Mɩnnɔ kʊsɩrɔ do, nka y'a hɩ awɔɔ m, a ʊ, a a si Woso nɔmaa gʊta k'a nɩ zɛm bɩ lɛɛ? 
K'a zibəə k'a n'a hɩnkarɛ a ʊ, awɔɔ heerlʊrlɛ yɩ bɩ ba. 
B'a b'a hɔɔn da a ʊ, awɔɔ zɩ m Abraham nɩ y. Bala, mɔɔ y'a hɩrɛ awɔɔ m, Woso a da ma an jaarɔ nɔɔn duro wusigə an ŋ ba Abraham nyɩnɔ. 
Bɩ dɩɩtaa naa, n sɩga banka k'an gɔrɔ zɩnzɛ ra ŋ məsi ma, gɔ niŋŋə k'a bɩr nyɩ mɩŋŋa darɛ y, n y'a zɛm la, n n a da sɛ ʊ.» 
«Mɔɔ ɩ batɛm karɛ awɔɔ ʊ hi ʊ a heer k'awɔɔ lʊr bɩ minto. Bɩ, an nɩ zɛm mɔɔ jɛ ʊ bɩ paŋŋa lɛ da mɔɔ ra, mɔɔ n m ga m, m a kʊsɔrɔ waa a gannɔ ʊ y, a batɛm karɛ awɔɔ ʊ *Sɛnt-Ɛspri kan sɛ kɩ nɔ ʊ. 
A y'a gʊr m a wɔ ʊ, a y'a tʊyaa waarɛ an a hɔyaa gʊr an a zar sɩ ʊ, bɩ, a sɛ k'a bɩr lihim bɩ nyam puur b'ʊ.» 
A m bɩ, Yeezuu n a to Galilee ʊ, an bʊr Zʊrdɛn ʊ Zaan zi, k'a bɩ batɛm si. 
Bɩ, Zaan a gu an n'a hɩrɛ, a ʊ: «Ɩbɩɩ y'ɩ ga m, ɩ batɛm ka mɔɔ ʊ, b'ɩbɩɩ ɩ ya, ɩ nɩ zɛm mɔɔ zi!» 
Yeezuu n a hɩ m, a ʊ: «A to k'an yɩ ncɩnaaʊ naa ma, bala, naa n'a karɛ, wɔɔ n hɔ haay k'an tɩrga Woso ma bɩ ba.» Zaan n yɛ kɛɛrɛ. 
Yeezuu k'a batɛm bɩ si bɩ, a bɔ hi b'ʊ. A m bɩ, brama n a gʊ, an Woso Ɛspri yɩ an nɩ zerle, am cirpʊʊrɛ hɔ bɩ m an bɩ zer la. 
Leer n a to mim ʊ an n'a hɩrɛ, a ʊ: «Ba mɔɔ Nyɩ m na. Mɔɔ ŋʊa gʊta. A mɔɔ nɔ yɩ paan….»
