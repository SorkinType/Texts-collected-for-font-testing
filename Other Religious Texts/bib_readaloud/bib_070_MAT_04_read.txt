Matiyee.
4.
Bɩ jɛ ʊ bɩ, Sɛnt-Ɛspri bɩ ta Yeezuu m poo nɔ ʊ, kʊ Sʊtaana n a gɩnga ma. 
K'a a lɛ kʊsɩ dɔmɩm busi sinuu ʊ kan a gunuu ʊ kɩ bɩ, nɔ b'a nyasʊ. 
Sʊtaana n a zɔ ma, an a hɩ m, a ʊ: «Kʊ Woso Nyɩ n'ɩbɩɩ m, k'ɩ ka kʊ jaarɔ nɔɔn duro n a ba hɔbɩrɛ.» 
Bɩ, Yeezuu a nyɩ bɔ nʊ, a ʊ: «N y'a gʊrsɩra ba Wosoci ʊ, n ʊ: Hɔbɩrɛ bɛɛ gʊaa n'a bɩm bala k'a mɩsɩrbaa ba y, bɩ lemim haay k'a nɩ bɔm Woso lɛ ʊ bɩ m.» 
Sʊtaana n a gaa an ta m Zerizalɛm ʊ, an a der Wosocɛ bɩ mim ʊ. 
A n a hɩ m, a ʊ: «Kʊ Woso Nyɩ n'ɩbɩɩ m k'ɩ hɩr ɩ ɩ da tara ʊ, bala, n y'a gʊrsɩra ba, n ʊ: A lɛrɔ karɛ a malɛɛkarɔ ʊ, kʊ ŋ n'ɩbɩɩ busu a wɔrɔ ʊ k'ɩbɩɩ b'ɩ gəm zɛ y.» 
Yeezuu n a hɩ m, a ʊ: «N y'a gʊrsɩra ba sɔ, n ʊ: Ɩ bɩ da Zuuba ɩ Woso lɛ ʊ y.» 
Sʊtaana n a ya, an ta m ci gʊta mim ʊ, an durnya cirbəəro kʊrɔ hɩnka m kan ŋ paŋŋa kɩ, 
an a hɩ, a ʊ: «K'ɩbɩɩ kukurə zɛ mɔɔ taa, ɩ mɔɔ da ʊ, mɔɔ naa haay karɛ ɩbɩɩ ʊ.» 
Yeezuu a nyɩ bɔ nʊ, a ʊ: «Ɩ jɛ bɔ mɔɔ jɩr ʊ, Sʊtaana! Bala, n y'a gʊrsɩra ba, n ʊ: Zuuba Woso y'a deem ɩbɩɩ nɩ yaa dam nɩ, ɩ da ʊ.» 
Bɩ jɛ ʊ bɩ, Sʊtaana n a bɔ a jɛ ʊ, lɛɛm bɩ malɛɛkarɔ n bʊr, ŋnɩ bɩ hɔbɩrɛ ka ʊ. 
Yeezuu k'a a ma, n ʊ, n Zaan du lɔnnɔcɛ ʊ bɩ, a a hʊr, an ta Galilee ʊ. 
A n gɔɔta Nazarɛtɩ ʊ y. A hʊr a ʊ, an aa nyɩnta Kapɛrnawum ʊ. Kʊ m bɩ, an nɩ Galilee higʊta lɛ ra Zabɩlɔn kan Nɛftalii kɩ kʊrɔ wɔɔ barla. 
Yɩ a ʊ ncɩnaaʊ kʊ wosolɛsinnɩsorazaa Ezayii meerbaarɔ nɔɔn duro n kʊ yɩ. 
Zabɩlɔn tara, Nɛftalii tara, higʊta zaa, kʊrɔ kʊ ŋ nɩ Zʊrdɛn kɔ gəm la kan doro vantɔɔ kʊ ŋ nɩ Galilee ʊ rɔ wɔɔ kɩ. 
Do k'a nɩ monsigə ʊ bɩ lɛɛgure gandaara yɩ, gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ nɩ zɛ kʊ ʊ kan zɛ hinni kɩ rɔ wɔɔ, lɛɛgure bʊr brɔɔ minto. 
A n a sa naa ma bɩ, Yeezuu a sɩŋŋɩda sa, an nɩ Woso lemim pa darɛ, an n'a hɩrɛ, a ʊ: «K'a bɔ a taam k'a sugur yɛ: Woso cirbəə b'a zɔ.» 
Yeezuu k'a nɩ Galilee higʊta lɛ ra an nɩ tam bɩ, a gʊɔɔ yɩ hɩɩya: Sɩmɔɔn kʊ n n'a birm Pɩyɛɛr kan a danzaa Andɩr kɩ, ŋn'ɩ zaŋŋa zom higʊta b'ʊ, bala, zɔbənno m. 
A n a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «K'a bʊr a zu mɔɔ ʊ, mɔɔ y'awɔɔ bam gʊɔɔ kama zannɔ.» 
Lɛɛm bɩ, ŋ y'a zaŋŋarɔ wɔɔ to, ŋn'ɩ zum Yeezuu ʊ. 
K'a ta lɛɛ bɩ, a Zakkɩ yɩ kan a danzaa Zaan kɩ, Zebedee nyɩnɔ m ŋn'ɩ kɔɔlʊʊ ʊ kan a zɩ Zebedee bɩ kɩ, ŋn'a zaŋŋarɔ bankam. A n ŋ bir. 
Ŋ n a bɔ a zɩ b'ʊ kan kɔɔlʊʊ bɩ kɩ lɛɛm, ŋn'ɩ zure a jɛ ʊ. 
Yeezuu gasʊ Galilee kʊ b'ʊ haay, an nɩ bɩmbɩrm, an nɩ hɔ dɩndam gʊɔɔ m zɩɩfʊrɔ sokʊmacɛrɔ wɔɔ ʊ, an nɩ Woso cirbəə lemim nyɩnta bɩ pa dam, an nɩ yaaba do haay waam kan kʊsɩ do haay kɩ zamaa bɩ bire ʊ. 
A tɔ bɔ lɛɛ haay ʊ, an ta kan Siirii kʊ kɩ haay, a m naa, n nɩ zɛm gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ nɩ cicir bam yaaba do haay kan kʊsɩ do haay nɔ ʊ rɔ wɔɔ kɩ m: Gʊɔɔ kʊ zinəro n ta ŋ nɩ, parparlɔzannɔ, mɛgarɛzannɔ kɩ an ŋ waa. 
Zamaa cir gʊta nɩ zure a jɛ ʊ, a to ŋ y'a ʊ Galilee ʊ, Taraburo ʊ, Zerizalɛm kan Zidee kɩ, kan Zʊrdɛn kɔ gəm la kɩ.
