Krista  ZIBƏƏZANNƆ CI.
1.
Teofil, m lɛɛkarɛ ci bɩ nɔ ʊ, mɔɔ Yeezuu zibəə haay k'a ba kan hɔ haay k'a dɩnda bɩ da ʊ, an a sa a sɩŋŋɩda ʊ, 
an ta, an hinni k'a zibəəzannɔ bɔ ʊ, an a lɛrɔ ka ŋ ʊ, kan *Sɛnt-Ɛspri paŋŋa kɩ, an ta an a dɔmɩm k'a der ʊ arzana ʊ bɩ ku. 
Brɔɔ a a hɩnka ŋ nɩ mɩsɩr a zɛ bɩ kur, kan makrarɔ gʊtɔɔ kʊ ŋ bɩsɩkarɛ ba ʊ kɩ, a ʊ: a a mɩm bɔ ʊ, a m mɩsɩr. A kasɛtɩ nyɩnta ʊ gʊta. Dɔmɩm busi nɔ ʊ, a a hɩnka ŋ nɩ, an nɩ Woso cirbəə bɩ mim dɩndarɛ ŋ nɩ. 
A nyɩnta an nɩ hɔbɩrɛ bɩm kan ŋ kɩ hindeem ʊ, an lɛ ka ŋ ʊ, a ʊ: ŋ bɩ bɔ Zerizalɛm nɔ ʊ y, bɩ kʊ ŋ nɩ gɔɔta ŋnɩ dɔ Zɩ Woso lerbɔrɛ bɩ ma. «Hɔ niŋŋə k'awɔɔ ma, mɔɔ n a hɩ m lɛ m bɩ. 
Zaan hi batɛm ka denter, b'awɔɔ, naa kan dɔmɩm poore kɩ, awɔɔ batɛm sim Sɛnt-Ɛspri nɔ ʊ.» 
Ŋ nyɩnta, ŋn'a zɛ kʊ ra, ŋnɩ laaka naa do da han, ŋ ʊ: «Zuuba, naa ma ɩbɩɩ n nɩ Israyɛl cirbəə bɩ bankaŋ ʊ gɛ?» 
A n a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «Awɔɔ n a ga m k'a dɔmɩnnɔ kan wakatɩrɔ kɩ kʊ Zɩ Woso a cɩnta a mɩŋŋa paŋŋa m bɩ dɔ y. 
Bɩ, awɔɔ paŋŋa yɩrɛ, Sɛnt-Ɛspri hɔ m, an nɩ zɛm bɩ zer awɔɔ mim ʊ, a paŋŋa karɛ awɔɔ ʊ. Awɔɔ nyɩntam mɔɔ kasɛtɩzannɔ Zerizalɛm ʊ, Zidee haay kan Samarii kɩ haay, kan tara lɛ cɩnta jɩ kɩ.» 
Kʊ Yeezuu minno nɔɔn duro hɩ ŋ nɩ bɩ, a der ŋ mɩnyaa m, warkʊ bʊr an b'a nyaam ŋ lɛɛ. 
Kʊ ŋ gɔɔta ŋn'ɩ dɩgam brama ma bɩ, gʊɔɔ hɩɩyɔɔ bʊr ŋnɩ huu fu du a ma, ŋnɩ bɩ jɩm ŋ jɩr ʊ. 
Ŋ n a hɩ ŋ nɩ, ŋ ʊ: «Galilee gʊɔɔ ra, bɔ minto m, awɔɔ n gɔɔta an nɩ dɩgam brama ncɩnaaʊ? Yeezuu naa do kʊ n y'a bɔ awɔɔ bire ʊ an der brama bɩ y'a bɔrɛ an bʊr maam, amba awɔɔ yɩ an der brama bɩ m.» 
Kʊ ŋ zer ci kʊ n n'a birm Oliviyee ci bɩ ra bɩ, ŋ y'a wusigə, ŋnɩ ta Zerizalɛm ʊ. Bɩ ci bɩ do, a ga a ʊ *sabaa dɔmɩm tarɛ m, amba n lɛ ka bɩ m. 
Kʊ ŋ yaa lee ku bɩ, ŋ der cɛ k'a nɩ mim ʊ b'ʊ, amba ŋ tɩr a ba biisi bɩ m. Bɩncɛ bɩ do, Pɩyɛɛr kan Zaan kɩ, Zakkɩ kɩ, Andɩr kɩ, Filipi kan Toma kɩ, Bartelemii kan Matiyee kɩ, Alfee nyɩ Zakkɩ kan Sɩmɔɔn kʊ n n'a birm *Zelɔtɩ bɩ kɩ, kan Zakkɩ nyɩ Ziidi kɩ tɩr kʊ zɛ ʊ. 
Ŋ haay a zɛ kʊ ra lɛ deem ŋn'a tɔŋ ʊ kan yaadarɛ kɩ, kan lannɔ gɔsɩnnɔ kɩ Yeezuu da Maarii kɩ, kan Yeezuu danyɩnɔ kɩ. 
Dɔmɩm bɩ do ʊ, gʊɔɔ a so kʊ ma b'ʊ, ŋ lɛ n a zɔ zɔrɔ buhɩra ma. Pɩyɛɛr n wuti a danyɩnɔ wɔɔ bire ʊ, an a hɩ, a ʊ: 
«M danyɩnɔ, a ga a wʊm kʊ Sɛnt-Ɛspri mim k'a hɩ Davɩd lɛ m Wosoci nɔ ʊ Zidas k'a ka lɛɛ, ŋnɩ Yeezuu nyasʊ bɩ minto bɩ n kʊ yɩ. 
A nyɩnta wɔɔ bire ʊ, an a hɔ jirle hɔ yɩ, wɔɔ zibəə bɩ nɔ ʊ. 
Bɩ, gʊaa bɩ do, zibəə bʊnyaa k'a a ba bɩ, a bɩ busoo bɩ sa, an tara si m. A do a ʊ a nɔ ra, a nɔ bɩ n haan, a nɔbaarɔ haay n bɔ, ŋnɩ sur tara ʊ. 
Zerizalɛm gʊɔɔ haay a dɔ. Bɩ y'a ka ŋn'ɩ tara bɩ birm a lemim m Hakɛldamaa, a lɛ taa k'a hɩ, a ʊ: Ma tara. 
Sɩra m, n y'a gʊrsɩra ba ncɩnaaʊ laarɔ ci nɔ ʊ: K'a har n gɔɔta zer, kʊ gɔsɩ bɩ nyɩnta ʊ y. Bɩ, a a ya an a hɩ, a ʊ: Kʊ gʊaa vanta n a zibəə bɩ sa. 
Gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ wɔɔ da zaa ʊ lɛɛ haay ʊ dɔmɩm kʊ Zuuba Yeezuu mɩsɩrbaa ba ʊ wɔɔ bire ʊ bɩ, 
an a sa Yeezuu batɛm k'a a si Zaan zi bɩ ma, an ta an dɔmɩm k'a der ʊ arzana ʊ bɩ ku bɩ, a a ga m, kʊ ŋ gʊaa deem n a ba kan wɔɔ kɩ a mɩmbɔʊrɛ kasɛtɩzaa.» 
N ŋ hɩnka hɩɩya, Zozɛf kʊ n n'a birm Barsabas, n nɩ so ra, n n'a tɔ ka Zistus bɩ m kan Matɩyas kɩ. 
Ŋ n yaa da ncɩnaaʊ: «Zuuba, ɩbɩɩ k'ɩ gʊaa haay heer dɔ, dɩga gʊaa hɩɩya naa bire ʊ ɩ gʊaa k'ɩbɩɩ bɔ ʊ bɩ hɩnka wɔɔ m, 
k'an nyɩnta lemim nyɩnta zibəəzaa, bɩncɛ kʊ Zidas a to an ta a bɩncɛ ʊ bɩ.» 
Ŋ n pʊɛɛga da, an jɩm Matɩyas mim ʊ. Bɩ y'a da kristazibəəzannɔ bueeredeenno wɔɔ barla.
