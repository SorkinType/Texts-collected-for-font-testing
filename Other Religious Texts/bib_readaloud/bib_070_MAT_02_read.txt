Matiyee.
2.
Yeezuu bɩ, yɩ a ʊ Betelehɛm ʊ Zidee kʊ ʊ, cir Hɩrɔɔdɩ dɔmɩm ʊ, bɩ hɔdonno a to dɔjɩr ʊ, ŋnɩ bʊr Zerizalɛm ʊ, 
ŋnɩ laaka da: «Zɩɩfʊrɔ cir k'a yɩ b'ɩ ka? Wɔɔ monyaa yɩ dɔjɩr ʊ bɩ y'a ka, wɔɔ n bʊr k'ʊ b'a da ʊ.» 
Hɩrɔɔdɩ k'a naa ma bɩ, a da bɔ ʊ kan Zerizalɛm zannɔ kɩ haay. 
A n wosocɛmannɩbənno jɩtannɔ wɔɔ so kʊ ma kan wosocikarɩnsaambɔɔ wɔɔ kɩ, an laaka da ŋ han kʊ niŋŋə kʊ Krista bɩ n nɩ yɩŋ ʊ bɩ ma. 
Brɔɔ n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, ŋ ʊ: Betelehɛm ʊ Zidee kʊ ʊ, bala, hɔ kʊ wosolɛsinnɩsorazaa b'a hɩ bɩ n naa do. 
B'ɩbɩɩ Betelehɛm, Zidee kʊ, ɩbɩɩ bɛɛ m poore Zidee kʊrɔ gʊtɔɔ wɔɔ barla y. Bala, cir bɔrɛ ɩbɩɩ bireyaa ʊ, an ka Israyɛl mɔɔ gʊɔɔ wɔɔ lɛɛ. 
A m bɩ, Hɩrɔɔdɩ n hɔdonno wɔɔ bir sɛɛrɛ, an laaka da ŋ han dɔmɩm kʊ monyaa bɩ bɔ ʊ bɩ ma. 
A n ŋ nyɔɔ Betelehɛm ʊ, an n'a hɩrɛ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «K'a ta a laaka da nyɩ bɩ mim ma mɩŋŋa, k'a nyɩ bɩ yɩ, k'a a wusigə a b'a hɩ mɔɔ m, kʊ m ta m aa da ʊ sɔ.» 
Cir bɩ k'a naa hɩ bɩ, ŋ y'a hʊr, ta kʊ ŋ nawʊm bɩ, monyaa kʊ ŋ lɛɛ ka ŋn'a yɩ dɔjɩr b'ɩ tarɛ ŋ lɛɛ, an ta an aa jɩm lɛɛ kʊ nyɩ bɩ n ta ʊ bɩ burə ʊ. 
Monyaa bɩ kʊ ŋ y'a yɩ bɩ, ŋ heer yɩ nyɩnta gʊta. 
Ŋ gasʊ cɛ b'ʊ, ŋn'aa nyɩ bɩ yɩ kan a da Maarii kɩ, ŋnɩ kukurə zɛ a taa, ŋnɩ yaa da m, bɩ jɛ ʊ, ŋn'a mɔdrɔ wɔɔ hʊr, ŋnɩ gan wʊ ma: Sarma m kan tidəər nyisi kɩ, *mɩɩr kɩ. 
Bɩ Woso a hɩ ŋ nɩ nɩnnarɛ nɔ ʊ, a ʊ: ŋ b'a ya, ŋnɩ bɔ Hɩrɔɔdɩ zi y. Naa minto bɩ, brɔɔ zaa vanta sa ŋnɩ doo a kʊ ʊ. 
A hʊr kʊ ŋ y'a ʊ bɩ, Zuuba malɛɛka a hɩnka Zozɛf m nɩnnarɛ nɔ ʊ, an a hɩ m, a ʊ: «Wuti ɩ nyɩ bɩ gaa kan a da bɩ kɩ, ɩ ɩ si, ɩ ta Eziptɩ ʊ, nyɩnta b'ʊ k'an ta an aa dɔmɩm kʊ mɔɔ n nɩ m yarɛ m meer ba kan ɩbɩɩ kɩ bɩ ku, bala, Hɩrɔɔdɩ y'a kam nyɩ bɩ ma, k'a zɛ.» 
Zozɛf n wuti, an nyɩ bɩ kan a da bɩ kɩ gaa gunuu ʊ, an a hʊr an ta Eziptɩ ʊ. 
A gɔɔta b'ʊ an ta, an Hɩrɔɔdɩ garɛ ku, kʊ Woso meerbaa k'a ba kan a lɛsinnɩsorazaa lɛ kɩ bɩ n kʊ yɩ. Mɔɔ m Nyɩ bɩ bir, k'an bɔ Eziptɩ ʊ. 
Hɩrɔɔdɩ k'a b'a dɔ a ʊ, hɔdonno wɔɔ a yamba a m bɩ, a wuti nɔmaa gandaara m. A n gʊɔɔ nyɔɔ Betelehɛm ʊ kan kʊrɔ vantɔɔ k'a a so ŋ ʊ rɔ wɔɔ kɩ ʊ, kʊ ŋ n'aa nyɩnɔ poorero yaarɔ kʊ ŋ dɔɔraa hɩɩya gɛɛ ŋ n a ku rɔ wɔɔ zɩnzɛ. Amba hɔdonno wɔɔ a hɩ m, nyɩnta k'a ʊ, an laaka da k'a nyɩ bɩ yɩrɛ dɔmɩm dɔ bɩ ma. 
Naa ma bɩ, hɔ kʊ wosolɛsinnɩsorazaa Zeremii a hɩ bɩ kʊ yɩ, a ʊ: 
N zeedarɛ ma Ramaa ʊ, nyikəre kan heerzarlɛ gʊta kɩ: Rasɛl nɩ nyi karɛ a nyɩnɔ minto, a lɛ baa kʊ n n'a wɔ zɩnzɛ a nɩ y, nyɩnɔ wɔɔ kʊ ŋ gɩnga bɩ tɔ ma. 
Hɩrɔɔdɩ garɛ jɛ ʊ bɩ, Zuuba malɛɛka a hɩnka Zozɛf m nɩnnarɛ nɔ ʊ Eziptɩ ʊ. 
A n meer ba kan kɩ, a ʊ: «Wuti, ɩ nyɩ bɩ kan a da bɩ kɩ gaa ɩ ɩ wusigə, ɩ ta Israyɛl tara ra. Bala, gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ n'a kam ma k'a nyɩ bɩ meeyaa bɔ ʊ rɔ wɔɔ gɩnga.» 
Zozɛf n wuti, an nyɩ bɩ kan a da bɩ kɩ gaa, an ta Israyɛl ʊ. 
Bɩ, Zozɛf k'a a ma, n ʊ, Arkelayus nɩ cirbəə m Zidee ʊ a zɩ Hɩrɔɔdɩ bɩncɛ ʊ bɩ, nyibəə a nyasʊ k'an ta b'ʊ bɩ kɩ, bɩ Woso meer ba kan kɩ nɩnnarɛ nɔ ʊ, an ta Galilee kʊ ʊ. 
A n aa nyɩnta kʊ kʊ n n'a hɩm ma Nazarɛtɩ ʊ b'ʊ, k'an bɩr yɩrɛ ɩ ʊ hɔ kʊ wosolɛsinnɩsorazannɔ wɔɔ a hɩ bɩ n kʊ yɩ, ŋ ʊ: N y'a birm Nazarɛtɩ gʊaa.
