Markɩ.
6.
Yeezuu a hʊr lɛɛ bɩ do ʊ, kan a karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ kɩ, an ta kʊ k'a tʊr ʊ b'ʊ. 
*Sabaa hinni k'a a ku bɩ, Yeezuu hɔ dɩnda gʊɔɔ m zɩɩfʊrɔ sokʊmacɛ ʊ. Zamaa bɩ n zɛ kədə a hɔdɩndarɛ bɩ tɔ ma. Gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ nyɩnta b'ʊ rɔ wɔɔ gʊta y'a hɩm kʊ m, ŋ ʊ: «Awɔɔ a to mindɔrɛ naa do m ka? A to awɔɔ ʊ hɔɔn naa do m ka, an a da ma, an nɩ hoserlo barɛ a wɔ nɩ? 
Awɔɔ bɛɛ m Maarii nyɩ, an nɩ gɔ wum bɩ ra? Zakkɩ, Zozɛs, Ziidi kan Sɩmɔɔn kɩ danyɩ bɛɛ ra? Awɔɔ danlʊrɔ ba wɔɔ bire ʊ naa ʊ ra?» Naa do a ka ŋ n kaanm Yeezuu m. 
Yeezuu n a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «Wosolɛsinnɩsorazaa bɩ daʊrɛ yɩrɛ a zɩ kʊ ʊ kan a yɩrɛrɔ kɩ, a har gʊɔɔ kɩ bire ʊ y.» 
Yeezuu n hoser ba b'ʊ y, kʊ gʊɔɔ k'a wɔ ka ŋ mim la, an ŋ waa rɔ wɔɔ bɛɛ y. 
Yeezuu zɛ kədə ŋ sɩrakarɛ war bɩ tɔ ma. Yeezuu cem an nɩ hɔ dɩndam kʊrɔ jɩnjɛɛnnɔ wɔɔ nɔ ʊ. 
Bɩ jɛ ʊ bɩ, Yeezuu n bueerehɩɩyɔɔ wɔɔ bir, an a sɩŋŋɩda ba, an nɩ ŋ nyɔɔm hɩɩya-hɩɩya, an lɛ ka ŋ ʊ kʊ ŋ n la zinəro ra. 
A a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «Ŋ bɩ hɔsɩ sa a wɔ ʊ zaa sarɛ minto y. Bur, hɛra, busoo, a b'a sa y, k'a maam bara m. 
Bɩ, k'a kʊsɔrɔ da ʊ, k'a huu sa deem.» 
A a ya, an a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «K'a lee ku kʊ ʊ, k'a gɔɔta har kʊ n y'awɔɔ si ʊ b'ʊ, an ta, an awɔɔ hʊrlɛ dɔmɩm ku. 
K'awɔɔ ta kʊ ʊ, ŋ nɩ kaanm awɔɔ m, kʊ ŋ n a tʊr ka awɔɔ mim ma, k'a ɩ doom, k'a kʊ bɩ taanyisi gʊngʊ a gam ma, k'an nyɩnta kasɛtɩ.» 
Bueerehɩɩyɔɔ wɔɔ a hʊr ŋn'a hɩm gʊɔɔ m, ŋ ʊ: «K'a a bɔ a taam, k'a sugur yɛ.» 
Ŋ la zinəro ra gʊta, ŋ nyɔɔ nɔ yaabazannɔ gʊta ma, ŋnɩ ŋ waa. 
Cir Hɩrɔɔdɩ Yeezuu mim ma, bala, a tɔ bɔ lɛɛ woo… ʊ. Gʊɔɔ gɔsɩnnɔ n'a hɩrɛ, ŋ ʊ: «Zaan-Batiisi a mɩm bɔ ʊ gəəno bire ʊ. Bɩ y'a ka, an paŋŋa yɩ, an nɩ hoserlo bam.» 
Nawɔnnɔ ʊ: «*Elii m.» Gɔsɩnnɔ ʊ: «Wosolɛsinnɩsorazannɔ wɔɔ deem m am biisi hɔɔ wɔɔ m.» 
Hɩrɔɔdɩ k'a hɔ kʊ Yeezuu a ba bɩ ma bɩ, a a hɩ, a ʊ: «Zaan-Batiisi m! Mɔɔ n a ka, n n a mim gʊ ma bɩ y'a a ya, an a mɩm bɔ ʊ!» 
A yɩ sɩra, Hɩrɔɔdɩ a ka, n nɩ Zaan nyasʊ, n n'a sur, n n'a du lɔnnɔcɛ ʊ. Hɩrɔɔdɩ bɩ, naa ba a ʊ: a dam Filipi lʊ k'a yɔ m a tɔ m Hɩrɔdɩyaadɩ bɩ minto. 
Bala, Zaan a hɩ Hɩrɔɔdɩ m, a ʊ: «A n a ga m, k'ɩbɩɩ m ɩ dam lʊ yɔm y!» 
Hɩrɔdɩyaadɩ nyɩnta an sɔ Zaan m, a lɛ n taa k'a ka kʊ n n a zɛ, b'a b'a dam ma y. 
Hɩrɔɔdɩ ɩ nyi bam Zaan m, an nɩ kum la, bala, a a dɔ, a ʊ: Zaan bɩ, gʊaa tɩrga nɩ, gʊaa mɩŋŋa m sɔ. K'a a tʊr ka Zaan meerbaa bɩ ma bɩ, a n a dɔ kʊ bɔ an n'a hɩm Zaan nɩ y, b'a a meerbaa bɩ ŋʊa, an n'a tʊr kam ma. 
Kɛɛrɛ bɩ, toore a ka Hɩrɔdɩyaadɩ ʊ. Hɩrɔɔdɩ a kʊ lɛɛzannɔ, yarbənno jɩtannɔ, kan Galilee gʊɔɔ gʊtɔɔ kɩ bir a yɩrɛ cibsa hɔbɩrɛ ma. 
Hɩrɔdɩyaadɩ nyɩmbʊɛɛrɛ gasʊ hɔbɩrɛ bɩrɛ cɛ ʊ b'ʊ, an sar ba zamaa bɩ bire ʊ; a Hɩrɔɔdɩ nɔ yɩ kan hɔbɩrɛbinno wɔɔ kɩ. Cir Hɩrɔɔdɩ n a hɩ nyɩmbʊɛɛrɛ bɩ m, a ʊ: «Hɔ k'ɩbɩɩ lɛ n taa bɩ yɛ mɔɔ han, kʊ m a ka ɩ ʊ.» 
Hɩrɔɔdɩ n a wer si bɩ m, a ʊ: «Hɔ k'ɩbɩɩ lɛ n taa bɩ, mɔɔ y'a karɛ ʊ, baa kʊ mɔɔ cirbəə tara bɩ zɛkumbir m.» 
Nyɩmbʊɛɛrɛ bɩ n bɔ, an aa a hɩ a da m, a ʊ: «M bɔ yɛ?» A n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «Zaan-Batiisi mim yɛ.» 
Nyɩmbʊɛɛrɛ bɩ n ta zɔɔ-zɔɔ, an aa a hɩ cir Hɩrɔɔdɩ m, a ʊ: «Mɔɔ lɛ n taa dɩɩtaa, k'ɩ Zaan-Batiisi mim da laaga ʊ, ɩ ka m ʊ.» 
Cir Hɩrɔɔdɩ heer a zar, bɩ, a b'a dam ma, an a gu ma y, k'a a wer si hɔbɩrɛbinno wɔɔ bire ʊ bɩ minto. 
Lɛɛm bɩ, cir Hɩrɔɔdɩ lɛ ka a dɔmazaa deem ʊ, k'an bʊr Zaan mim m. A dɔmazaa bɩ n ta lɔnnɔcɛ ʊ b'ʊ, an aa Zaan mim gʊ ma, 
an bʊr m laaga ʊ, an b'a ka nyɩmbʊɛɛrɛ b'ʊ, an a si, an a ka a da ʊ. 
Zaan karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ kʊ ŋ naa ma bɩ, ŋ bʊr, ŋnɩ bɩ Zaan gər bɩ sa, ŋn'aa bir. 
Yeezuu zibəəzannɔ wɔɔ a bɔ, ŋnɩ bʊr zi, ŋnɩ hɔ haay kʊ ŋ y'a ba bɩ hɩ Yeezuu m, kan hɔ haay kʊ ŋ y'a dɩnda gʊɔɔ wɔɔ m bɩ kɩ. 
Yeezuu kan a karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ kɩ n toore yɩ k'a hɔ bɩ gʊɔɔ gʊta kʊ ŋ nɩ zɛm, ŋn'a wusigəm bɩ minto. Bɩ minto y'a ka, a ʊ: «K'a bʊr k'ʊ bɔ toore ma, bɩncɛ kʊ gʊɔɔ ba ʊ b'ʊ, k'a hoon si poore.» 
Ŋ yaa da kɔɔlʊʊ ʊ, ŋnɩ ta toore ma bɩncɛ kʊ gɔsɩ ba ʊ b'ʊ. 
Bɩ gʊɔɔ gʊta ŋ cenle yɩ, ŋnɩ ŋ dɔ, ŋnɩ bra si a gam ma, ŋnɩ lɛɛ ka Yeezuu m kan a karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ kɩ lɛɛ bɩ do ʊ. 
Kʊ Yeezuu bɔ kɔɔlʊʊ b'ʊ bɩ, a zamaa cir gʊta yɩ. Gʊɔɔ wɔɔ cicir n a nyasʊ, bala, bɔ ŋ y'a ʊ kʊ ʊ kan hɔbɩsɩ cir kʊ ŋ dɔmazaa ba ʊ bɩ kɩ. A a sɩŋŋɩda ba, an nɩ hɔ gʊta dɩndarɛ ŋ nɩ. 
Gum k'a bʊr an nɩ dam bɩ, Yeezuu karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ a zɔ ma, ŋn'a hɩ m, ŋ ʊ: «Gum da, bɩ pooyaa m naa, 
gʊɔɔ wɔɔ da zaa ra kʊ ŋ nɩ ta kʊrɔ jɩnjɛɛnnɔ wɔɔ ʊ, ŋn'aa hɔbɩrɛ si, ŋn'a bɩ!» 
Yeezuu n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «K'a hɔbɩrɛ ka ŋ ʊ, ŋn'a bɩ!» Ŋ n a lar, ŋ ʊ: «Ɩbɩɩ lɛ n taa k'ʊ aa bur si *deniyee zɔɔhɩra k'ʊ b'a ka ŋ ʊ gɛ?» 
Yeezuu n a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «K'a yaa dɩga a, bur karɔ n ta awɔɔ ʊ?» Kʊ ŋ yaa dɩga a bɩ, ŋ b'a hɩ, ŋ ʊ: «Bur soor n ta ʊ kan zɔ kɩ hɩɩya.» 
A n lɛ ka ŋ ʊ, kʊ ŋ n zamaa bɩ nyɩnta bur butur bɩ ra. 
Ŋ nɩ nyɩnyɩnta zɔrɔ-zɔrɔ, busoor-busoor. 
Yeezuu bur soor bɩ si kan zɔ hɩɩya bɩ kɩ a wɔ ʊ, an dɩga brama, an a barka da ʊ, an ŋ zɩnzɛra, an a ka a karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ ʊ, kʊ ŋ n a ka zamaa b'ʊ. A zɔ hɩɩya bɩ jir zamaa bɩ m sɔ. 
Gʊɔɔ wɔɔ haay a bɩ, an ŋ ka. 
Ŋ bur kan zɔ kɩ kuuro gʊr kasɩ lɛ ma bueerehɩɩya. 
Bur bɩ binno wɔɔ barla, yar tisi soor ɩ nyɩnta ʊ. 
Lɛɛm bɩ, Yeezuu a hɩ a karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ m, a ʊ: «K'a da kɔɔlʊʊ ʊ, k'a bra gəm la Bɛtsayɩdaa zeezi.» A mɩŋŋa n gɔɔta k'a zamaa bɩ da zaa ra. 
K'a ŋ da zaa ra, an a nya bɩ, a a hʊr, an ta ci ra k'a yaa da. 
Yirbəə k'a da bɩ, kɔɔlʊʊ bɩ hi bireyaa ku, a mɩŋŋa nɩ gəm la. 
K'a yɩ hɩnhɛr n a nya ŋ lɛ, ŋn'a yɔm kan kɔɔlʊʊ bɩ bɔrɛ kɩ bɩ, dɔ k'a bʊr an nɩ mɛm bɩ, a wuti an tɔ hi ra, an nɩ tarɛ ŋ zi, an a yo k'a cem ŋ han. 
Kʊ ŋ y'a yɩ an nɩ tam hi ra bɩ, ŋ yɩ dɩgam ma, ŋ ʊ: «Toor m,» ŋn'ɩ zee dam. 
Bala, ŋ haay a yɩ, nyibəə n ŋ nyasʊ. Lɛɛm bɩ, Yeezuu meer ba kan ŋ kɩ, a ʊ: «K'a sɩra ka, mɔɔ m, a da bɩ bɔ ʊ y!» 
A n da kɔɔlʊʊ b'ʊ kan ŋ kɩ, hɩnhɛr bɩ n jɩm. Ŋ n zɛ kədə, ŋ n a so a ʊ y. 
Baa kʊ ŋ bur bɩ jirle yɩ bɩ, ŋ n hɔsɩ jɩ ma ŋn'a dɔ ŋ ʊ, Yeezuu a da hɔ haay ma y. 
Kʊ ŋ higʊta bɩ zɛ ra bɩ, ŋ yaa lee ku Zenezarɛtɩ gəm la, ŋn'a kɔɔlʊʊ bɩ kʊsɩ. 
Kʊ ŋ zer lɛɛm bɩ, gʊɔɔ wɔɔ Yeezuu dɔ. 
Ŋ n a si, ŋnɩ ta kʊrɔ haay ʊ, ŋn'aa yaabazannɔ busu a gʊraŋŋarɔ m, ŋnɩ bʊr ŋ nɩ bɩncɛ kʊ Yeezuu n ta ʊ b'ʊ. 
Bɩncɛ k'a nɩ taŋ ʊ, kʊrɔ gʊtɔɔ ʊ, kʊrɔ poorero ʊ, bɔlɛɛmarɔ ʊ, zɛ n nawʊm yaabazannɔ m daasɩrɔ ʊ, ŋn'a yɛm han, k'an yɛ kʊ ŋ n'a wɔ da a huu lɛ ma bala. Bɩ gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ y'a wɔ da ma rɔ wɔɔ waarɛ yɩ haay.
