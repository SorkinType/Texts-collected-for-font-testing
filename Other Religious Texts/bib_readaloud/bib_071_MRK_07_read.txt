Markɩ.
7.
Farizɛɛnrɔ kan wosocikarɩnsaambɔɔ gɔsɩnnɔ kɩ y'a to Zerizalɛm ʊ, ŋn'a zɛ kʊ ra, ŋnɩ bʊr Yeezuu zi, 
ŋnɩ Yeezuu karɩndanyɩnɔ gɔsɩnnɔ yɩ, ŋn'ɩ hɔ bɩm kan dintim kɩ, a lɛ taa k'a hɩ, a ʊ: ŋ n a wɔ hir y. 
Kʊ sɩra nɩ, farizɛɛnrɔ kan zɩɩfʊrɔ kɩ haay hɔbɩrɛ bɩrɛ bɩ, a wɔ ŋn'a hirm mɩŋŋa, amba ŋ yaabɔɔ yɩnnɩbayɩ m. 
Kʊ ŋ y'a to daasɩ ʊ, ŋnɩ bʊr, ŋ bɩr hɔ bɩm kʊ ŋ n dintim gʊ a ma y. Ŋ yɩnnɩbayɩrɔ vantɔɔ ta ʊ gʊta, amba hɛrɔ sʊrgɔrɛ, gərlo kan hɔbɩrɛ mɔdrɔ kɩ. 
Farizɛɛnrɔ kan wosocikarɩnsaambɔɔ wɔɔ kɩ laaka da Yeezuu han, ŋ ʊ: «Bɔ m, ɩbɩɩ karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ bɩr zum wɔɔ yaabɔɔ yɩnnɩbayɩ b'ʊ, ŋnɩ bɔkarɛ ŋn'ɩ hɔ bɩm, bɩ ŋ n hi nyasʊ?» 
Yeezuu n a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «Zambʊzannɔ, Ezayii gumə ta ʊ, Woso lɛ k'a a si an so ra, an awɔɔ mim hɩ bɩ nɔ ʊ, bala, a a gʊrsɩra ba ncɩnaaʊ, a ʊ: Gʊɔɔ nɔɔn duro, mɔɔ da ŋ nawʊŋ ʊ a lɛ m, bɩ ŋ heer laatʊ mɔɔ ma, 
bɩ mɔɔ daʊrɛ bɩ vam m. Durnya mim ŋ n'a dɩndam gʊɔɔ m, Woso hɔ bɛɛ y. 
Woso lɛrɔ wɔɔ awɔɔ to, an nɩ zum gʊɔɔ yɩnnɩbayɩrɔ ʊ.» 
Yeezuu n a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «Woso lɛrɔ wɔɔ bɔ awɔɔ ʊ a jɛ m, an nɩ zum a yɩnnɩbayɩ ʊ. 
Bala, Moyiisi a hɩ, a ʊ: ‹Ɩ zɩ kan ɩ da kɩ da ʊ,› bɩ, ‹Gʊaa k'a zɩ bɔ ʊ kan a da kɩ, kʊ ŋ n a zɛ gər.› 
B'awɔɔ, awɔɔ y'a hɩm, a ʊ: Kʊ gʊaa a hɩ a zɩ m gɛɛ a da m, a ʊ: hɔ kʊ mɔɔ m ga m, m m dam ɩbɩɩ han bɩ ‹kɔrbaan m› a lɛ taa k'a hɩ, a ʊ: ‹Woso mannɩ hɔ m.› 
A n a ga m, k'an a da a zɩ han gɛɛ a da han dɔ y. 
A ba awɔɔ nawʊm ncɩnaaʊ an nɩ Woso lemim bɩ zaar bam a yɩnnɩbayɩrɔ wɔɔ m. Ziro ncɩnaaʊ rɔ wɔɔ lɔɔrɔ awɔɔ nɩ ŋ bam gʊta.» 
Bɩ jɛ ʊ bɩ, Yeezuu zamaa bɩ bir, an a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «K'a tʊr ka mɔɔ ma, k'a da a heer ʊ. 
Hɔ ba ʊ, an n'a tom garga ra, an gasʊ gʊaa nɔ ʊ k'a dintim ka ma y. Bɔ n'a tom gʊaa nɔ ʊ bɩ nɩ dintim kam ma.  
Gʊaa k'a nɩ tʊr m bɩ, k'an a tʊr ka ma.» 
K'a a gʊ zamaa bɩ ma, an gasʊ har ʊ bɩ, a karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ a lar hɔbɔkʊrɛnoom naa do jɩ ma. 
A n a nyɩ bɔ ŋ nʊ, a ʊ: «Awɔɔ sɔ, awɔɔ n naa do jɩ dɔ ra? Awɔɔ n a dɔ, a ʊ: hɔ k'a n'a tore, an nɩ gasʊm gʊaa nɔ ʊ bɩ b'a dam ma, an dintim ka ma ra? 
Bala, a nɔ an nɩ gasʊŋ ʊ, an ta, an nɩ bɔm garga ra, a bɩr gasʊrɛ a heer ʊ y.» Ncɩnaaʊ bɩ, Yeezuu y'a hɩnkarɛ, a ʊ: «hɔbɩrɛ haay mɩŋŋa.» 
A a ya, an a hɩ, a ʊ: «Bɔ nɩ bɔm gʊaa nɔ ʊ bɩ nɩ dintim kam ma. 
Bala, gʊaa heer hɔɔndarɛ bʊnyaa bɩ n nɩ bɔŋ ʊ: so a mɩŋŋa ʊ, koorbarɛ, gərzɛrɛ, 
jaandarɛ, gʊaa hɔ ŋʊarɛ, zibʊnyaa do haay barɛ, bra ba gʊaa m, mɩsɩrbaa bʊnyaa barɛ, jɩm barɛ, gʊaa tɔ zarlɛ, mɩŋŋa busure kan yarmabaa kɩ. 
Hɔɔ bʊnyaarɔ nɔɔn duro haay, bɔ ŋ nawʊm gʊaa heer ʊ, ŋn'ɩ dintim kam ma.» 
Yeezuu k'a cem b'ʊ bɩ, a ta Tiir kʊ ʊ, an aa gasʊ har ʊ, a lɛ baa kʊ ŋ n a yɩ y. Bɩ, a n a da ma, an a nyaakʊm da y. 
Bala, lʊ nɩ nyɩmbʊɛɛrɛ m, zinə nɩ kan kɩ. Bɩ, lʊ bɩ Yeezuu mim ma, an bra si, an bɩ kukurə zɛ Yeezuu taa. 
Lʊ bɩ, grɛkkɩ do m, yɩ a ʊ Siirii kʊ Fenisii ʊ. A n a yɛ Yeezuu han k'an la zinə bɩ ra nyɩmbʊɛɛrɛ bɩ mɛ ʊ. 
Yeezuu n a hɩ m, a ʊ: «A to kʊ nyɩnɔ wɔɔ ŋ hɔ bɩ, an ŋ ka. A n a ga m, ŋnɩ nyɩnɔ hɔbɩrɛ sa, ŋn'a ka jinnyɩnɔ ʊ y.» 
Lʊ bɩ n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «Sɩra m, Zuuba, bɩ, jinnyɩnɔ wɔɔ tɩr hɔbɩrɛ kuuro kʊ ŋ y'a lunlo taabɩr bɩ taa bɩ gʊr, ŋn'a bɩ.» Yeezuu n a hɩ m, a ʊ: 
«Meerbaa naa do minto, doo, zinə bɩ bɔ ɩ nyɩmbʊɛɛrɛ bɩ mɛ ʊ.» 
Lʊ bɩ n a hʊr, an doo, an aa a nyɩ bɩ yɩ, an nɩ wʊtam gargʊ burə ʊ. Zinə bɩ n bɔ a mɛ ʊ. 
Yeezuu a hʊr Tiir kʊ ʊ, an a bɔ, an bɔ Sidɔn ʊ kan Taraburo wɔɔ kɩ nɔ ʊ, an ta Galilee higʊta zeezi. 
N bʊr tikki m Yeezuu zi. Tikki bɩ, bɛkka m sɔ. N n a yɛ han k'an a wɔ ka a mim la, k'an a waa. 
Yeezuu n a gaa zamaa bɩ jɩr ʊ, an bɔ m toore ma, an a nyʊkʊnnyɩnɔ dundu tikki bɩ tʊr ʊ, an lihi da, a nyʊkʊr ma, an a nɔ a nɛɛm ma, 
an a bɔɔta an dɩga brama, an hoonsi bɔ a rɛ, an a hɩ m, a ʊ: «Ɛfata!» 
A lɛ taa k'a hɩ, a ʊ: «Ɩ gʊ!» A tʊʊrɔ wɔɔ n a gʊ lɛɛm, a nɛɛm bɩ n a bɔ kʊ zi, an nɩ meer bam mɩŋŋa. 
Yeezuu n a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «K'a b'a hɩ gɔsɩ nɩ y.» Yeezuu k'a lɛ ka ŋ ʊ, a ʊ: ŋ b'a hɩ bɩ, ŋ y'a pa da, an lɛ da biisi ra. 
Gʊɔɔ wɔɔ zɛ kədə, ŋn'a hɩrɛ, ŋ ʊ: «A ɩ hɔ haay bam mɩŋŋa; A ɩ tikkiro tʊr gʊm, a a ka bəətinno nɩ meer bam!»
