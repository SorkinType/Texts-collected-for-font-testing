Markɩ.
12.
Yeezuu n a sɩŋŋɩda ba, an nɩ meer bam kan ŋ kɩ hɔbɔkʊrɛnoom darɛ m, a ʊ: «Gʊaa ɩ nyɩnta an dɩvɛn gɔ sɔɔn, an lambaar da ma, an yaa hɔn, k'a bɩr dɩvɛn nyɩ bɩ hi bɔŋ ʊ, an bɛŋŋʊ dɔ sɔ, an gʊɔɔ sa kʊ ŋ n dɔ dɩvɛnsɩra bɩ ma, an a hʊr an ta zaa ra. 
Dɩvɛn mɔsa k'a a ku bɩ, a n a zibəənyɩ deem nyɔɔ wutu zibəəzannɔ wɔɔ zi, k'an aa a hɔ si, an b'a ka a ʊ. 
Ŋ n a nyasʊ, ŋn'a zɛ, ŋn'a da zaa ra a wɔ n tu. 
Wutu zaa bɩ n a ya, an zibəənyɩ vanta nyɔɔ. Wutu zibəəzannɔ wɔɔ n a zɛ, ŋnɩ yaaba da a mim ma, ŋnɩ fɩr ka ma. 
A n a ya, an a kaakʊ hɔ nyɔɔ, ŋn'a zɛ gər. Bɩ jɛ ʊ, an gʊɔɔ vantɔɔ nyɔɔ, ŋnɩ fɩr ka gɔsɩnnɔ ma, ŋnɩ gɔsɩnnɔ zɩnzɛ. 
A kur ma hɔ ma bɩ, a nyɩ k'a ŋʊa bɩ y'a deem ɩ gɔɔta, an a nyɔɔ ŋ zi, an n'a hɩrɛ, a ʊ: ‹Ŋ mɔɔ nyɩ bɩ daŋ ʊ.› 
Wutu zibəəzannɔ wɔɔ n a dɩnda kan kʊ kɩ, ŋ ʊ: ‹A n nɩ zɛm b'a so arzaka b'ʊ. K'a bʊr, k'ʊ a zɛ, k'ʊ ʊ so wutu b'ʊ.› 
Ŋ n a nyasʊ, ŋn'a zɛ gər, ŋnɩ bɔ m dɩvɛnsɩra b'ʊ, ŋn'a zo.» 
«Bɔ dɩvɛnsɩra zaa bɩ n'a barɛ? A zɛm, an b'a dɩvɛnsɩra zibəəzannɔ wɔɔ zɩnzɛ, an a sa, an a ka a vantɔɔ ʊ. 
Awɔɔ n naa karɩnda ba ra? Jaa kʊ cɛdonno a bɔ ʊ ŋn'a zo bɩ, bɩ y'a ba jaa paŋŋazaa, cɛ bɩ dɔrɛ minto. 
Zuuba ɩ naa ba, bɩ wɔɔ yɩ hɔ gʊta m.» 
Gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ y'a tʊr ka ma rɔ wɔɔ n'a kam ma k'a nyasʊ, bɩ, ŋ yɩ nyi bam zamaa bɩ m. Ŋ y'a dɔ, ŋ ʊ: hɔbɔkʊrɛnoom naa do da Yeezuu ʊ awɔɔ minto. Ŋ n a hʊr, ŋn'a gʊ ma. 
N farizɛɛnrɔ kan Hɩrɔɔdɩ gʊɔɔ kɩ nyɔɔ Yeezuu zi kʊ ŋ n'aa a nyasʊ a meerbaa nɔ ʊ. 
Ŋ n b'a hɩ, ŋ ʊ: «Zuuba, wɔɔ dɔ sɩrazaa n'ɩbɩɩ m, b'ɩbɩɩ heer tɩrga kan gʊaa biyəə kɩ. Ɩbɩɩ bɩr dɩgam gʊaa meer ma bala y, Woso zaa ɩbɩɩ n'a dɩndam sɩra bɩncɛ ʊ. Zaa ta ʊ k'ʊ *Rɔm cir yawʊr ka gɛ? A a ga m gɛɛ a n a ga nɩ?» 
Bɩ, kʊ Yeezuu ŋ zambʊ bɩ dɔ bɩ, a a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «Bɔ m, awɔɔ n'a gɩngam mɔɔ ma ncɩnaaʊ? K'a bʊr wacɩr m deem, kʊ m dɩga a.» 
Ŋ n bʊr m. Yeezuu n a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «Ŋʊasa naa do kan gʊrsɩra naa do kɩ, nka hɔ nɩ?» Ŋ ʊ: «*Rɔm cir hɔ m.» 
Yeezuu n a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «K'a hɔ kʊ cir a so ʊ bɩ ka ʊ, k'a hɔ kʊ Woso a so ʊ bɩ ka ʊ.» Meerbaa naa do nyɩbɔʊrɛ a ka, ŋ n zɛ kədə. 
Sadisɛɛnrɔ kʊ ŋ ʊ, gəəno mɩmbɔʊrɛ ba ʊ bɩ, ŋ bʊr Yeezuu zi, ŋnɩ laaka da han, ŋ ʊ: 
«Zuuba, Moyiisi a gʊrsɩra ba an a ka wɔɔ ʊ, a ʊ, ‹Kʊ gʊaa ga, an a lʊ to, b'a nyɩ ba ʊ y, k'a danyɩ n lʊ bɩ si, k'an nyɩ yɩ kan kɩ a danyɩ bɩ minto.› 
Danyɩnɔ ɩ nyɩnta saanhɩna. Lɛɛkarɛzaa bɩ n lʊ sa, an ga, an a gʊ lʊ bɩ ma, bɩ nyɩ ba ʊ y. 
A hɩɩya hɔ bɩ n lʊ bɩ si, an ga, b'a n nyɩ yɩ kan kɩ y. A kaakʊ hɔ bɩ maam sɔ. 
Saanhɩnarɔ wɔɔ haay ŋ gɩnga, bɩ ŋ n nyɩ yɩ kan lʊ bɩ kɩ y. Ŋ haay kur bɩ, lʊ bɩ n bɩ ga. 
Gəəno mɩmbɔʊrɛ dɔmɩm ʊ, kʊ ŋ y'a mɩm bɔ ʊ, ŋ haay bireyaa ʊ, nka y'a so lʊ b'ʊ? Bala, gʊɔɔ saanhɩnarɔ wɔɔ haay lʊ bɩ si.» 
Yeezuu n a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «K'awɔɔ n gʊrsɩrarɔ mɩŋŋɔɔ kan Woso paŋŋa kɩ dɔ bɩ, bɩ y'a ka awɔɔ nɩ nyʊnnyɔɔrɛ nɔ ʊ. 
Bala, kʊ gəəno b'a mɩm bɔ ʊ, yar bɩr lʊ sarɛ y, lʊ bɩr yar sarɛ y. Bɔ ŋ y'a ʊ kʊ ʊ kan malɛɛkarɔ kʊ ŋ nɩ brama rɔ wɔɔ kɩ. 
Bɩ kʊ gəəno mɩmbɔʊrɛ bɩ mim nɩ, awɔɔ n a karɩnda ba Moyiisi ci bɩ nɔ ʊ ra, dɔmɩm kʊ Woso meer ba kan Moyiisi kɩ tutugu nɔ ʊ bɩ, a ʊ: ‹Abraham kan Izakkɩ kɩ, Zakɔɔb kɩ Woso m mɔɔ m.› 
Gəəno Woso bɛɛ y, mɩsɩɩrɔ Woso m. Awɔɔ ɩ nyʊnnyɔɔrɛ nɔ ʊ gʊta.» 
Wosocikarɩnsaamba deem ŋ bɩsɩkarɛ bɩ ma, a a yɩ, Yeezuu a nyɩ bɔ ʊ mɩŋŋa, an a zɔ, an Yeezuu lar, a ʊ: «Lɛrɔ wɔɔ haay bire ʊ kara ɩ lɛ da?» 
Yeezuu n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «Lɛ lɛɛkarɛ hɔ bɩ n naa do: ‹K'a tʊr ka Israyɛl zannɔ, Zuuba k'a m wɔɔ Woso bɩ, a y'a deem m Zuuba. 
K'ɩ Zuuba ɩ Woso bɩ ŋʊa kan ɩ heer kɩ haay, kan ɩ nyi kɩ haay, kan ɩ hɔɔndarɛ kɩ haay, kan ɩ paŋŋa kɩ haay.› 
A hɩɩya hɔ bɩ ba n nɩ: ‹K'ɩ mim ŋʊa amba ɩ mɩŋŋa m.› Lɛ vanta ba ʊ an lɛ da nawɔnnɔ ra y.» 
Wosocikarɩnsaamba bɩ n a hɩ m, a ʊ: «Sɩra ɩbɩɩ hɩ, karɩnsaamba, a mɩŋŋa gʊta: ‹Woso y'a deem m, bɩ a vanta ba ʊ, an a na ma y. 
Ɩ Woso ŋʊa kan ɩ heer kɩ haay, ɩ hɔɔn kɩ haay, ɩ paŋŋa kɩ haay, k'ɩ mim ŋʊa amba ɩ mɩŋŋa m,› bɩ jɩra mannɩ haay kʊ n nɩ sɛ nyaŋ ʊ bɩ m, kan ganwʊrɛrɔ k'awɔɔ nɩ ŋ kam Woso ʊ rɔ wɔɔ kɩ.» 
Yeezuu k'a yɩ gʊaa b'a nyɩ bɔ ʊ mɩŋŋa bɩ, a a hɩ, a ʊ: «Ɩbɩɩ bɩ laatʊ Woso cirbəə bɩ ma y.» Bɩ kur bɩ, gʊaa biyəə ɩ nyi bam Yeezuu larlɛ m. 
Yeezuu k'a nɩ hɔ dɩndam Zerizalɛm Wosocɛ ʊ bɩ, a a hɩ, a ʊ: «Bɔ minto m wosocikarɩnsaambɔɔ wɔɔ ʊ: *Davɩd Nyɩ m Krista bɩ m gɛ? 
*Sɛnt-Ɛspri a ka Davɩd n a hɩ, a ʊ: Zuuba Woso a hɩ mɔɔ Zuuba m: nyɩnta mɔɔ bɩsɩ ʊ, kʊ m b'a ka k'ɩ jɩnnɔ wɔɔ n nyɩnta ɩ gam taa. 
Davɩd mɩŋŋa y'a birm Zuuba. Kʊ Zuuba n a ba lɔ, an nyɩnta *Davɩd Nyɩ gɛ?» Zamaa bɩ n'a tʊr kam ma kan heernyɩnta kɩ. 
Yeezuu y'a hɩrɛ ŋ nɩ a hɔdɩndarɛ nɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «K'a dundo a zi kan wosocikarɩnsaambɔɔ kʊ ŋ nɩ bɩmbɩrm zabarɔ m, ŋn'a kam daʊrɛ ma zamaa gʊta bire ʊ rɔ wɔɔ kɩ, 
ŋn'a kam lɛɛkarɛ bɩncɛ ma hɔbɩrɛ bɩncɛ ʊ, kan sokʊma bɩncɛ kɩ. 
Ŋ n'ɩ zonno hɔ yɔrɛ ŋ nɩ, ŋn'a mɩŋŋabaa hɩnkam kan yaadarɛrɔ jintoo kɩ. Ŋ hʊnhʊrlɛ yɩm maam-maam.» 
Yeezuu nyɩnta an a meer nya busoo daʊrɛ bɩncɛ bɩ rɛ, an nɩ dɩgam gʊɔɔ wɔɔ busoo bɩ daʊrɛ ma. Arzakazannɔ wɔɔ barla gɔsɩnnɔ y'a daŋ ʊ gʊta. 
Zoro n bɩ tanka hɩɩya zar ʊ. 
Yeezuu n a karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ bir, an a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «Sɩra mɔɔ n'a hɩrɛ awɔɔ m, zoro ŋʊazaa naa do busoo bɩ zar ʊ gʊta, an lɛ da gʊaa haay k'a n'a daŋ ʊ bɩ ra. 
Bala, ŋ haay busoo k'a zibəə ba ŋ ma bɩ, ŋ y'a da ʊ. Bɩ zoro bɩ kan a cicir bɩ kɩ, a a mɩsɩrbaa hɔ sa haay, an a da ʊ.»
