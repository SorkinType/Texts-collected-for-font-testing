Matiyee.
17.
Dɔmɩm sɔrdɩ kur bɩ, Yeezuu Pɩyɛɛr gaa, kan Zakkɩ kɩ a danzaa Zaan kɩ, an bɔ ŋ nɩ toore ma a minto, ci jintə mim ʊ. 
A n a lʊr ŋ mɩm ʊ. A meer nɩ yɔm am wosogure hɔ bɩ m, a huuro wɔɔ nɩ nyakɩ bɔm, ŋn'a ba fu fɩrr…. 
*Elii n a hɩnka ŋ nɩ kan Moyiisi kɩ, ŋn'ɩ meer bam kan Yeezuu kɩ. 
Pɩyɛɛr n a hɩ Yeezuu m, a ʊ: «Zuuba, kʊ wɔɔ ɩ naa ʊ, a mɩŋŋa. K'ɩ lɛ taa, kʊ m bur ba kaakʊ: ɩbɩɩ hɔ deem, Moyiisi hɔ deem, Elii hɔ deem.» 
K'a nɩ meer barɛ ncɩnaaʊ bɩ, warkʊ k'a nɩ yɔm bʊr an b'a ku ŋ la. Meerbaa n a to warkʊ bɩ nɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «Ba mɔɔ Nyɩ m na. Mɔɔ ŋʊa gʊta. A mɔɔ nɔ yɩ paan…. K'a a tʊr ka ma.» 
Karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ kʊ ŋ naa ma bɩ, ŋ y'a dundo, ŋn'a meer kunku tara ma, dabɔʊ gʊta k'a ŋ nyasʊ bɩ minto. 
Yeezuu n a zɔ ŋ ma, an a wɔ da ŋ ma, an a hɩ, a ʊ: «K'a wuti, a da bɩ bɔ ʊ y!» 
Kʊ ŋ y'a bɔ ŋnɩ dɩga bɩ, ŋ n gɔsɩ yɩ dɔ, kʊ Yeezuu y'a deem bɛɛ y. 
Kʊ ŋ n'ɩ zerm ci bɩ ra bɩ, a lɛ ka ŋ ʊ, a ʊ: «Hɔ k'awɔɔ yɩ naa, a b'a hɩ gɔsɩ nɩ y, k'an ta, an Gʊaanyɩ bɩ mɩmbɔʊrɛ ku gəəno bire ʊ.» 
A karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ n a lar, ŋ ʊ: «Bɔ m wosocikarɩnsaambɔɔ wɔɔ ʊ, Elii lɛɛkarɛ an bʊr?» 
Yeezuu n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «Sɩra m, Elii nɩ lɛɛkarɛ an bʊr k'a bɩ hɔ haay banka. 
Sɩra mɔɔ n'a hɩrɛ awɔɔ m, Elii bʊr kara, bɩ ŋ n a dɔ y, a hɔɔnhɔ ŋ y'a ba m. Gʊaanyɩ bɩ fɩr yɩrɛ ŋ wɔ ʊ maam sɔ.» 
A m naa, karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ n a dɔ, ŋ ʊ, Zaan-Batiisi an n'a hɩm ma. 
Kʊ ŋ lee ku zamaa cir gʊta bɩ zi bɩ, gʊaa a zɔ Yeezuu ma, an kukurə zɛ a taa, an a hɩ m, a ʊ: 
«Karɩnsaamba, m nyɩnnɩ cicir zɛ, hɔɔ n'a zɛm, an nɩ fɩr yɩm gʊta, a tɩr a da sɛ ʊ, gɛɛ hi ʊ. 
Mɔɔ n bʊr m ɩ karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ zi, bɩ, ŋ n a da ma, ŋn'a waa y.» 
Yeezuu n meerbaa bɩ sa, a ʊ: «Zamaana sɩrakarɛwar zannɔ, gʊɔɔ bʊnyaarɔ, mɔɔ dɔmɩm bam karɔ kan awɔɔ kɩ? Dɔmɩm karɔ mɔɔ nɩ m yarɛ, m yɛ kɔrɛ ma awɔɔ minto? K'a bʊr m mɔɔ zi.» 
Yeezuu n həən si zinə bɩ ma, an bɔ nyɩ bɩ mɛ ʊ, nyɩ bɩ n laafɩɩ yɩ wakatɩ bɩ do ʊ. 
Karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ n a zɔ Yeezuu ma, ŋn'a lar a deem, ŋ ʊ: «Bɔ y'a ka wɔɔ n ʊ da ma, ʊ la zinə bɩ ra?» 
A n a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «Awɔɔ sɩrakarɛ k'an poore bɩ m. Bala, sɩra mɔɔ n'a hɩrɛ awɔɔ m, k'awɔɔ sɩrakarɛ bɔ kʊ ʊ kan mutardɩ yaa kɩ, awɔɔ y'a hɩm ci naa do m, a ʊ: wuti, ɩ ta lɛɛ naa do ʊ! A wutire an ta, awɔɔ bɩ jɩram hɔsɩ barɛ ra y.  
Bɩ, zinə ncɩnaaʊ bɩ, hɔ ba ʊ an b'a dam ma an a ka, an bɔ, kʊ yaadarɛ bɛɛ kan lɛkʊsɩrɛ kɩ y.» 
Hindeem ʊ, karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ kʊ ŋ y'a so kʊ ma haay Galilee ʊ bɩ, Yeezuu a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «N Gʊaanyɩ bɩ dam gʊɔɔ wɔ ʊ. 
Ŋ y'a zɛm, b'a dɔmɩm kaakʊ hinni ʊ, Woso y'a mɩm bɔŋ ʊ.» Meerbaa naa do ŋ heer zar gʊta. 
Kʊ Yeezuu kan a karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ kɩ lee ku Kapɛrnawum ʊ bɩ, Wosocɛ yawʊrsinno a zɔ Pɩyɛɛr ma, ŋn'a lar, ŋ ʊ: «Awɔɔ karɩnsaamba bɩ bɩr Wosocɛ yawʊr kam na?» 
Pɩyɛɛr n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «A y'a kam.» Pɩyɛɛr k'a lee ku har ʊ bɩ, Yeezuu lɛɛ ka m an a lar, a ʊ: «Sɩmɔɔn, kan ɩbɩɩ kɩ, nkɔɔ durnya cinno nɔɔm nɩ yawʊr sire ŋ han? Ŋ nyɩnɔ m gɛɛ, saanɔɔ nɩ?» 
Pɩyɛɛr n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «Saanɔɔ m.» Yeezuu n a hɩ m, a ʊ: «K'a maam ŋ nyɩnɔ wɔɔ ba ŋ barla y. 
Bɩ k'ʊ lɛ baa kʊ ŋ n'a hɩ, ŋ ʊ, ʊ ʊ zar a ma bɩ, ta higʊta b'ʊ, ɩ yaa ka zo hi b'ʊ. Zɔ niŋŋə k'a nɩ lɛɛ kam an ka bɩ mom bɩ, bɔ zɔ bɩ do m, ɩ lɛ gʊ, ɩbɩɩ busoo yɩrɛ a lɛ b'ʊ, an nɩ mɔɔ kan ɩbɩɩ kɩ yawʊr karɛ bɔm. K'ɩ bɔ ʊ, ɩ b'a ka yawʊrsinno wɔɔ ʊ.»
