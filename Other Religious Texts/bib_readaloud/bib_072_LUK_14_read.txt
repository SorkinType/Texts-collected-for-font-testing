Likki.
14.
Yeezuu gasʊ farizɛɛnrɔ jɩtaarɛ deem har ʊ, *sabaa hinni ʊ, k'a hɔbɩrɛ bɩ. Farizɛɛnrɔ wɔɔ nɩ dundom zi. 
Gʊaa deem ɩ bɩ jɩm Yeezuu lɛɛ, a mɛ n hɩnhanrɛ. 
Yeezuu n a hɩ wosocikarɩnsaambɔɔ wɔɔ m kan farizɛɛnrɔ wɔɔ kɩ, a ʊ: «Zaa ta ʊ k'ʊ yaabazaa waa sabaa hinni ʊ gɛɛ, zaa ba ʊ y?» 
Ŋ nɩ zɛ pi. Kɛɛrɛ bɩ, Yeezuu n yaabazaa bɩ gaa, an a waa, an a da zaa ra. 
Bɩ kur bɩ, an a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «Gʊaa bɩ kara n'awɔɔ bire ʊ naa ʊ, a nyɩ, gɛɛ, a der n a da hiyəə ʊ sabaa hinni ʊ, a bɩ bra sire, an a bɔ ʊ lɛɛm nɩ?» 
Bɩ ŋ n a da ma ŋn'a nyɩ bɔ ʊ y. 
Yeezuu k'a gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ yɩ ŋ bir hɔbɩrɛ ma rɔ wɔɔ yɩ, ŋn'ɩ lɛɛkarɛ bɩncɛrɔ bʊmbɔŋ ʊ bɩ, a hɔbɔkʊrɛnoom naa do da ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: 
«Kʊ gʊaa ɩbɩɩ bir harcɩntarɛ cibsa ma, ɩ b'aa nyɩnta lɛɛkarɛ bɩncɛ ʊ y. Bala, a a da ma, a gʊaa vanta bir, an lɛ da ɩbɩɩ ra. 
Gʊaa k'awɔɔ bir bɩ, a b'a ka, an b'a hɩ awɔɔ gʊaa hɩɩya bɩ m, a ʊ: toore ka ɩ mim b'ʊ y. Ɩbɩɩ zɛm bɩ wuti kan nyannɩ kɩ, ɩ ta jɛ bɩncɛ ʊ. 
Bɩ kʊ n y'ɩbɩɩ bir hɔbɩrɛ ma, k'ɩ yaa nyɩnta jɛ bɩncɛ ʊ, gʊaa k'a ɩbɩɩ bir bɩ, k'a bɩ bʊr, a y'a hɩrɛ ɩbɩɩ m, a ʊ: M hannyɩ, ɩ zɔ lɛɛ bɩncɛ b'ʊ. A nyɩntarɛ ɩbɩɩ tɔ busure gʊaa haay kʊ ŋ nɩ hɔ bɩrɛ kan ɩbɩɩ kɩ rɔ kɩ lɛɛ. 
Bala, gʊaa k'a n'a mɩŋŋa busum bɩ, n y'a zerm, bɔ n'a mɩŋŋa zerm bɩ, n y'a busum.» 
A n a hɩ gʊaa k'a a bir bɩ m sɔ, a ʊ: «K'ɩbɩɩ sinuu hɔbɩrɛ ba gɛɛ gunuu hɔ, ɩ b'ɩ hannyɩnɔ, gɛɛ, ɩ dogʊɔɔ, gɛɛ ɩ cɩsɔnnɔ arzakazannɔ kɩ bir y. Kʊ n bɛɛ, ŋ y'ɩbɩɩ birm sɔ a zɩm ma, ŋn'a so a yaa ʊ. 
Bɩ, k'ɩ cibsa hɔbɩrɛ ba, k'ɩ cicirzannɔ, paŋŋawarzannɔ, lʊgɔrɔ, bʊʊrɔ kɩ bir, 
b'a nyɩntam mimbirenyɩnta kan ɩbɩɩ kɩ, bala, ŋ b'a dam ma ŋ n a ba, ŋ n a so a yaa ʊ y. Bala, Woso naa sompire ɩbɩɩ m, gʊɔɔ tɩrgɔɔ mɩmbɔʊrɛ dɔmɩm ʊ.» 
Gʊaa deem k'a nyɩnta b'ʊ an naa haay ma bɩ, a a hɩ Yeezuu m, a ʊ: «Mimbirenyɩntazaa m gʊaa k'a nɩ zɛm bɩ hɔbɩrɛ bɩ Woso cirbəə nɔ ʊ bɩ m.» 
Yeezuu n a hɩ m, a ʊ: «Gʊaa deem ɩ nyɩnta an cibsa gʊta hɔbɩrɛ ba. A n zamaa bir gʊta kʊ ŋ nɩ b'a bɩ. 
Hɔbɩrɛ bɩ dɔmɩm k'a ku bɩ, a a zibəənyɩ nyɔɔ gʊɔɔ k'a ŋ bir hɔbɩrɛ bɩ ma rɔ wɔɔ zi, a ʊ: K'a bʊr kɛɛrɛ, n hɔ haay banka, an nya. 
Ŋ haay n a lɛ ba deem, ŋ ʊ: a b'a dam ma y. Lɛɛkarɛ zaa bɩ n a hɩ zibəənyɩ bɩ m, a ʊ: hɔsɩra mɔɔ si, pakra m kʊ m ta, m aa dɩga a. Mɔɔ y'a yɛrɛ ɩbɩɩ han, k'ɩ sugur ba m ma. 
A vanta n a hɩ, a ʊ: Dono mɔɔ n si bu, kʊ m aa nɩ ŋ makra ba ŋ dɩga a. Mɔɔ y'a yɛrɛ ɩbɩɩ han k'ɩ sugur ba m ma. 
A vanta n a ya, an a hɩ, a ʊ: lʊ mɔɔ sa daa, bɩ y'a ka mɔɔ bɩ m dam ma, m bʊr y. 
Zibəənyɩ bɩ k'a a wusigə bɩ, a yaa naa hɩ a harzaa bɩ m. Harzaa bɩ nɔ n ma ʊ, an a hɩ, a ʊ: ta zɔɔ-zɔɔ kʊ bɩ daasɩrɔ ʊ, kan kʊ bɩ zaa bire rɔ kɩ, ɩ cicirzannɔ gaa kan paŋŋawarzannɔ kɩ, lʊgɔrɔ kɩ, bʊʊrɔ kɩ ɩ bʊr ŋ nɩ naa ʊ. 
Zibəənyɩ bɩ n ta an a bɔ an bʊr an b'a hɩ, a ʊ: harzaa, ʊ lɛ k'ɩbɩɩ ka bɩ ba, bɩ lɛ gɔɔta. 
Harzaa bɩ n a hɩ zibəənyɩ bɩ m, a ʊ: ta zaa poorero wɔɔ ra kan brikəro kɩ, ɩ ɩ ka gʊɔɔ wɔɔ ma ɩ gasʊ ŋ nɩ, kʊ lɛ bɩ gɔɔta mɔɔ har b'ʊ y. 
Bala, a hɩ mɔɔ nawʊrɛ awɔɔ m, gʊɔɔ kʊ mɔɔ n bir lɔ wɔɔ, gʊaa deem taan… ba ʊ an nɩ mɔɔ hɔbɩrɛ bɩ darɛ a lɛ ʊ y.» 
Bɩ jɛ ʊ, gʊɔɔ cir gʊta zu Yeezuu ʊ. A n a bɔ an a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: 
«Kʊ gʊaa bʊr mɔɔ zi, a n mɔɔ ŋʊa an lɛ da a zɩ ra, kan a da kɩ, a lʊ kɩ, a nyɩnɔ kɩ, a cɛmbɔɔ kɩ, a danlʊrɔ kɩ, baa kan a mɩŋŋa mɩsɩrbaa kɩ, a b'a dam ma an nyɩnta mɔɔ karɩndanyɩ y. 
Gʊaa k'a bɩr a gɔdarkʊra busure an nɩ zum mɔɔ jɛ ʊ, a b'a dam ma an nyɩnta mɔɔ karɩndanyɩ y. 
Kʊ sɩra nɩ, gʊaa bɩ kara n'awɔɔ bire ʊ a lɛ n taa k'a cɛ gʊta dɔ, a n lɛɛ ka an nyɩnta, an busoo k'a n'a bɩm bɩ lɛ zɛ, an a dɔ, k'a cɛ bɩ dɔm gɛ ra? 
Kʊ bɩ bɛɛ, k'a lɛɛ ka an a jɩr cɩnta b'a b'a dam ma an a nyambʊr y, gʊɔɔ haay kʊ ŋ n'a yɩm bɩ, ŋ yaa wʊm ma, 
ŋnɩ bɩr a hɩrɛ, ŋ ʊ: Ba gʊaa cɛ dɔrɛ sɩŋŋɩda sa na, b'a n a nyambʊr na. 
Gɛɛ cir bɔ n ta ʊ, k'a ɩ tam k'a yaa yar ba kan cir vanta kɩ, a n lɛɛ ka an nyɩnta an dɩga, k'a y'a dam ma kan gʊaa tisi bu kɩ, a yar ba kan gʊaa k'a nɩ gʊaa m tisi buhɩra bɩ kɩ gɛ? 
Kʊ bɩ bɛɛ, cir deem bɩ k'a gɔɔta an laatʊ bɩ, a gʊaa deem nyɔɔ n zi, an aa laafɩɩ mim dɩnda kan kɩ. 
Ncɩnaaʊ sɔ bɩ, awɔɔ bire ʊ kʊ gʊaa lɛ baa k'a wɔ saa hɔ k'an ta m bɩ ma, a b'a dam ma an nyɩnta mɔɔ karɩndanyɩ y.» 
«Sɩra m, yɛ k'an naa, hɔ mɩŋŋa m. Bɩ kʊ yɛ nyɩntabaa bɩ bɔ, bɔ n'a yarɛ an bʊr a nyɩntabaa bɩ nɩ? 
A bɩ mɩŋŋa tara minto, b'a bɩ yɩm buundi sɔ y. A lo n nawʊm garga ra. Gʊaa k'a nɩ tʊr m k'an a ma.»
