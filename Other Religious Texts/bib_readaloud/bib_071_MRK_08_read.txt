Markɩ.
8.
Dɔmɩnnɔ wɔɔ duro ʊ bɩ, zamaa gʊta a ya, an ta ʊ, bɩ hɔbɩrɛ ba ʊ kʊ ŋ n a bɩ y. Yeezuu n a karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ bir, an a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: 
«Zamaa bɩ cicir ɩ mɔɔ zɛm, bala, ŋ dɔmɩm kaakʊ mɔɔ zi, bɩ, hɔbɩrɛ ba ŋ ʊ y. 
Kʊ mɔɔ ŋ da zaa ra kan nɔ kɩ, ŋ y'a dundom zaa ra, bɩ gɔsɩnnɔ a to laatʊ.» 
Karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ n a nyɩ bɔ nʊ, ŋ ʊ: «K'ʊ bur yɩ ka poohɔɔn ʊ naa ʊ, ʊ a ka ŋ ʊ, ŋn'a bɩ k'an ŋ ka?» 
Yeezuu n ŋ lar, a ʊ: «Bur karɔ n ta awɔɔ ʊ?» Ŋ n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, ŋ ʊ: «Saanhɩna» 
A n lɛ ka, a ʊ, ŋ n nyɩnta tara ʊ. A n bur saanhɩnarɔ wɔɔ gʊr, an a barka da ʊ, an n'a zɩnzɛ ra, an n'a kam a karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ ʊ, ŋn'a kam zamaa b'ʊ. 
Zɔnnyɩnɔ nyɩnta ŋ ʊ sɔ. Yeezuu n ŋ barka da ʊ, a ʊ: ŋ n a ka ŋ ʊ sɔ. 
Ŋ n a bɩ, an ŋ ka. Ŋ n a kuuro gʊr kasɩ lɛ ma saanhɩna. 
Gʊɔɔ wɔɔ nyɩnta, ŋn'a zɔ tisi si ma. Bɩ jɛ ʊ bɩ, Yeezuu ŋ da zaa ra. 
Lɛɛm bɩ, a da kɔɔlʊʊ b'ʊ kan a karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ kɩ, ŋn'ɩ tam Dalmanutaa zeezi. 
Farizɛɛnrɔ bʊr, ŋn'a sɩŋŋɩda ba, ŋn'ɩ bɩsɩ kam kan Yeezuu kɩ, ŋn'a yɛ han, k'an hoser kʊ Woso a ka ʊ bɩ ba, k'a dɩga a, a y'a dam ma, an a ba gɛ. 
Yeezuu hoon gʊta si, an n'a hɩrɛ, a ʊ: «Bɔ minto her zamaana naa nɩ hoser yɛrɛ? Sɩra mɔɔ n'a hɩrɛ awɔɔ m, hoser ba ʊ mɔɔ n n'a bam awɔɔ zamaana naa do minto y.» 
A n a gʊ farizɛɛnrɔ wɔɔ ma, an aa da kɔɔlʊʊ b'ʊ, an bra gəm deem bɩ ra. 
Hinni bɩ do ʊ bɩ, bur sarɛ tɔ bɔ karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ nɔ ʊ. Bur deem bala nɩ ŋ ʊ kɔɔlʊʊ bɩ nɔ ʊ. 
Yeezuu lɛ ka ŋ ʊ, a ʊ: «K'a dundo a zi, k'a ba laatʊ farizɛɛnrɔ nyʊnyɔɔ bɩ ma, kan Hɩrɔɔdɩ hɔ bɩ kɩ ma sɔ.» 
Ŋ n bɔkarɛ, ŋn'ɩ bɩsɩ kam kan kʊ kɩ, kʊ bur ba ŋ ʊ bɩ minto. 
Yeezuu n a ma, an n'a hɩrɛ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «Bɔ bɩsɩ awɔɔ n'a karɛ? Bur k'a ba awɔɔ ʊ bɩ minto m gɛ? Awɔɔ hɔɔn gɔɔta a n gu k'a jɩ dɔ ra? Awɔɔ heer ɩ ka gɛ? 
Awɔɔ mɩnyaarɔ kʊ ŋ nɩ ta ʊ bɩ, ŋ bɩr lɛɛ yɩm na? Awɔɔ tʊʊrɔ kʊ ŋ nɩ ta ʊ bɩ, ŋ bɩr mim mam na? 
Kʊ mɔɔ bur soor zɩnzɛ ra, m a ka gʊaa tisi soor ʊ bɩ, a tɔ ba awɔɔ nɔ ʊ ra? Awɔɔ kuuro gʊr kasɩ lɛ ma karɔ?» Ŋ n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, ŋ ʊ: «Bueerehɩɩya.» 
«Bɩ kʊ mɔɔ bur saanhɩna zɩnzɛ ra m a ka gʊaa tisi si ʊ bɩ, kasɩ lɛ ma karɔ awɔɔ kuuro gʊr ʊ?» Ŋ n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, ŋ ʊ: «Saanhɩna.» 
A n a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «Awɔɔ gɔɔta a n a dɔ, a ʊ, bur mim bɛɛ mɔɔ n'a hɩm ma ra?» 
Ŋ lee ku Bɛtsayɩdaa ʊ, n n bʊr bʊʊ m Yeezuu zi, n n a yɛ han, k'an a wɔ da bʊʊ bɩ ma, k'an lɛɛ yɩ. 
A n bʊʊ bɩ gaa a wɔ ma, an bɔ m bɔlɛɛma, an lihi da a mɩm ma, an a wɔ ka a mim la, an n'a larm, a ʊ: «Ɩbɩɩ ɩ hɔsɩ yɩm na?» 
K'a mɩm bɩ gʊ bɩ, a a hɩ, a ʊ: «Mɔɔ ɩ gʊɔɔ yɩm, ɩ ʊ, gɔrɔ m, ŋn'ɩ tarɛ.» 
Bɩ, Yeezuu a ya, an a wɔ ka gʊaa bɩ mɩnyaarɔ wɔɔ ra. Gʊaa bɩ nɩ dɩgam ma sɩɩ…, an a waa, an nɩ lɛɛ yɩm mɩŋŋa. 
Yeezuu n a da a har zaa ra, a ʊ: «Ɩ b'a ka ɩ gasʊ kʊnɔ b'ʊ y!» 
Yeezuu ɩ tam kan a karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ kɩ Filipi-Sezaaree kʊrɔ jɩnjɛɛnnɔ wɔɔ nɔ ʊ. Zaa ra bɩ, a a karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ lar, a ʊ: «Gʊɔɔ wɔɔ y'a hɩm, ŋ ʊ, nka m mɔɔ nɩ?» 
Ŋ n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, ŋ ʊ: «Gɔsɩnnɔ ʊ: Zaan-Batiisi m, nawɔnnɔ ʊ: *Elii m, gɔsɩnnɔ ʊ: wosolɛsinnɩsorazaa deem m.» 
Yeezuu n ŋ lar, a ʊ: «B'awɔɔ, awɔɔ y'a hɩrɛ, a ʊ: Nka m mɔɔ nɩ?» Pɩyɛɛr n meerbaa bɩ sa, an a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «Krista n'ɩbɩɩ m.» 
A n lɛ ka ŋ ʊ mɩŋŋa, a ʊ: ŋ b'awɔɔ mim bɩ hɩ gɔsɩ nɩ y. 
Kɛɛrɛ bɩ, a n a sɩŋŋɩda ba, an n'a dɩndarɛ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: pakra m, kʊ Gʊaanyɩ bɩ n cicir ba gʊta. Bɩ, gʊɔɔgusinno kan wosocɛmannɩbənno jɩtannɔ kɩ, kan wosocikarɩnsaambɔɔ kɩ kaanɩm nɩ, an ta kan a zɛrɛ kɩ, k'an a mɩm bɔ ʊ dɔmɩm kaakʊ hinni ʊ. 
A n minno nɔɔn duro nyaakʊm da an n'a hɩrɛ y. Pɩyɛɛr n a gaa an bɔ m toore ma, an nɩ nyaa bam kan Yeezuu kɩ, a ʊ: a n a ga m, an naa hɩ y. 
Bɩ, Yeezuu k'a wusigə an dɩga a karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ ma bɩ, a həən si Pɩyɛɛr ma, a ʊ: «Ɩ jɛ bɔ mɔɔ jɩr ʊ! Sʊtaana, ɩbɩɩ hɔɔndarɛ bɩ n bɔ kʊ ʊ kan Woso hɔ kɩ y. Gʊɔɔ hɔɔndarɛ m.» 
Yeezuu n bɔkarɛ, an a karɩndanyɩnɔ bir kan zamaa bɩ kɩ, an a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «Kʊ gʊaa lɛ taa k'a zu mɔɔ ʊ, k'an a mɩŋŋa lɛtaamarɔ to, k'an a gɔdarkʊra busu, an zu mɔɔ ʊ. 
Bala, gʊaa k'a a lɛ n taa k'a meeyaa bʊmbɔ bɩ, a b'a meeyaa bʊmbɔrɛ yɩrɛ y. Bɩ, kʊ gʊaa ga mɔɔ tɔ ma, kan lemim nyɩnta bɩ kɩ tɔ ma, a y'a meeyaa bʊmbɔrɛ yɩrɛ. 
Bɩ, kʊ gʊaa a so durnya hɔ haay ʊ, an bɔkarɛ, an ga, a nyɔɔdʊ ɩ ka? 
Bɔ gʊaa bɩ n n'a sam, k'a mɩsɩrbaa k'a nyarɛ ba ʊ bɩ yɩ nɩ? 
Bala, gʊaa k'a nɩ nyannɩ zɛm mɔɔ tɔ ma kan mɔɔ lemim nyɩnta bɩ kɩ tɔ ma, her zamaana jaandənno nɔɔn duro kan mimbʊnyaabənno nɔɔm kɩ bireyaa ʊ, Gʊaanyɩ bɩ b'a zaa nyannɩ zɛm, dɔmɩm k'a nɩ zɛŋ ʊ, kan malɛɛkarɔ mɩŋŋɔɔ kɩ, Woso lɛbɔʊrɛ nɔ ʊ kɩ b'ʊ y.»
