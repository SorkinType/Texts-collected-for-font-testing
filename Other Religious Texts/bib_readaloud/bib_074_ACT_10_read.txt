Zibəəzannɔ Ci.
10.
Gʊaa ɩ nyɩnta Sezaree ʊ, a tɔ m Kɔrnɛy. Sʊrdaa zɔrɔ lɛɛzaa m. Sʊrdaarɔ zɔrsɔddɩ zɛkʊra kʊ n n'a birm «Italik» bɩ barla an ta ʊ. 
Gʊaa m an sɩra ka Woso ʊ paan…, an nɩ nyi bam Woso m kan a har zannɔ kɩ haay. A y'a dam zɩɩfʊrɔ cicirzannɔ gʊtɔɔ han, an nɩ yaa dam Woso m dɔmɩm haay. 
Hindeem ʊ, wakatɩ kaakʊ zeezi yirbəə n bɩ, a Woso malɛɛka yɩ paan… Makra nɔ ʊ, an gasʊ a har ʊ, an n'a birm, a ʊ: «Kɔrnɛy!» 
Kɔrnɛy n dɩga ma sɩɩ…, dabɔʊ n a nyasʊ, an a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «A lɔ, Zuuba?» Malɛɛka bɩ n a hɩ m, a ʊ: «Ɩbɩɩ yaadarɛrɔ kan ɩ zibəəro mɩŋŋɔɔ k'ɩbɩɩ ba rɔ wɔɔ kɩ minto, Woso a hɔɔn da ɩbɩɩ ma. 
Bɩ, naa ma bɩ, gʊɔɔ nyɔɔ Zoppee ʊ kʊ ŋ n'aa Sɩmɔɔn, gʊaa niŋŋə kʊ n so ra, n n'a tɔ ka Pɩyɛɛr bɩ gaa ŋnɩ bʊr m. 
Zer a ʊ Sɩmɔɔn vanta k'an zappa bɩ har ʊ higʊta lɛ ra.» 
Malɛɛka k'a meer ba kan kɩ bɩ k'a nyaam bɩ, Kɔrnɛy a zibəənyɩnɔ bir hɩɩya kan a sʊrdaarɔ gʊaa deem k'a sɩra ka Woso ʊ paan… bɩ kɩ, 
an mim haay k'a ba bɩ dɩnda ŋ nɩ, an ŋ nyɔɔ Zoppee ʊ. 
Bɩ dɔ k'a mɛ bɩ, kʊ ŋ zaa sa ŋn'ɩ tam bɩ, ŋ y'a zɔ kʊ bɩ ma. Bɩ ma bɩ, Pɩyɛɛr der cɛ bɩ bɛŋŋʊ ra k'a yaa da. Nyɩnta a ʊ sinuu zeezi. 
Bɩ jɛ ʊ, nɔ a nyasʊ, an nyɩnta an a ŋʊa k'a hɔbɩrɛ bɩ. N nɩ hɔbɩrɛ bam k'a ka ʊ. Lɛɛm bɩ, a hɔhɩnkarɛ yɩ. 
A n brama yɩ an a gʊ, hɔ niŋŋə n a to brama, an bɔ kʊ ʊ kan pɛɛra gʊta kɩ, n n a bɔ kʊ ʊ, an b'a cɩnta tara ʊ. 
A nɔ ʊ bɩ, hɔmɩsɩr do haay n ta ʊ: hɔ gam si, bɔɔ n n'a gaatam tara ʊ, kan bɔɔ n nɩ werm brama rɔ wɔɔ kɩ. 
Leer n a hɩ m, a ʊ: «Pɩyɛɛr, aya, ŋ zɩnzɛ, ɩ ŋ bɩ!» Pɩyɛɛr n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: 
«Ayo Zuuba, mɔɔ mɩsɩrbaa nɔ ʊ, mɔɔ n hɔ bʊnyaa kan dintim hɔ kɩ bɩ cɩna y. Wɔɔ do sɩhɔ m.» 
Leer n a ya, an a hɩ m a hɩɩya hɔ ma, a ʊ: «Hɔ kʊ Woso dintim gʊ ma bɩ, ɩ b'a bir dintim hɔ y.» 
Naa do ba gallɛm kaakʊ, hɔ bɩ n a bɔ, an ta brama lɛɛm. 
Pɩyɛɛr n a mɩŋŋa lar a hɔɔn ʊ gallɛm hɔka hɔhɩnkarɛ naa do jɩ ma. Lɛɛm bɩ, Kɔrnɛy zibəəzannɔ kʊ ŋ laaka da jɩsa kan bɩsɩ kɩ Sɩmɔɔn har ma rɔ wɔɔ lee ku hallɛ ma. 
Ŋ nɩ bir darɛ, k'a a dɔ kʊ Sɩmɔɔn kʊ n so ra, n n a tɔ ka Pɩyɛɛr bɩ, zer a ʊ har bɩ do ʊ gɛ. 
Pɩyɛɛr n gɔɔta an n'a hɔɔn dam hɔhɩnkarɛ k'a a yɩ bɩ ma. Sɛnt-Ɛspri n a hɩ m, a ʊ: «Ba gʊɔɔ kaakʊrɔ n n'a karɛ ɩ ma na. 
Zer zɔɔ-zɔɔ ɩ zaa sa kan ŋ kɩ, ɩ bɩ bɩsɩ ka y. Bala, mɔɔ yɩ ŋ nyɔɔ.» 
Pɩyɛɛr n zer, an gʊɔɔ wɔɔ zɛ ʊ, an a hɩ, a ʊ: «Mɔɔ ba n nɩ. Mɔɔ awɔɔ n'a karɛ m ma bɩ. Bɔ minto y'a ka awɔɔ n bʊr naa ʊ?» 
Ŋ n'a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, ŋ ʊ: «Sʊrdaa zɔrɔ lɛɛzaa Kɔrnɛy k'an gʊaa tɩrga, an nɩ nyi bam Woso m, a tɔ n nɩ lɛbɔʊrɛ yɩm zɩɩfʊrɔ do barla bɩ, Woso malɛɛka a hɩnka m, a ʊ, an a ka, k'ɩbɩɩ m bʊr a har ʊ, k'a b'a tʊr ka ɩ ma.» 
Pɩyɛɛr n ŋ si, an a ka ŋnɩ gasʊ, an wʊtaʊrɛcɛ ka ŋ ʊ. Bɩ, dɔ k'a mɛ bɩ, a cem kan ŋ kɩ, kan Zoppee danyɩnɔ gɔsɩnnɔ kɩ. 
Ŋ lee ku Sezaree ʊ, dɔmɩm hɩɩya hinni ʊ. Kɔrnɛy n a banka, an nɩ dɔrɛ ŋ ma. A a do gʊɔɔ bir kan a gʊaasɩbɔɔ paan…rɔ wɔɔ kɩ. 
Dɔmɩm kʊ Pɩyɛɛr lee ku ma bɩ, Kɔrnɛy wuti, an a zɛ ʊ, an sʊnta a taa, an a da ʊ. 
Pɩyɛɛr n a wɔ dɔrɛ, an a busu brama, an n'a hɩrɛ, a ʊ: «Wuti! Gʊaa m mɔɔ m sɔ.» 
Ŋ n sʊʊra ba kan kʊ kɩ, ŋn'ɩ gasʊm. K'a bɩ sʊr gʊɔɔ ma, ŋn'a zɛ kʊ ra gʊta bɩ, 
a a hɩ, a ʊ: «Awɔɔ dɔ a ʊ a cisə ba kan zɩɩfʊ kɩ k'an a zɛ kʊ ra kan saana kɩ, gɛɛ an gasʊ a har ʊ ra! Bɩ, Woso a hɩnka mɔɔ m, a ʊ, mɔɔ n m ga m, kʊ m dɩga gɔsɩ ma, m a ba hɔ bʊnyaa gɛɛ dintim zaa y. 
Bɩ y'a ka, k'ɩbɩɩ mɔɔ bir bɩ, mɔɔ n bɩsɩ ka zɛrɛ ma y. Bɩ naa ma bɩ, mɔɔ lɛ n taa kʊ m m birle bɩ jɩ dɔ.» 
Kɔrnɛy n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «A dɔmɩm kaakʊ her, ncɩnaaʊ nyi m, mɔɔ n nɩ yaa darɛ m har ʊ yirbəə wakatɩ kaakʊ ma. Lɛɛm bɩ, gʊaa n a hɩnka mɔɔ lɛɛ, a huuro n nɩ gum, 
an a hɩ mɔɔ m, a ʊ: Kɔrnɛy, n y'ɩbɩɩ yaadarɛ si, bɩ Woso ɩbɩɩ ganwʊrɛrɔ k'ɩbɩɩ ŋ ka cicirzannɔ ʊ rɔ wɔɔ tɔ da a nɔ ʊ. 
Bɩ minto bɩ, gʊaa nyɔɔ Zoppee ʊ k'an aa gʊaa k'a tɔ m Sɩmɔɔn n nɩ so ra, n n a tɔ ka Pɩyɛɛr bɩ bir, k'an bʊr naa ʊ. Zer a ʊ Zoppee zappa k'a tɔ m Sɩmɔɔn bɩ har ʊ, higʊta lɛ ra. 
Bɩ y'a ka, mɔɔ n nyɔɔ da lɛɛm kʊ n n'ɩbɩɩ bir, ɩbɩɩ yɛ sɔ, k'ɩ bʊr wɔɔ zi. Ba wɔɔ n'ɩbɩɩ taa naa ʊ haay na, k'ʊ ʊ tʊr ka hɔ haay kʊ Zuuba a hɩ ɩbɩɩ m k'ɩ hɩ wɔɔ m bɩ ma.» 
Pɩyɛɛr n bɔkarɛ, an a lɛ gʊ, an a hɩ, a ʊ: «Sɩra m, mɔɔ dɔ kɛɛrɛ mɔɔ ʊ, Woso bɩr gʊɔɔ jirə bɔrɛ y. 
Bɩ do haay nɔ ʊ, gʊaa k'a nɩ nyi bam nɩ, an nɩ tɩrgabaa zi bam bɩ, a y'a sire a jɩr ʊ. 
Woso a mɩŋŋa lemim nyɔɔ Israyɛl zannɔ ma, an Yeezuu Krista k'an gʊɔɔ haay Zuuba bɩ laafɩɩ lemim nyɩnta bɩ hɩ. 
Awɔɔ hɔ niŋŋə k'a ba Zidee kʊ haay ʊ bɩ dɔ. A sɩŋŋɩda a sa Galilee ʊ Zaan batɛm lemim hɩrɛ bɩ jɛ ʊ. 
Nazarɛtɩ Yeezuu bɩ do, awɔɔ dɔ a ʊ, bɩ Woso a bɔ ʊ, an Sɛnt-Ɛspri kan a paŋŋa kɩ zer a mim ʊ. A bɔ lɛɛ haay ʊ, an mɩŋŋabaa zi ba, an nɩ gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ nɩ Sʊtaana lɔmbaa nɔ ʊ rɔ wɔɔ waam, bala, Woso ɩ kan kɩ. 
A zibəə haay k'a a ba zɩɩfʊrɔ kʊ ʊ, kan Zerizalɛm kɩ ʊ bɩ, a kasɛtɩzannɔ m wɔɔ m. Ŋ y'a zeŋŋə gɔ ma, ŋn'a zɛ gər. 
Woso a mɩm bɔ ʊ a dɔmɩm kaakʊ hɔ ma, an lɛ ka ʊ k'an a taʊrɛ hɩnka. 
Gʊaa haay bɛɛ a a hɩnka nɩ y, a hɩnka a ʊ kasɛtɩzannɔ kʊ Woso lɛɛ ka an ŋ bɔ ʊ rɔ wɔɔ m, wɔɔ k'ʊ hɔ bɩ, ʊ hɔ mi kan kɩ a mɩmbɔʊrɛ kur gəəno bire ʊ rɔ wɔɔ. 
A lɛ ka wɔɔ ʊ kɛɛrɛ k'ʊ a hɩ do bɩ m, k'ʊ a kasɛtɩ naa do ka, ʊ ʊ: A mɩŋŋa Woso a bɔ ʊ kan nyɩnta mɩsɩɩrɔ kan gəəno kɩ dʊdɔkər. 
A mɩŋŋa wosolɛsinnɩsorazannɔ n'a kasɛtɩ kam, ŋ ʊ: Gʊaa haay k'a sɩra ka ʊ bɩ y'a mimbʊnyaarɔ sugur yɩrɛ, a tɔ ma.» 
Kʊ Pɩyɛɛr gɔɔta an nɩ meer bɩ bam bɩ, Sɛnt-Ɛspri bɩ zer gʊaa haay k'a nyɩnta an n'a tʊr kam meerbaa bɩ ma rɔ wɔɔ ra. 
Zɩɩfʊrɔ sɩrakarɛzannɔ kʊ ŋ Pɩyɛɛr da zaa ʊ rɔ wɔɔ zɛ kədə, kʊ ŋ Sɛnt-Ɛspri ganwʊrɛ yɩ an zer do vantɔɔ gʊɔɔ gʊta ra sɔ bɩ minto. 
Ŋ gʊɔɔ wɔɔ duro ma, ŋn'ɩ meer bam do y'a hʊnhɔ lemim m, ŋn'a lɛ bɔm Woso ʊ. Pɩyɛɛr n a ya, an a hɩ, a ʊ: 
«Gʊaa y'a dam ma, an a ta tolle, a ʊ, ŋ bɩ batɛm ka hi m gʊɔɔ nɔɔn duro ʊ ra? Ŋ y'a ba, am wɔɔ hɔ bɩ m, ŋnɩ Sɛnt-Ɛspri yɩ ra!» 
A n lɛ ka, kʊ n nɩ batɛm ka ŋ ʊ Yeezuu Krista tɔ ma, ŋn'a yɛ han k'a n a ya, an gɔɔta kan a kɩ dɔmɩm poore nɔ ʊ.
