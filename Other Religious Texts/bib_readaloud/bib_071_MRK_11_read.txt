Markɩ.
11.
Kʊ ŋ n'a zɔm Zerizalɛm ma, Bɛtfazee kan Betanii kɩ jɩr ʊ, ci kʊ n n'a birm Oliviyee ci bɩ zeezi bɩ, Yeezuu a karɩndanyɩnɔ nyɔɔ hɩɩya, 
an a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «K'a ta kʊ k'a n'awɔɔ lɛɛ naa ʊ. Bɩ, k'awɔɔ yaa gasʊ b'ʊ lɛɛm nɩ, awɔɔ brapoo yɩm, n n a kʊsɩ, bɩ gʊaa n da ʊ cɩna y. K'a hʊr a bʊr m. 
Kʊ gʊaa awɔɔ lar, a ʊ: bɔ minto m awɔɔ nɩ naa barɛ, k'a hɩ m, a ʊ: Zuuba lɛ n taa, a y'a tore awɔɔ m bʊr m lɛɛm.» 
Karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ n ta, ŋ n'aa brapoo yɩ, n n a kʊsɩ tɩnsara harlɛ ma zaa lɛ zi, karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ n a hʊr. 
Gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ nyɩnta b'ʊ rɔ wɔɔ gɔsɩnnɔ ŋ lar, ŋ ʊ: «Bɔ m, awɔɔ n nɩ brapoo bɩ hʊrlɛ?» 
Ŋ n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, amba Yeezuu a hɩ bɩ m, gʊɔɔ wɔɔ n ŋ to, ŋn'a hʊr. 
Ŋ n ta brapoo bɩ m Yeezuu zi, ŋn'a huuro ka brapoo bɩ ra, Yeezuu n da ʊ. 
Gʊɔɔ gʊta n a huuro ka zaa bɩ ra, gɔsɩnnɔ n gɔ lɛɛ yɩnyar hɔsɩrarɔ ʊ, ŋn'a ka ra. 
Bɔɔ nɩ Yeezuu lɛɛ, kan bɔɔ n'a kur lɔ wɔɔ kɩ ɩ zee dam, ŋ ʊ: «*Hozaana! Kʊ gʊaa k'a nɩ zɛm Zuuba tɔ ma bɩ n barka yɩ! 
Kʊ cirbəə k'a nɩ zɛm naa n barka yɩ, am wɔɔ yaaba Davɩd hɔ bɩ m. *Hozaana! K'ɩ tɔ n lɛbɔʊrɛ yɩ brama.» 
A n gasʊ Zerizalɛm ʊ, Wosocɛ ʊ. K'a dɩga hɔ haay ma bɩ, a a hʊr an ta Betanii ʊ kan bueerehɩɩyɔɔ wɔɔ kɩ, kʊ gum da bɩ minto. 
A dɔ k'a mɛ ŋn'ɩ bɔm Betanii ʊ bɩ, nɔ Yeezuu nyasʊ. 
A n a to, an sɔɔn yɩ laatʊ a lɛɛ n ta ʊ, an ta kan a karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ kɩ, k'a yaa dɩga a hɔsɩ nyɩntaŋ ʊ gɛ. A zɔ k'a ʊ sɔɔn bɩ ma bɩ, a lɛɛ a yɩ, bala, sɔɔn bɔɔnrɛ dɔmɩm bɛɛ y. 
A n a hɩ sɔɔn bɩ m, a ʊ: «Gʊaa b'a yarɛ an ɩbɩɩ nyɩ bɩ dɔ y!» A karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ n a ma. 
Yeezuu yaa lee ku Zerizalɛm ʊ kan a karɩndanyɩnɔ kɩ. Gasʊ kʊ Yeezuu ʊ Wosocɛ ʊ b'ʊ bɩ, a a sɩŋŋɩda ba, an nɩ lam gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ nɩ hɔ hɛɛnm, ŋn'ɩ hɔ sim nɔ wɔɔ ra Wosocɛ hallu ʊ b'ʊ. A busoolʊrlɛzannɔ mɔdrɔkuntərehɔɔ hɩnhɩsɩga kan cirpʊnnɔ hɛɛnno nyɩntaragɔrɔ kɩ. 
Bɩ, a n gɔsɩ to an nɩ cenim mɔr m Wosocɛ hallu ʊ b'ʊ y. 
A n n'a hɩrɛ ŋ nɩ hɔdɩndarɛ nɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «N y'a gʊrsɩra ba, n ʊ: ‹N mɔɔ cɛ bɩ birm yaadarɛ cɛ, do haay minto.› Awɔɔ n bɔkarɛ a sa, a ba konno bɩncɛ.» 
Kʊ Yeezuu naa hɩ wosocɛmannɩbənno jɩtannɔ kan wosocikarɩnsaambɔɔ wɔɔ kɩ n a ma bɩ, ŋ y'a karɛ a zɛrɛ zaa ma. Bɩ, ŋ yɩ nyi bam. Bala, zamaa b'a lɛ bɔ Yeezuu hɔdɩndarɛ b'ʊ. 
Yirbəə k'a da bɩ, Yeezuu bɔ tara bɩ nɔ ʊ, kan a karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ kɩ. 
A dɔ k'a mɛ Yeezuu kan a karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ kɩ nɩ cenim bɩ, ŋ sɔɔn bɩ yɩ, an ga kan a məsi kɩ. 
Pɩyɛɛr n hɔ kʊ Yeezuu a hɩ bɩ tɔ da a nɔ ʊ, a a hɩ, a ʊ: «Dɩga, Zuuba, sɔɔn k'ɩbɩɩ ɩ lɛ da ʊ bɩ ga haay!» 
Yeezuu n a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «K'a sɩra ka Woso ʊ. 
Sɩra mɔɔ n'a hɩrɛ awɔɔ m, kʊ gʊaa a hɩ ci naa do m: ‹Ɩ bɔɔta, ɩ ɩ da kɔ ʊ,› bɩ bɩsɩkarɛ ba a heer ʊ, k'a a sɩra ka, a ʊ: hɔ k'awɔɔ hɩ bɩ barɛ, a bam, amba a a hɩ bɩ m. 
Bɩ y'a ka mɔɔ n'a hɩrɛ awɔɔ m: hɔ k'awɔɔ n'a yɛm a yaadarɛ nɔ ʊ bɩ, k'a sɩra ka, a ʊ, awɔɔ yɩ, bɩ n y'a karɛ awɔɔ ʊ sɔ. 
Bɩ k'awɔɔ jɩm brama, an nɩ yaa dam, kʊ hɔ y'awɔɔ heer ʊ kan gʊaa kɩ, k'a sugur ka ʊ, k'awɔɔ Zɩ k'a nɩ arzana ʊ bɩ n awɔɔ kʊrɔmarɔ sugur ka awɔɔ ʊ sɔ.  
Bɩ k'awɔɔ n sugur ka a muno ʊ, awɔɔ Zɩ k'a nɩ arzana ʊ bɩ b'awɔɔ kʊrɔmarɔ wɔɔ sugur karɛ y.» 
Yeezuu kan a karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ kɩ a ya, ŋnɩ ta Zerizalɛm ʊ. Yeezuu k'a nɩ bɩmbɩrm Wosocɛ ʊ b'ʊ bɩ, wosocɛmannɩbənno jɩtannɔ kan wosocikarɩnsaambɔɔ wɔɔ kɩ, gʊɔɔgusinno kɩ bʊr zi, ŋ ʊ: 
«Nka ɩ lɛ ka ɩbɩɩ ʊ naa barɛ minto? Nka ɩ zaa ka?» 
Yeezuu n a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «Kʊ m awɔɔ lar mim deem ma, k'a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, kʊ m bɔkarɛ, m bɩncɛ kʊ n lɛ ka mɔɔ ʊ bɩ hɩ awɔɔ m. 
Batɛm kʊ Zaan nyɩnta an n'a kam bɩ, a to a ʊ Woso zi gɛɛ, gʊɔɔ zi? K'a nyɩ bɔ ʊ!» 
Ŋ n'ɩ bɩsɩ kɩnkam kʊ ma, ŋ ʊ: «Kʊ wɔɔ ʊ: a to a ʊ Woso zi, a y'a hɩrɛ a ʊ: bɔ minto m wɔɔ n a mim si gɛ? 
Bɩ kʊ wɔɔ ʊ: a to a ʊ gʊɔɔ zi, zamaa bɩ bɩ yɛrɛ y. Kʊ zamaa bɩ Zaan dɔ wosolɛsinnɩsorazaa paan… bɩ minto.» 
Ŋ n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ Yeezuu m, ŋ ʊ: «Wɔɔ n a dɔ y.» Yeezuu n a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «Bɩ minto bɩ, mɔɔ bɩ bɩncɛ niŋŋə kʊ n lɛ ka mɔɔ ʊ bɩ hɩrɛ awɔɔ m sɔ y.»
