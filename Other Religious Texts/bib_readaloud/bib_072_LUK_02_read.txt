Likki.
2.
Dɔmɩm bɩ do ʊ bɩ, cir Ogisti bɩ lɛ ka, a ʊ, n n durnya haay gʊɔɔ lɛ sa. 
Lɛɛkarɛ lɛsarɛ n naa, an ba Kiriniyusi k'a nyɩnta Siirii kʊzaa bɩ dɔmɩm ʊ. 
Gʊaa haay ta, kʊ n n'a lɛ sa, gʊaa biyəə lɛ sa n nawʊrɛ a kʊ ʊ. 
Zozɛf wuti sɔ Galilee kʊ Nazarɛtɩ ʊ, an ta Zidee gʊrga deem kʊ n n'a birm Betelehɛm ʊ. Bɩncɛ bɩ do cir Davɩd yɩ ʊ. Bala a mɩŋŋa naa, cir Davɩd do gʊaa m. 
Ta a ʊ k'a yaa lɛsarɛ bɩ wʊ kan a jaan Maarii kɩ. Bɩ ma bɩ, Maarii ɩ jɩsɩ m. 
Kʊ ŋ nɩ b'ʊ bɩ, Maarii nyɩ yɩrɛ dɔmɩm a ku. 
A a dʊndɔmbaa nyɩ yɩ yar, an pɛɛra burgu ma, an a wʊta hɔbɩsɩrɔ hɔbɩrɛ mɔr ʊ. Bala, ŋ n toore yɩ saanɔɔ cɛ ʊ b'ʊ y. 
Gʊrga bɩ do poo ʊ bɩ, dundonno nɩ b'ʊ, ŋn'ɩ dɔrɛ a hɔbɩsɩrɔ ma gunuu ʊ. 
Zuuba malɛɛka n a hɩnka ŋ nɩ. Zuuba lɛɛgure n yɔ ŋ nɩ. Nyibəə gʊta n ŋ nyasʊ. 
Malɛɛka bɩ n a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «A da bɩ bɔ ʊ y, bala, zɛ mɔɔ nawʊm lemim nyɩnta m kʊ m b'a hɩ awɔɔ m. A nyɩntam heernyɔɔ gʊta do bɩ haay minto. 
Bʊmbɔrɛzaa bɩ yɩ her Davɩd tara ra awɔɔ tɔ ma. A m Krista, Zuuba. 
Makra k'a n'a kam awɔɔ n a dɔ bɩ ba n nɩ: awɔɔ yaa nyɩhure yɩ, n n pɛɛra burgu ma, n n a wʊta hɔbɩsɩrɔ hɔbɩrɛ mɔr ʊ.» 
Lɛɛm bɩ, arzana malɛɛkarɔ cir gʊta bʊr, ŋ n b'a zɛ kʊ ra kan malɛɛka bɩ kɩ, ŋn'a lɛ bɔm Woso ʊ, ŋ ʊ: 
«Woso n lɛbɔʊrɛ yɩ brama, kʊ gʊɔɔ k'a nɔ yɩ ŋ la tara burə ʊ rɔ wɔɔ m mɛnyɔɔ yɩ.» 
Kʊ malɛɛkarɔ wɔɔ a gʊ ŋ ma ŋnɩ der brama bɩ, dundonno wɔɔ gɔɔta ŋn'a hɩm kʊ m, ŋ ʊ: «Ʊ ta Betelehɛm ʊ, k'ʊ aa hɔ k'a ba bɩ yɩ, Zuuba n a hɩ wɔɔ m bɩ.» 
Ŋ n ta zɔɔ-zɔɔ, ŋn'aa Maarii yɩ kan Zozɛf kɩ, kan nyɩhure bɩ kɩ, n n a wʊta hɔbɩsɩrɔ hɔbɩrɛ mɔr ʊ. 
Kʊ ŋ y'a yɩ bɩ, ŋnɩ hɔ kʊ malɛɛka b'a hɩ ŋ nɩ nyɩ naa do minto bɩ dɩnda. 
Gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ y'a tʊr ka dundonno wɔɔ meerbaa bɩ ma haay rɔ wɔɔ, ŋ zɛ kədə. 
Bɩ, Maarii minno nɔɔn duro da a heer ʊ, an n'a hɔɔn darɛ ŋ ma. 
Dundonno wɔɔ n a wusigə, ŋn'a lɛ bɔm Woso ʊ, ŋn'a daŋ ʊ, hɔ haay kʊ ŋ y'a ma, ŋn'a yɩ sɔ bɩ minto. Bala, hɔ haay ba amba malɛɛka b'a hɩ bɩ m. 
Dɔmɩm sinnyə k'a ku bɩ, n nyɩ bɩ gasʊ baŋŋʊ ʊ. N n a tɔ ka Yeezuu, tɔ kʊ malɛɛka bɩ nyɩnta an a ka, bɩ ma bɩ, a da n a jɩsɩ sa bɩ m. 
Kʊ ŋ hubɔrɛ dɔmɩnnɔ a ku amba Moyiisi lɛ ka bɩ m bɩ, Zozɛf kan Maarii kɩ nyɩ bɩ busu, ŋnɩ ta m Zerizalɛm ʊ, k'a yaa ka Woso ʊ, 
amba n y'a gʊrsɩra ba Zuuba lɛrɔ wɔɔ nɔ ʊ bɩ m: ‹Nyɩyar haay k'an a da nyɩ lɛɛkarɛzaa bɩ, Zuuba hɔ m.› 
N n ‹pʊnnɔ hɩɩyɔɔ gɛɛ cirpʊʊrɛ nyɩnɔ hɩɩyɔɔ› ka, kʊ n n mannɩ ba m, amba n y'a gʊrsɩra ba Zuuba Wosoci ʊ bɩ m. 
Gʊaa ɩ nyɩnta Zerizalɛm ʊ, a tɔ m Sɩmɩnyɔɔ. Gʊaa tɩrga m, an nɩ zum Woso ʊ mɩŋŋa. Nyɩnta a ʊ, an nɩ Israyɛl bʊmbɔrɛzaa mɩm dam. *Sɛnt-Ɛspri nyɩnta kan kɩ. 
Bala, Sɛnt-Ɛspri nyɩnta an a hɩnka m, a ʊ, awɔɔ bɩ gam, k'a n Krista, kʊ Zuuba n n'a nyɔɔm bɩ yɩ y. 
Sɛnt-Ɛspri a ka, an ta Wosocɛ ʊ. Bɩ ma bɩ, a Yeezuu zɩrɔ wɔɔ yɩ, ŋn'ɩ zɛrɛ a nyɩ bɩ m k'a b'a ka Woso ʊ amba n y'a gʊrsɩra ba bɩ m, 
Sɩmɩnyɔɔ nyɩ bɩ si a wɔ ʊ, an barka da Woso m, an n'a hɩrɛ, a ʊ: 
«Naa ma bɩ, Zuuba, ɩbɩɩ lerbɔrɛ bɩ kʊ yɩ. Ɩbɩɩ ɩ da ma, ɩ ɩ zibəənyɩ bɩ to an cem kan laafɩɩ kɩ amba ɩbɩɩ hɩ bɩ m. 
Mɔɔ ɩbɩɩ bʊmbɔrɛzaa bɩ yɩ, 
ɩbɩɩ n a banka doro haay lɛɛ. 
Ɩbɩɩ do Israyɛl lɛbɔʊrɛ m. Lɛɛgure m, an n'ɩbɩɩ hɩnkam doro haay m.» 
K'a nɩ meer bɩ barɛ ncɩnaaʊ bɩ, nyɩ bɩ zɩ kan a da kɩ zɛ kədə. 
Sɩmɩnyɔɔ n ŋ barka da ʊ, an a hɩ nyɩ bɩ da Maarii m, a ʊ: «Nyɩ naa do, bʊr a ʊ k'a bɩ gʊɔɔ gʊta do, k'a gʊɔɔ gʊta wuti Israyɛl nɔ ʊ sɔ. A nyɩntam Woso makra, bɩ gʊɔɔ bɩsɩ kam ma. 
A gʊɔɔ gʊta hɔɔndarɛrɔ kʊ ŋ y'a nyaakʊm da rɔ wɔɔ hɩnkam lɛɛgure ʊ. Bɩ k'ɩbɩɩ nɩ, kargʊ n n'ɩbɩɩ heer hɔnm.» 
Lʊ wosolɛsinnɩsorazaa deem ɩ nyɩnta b'ʊ, a tɔ m Annɩ. Nyɩnta a ʊ Fanɩyɛl nyɩmbʊɛɛrɛ, Asɛɛr do m. A gusi gʊta. A nyɩnta kan a zim k'a sa a poombaa ma bɩ kɩ dɔɔraa saanhɩna. 
A gɔɔta zoro, a dɔɔraa businnyə a yaa si ku. A n nɩ Wosocɛ ʊ b'ʊ dɔmɩm haay, an nɩ Woso daŋ ʊ sinuu ʊ kan a gunuu ʊ kɩ. A n n'a lɛ kʊsɩm, an nɩ yaa dam sɔ. 
A mɩŋŋa bʊr dɔmɩm kʊ n nɩ nyɩ bɩ kam Woso ʊ b'ʊ, an nɩ barka dam Woso m, an nɩ nyɩ bɩ mim dɩndam gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ nɩ dɔm ma kʊ Woso n bʊr Zerizalɛm bʊmbɔrɛ m nɔ wɔɔ m. 
Kʊ Yeezuu zɩrɔ wɔɔ hɔ kʊ n y'a gʊrsɩra ba Zuuba lɛrɔ wɔɔ nɔ ʊ bɩ ba ŋn'a nya bɩ, ŋ y'a bɔ, ŋnɩ ta Galilee kʊ Nazarɛtɩ ʊ. 
Nyɩ bɩ n nɩ marɛ, a paŋŋa n n'a dam la. A mindɔrɛ nyɩnta ʊ gʊta, Woso barka nɩ kan kɩ. 
Dɔɔraa haay, Yeezuu zɩrɔ wɔɔ tɩr ta Zerizalɛm ʊ *pakkɩ cibsa ma. 
Kʊ Yeezuu dɔɔraa bueerehɩɩya kʊ bɩ, ŋ ta Zerizalɛm ʊ cibsa bɩ ma, amba ŋn'a bam dɔɔraa haay bɩ m. 
Kʊ cibsa bɩ dɔmɩnnɔ wɔɔ cem bɩ, ŋ y'a wusigə, ŋnɩ doore. Nyukumbueere Yeezuu bɩ gɔɔta Zerizalɛm ʊ, b'a zɩrɔ wɔɔ n a dɔ y. 
Ŋ y'a hɔɔn darɛ ŋ ʊ, zaasənno an nɩ kan ŋ kɩ. Ŋ tarɛ bɩ yɩ dɔmɩm deem, ŋnɩ bɔkarɛ, ŋn'a sɩŋŋɩda sa, ŋn'a kam ma a yɩrɛrɔ kan a dɔrɛrɔ kɩ bire ʊ. 
Bɩ, ŋ n a yɩ y. Ŋ n'a wusigə, ŋnɩ ta Zerizalɛm ʊ, a kama jɩ ʊ. 
A dɔmɩm kaakʊ hinni ʊ, ŋ yaa a yɩ Wosocɛ ʊ, an nɩ nyɩntam karɩnsaambɔɔ bire ʊ, an n'a tʊr karɛ ŋ meerbaa ma, an nɩ ŋ larm sɔ. 
Gʊaa haay k'a n'a tʊr kam ma bɩ, zɛ a nawʊm kədə a hɔɔntaʊrɛ meerbaa nyɩbɔʊrɛ tɔ ma. 
K'a zɩrɔ wɔɔ a yɩ bɩ, ŋ zɛ kədə, a da bɩ n a hɩ m, a ʊ: «M nyɩnnɩ, bɔ m ɩbɩɩ m naa do ba wɔɔ nɩ? Dɩga a, ɩ zɩ kan mɔɔ kɩ da bɔ ʊ, ʊ n'ʊ karɛ ɩbɩɩ ma.» 
A n a nyɩ bɔ ŋ nʊ, a ʊ: «Bɔ m an n'a kam mɔɔ ma? Awɔɔ n a dɔ a ʊ, mɔɔ m ga m, m bɩr m Zɩ har ʊ ra?» 
Bɩ, hɔ k'a hɩ ŋ nɩ bɩ, ŋ n a jɩ ma y. 
Yeezuu n a wusigə kan ŋ kɩ Nazarɛtɩ ʊ. A n nɩ ŋ lɛ sire. A da bɩ minno nɔɔn duro da a heer ʊ. 
Yeezuu nɩ marɛ, a mindɔrɛ n'a dam la. A Woso nɔ yɩ, an gʊɔɔ nɔ yɩ sɔ.
