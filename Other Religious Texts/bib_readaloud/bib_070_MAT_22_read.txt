Matiyee.
22.
Yeezuu a ya, an hɔbɔkʊrɛnoom da ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: 
«Woso cirbəə bɔ kʊ rɛ kan cir k'a a nyɩnnɩ lʊsarɛ cibsa ba kɩ. 
A n a zibəənyɩnɔ nyɔɔ kʊ ŋ n'aa gʊɔɔ k'a hɩ ŋ nɩ kʊ ŋ nɩ bɩ cibsa bɩ rɔ wɔɔ bir, ŋnɩ bɔkarɛ ŋ ʊ, a bɩ zɛrɛ y. 
A n a ya, an zibəənyɩnɔ vantɔɔ nyɔɔ an a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: ‹K'a yaa hɩ gʊɔɔ kʊ mɔɔ ŋ bir cibsa bɩ ma rɔ wɔɔ m, a ʊ: mɔɔ cibsa bɩ banka m a nya, mɔɔ m dono zɩnzɛ kan m hɔbɩsɩrɔ gʊtɔɔ kɩ, hɔ haay banka, k'a bʊr cibsa bɩ ma kɛɛrɛ.› 
Bɩ, gʊɔɔ wɔɔ n dɩga cibsa birle bɩ ma hɔsɩ y, ŋn'a hʊr ŋnɩ ta a zibəəjɩrɔ ʊ, naa n ta hɔsɩra ʊ, naa n ta daasɩ ʊ mɔdrɔ hɛɛjɩ ʊ. 
Ŋ n gɔɔta rɔ wɔɔ n zibəənyɩnɔ wɔɔ nyasʊ, ŋnɩ nyannɩ da ŋ ma, ŋnɩ bɔkarɛ, ŋnɩ ŋ zɩnzɛ. 
Cir bɩ nɔ n ma ʊ, an sʊrdaarɔ nyɔɔ ŋn'aa gərzenno nɔɔn duro zɩnzɛ, ŋnɩ sɛ da ŋ kʊ bɩ ma. 
A m naa, an a hɩ a zibəənyɩnɔ m, a ʊ: ‹Cibsa bɩ hɔbɩrɛ bɩ bankarɛ nya, bɩ gʊɔɔ kʊ mɔɔ hɩ ŋ nɩ rɔ wɔɔ n a ga kʊ ʊ ba y. 
K'a ta zaazɛkʊrarɔ wɔɔ ra, kʊ nka awɔɔ yɩ, k'a hɩ m k'an bʊr cibsa bɩ ma.› 
A zibəənyɩnɔ wɔɔ n a hʊr, ŋnɩ zaarɔ sa ŋnɩ gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ yɩ ŋ yɩ rɔ wɔɔ so kʊ ma, gʊɔɔ mɩŋŋɔɔ, kan gʊɔɔ bʊnyaarɔ kɩ. Gʊɔɔ n cɛ kʊ n nɩ cibsa bɩ baŋ ʊ bɩ han. 
Cir bɩ gasʊ k'a dɩga gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ bʊr cibsa bɩ ma rɔ wɔɔ ma, an a to, an gʊaa yɩ, gʊaa bɩ do n harcɩntarɛ cibsa huu du a ma y. 
A n a hɩ m, a ʊ: ‹Ɩbɩɩ ba lɔ ɩ gasʊ naa ʊ, b'ɩbɩɩ ba harcɩntarɛ cibsa huu nɩ?› Bɩ gʊaa bɩ n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ y. 
A m bɩ, cir bɩ n a hɩ zibəənyɩnɔ wɔɔ m, a ʊ: ‹K'a gannɔ kan a wɔrɔ kɩ kʊsɩ a zo garga ra, monsigə ʊ. Lɛɛ bɩ do an nɩ nyi kaŋ ʊ, an a sɔ sʊ.› » 
Yeezuu n a ya, an a hɩ, a ʊ: «N gʊɔɔ bir gʊta, bɩ, n nɩ ŋ nyʊkɔ ʊ rɔ wɔɔ poore.» 
Naa jɛ ʊ bɩ, farizɛɛnrɔ ta, ŋn'aa a so kʊ ma, k'a a ka zaa k'a nɩ Yeezuu nyasʊm ma a meerbaa nɔ ʊ bɩ dɔ. 
Ŋ n a karɩndanyɩnɔ kan Hɩrɔɔdɩ jɛzannɔ kɩ nyɔɔ Yeezuu zi, kʊ ŋ n'aa hɩ m, ŋ ʊ: «Karɩnsaamba, wɔɔ dɔ wɔɔ ʊ sɩra ɩbɩɩ n'a hɩm, Woso zaa ɩbɩɩ n'a dɩndam sɩra bɩncɛ ʊ, ɩbɩɩ bɩr nyi bam gʊɔɔ nɩ y, b'ɩbɩɩ bɩr gʊɔɔ nyʊkɔŋ ʊ sɔ y. 
Bɩ minto bɩ, ɩ hɔɔnhɔ hɩ wɔɔ m, wɔɔ lɛ bɩ zaa ka k'ʊ Rɔm cir yawʊr ka ʊ gɛɛ, ʊ b'a ka ʊ gɛ?» 
Bɩ, kʊ Yeezuu ŋ zambʊ bɩ dɔ bɩ, a a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «Zambʊzannɔ! Bɔ m, awɔɔ nɩ mɔɔ makra barɛ? 
K'a busoo kʊ n nɩ Rɔm cir yawʊr kam nɩ bɩ hɩnka m m.» Ŋ n deniyee deem hɩnka m. 
Yeezuu n ŋ lar, a ʊ: «Gʊaa mim kʊ n y'a da ʊ naa kan gʊrsɩra naa do kɩ, nka hɔɔ nɩ?» 
Ŋ ʊ: «Rɔm cir hɔɔ m.» A n a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «K'a maam k'a hɔ kʊ Rɔm cir hɔ m bɩ ka ʊ, k'a hɔ kʊ Woso hɔ m bɩ ka ʊ sɔ.» 
Meerbaa naa do a ka ŋ n zɛ kədə, ŋn'a hʊr, ŋnɩ Yeezuu to. 
Hinni bɩ do ʊ bɩ, sadisɛɛnrɔ gɔsɩnnɔ bʊr Yeezuu zi. Bala, sadisɛɛnrɔ wɔɔ ʊ gəəno mɩmbɔʊrɛ b'a ʊ y. Ŋ n laaka da Yeezuu han, ŋ ʊ: 
«Karɩnsaamba, Moyiisi a gʊrsɩra ba an a ka wɔɔ ʊ, a ʊ, kʊ gʊaa cɛmba ta ʊ, an ga an a lʊ to, b'a nyɩ ba ʊ y, k'a dam n lʊ bɩ si, k'an nyɩ yɩ kan kɩ a cɛmba bɩ minto. 
A n bɔkarɛ, danyɩnɔ ɩ nyɩnta saanhɩna wɔɔ bire ʊ, lɛɛkarɛzaa bɩ n lʊ sa an ga, b'a n nyɩ yɩ kan lʊ bɩ kɩ y. 
A hɩɩya hɔ bɩ n lʊ bɩ si, an ga, an lʊ bɩ to b'a n nyɩ yɩ kan kɩ y. A kaakʊ hɔ bɩ maam sɔ, saanhɩnarɔ wɔɔ haay ŋ gɩnga bɩ ŋ n nyɩ yɩ kan lʊ bɩ kɩ y. 
Ŋ haay kur bɩ, lʊ bɩ n ga sɔ. 
Gəəno mɩmbɔʊrɛ dɔmɩm ʊ, kʊ ŋ y'a mɩm bɔ ʊ, nka y'a so lʊ b'ʊ ŋ bireyaa ʊ? Bala, gʊɔɔ saanhɩnarɔ wɔɔ haay lʊ bɩ si.» 
Yeezuu n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «K'awɔɔ n gʊrsɩra mɩŋŋɔɔ wɔɔ dɔ kan Woso paŋŋa kɩ bɩ y'a ka awɔɔ n'a nyʊnnyɔɔm. 
Bala, gəəno mɩmbɔʊrɛ dɔmɩm ʊ, yar bɩr lʊ sarɛ y, lʊ bɩr yar sarɛ y, bɔ ŋ y'a ʊ kʊ ʊ kan malɛɛkarɔ kʊ ŋ nɩ brama rɔ wɔɔ kɩ. 
Bɩ, kʊ gəəno mɩmbɔʊrɛ mim nɩ, awɔɔ n hɔ kʊ Woso a hɩ awɔɔ m bɩ karɩnda ba ra? A a hɩ, a ʊ: 
‹Abraham kan Izakkɩ kɩ, Zakɔɔb kɩ Woso m mɔɔ m.› Gəəno Woso bɛɛ y, gʊɔɔ mɩsɩɩrɔ Woso m.» 
Yeezuu hɔdɩndarɛ naa do cem zamaa bɩ hɔɔndarɛ han. 
Farizɛɛnrɔ wɔɔ kʊ ŋ y'a ma, ŋ ʊ, Yeezuu a ka sadisɛɛnrɔ wɔɔ b'a dam ma ŋn'a ya ŋnɩ meer ba bɩ, ŋ y'a so kʊ ma. 
Ŋ gʊaa deem kʊ wosocikarɩnsaamba gʊta m bɩ laaka da Yeezuu han, k'a makra ba. 
A ʊ: «Karɩnsaamba, lɛrɔ wɔɔ haay bire ʊ, kara ɩ lɛ da?» 
Yeezuu n a nyɩ bɔ nʊ, a ʊ: « ‹K'ɩ Zuuba Woso ŋʊa kan ɩ heer kɩ haay, kan ɩ nyi kɩ haay, kan ɩ hɔɔndarɛ kɩ haay.› 
Lɛ k'a lɛ da, a m lɛ gʊta bɩ m naa do. 
Lɛ hɩɩya hɔ k'a gʊtabaa lɛɛkarɛ hɔ bɩ ku bɩ m naa do: ‹K'ɩ mim ŋʊa amba ɩ mɩŋŋa m.› 
Moyiisi lɛrɔ wɔɔ haay kan wosolɛsinnɩsorazannɔ wɔɔ meerbaarɔ kɩ haay məsi m lɛrɔ hɩɩyɔɔ nɔɔn duro m.» 
Farizɛɛnrɔ wɔɔ kʊ ŋ y'a so kʊ ma bɩ, Yeezuu laaka da ŋ han, a ʊ: 
«Awɔɔ y'a hɔɔn dam Krista ma lʊnlɔ? Nka nyɩ nɩ?» Ŋ n a hɩ m, ŋ ʊ: «*Davɩd Nyɩ m.» 
Yeezuu n a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «B'a yɩ lɔ, Sɛnt-Ɛspri n a ka Davɩd n a bir a Zuuba? Bala, Davɩd b'a hɩ, a ʊ: 
Zuuba Woso a hɩ mɔɔ zuuba m, a ʊ: nyɩnta mɔɔ bɩsɩ ʊ, kʊ m tolle ba m a ka, k'ɩ jɩnnɔ wɔɔ n nyɩnta ɩ gam taa. 
K'a ɩ yɩrɛ ɩ ʊ Davɩd y'a birm a Zuuba, yɩ an nawʊm lɔ kɛɛrɛ kʊ Krista n yɩ *Davɩd Nyɩ?» 
Gɔsɩ n a da ma, an a nyɩ bɔ nʊ y. Hinni bɩ do tɛkka ma, gʊaa ba ʊ dɔ, a heer n kookoo k'a lar mim ma y.
