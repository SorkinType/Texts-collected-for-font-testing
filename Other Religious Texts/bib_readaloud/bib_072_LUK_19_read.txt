Likki.
19.
Yeezuu gasʊ Zeriko ʊ, an nɩ kʊ bɩ zɛm la. 
Gʊaa nɩ b'ʊ a tɔ m Zasee, yawʊrsinno cir m, arzakazaa m sɔ. 
A n n'a kam Yeezuu ma k'a yɩ, kʊ nka m, k'a a dɔ. Zamaa bɩ minto, kʊ gʊaa kutiim m bɩ, a b'a dam ma, a n a yɩ y. 
A n bra si an ta lɛɛ, an aa der gɔ ra, kʊ Yeezuu bʊr an nɩ cenim b'ʊ, k'a yɩ. 
Kʊ Yeezuu lee ku bɩncɛ bɩ do ʊ bɩ, a a bɔɔta an a hɩ m, a ʊ: «Zasee, a ba zɔɔ ɩ zer, bala, mɔɔ cɩnta kʊ m zer ɩbɩɩ har ʊ her.» 
Zasee n zer zɔɔ-zɔɔ, an a si kan heernyɔɔ kɩ. 
Kʊ zamaa bɩ naa yɩ bɩ, ŋn'ɩ kukur barɛ, ŋ ʊ: «Zer a ʊ mimbʊnyaabər har ʊ.» 
Zasee n a zɔ Zuuba ma an a hɩ m, a ʊ: «Dɩga, Zuuba, mɔɔ yɩ m arzaka bɩ haay, zɛkumbir sam, m a ka cicirzannɔ ʊ, bɩ, kʊ mɔɔ mim zu ʊ gʊaa ma, mɔɔ y'a sompim nʊ gallɛm si.» 
Yeezuu n a hɩ, a ʊ: «Bʊmbɔrɛ gasʊ har naa do ʊ her, bala, gʊaa naa do Abraham yaŋŋa m sɔ. 
Bala, Gʊaanyɩ bɩ bʊr a ʊ, an b'a ka gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ nyaam nɔ wɔɔ ma, an ŋ bʊmbɔ.» 
Kʊ ŋ gɔɔta ŋn'a tʊr kam a meerbaarɔ wɔɔ ma bɩ, Yeezuu a ya, an hɔbɔkʊrɛnoom naa do da ŋ nɩ. Bala, a a zɔ Zerizalɛm ma. Bɩ, gʊɔɔ wɔɔ ɩ dɩgam ma, ŋ ʊ, Woso cirbəə bɩ y'a hɩnkarɛ a m b'ʊ lɛɛm. 
Bɩ y'a ka Yeezuu n a hɩ, a ʊ: «Cir nyɩ deem ɩ ta kʊ laatʊ ʊ k'a yaa cirbəə bɩ, k'a bɔ a bʊr. 
A n a zibəənyɩnɔ bir bu, an busoo jir ŋ nɩ minnɩ deem-deem, an a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: ‹K'a zi ba m, k'an ta an mɔɔ wusigəre dɔmɩm ku.› 
B'a gʊrgazannɔ wɔɔ sɔ m, ŋnɩ gʊɔɔ nyɔɔ a jɛ ʊ kʊ ŋ n'a a hɩ cirbəə karɛzaa bɩ m, ŋ ʊ: ‹Wɔɔ lɛ baa k'an a so wɔɔ ʊ y.› 
K'a yaa cirbəə bɩ bɩ an bʊr bɩ, a a ka ŋnɩ zibəənyɩnɔ k'a busoo jinjir ŋ nɩ rɔ wɔɔ bir a taa, k'a dɩga a kʊ bɔ zi ŋ y'a ba m gɛ. 
Lɛɛkarɛzaa bɩ n bʊr, a ʊ: ‹Zuuba, minnɩ deem k'ɩbɩɩ ka mɔɔ ʊ bɩ, minnɩ bu y'a da ra.› 
A n a hɩ m, a ʊ: ‹Zibəənyɩ mɩŋŋa n'ɩbɩɩ m, bala, ɩbɩɩ noonhubaa hɩnka hɔ poorero nɔ ʊ, mɔɔ cirbəə karɛ ɩbɩɩ ʊ, k'ɩ ɩ so tara bu ʊ.› 
A hɩɩya hɔ bɩ n b'a hɩ, a ʊ: ‹Zuuba, ɩbɩɩ busoo bɩ nyɩ da minnɩ soor. 
A n a hɩ bɩ m sɔ, a ʊ: Ɩ so tara soor ʊ.› 
A vanta n bʊr, a ʊ: ‹Zuuba, ba ɩbɩɩ busoo bɩ n na, pɛɛra mɔɔ burgu ma, m a nyaakʊm da.› 
Mɔɔ ɩ nyi barɛ ɩbɩɩ m, ɩbɩɩ heer kʊsɩrabaa bɩ minto. Hɔ k'ɩbɩɩ n a cɩnta bɩ ɩbɩɩ n n'a sam. Hɔ k'ɩbɩɩ n a duu bɩ, ɩbɩɩ n n'a dam. 
A n a hɩ m, a ʊ: Ɩbɩɩ mɩŋŋa meerbaa bɩ mɔɔ n n'ɩbɩɩ dʊdɔ yarm ma, zibəənyɩ bʊnyaa. ‹Ɩbɩɩ dɔ, ɩ ʊ: gʊaa kʊsɩ m mɔɔ m, mɔɔ nɩ hɔ kʊ mɔɔ n a cɩnta bɩ sam, m nɩ hɔ kʊ mɔɔ n a duu bɩ dam. 
Bɔ minto, ɩbɩɩ n mɔɔ busoo bɩ cɩnta busoo cɛ ʊ y? Kʊ mɔɔ m bɔ, m bʊr kʊ m bɩ nyɔɔdʊ yɩ?› 
A n a hɩ gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ nɩ b'ʊ rɔ wɔɔ m, a ʊ: ‹K'a busoo bɩ yɔ m, a ka gʊaa k'an ta m bu b'ʊ.› 
Ŋ n a hɩ m, ŋ ʊ: ‹Zuuba, a lɛɛ ka an a yɩ bu.› 
A hɩ mɔɔ nawʊrɛ awɔɔ m, gʊaa biyəə k'an ta hɔ m bɩ, n y'a kaŋ ʊ, gʊaa k'a ba m bɩ, baa k'a ta m poore bɩ, n y'a yɔm nɩ. 
Mɔɔ jɩnnɔ kʊ ŋ nɩ mɔɔ ŋʊa kan cirbəə kɩ rɔ wɔɔ, k'a bʊr ŋ nɩ mɔɔ taa naa ʊ, a ŋ zɩnzɛ.» 
Yeezuu k'a naa hɩ bɩ, a ka ŋ lɛɛ an nɩ tam Zerizalɛm ʊ. 
K'a ta an n'a zɔm Bɛtfazee ma kan Betanii kɩ, Oliviyee ci bɩ zeezi bɩ, a a karɩndanyɩnɔ nyɔɔ hɩɩya. 
A n a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «K'a ta kʊ k'a nɩ lɛɛ naa ʊ. K'awɔɔ ta an nɩ gasʊm, awɔɔ brapoo yɩm n n'a kʊsɩ, bɩ gɔsɩ n da ʊ cɩna y. K'a hʊr a bʊr m. 
Bɩ kʊ gʊaa awɔɔ lar, a ʊ: Bɔ m awɔɔ n'a hʊrlɛ? K'a hɩ m, a ʊ: Zuuba lɛ n taa.» 
Gʊɔɔ k'a ŋ nyɔɔ rɔ wɔɔ, kʊ ŋ ta bɩ, ŋ yaa hɔ haay bɩ yɩ amba Yeezuu a hɩ bɩ m. 
Kʊ ŋn'ɩ brapoo bɩ hʊrm bɩ, a zannɔ wɔɔ n a hɩ ŋ nɩ, ŋ ʊ: «Bɔ minto m, awɔɔ n'a hʊrlɛ?» 
Ŋ n'a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, ŋ ʊ: «Zuuba lɛ n taa.» 
Ŋ n brapoo bɩ gaa, ŋnɩ ta m Yeezuu zi, ŋn'a huuro ka ra, ŋn'a ka Yeezuu n da ʊ. 
K'a nɩ tam bɩ, gʊɔɔ wɔɔ n'a huuro kam zaa bɩ ra. 
K'a a zɔ Oliviyee ci bɩ zerjɩ bɩ ma bɩ, heernyɔɔ gasʊ karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ ʊ, zamaa bɩ haay, ŋn'a leer busu, ŋn'a lɛ bɔm Woso ʊ hoserlo haay kʊ ŋ y'a yɩ bɩ minto. 
Ŋ ʊ: «Cir k'a nɩ zɛm Zuuba Woso tɔ ma bɩ n barka yɩ. Kʊ laafɩɩ n nyɩnta arzana ʊ, kʊ lɛbɔʊrɛ n nyɩnta brama.» 
Farizɛɛnrɔ gɔsɩnnɔ a hɩ Yeezuu m zamaa bɩ bire ʊ, ŋ ʊ: «Karɩnsaamba, a ka k'ɩ karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ n zɛ pi.» 
A n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «A hɩ mɔɔ nawʊrɛ awɔɔ m: Kʊ ŋ zɛ pi, jaarɔ n nɩ zee dam.» 
K'a a zɔ Zerizalɛm kʊnɔ bɩ ma, an a to an a yɩ bɩ, a nyi ka a minto. 
A n n'a hɩrɛ, a ʊ: «Ɩbɩɩ sɔ, k'a dɔ ɩbɩɩ ʊ her, kʊ nka n nɩ laafɩɩ karɛ ɩbɩɩ ʊ! Bɩ naa ma bɩ, a a nyaakʊm da ɩbɩɩ lɛɛ, ɩbɩɩ b'ɩ dam ma, ɩ yɩ y. 
Sɩra m, dɔmɩnnɔ ɩ zɛrɛ ɩbɩɩ minto, ɩbɩɩ jɩnnɔ wɔɔ tee kɩnkarɛ ɩbɩɩ pəŋŋə bɩ ma, ŋ bɩrlɛ ɩbɩɩ ma, k'a ɩbɩɩ da, ŋn'a so bɩncɛ haay ʊ. 
Ŋ y'ɩbɩɩ wurgəm haay, ɩbɩɩ kan ɩ zamaa kɩ. Ŋ bɩ jaa deem taan… tore, an bɩr a mim burə ʊ y, bala, ɩbɩɩ n dɔmɩm kʊ Woso bʊr k'a b'a da ɩ han bɩ dɔ y.» 
Yeezuu gasʊ Wosocɛ ʊ, an nɩ lam hɔheenno wɔɔ ra. 
A n a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «N y'a gʊrsɩra ba, ŋ ʊ: ‹Mɔɔ cɛ bɩ nyɩntam yaadarɛ cɛ.› B'awɔɔ, awɔɔ sa a ba konno bɩncɛ.» 
Yeezuu ɩ hɔ dɩndam dɔmɩm haay Wosocɛ ʊ b'ʊ. Wosocɛmannɩbənno jɩtannɔ kan wosocikarɩnsaambɔɔ kɩ, tara cinno kɩ y'a karɛ a zɛrɛ zaa ma. 
Bɩ hɔ kʊ ŋ y'a cɩnta k'a ba bɩ, ŋn'a zaa yɩ y, bala, zamaa bɩ haay y'a tʊr karɛ a meerbaa ma kan bʊraama gʊta kɩ.
