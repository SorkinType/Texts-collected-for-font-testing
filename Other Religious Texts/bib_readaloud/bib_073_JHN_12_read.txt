Zaan.
12.
*Pakkɩ cibsa k'a gɔɔta dɔmɩm sɔrdɩ bɩ, Yeezuu ta Betanii ʊ, kʊ kʊ Lazaar n ta ʊ b'ʊ. Kʊ bɩ do a Lazaar mɩm bɔ ʊ bɩ. 
N yirbəə hɔbɩrɛ ba b'ʊ, n n a ka Yeezuu ʊ. Martɩ nyɩnta an nɩ hɔbɩrɛ jirm, bɩ ma bɩ, Lazaar ɩ gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ nɩ hɔbɩrɛ bɩm kan Yeezuu kɩ rɔ wɔɔ barla. 
Maarii n tidəər nyɔɔ kʊ n y'a ba naar paan… m a lɛ n kʊsɩ bɩ sa gedre ʊ, an a nɔ Yeezuu gannɔ ma, an a minkə gaa an a nʊnnɔ ma, tidəər ji bɩ n sʊr har bɩ ma haay. 
Karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ gʊaa deem Zidas Iskarɩyɔtɩ kʊ gʊaa k'a nɩ zɛm k'a bɩ Yeezuu nyasʊ a ka b'a hɩ, a ʊ: 
«Bɔ minto, n n tidəər naa do sa, n n a hɛɛ *deniyee zɔɔkarkʊ, n n a ka cicirzannɔ ʊ?» 
K'a hɩ ncɩnaaʊ bɩ, a bɩr yɩrɛ ɩ ʊ, a hɔɔn an n'a dam cicirzannɔ ma y. Bala, a mɩŋŋa koor m, k'a y'a so busoo bɩ zibəə b'ʊ bɩ, a ɩ hɔ kʊ n n'a cɩntam bɩ kʊnam. 
Yeezuu n a hɩ, a ʊ: «K'a to! Naa do ba an nawʊm mɔɔ birle dɔmɩm minto. 
Cicirzannɔ wɔɔ ɩ kan awɔɔ kɩ dɔmɩm haay, bɩ, mɔɔ bɩ kan awɔɔ kɩ dɔmɩm haay y.» 
Bɩ ma bɩ, zɩɩfʊrɔ cir gʊta a ma, ŋ ʊ, Yeezuu bʊr b'ʊ. Ŋ n bʊr zi, bɩ ŋ n bʊr a minto bala y, bʊr ŋ y'a ʊ k'a bɩ Lazaar k'a mɩm bɔ ʊ gəəno bire ʊ bɩ yɩ. 
*Wosocɛmannɩbənno jɩtannɔ wɔɔ a cɩnta k'a Lazaar zɛ gər sɔ, 
bala, a minto y'a ka, zɩɩfʊrɔ gʊɔɔ gʊta n'a gʊrɛ ŋ ma, ŋn'ɩ Yeezuu mim sim. 
Dɔ k'a mɛ bɩ, gʊɔɔ gʊta kʊ ŋ bʊr cibsa bɩ ma rɔ wɔɔ kʊ ŋ y'a ma, ŋ ʊ, Yeezuu ɩ zɛm Zerizalɛm ʊ bɩ, 
ŋ bɛ gɔ lɛɛ yɩnyar, ŋnɩ ta k'a yaa a zɛ ʊ, ŋn'ɩ zee darɛ, ŋ ʊ: «*Hozaana! Gʊaa k'a nɩ zɛm Woso tɔ ma, *Israyɛl cir bɩ n barka yɩ.» 
Yeezuu brapoo yɩ, an da ʊ, amba n y'a gʊrsɩra ba bɩ m: 
*Siyɔn zannɔ, a da bɩ bɔ ʊ y. Ba awɔɔ cir nɩ zɛm n na. Da a ʊ brapoo ʊ. 
Karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ n lɛɛ ka ŋnɩ hɔ k'a nɩ zɛm naa jɩ dɔ hɔɔ y. Bɩ, kʊ ŋ Yeezuu lɛbɔʊrɛ yɩ bɩ, ŋ hɔ kʊ ŋ y'a gʊrsɩra ba a minto bɩ tɔ da a nɔ ʊ, bɩ naa ba ŋ y'a ʊ a minto. 
Bɩ, gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ nyɩnta kan Yeezuu kɩ dɔmɩm k'a Lazaar bir yaa ʊ an bɔ, an a mɩm bɔ ʊ gəəno bire ʊ rɔ wɔɔ a kasɛtɩ ka. 
Bɩ gʊɔɔ gʊta bʊr zi. Kʊ ŋ y'a ma, ŋ ʊ, a hoser naa do ba bɩ minto y'a ka, ŋnɩ bʊr k'a b'a zɛ ʊ. 
*Farizɛɛnrɔ wɔɔ n'a hɩm kʊ m, ŋ ʊ: «Awɔɔ yɩ, a dɔ a ʊ, a b'a dam ma, a hɔsɩ ba y. K'a dɩga gʊɔɔ haay kʊ ŋ nɩ zure a jɛ ʊ!» 
Cibsa k'a n ta ʊ bɩ minto, grɛkkɩrɔ gɔsɩnnɔ der k'a Woso da ʊ. 
Ŋ n a hɩ Galilee kʊ Bɛtsayɩdaa gʊaa Filipi m, ŋ ʊ: «Wɔɔ harzaa, wɔɔ lɛ n taa k'ʊ Yeezuu yɩ.» 
Filipi n ta, an aa hɩ Andɩr m, ŋn'a zɛ kʊ ra, ŋn'a hɩ Yeezuu m. 
Yeezuu n a nyɩ bɔ ŋ nʊ, a ʊ: «Wakatɩ a ku kʊ *Gʊaanyɩ bɩ n lɛbɔʊrɛ yɩ. 
Sɩra paan… mɔɔ n'a hɩrɛ awɔɔ m: kʊ hɔyaa a zo tara ʊ, a n ga y, gɔɔta an nawʊrɛ a deem. Bɩ, k'a ga, nyɩ an n'a dam gʊta. 
Gʊaa k'a a mɩsɩrbaa ŋʊa, a b'a yɩrɛ y. Bɩ, gʊaa k'a kaanm a mɩsɩrbaa m durnya naa do nɔ ʊ, a mɩsɩrbaa yɩm *mɩsɩrbaa k'a nyarɛ ba ʊ bɩ nɔ ʊ. 
Kʊ gʊaa lɛ taa k'a mɔɔ zi ba k'an zu mɔɔ ʊ, bɩncɛ kʊ mɔɔ n ta ʊ bɩ, mɔɔ zibəənyɩ bɩ nyɩntam b'ʊ sɔ. Gʊaa k'a mɔɔ zi ba, Zɩ y'a daŋ ʊ. 
Naa ma bɩ, mɔɔ heer a zɛ kʊ ma, bɩ, kʊ m a hɩ mɔɔ ʊ lɔ? Kʊ m a hɩ, mɔɔ ʊ: M Zɩ, mɔɔ bʊmbɔ wakatɩ naa do ma gɛɛ? Bɩ, wakatɩ naa do minto y'a ka, mɔɔ n bʊr. 
Zɩ, ɩ lɛ bɔ ɩ tɔ ʊ.» Leer n a to brama, an a hɩ, a ʊ: «Mɔɔ n lɛ bɔ ʊ, bɩ mɔɔ yɩ m yarɛ, m m lɛ bɔ ʊ.» 
Gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ nyɩnta b'ʊ, ŋnɩ leer bɩ ma rɔ wɔɔ y'a hɩrɛ, ŋ ʊ, brama ɩ həən si. Gɔsɩnnɔ ʊ, malɛɛka ɩ meer ba kan kɩ. 
Yeezuu n a hɩ, a ʊ: «Leer naa do n bɔ mɔɔ minto y, bɔ a ʊ, awɔɔ minto. 
Durnya naa do dʊdɔ karɛ n naa kɛɛrɛ, bɩ, n lam durnya naa do cir bɩ ra, an bɔ garga ra. 
Bɩ kʊ n mɔɔ busu brama durnya burə ʊ, mɔɔ gʊɔɔ haay gaarɛ m zi.» 
Meerbaarɔ nɔɔn duro ba Yeezuu ʊ, k'a a zɛ zaa hɩnka. 
Gʊɔɔ wɔɔ n a nyɩ bɔ nʊ, ŋ ʊ: «*Lɛrɔ ci b'a dɩnda wɔɔ m, a ʊ: *Krista gɔɔtam dɔmɩm k'a nyarɛ ba ʊ bɩ nɔ ʊ. Ɩbɩɩ ba lɔ, ɩ n'a hɩrɛ, ɩ ʊ, n Gʊaanyɩ bɩ busum brama? Gʊaanyɩ naa do, nka nɩ?» 
Yeezuu n a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «Lɛɛgure gɔɔta awɔɔ minto dɔmɩm poore nɔ ʊ. Bala, lɛɛgure k'a gɔɔta an ta ʊ bɩ, k'a ta a nɔ ʊ, a b'a ka monsigə bɩ n zɛ awɔɔ ra y. Bala, gʊaa k'a nɩ tam monsigə ʊ bɩ n a dɔ kʊ ta an nawʊm ka y. 
Kʊ lɛɛgure bɩ gɔɔta an ta ʊ bɩ, k'a sɩra ka lɛɛgure b'ʊ, k'a a ba lɛɛgure nyɩnɔ.» Yeezuu k'a meer ba kan ŋ kɩ ncɩnaaʊ bɩ, a a hʊr an a nyaakʊm da ŋ lɛɛ. 
Baa kan hoserlo k'a ŋ ba ŋ mɩm ʊ rɔ wɔɔ kɩ haay, ŋ n sɩra ka ʊ y, 
kʊ hɔ kʊ *wosolɛsinnɩsorazaa Ezayii a hɩ n bɩ n kʊ yɩ: ‹Zuuba, nka ɩ hɔ kʊ wɔɔ hɩ bɩ si? Nka Zuuba a paŋŋa hɩnka nɩ?› 
Ezayii deem-deem b'a hɩ kʊ bɔ minto y'a ka, ŋ b'a dam ma, ŋn'a si y: 
‹A ŋ mɩm ta, an ŋ heer ka, kʊ ŋ mɩm b'a da ma an lɛɛ yɩ, kʊ ŋ heer b'a da ma an a jɩ ma y, kʊ ŋ b'a bɔ a taam, kʊ m ŋ waa y.› 
Ezayii naa do hɩ, bala, a Yeezuu lɛbɔʊrɛ bɩ yɩ, an a mim dɩnda. 
Kan bɩ kɩ haay, jɩtannɔ wɔɔ barla, gʊta a mim si. Bɩ, *farizɛɛnrɔ wɔɔ minto, ŋ bɩr a kasɛtɩ karɛ y. Bala, ŋ yɩ nyi bam, ŋ y'a la a ra *sokʊmacɛ b'ʊ. 
Gʊɔɔ lɛbɔʊrɛ ŋ n'a ŋʊarɛ, ŋ bɩr a kam Woso hɔ ma y. 
Yeezuu n a leer busu, an a hɩ, a ʊ: «Gʊaa k'a sɩra ka mɔɔ ʊ bɩ, sɩra bɛɛ a ka mɔɔ ʊ y, Zɩ k'a mɔɔ nyɔɔ b'a sɩra ka ʊ. 
Bɩ gʊaa k'a nɩ mɔɔ yɩrɛ, Zɩ k'a mɔɔ nyɔɔ b'a n'a yɩrɛ. 
Mɔɔ m lɛɛgure, m bʊr durnya ʊ, kʊ gʊaa k'a nɩ sɩra kam mɔɔ ʊ bɩ bɩ gɔɔta monsigə ʊ y. 
Kʊ gʊaa mɔɔ lemim ma, b'a n a si y, mɔɔ bɛɛ n n'a dʊdɔ karɛ y. Bala, mɔɔ n bʊr kʊ m bɩ durnya dʊdɔ ka y, bʊr mɔɔ ʊ kʊ m bɩ durnya bʊmbɔ. 
Gʊaa k'a kaanm mɔɔ m, a n mɔɔ lemim bɩ si, a zaa dʊdɔkər ta ʊ. Lemim kʊ mɔɔ hɩ bɩ y'a dʊdɔ kam durnya nyarɛ hinni ʊ. 
Mɔɔ n m mɩŋŋa mim dɩnda y, Mɔɔ Zɩ k'a mɔɔ nyɔɔ b'ɩ hɔ kʊ mɔɔ m ga m, m a hɩ bɩ hɩ mɔɔ m, kʊ m a hɩ. 
Mɔɔ dɔ mɔɔ ʊ, a lɛ bɩ, *mɩsɩrbaa k'a nyarɛ ba ʊ bɩ m. Hɔ kʊ mɔɔ n'a hɩm bɩ, a hɩ mɔɔ nawʊrɛ amba mɔɔ Zɩ a hɩ mɔɔ m bɩ m.»
