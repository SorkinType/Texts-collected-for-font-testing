Zibəəzannɔ Ci.
5.
Gʊaa deem n ta ʊ, a tɔ m Ananɩyas, an kʊ ma kan a lʊ Safɩɩra kɩ ŋn'a tara hɛɛ. 
A n kʊ ma kan a lʊ bɩ kɩ, an busoo bɩ bɔ ʊ an a cɩnta, an a mɩsɩ bɩ sa an aa a ka kristazibəəzannɔ wɔɔ ʊ. 
Pɩyɛɛr n a hɩ, a ʊ: «Ananɩyas, bɔ m Sʊtaana n'ɩbɩɩ heer han? Ɩbɩɩ ŋʊaar ba Sɛnt-Ɛspri m, ɩ tara k'ɩbɩɩ hɛɛ bɩ busoo bɩ bɔ ʊ. 
Ɩbɩɩ n ɩ da ma ɩ tara bɩ hɛɛrɛ to, ɩ ba ɩ hɔ ra? Gɛɛ, k'ɩ hɛɛ, ɩ busoo bɩ sa ɩ hɔ k'ɩ lɛ n taa bɩ bam na? A ba ɩbɩɩ ʊ lɔ, ɩ naa da ɩ heer ʊ? Ŋʊaar bɛɛ ɩbɩɩ ba gʊɔɔ nɩ y, a ba ɩbɩɩ ʊ Woso m.» 
Ananɩyas k'a naa ma bɩ, an a do, an ga. Nyibəə gʊta gasʊ gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ minno nɔɔn duro ma rɔ wɔɔ ʊ. 
Gʊɔɔ pooro n bʊr, ŋnɩ b'a gər bɩ sa, ŋnɩ pɛɛra burgu ma, ŋn'aa a bir. 
A maasɩrɛ ba, an a zɔ wakatɩ kaakʊ ma, a lʊ bɩ n bɩ gasʊ, an a dɔ kʊ bɔ ɩ ba y. 
Pɩyɛɛr n a lar, a ʊ: «Tara bɩ k'awɔɔ hɛɛ bɩ, a lɛ paan… m naa ra?» A n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «Ɩɩn…, a lɛ n bɩ bala.» 
Pɩyɛɛr n a ya, an a lar, a ʊ: «A ba awɔɔ lɔ a a lɛ ba deem k'a Zuuba Sɛnt-Ɛspri bɩ makra ba? Ɩ tʊr ka gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ yaa ɩbɩɩ zim bɩ bir lɔ wɔɔ gam heer ma hallɛ ma, ŋ y'ɩbɩɩ busum sɔ.» 
Lɛɛm bɩ, Safɩɩra a do Pɩyɛɛr jɩr ʊ, an ga. Kʊ gʊɔɔ pooro wɔɔ bʊr bɩ, ŋ bɩ dɩga a a ga, ŋn'a busu k'a yaa bir a zim bɩ jɩr ʊ. 
Dabɔʊ gʊta sɩrakarɛzannɔ wɔɔ nyasʊ haay, kan gʊaa haay k'a minno nɔɔn duro ma bɩ kɩ. 
Kristazibəəzannɔ wɔɔ ɩ makrarɔ kan hoserlo kɩ bam gʊta zamaa bɩ bire ʊ. Ŋ haay a zɛ kʊ ra deem Salomɔɔn laŋŋa taa b'ʊ. 
Bɩ, gʊaa k'a n sɩra ka Woso ʊ paan… bɩ bɩr yɛm an n'a zɛm kʊ ra kan ŋ kɩ y. Kan bɩ kɩ, zamaa bɩ n n'a lɛ bɔrɛ ŋ ʊ. 
Gʊɔɔ cir gʊta, yaarɔ kan lannɔ kɩ ɩ sɩra kam Zuuba ʊ, ŋn'a zarlɛ ŋ la. 
N n bʊr yaabazannɔ m zaarɔ bire ʊ, ŋnɩ ŋ wʊta gargʊrɔ ʊ gɛɛ gʊraŋŋarɔ ʊ, kʊ Pɩyɛɛr bʊr an nɩ cenim b'ʊ gɛɛ a hinni y'a kam niŋŋoo ra gɛ. 
Gʊɔɔ gʊta n a to yaabazannɔ m Zerizalɛm gʊrgarɔ ʊ kan gʊɔɔ kʊ zinəro nɩ kan ŋ kɩ rɔ wɔɔ kɩ, ŋnɩ bʊr ŋ nɩ, ŋn'a waa haay. 
Dɔmɩm bɩ do ʊ bɩ, wosocɛmannɩbər jɩtaarɛ kan a jɛzannɔ kɩ, Sadisɛɛnrɔ wɔɔ, wuti kan jɩmbaa heerbusu kɩ. 
Ŋ n a ka, ŋnɩ kristazibəəzannɔ wɔɔ gʊr gʊɔɔ mɩm m, ŋnɩ ŋ du lɔnnɔcɛ ʊ. 
Gunuu ʊ bɩ, Zuuba malɛɛka bʊr, an bɩ lɔnnɔcɛ bɩ lɛ rɔ wɔɔ gʊngʊ, an ŋ bɔ, an a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: 
«K'a ta a yaa jɩm Wosocɛ ʊ b'ʊ, a mɩsɩrbaa daa naa do minno nɔɔn duro haay hɩ zamaa bɩ m.» 
Kʊ ŋ naa ma bɩ, dɔ k'a mɛ bɩ, ŋ ta Wosocɛ ʊ b'ʊ k'a yaa dɩnda gʊɔɔ wɔɔ m. Wosocɛmannɩbər jɩtaarɛ bɩ n lee ku kan a jɛzannɔ wɔɔ kɩ. A n a ka ŋnɩ dʊdɔkənno wɔɔ bir haay, Israyɛl gʊɔɔgusinno wɔɔ haay, an nyɔɔ da kʊ n aa kristazibəəzannɔ wɔɔ bɔ lɔnnɔcɛ ʊ, n nɩ bʊr ŋ nɩ. 
Gʊɔɔ wɔɔ kʊ ŋ ta bɩ, kʊ ŋ yaa lee ku b'ʊ bɩ, ŋ n ŋ yɩ lɔnnɔcɛ ʊ b'ʊ y. Ŋ n a wusigə ŋn'aa a hɩ, ŋ ʊ: 
«Wɔɔ yaa lɔnnɔcɛ bɩ yɩ ŋn'a ta mɩŋŋa, a dɔmazannɔ wɔɔ n nɩ jɩnɩm cɛrɔ wɔɔ lɛ ma. Kʊ wɔɔ gʊ bɩ, wɔɔ n gɔsɩ yɩ a nɔ ʊ y.» 
Wosocɛ dɔmazannɔ cir bɩ k'a naa ma kan wosocɛmannɩbənno jɩtannɔ wɔɔ kɩ bɩ, ŋ zɛ kədə kristazibəəzannɔ wɔɔ minto, ŋn'a mɩŋŋa larm naa k'a ba ncɩnaaʊ bɩ, a jɩ nyɩntam lɔ? 
Gʊaa n bʊr an b'a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «Gʊɔɔ k'awɔɔ n gʊr a ŋ du rɔ wɔɔ ɩ Wosocɛ ʊ ŋn'ɩ hɔ dɩndam gʊɔɔ m.» 
Wosocɛ dɔmazaa bɩ n ta kan a zibəəzannɔ kɩ kristazibəəzannɔ wɔɔ zi, an aa ŋ gaa an bʊr ŋ nɩ. Ŋ n paŋŋa ba ŋ ma y, bala, ŋ y'ɩ nyi bam zamaa bɩ m, ŋ y'a a gʊngʊɛɛn jaarɔ m. 
Ŋ n ŋ gaa, ŋnɩ bɩ ŋ hɩnka dʊdɔkənno wɔɔ m. Wosocɛmannɩbər jɩtaarɛ bɩ n ŋ lar, a ʊ: 
«Wɔɔ hɩ awɔɔ m mɩŋŋa, ʊ ʊ: A b'a ya, a tɔ naa do hɩ a karɩnda nɔ ʊ y, awɔɔ karɩnda bɩ n bɔkarɛ an Zerizalɛm han, a lɛ n taa k'a gʊaa naa do ma bɩ ka wɔɔ mim la.» 
Pɩyɛɛr kan kristazibəəzannɔ wɔɔ kɩ a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, ŋ ʊ: «A a ga m, k'ɩ Woso lɛ si, k'ɩ gʊɔɔ to. 
Wɔɔ zɩrɔ Woso, Yeezuu k'awɔɔ zeŋŋə gɔ ma a zɛ gər bɩ, mɩm bɔ ʊ. 
Bɩ, Woso a busu a bɩsɩ ʊ arzana ʊ, an cirbəə ka ʊ, an a ba bʊmbɔrɛzaa, k'an a ka kʊ Israyɛl gʊɔɔ n a bɔ a taam, ŋnɩ ŋ sugur yɩ. 
Minno nɔɔn duro kasɛtɩzannɔ m wɔɔ m, wɔɔ kan *Sɛnt-Ɛspri kʊ Woso a ka gʊɔɔ kʊ n n'a lɛ sim nɔ wɔɔ ʊ kɩ.» 
Minno nɔɔn duro ŋ heer busu, ŋn'a hɔɔn darɛ ŋ zɩnzɛrɛ ma. 
Gʊaa deem n wuti dʊdɔkənno wɔɔ bire ʊ. Farizɛn m, a tɔ m Gamalɩyɛl, wosocikarɩnsaamba m, gʊɔɔ wɔɔ n'a daŋ ʊ. A n lɛ ka kʊ ŋ nɩ bɔ kristazibəəzannɔ wɔɔ m cɩna, 
an a hɩ, a ʊ: «Israyɛl zannɔ, k'a dundo a zi mɩŋŋa kan hɔ k'awɔɔ k'a ba gʊɔɔ nɔɔn duro m bɩ kɩ. 
Dɔɔraarɔ kʊ ŋ cem dɩɩtaa rɔ nɔɔn nɔ ʊ, Tedas a hɩnka wɔɔ m, a ʊ: Gʊaa gʊta n'awɔɔ m. A n gʊɔɔ gaa, an ŋ so kʊ ma, ŋ lɛ n a zɔ zɔrsi ma. Ŋ y'a mɩŋŋa zɛ, a karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ haay hɩsɩga da. Gɔsɩ n gɔɔta y. 
Bɩ jɛ ʊ, lɛsarɛ dɔmɩm ʊ, Galilee gʊaa Zidas a hɩnka wɔɔ m. Gʊɔɔ gʊta nɩ zure a jɛ ʊ. Bɩ mɩŋŋa ga sɔ, gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ nɩ zure a jɛ ʊ rɔ wɔɔ n hɩsɩga da. 
Bɩ y'a ka, mɔɔ n'a hɩrɛ awɔɔ m kɛɛrɛ, k'a gʊɔɔ nɔɔn duro to kʊ ŋ nɩ cem, a bɩ hɔsɩ ba ŋ nɩ y. Meerbaa naa do kan zibəə naa do kɩ, kʊ gʊɔɔ hɔɔ nɩ, ŋ nyaanlɛ a zim. 
Bɩ kʊ Woso hɔɔ nɩ, awɔɔ b'a dam ma a ka, ŋ n nyaam nɩ y. K'a dundo a zi, a b'a ka, a bɩ yar ba kan Woso kɩ y.» Ŋ yɛ Gamalɩyɛl meerbaa bɩ ma. 
Ŋ nɩ kristazibəəzannɔ wɔɔ bir, ŋn'a ka, ŋnɩ ŋ zɛ madɩɩrɔ m, ŋn'a hɩ ŋ nɩ, ŋ ʊ, ŋ b'a ya, ŋnɩ Yeezuu tɔ hɩ y, ŋnɩ wɔ saa ŋ ma. 
Kristazibəəzannɔ wɔɔ n bɔ dʊdɔkarɛ bɩncɛ ʊ b'ʊ kan heernyɔɔ kɩ, kʊ Woso yɛ ŋn'a ga fɩryɩrɛ m Yeezuu tɔ ma bɩ minto. 
Dɔmɩm biyəə, amba Wosocɛ ʊ gɛɛ har ʊ, ŋ bɩr Yeezuu Krista lemim nyɩnta bɩ dɩndarɛ kan a padarɛ kɩ tore y.
