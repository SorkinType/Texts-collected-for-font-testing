Likki.
16.
Yeezuu n a hɩ a karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ m, a ʊ: «Arzakazaa deem ɩ nyɩnta ʊ, a zibəənyɩ n ta ʊ, an nɩ dɩgarɛ a hɔnyɩ mim ma. Gʊɔɔ n b'a hɩ arzakazaa bɩ m, ŋ ʊ: Awɔɔ zibəənyɩ bɩ y'awɔɔ arzaka bɩ zarm. 
A n a bir, an a hɩ m, a ʊ: bɔ minto m n n'ɩbɩɩ mim dɩndam mɔɔ m, ncɩnaaʊ? Ɩ hɔcɩntarɛ ciro ka m ʊ, bala ɩbɩɩ b'ɩ yarɛ, ɩ mɔɔ zi bɩ ba y. 
Zibəənyɩ bɩ n'a mɩŋŋa larm, a ʊ: Kʊ m a ba lɔ? Kʊ mɔɔ zuuba mɔɔ bɔ zibəə b'ʊ bɩ? Mɔɔ paŋŋa ba ʊ kʊ m dam wʊ ra? Hɔyɩnyɛrɛ nyannɩ ɩ mɔɔ zɛm. 
Hɔ kʊ mɔɔ n n'a bam bɩ mɔɔ dɔ, kʊ m bɩ bɔ zibəə b'ʊ, kʊ gʊɔɔ n m si a har ʊ. 
A n gʊɔɔ kʊ zuuba bɩ zɩnnɔ ta ŋ lɛ rɔ wɔɔ bir deem-deem, an lɛɛkarɛzaa bɩ lar, a ʊ: Ɩbɩɩ ta mɔɔ zuuba bɩ zɩm m karɔ? 
Bɩ n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: Nyɔɔ gər zɔrɔ. A n'a hɩ bɩ m, a ʊ: Ba ɩ sɩraci m na, a si, ɩ nyɩnta zɔɔ-zɔɔ ɩ gʊrsɩra ba busoor. 
A n a ya, an a vanta lar, a ʊ: B'ɩbɩɩ, ɩ ta m karɔ? A ʊ: Hɔyaa yɔrgɔ zɔrɔ. Zibəənyɩ bɩ n a hɩ m, a ʊ: Ba ɩbɩɩ sɩraci n na, a gʊrsɩra ba businnyə. 
Zuuba bɩ n a lɛ bɔ zibəənyɩ b'ʊ, zi k'a ba ncɩnaaʊ bɩ tɔ ma. Her naa ʊ gʊɔɔ wɔɔ hɔɔn ka kan kʊ kɩ, an lɛ da lɛɛgurezannɔ wɔɔ ra.» 
«Sɩra mɔɔ n'a hɩrɛ awɔɔ m, k'a durnya arzaka bɩ sa a gʊaasɩbabaa da m gʊɔɔ ma. Dɔmɩm niŋŋə k'a bʊr a b'awɔɔ ʊ bɩ, Woso y'awɔɔ sire a har ʊ. 
Gʊaa k'a sɩra ba kan hɔ poore kɩ, a sɩra barɛ a gʊta m sɔ. Bɩ gʊaa k'a n sɩra ba hɔ poore m, a bɩ sɩra bam hɔ gʊta m sɔ y. 
Bɩ minto bɩ, k'awɔɔ n sɩra ba kan durnya naa arzakarɔ kɩ y, nka nɩ hɔnyɩ paan… bɩ karɛ awɔɔ ʊ? 
Bɩ k'awɔɔ n sɩra ba gʊaa hɔ m, nka n n'awɔɔ mɩŋŋɔɔ hɔ bɩ karɛ awɔɔ ʊ? 
Zibəənyɩ ba ʊ a n n'a dam ma, an zuubɔɔ hɩɩyɔɔ zi ba y. A deem ŋʊarɛ, an sɔ deem m. Gɛɛ an bɩr kan deem kɩ, an kaanm deem m. Awɔɔ b'a dam ma a Woso ŋʊa, a busoo ŋʊa sɔ y.» 
Farizɛɛnrɔ busoo ŋuənno wɔɔ y'a tʊr kam naa haay ma, ŋn'ɩ yaa wʊrɛ a meerbaa bɩ ma. 
Yeezuu n a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «A ka awɔɔ nawʊm ma k'a bɩr tɩrga gʊɔɔ mɩm ʊ. Bɩ Woso awɔɔ heer dɔ, bala, hɔ niŋŋə kʊ gʊɔɔ n'a lɛ bɔŋ ʊ bɩ, hɔ vəm m bɩ Woso mɩm ʊ. 
Lɛrɔ kan wosolɛsinnɩsorazannɔ wɔɔ kɩ ta an Zaan dɔmɩm ku. N y'a sɩŋŋɩda ba dɔmɩm bɩ do ʊ, n nɩ Woso cirbəə lemim nyɩnta pa dam, gʊaa haay n'a tɔŋ ʊ k'a gasʊ ʊ kan paŋŋa kɩ. 
Brama kan tara kɩ cenle naanaa an lɛ da lɛ minnyɩ deem taan… k'a nɩ gɔɔtam bɩ ra. 
Bɩ y'a ka, gʊaa biyəə k'a nɩ larɛ a lʊ ra, an n'a vanta sam bɩ, jaandər m. Bɩ gʊaa k'a lʊ k'a zim la ra bɩ sa, jaandər m sɔ.» 
«Arzakazaa deem ɩ nyɩnta ʊ, an nɩ huu wɩsɩrɔ kʊ ŋ lɛ ŋ kʊsɩ dure a ma, an nɩ hɔbɩrɛ mɩŋŋa bɩrɛ dɔmɩm haay, an nɩ mɩsɩrbaa nyɩnta bam. 
Cicirzaa deem k'a tɔ m Lazaar bɩ nɩ wʊtam arzakazaa bɩ cɛlɛ ma, a mɛ haay bɩ, nyinno m. 
A ɩ hɔbɩrɛ kuuro kʊ ŋ n'a lom arzakazaa bɩ hɔbɩrɛ gɔ bɩ ra bɩ ŋʊam k'a bɩ, b'a bɩr a yɩrɛ y. Kan naa kɩ haay, jino bʊr ŋn'a nyinno mɩmmɛɛsʊm. 
Cicirzaa bɩ n bɩ ga. Malɛɛkarɔ n a busu ŋnɩ ta m Abraham jɩr ʊ. Arzakazaa bɩ m bɩ ga sɔ, n n a bir. 
Gəəno bɩncɛ ʊ b'ʊ bɩ, arzakazaa b'ɩ fɩr yɩm gʊta. A n a mɩm busu brama an a to an Abraham yɩ laatʊ, Lazaar n'a jɩr ʊ. 
A n zee da, a ʊ: M zɩ Abraham, mɔɔ cicir zɛ, k'ɩ Lazaar nyɔɔ k'an a nyʊkʊm nyɔɔ hi ma k'an b'a tɔkʊ m nɛɛm la, k'an bɩsa, bala, mɔɔ ɩ fɩr yɩm gʊta sɛ naa do nɔ ʊ. 
Abraham n a nyɩ bɔ nʊ, a ʊ: M nyɩnnɩ, a tɔ da ɩ nɔ ʊ, ɩ ʊ, ɩbɩɩ nyɩnta mɩŋŋa ɩ mɩsɩrbaa nɔ ʊ, Lazaar n a bʊnyaa yɩ. Naa ma bɩ, a heernyɔɔ yɩ naa ʊ, ɩbɩɩ nɩ fɩr nɔ ʊ. 
Bɩ, yaa gʊta y'awɔɔ kan wɔɔ kɩ bire ʊ kʊ gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ n'a tom wɔɔ zeezi naa ʊ k'a ta awɔɔ zi rɔ wɔɔ b'a da ma, ŋnɩ cem y, awɔɔ zeezi sɔ, kʊ ŋ b'a da ma ŋnɩ bʊr naa ʊ y. 
Arzakazaa bɩ n a hɩ, a ʊ: M zɩ, mɔɔ y'a yɛrɛ ɩbɩɩ han, k'ɩ Lazaar nyɔɔ mɔɔ zɩ har ʊ, 
bala, mɔɔ danzannɔ ta ʊ soor, k'an aa a hɩ ŋ nɩ, kʊ brɔɔ bɩ bʊr fɩr yɩrɛ bɩncɛ naa do ʊ sɔ y. 
Abraham n a hɩ m, a ʊ: Ŋ yɩ Moyiisi ciro m kan wosolɛsinnɩsorazannɔ hɔɔ kɩ, ŋn'a tʊr ka ŋ lemim ma. 
Arzakazaa bɩ n a hɩ, a ʊ: Ayy…, m zɩ Abraham, kʊ gʊaa a to gəəno zi naa ʊ an ta b'ʊ, ŋ y'a bɔrɛ a taam. 
Abraham n a hɩ, a ʊ: Kʊ ŋ n a tʊr ka Moyiisi ma kan wosolɛsinnɩsorazannɔ wɔɔ kɩ, baa kʊ gʊaa a mɩm bɔ ʊ gəəno bire ʊ, ŋ b'a sire y.»
