Zaan.
11.
Gʊaa ɩ nyɩnta ʊ, an nɩ yaaba m. A tɔ m Lazaar. Betanii gʊaa m. Maarii kan a cɛmba Martɩ kɩ kʊ m bɩ. 
Maarii bɩ do ɩ tidəər nɔ Zuuba ma, an a minkə gaa, an a nʊnnɔ a gannɔ ma bɩ. A dakumboor Lazaar ɩ bɩ laafɩɩ bɩ. 
A danlʊrɔ wɔɔ n nyɔɔ da, ŋn'aa a hɩ Yeezuu m, ŋ ʊ: «Gʊaa k'ɩbɩɩ ŋʊa bɩ bɩ laafɩɩ y.» 
Kʊ Yeezuu a ma bɩ, a a hɩ, a ʊ: «Yaaba naa do, zɛ yaaba bɛɛ y, zɛ an nawʊm Woso lɛbɔʊrɛ m. A minto n'a kam Woso Nyɩ bɩ n daʊrɛ yɩ.» 
Yeezuu nyɩnta an Martɩ ŋʊa kan a danzaa bɩ kɩ, Lazaar kɩ. 
Baa k'a a dɔ a ʊ, Lazaar bɩ laafɩɩ bɩ, a gɔɔta bɩncɛ k'an ta ʊ b'ʊ dɔmɩm hɩɩya. 
Bɩ jɛ ʊ bɩ, a a hɩ a karɩndanyɩnɔ m, a ʊ: «Ʊ ʊ bɔ, ʊ ta Zidee ʊ.» 
Karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ n a hɩ, ŋ ʊ: «Karɩnsaamba, zɩɩfʊrɔ a ka ma dɩɩtaa k'a ɩbɩɩ gʊngʊɛɛn jaarɔ m, ɩbɩɩ lɛ taa k'ɩ ɩ ya, ɩ ɩ bɔ, ɩ ta b'ʊ ra?» 
Yeezuu n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «A n a sa dɔgta m an ta yirbəə ra lɛɛgure bɛɛ m ta ʊ wakatɩ bueerehɩɩya ra? Kʊ gʊaa ɩ tam lɛɛgure ʊ a bɩr a gəm zɛrɛ y, bala, a ɩ durnya naa do lɛɛgure yɩm. 
Bɩ kʊ gʊaa ɩ tam gunuu ʊ, a y'a gəm zɛm, bala, a mɩm bɩr lɛɛ yɩrɛ y.» 
K'a meer naa do ba an a nya bɩ, a a hɩ, a ʊ: «Wɔɔ gʊaasɩba Lazaar hinceem ba, bɩ mɔɔ tam, m aa a gu.» 
A karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ n a hɩ m, ŋ ʊ: «Zuuba, kʊ hinceem a ba, a laafɩɩ yɩrɛ.» 
Kʊ sɩra nɩ, Yeezuu bɩ, a ŋʊa a ʊ, k'a hɩ a ʊ, Lazaar ga. Ŋ n bɔkarɛ, ŋn'a hɔɔn darɛ, ŋ ʊ, hinceem paan… mim an n'a dɩndarɛ. 
Yeezuu n a hɩ ŋ nɩ paan…, a ʊ: «Lazaar ga. 
Bɩ kʊ mɔɔ n nyɩnta b'ʊ bɩ, mɔɔ ɩ heernyɔɔ m awɔɔ tɔ ma, k'a a da ma, a sɩra ka. K'a wuti, k'ʊ ta zi.» 
Toma kʊ n n'a birm Didim b'a hɩ karɩndanyɩnɔ gɔsɩnnɔ wɔɔ m, a ʊ: «Ʊ ta, kan kɩ, ʊ aa gɩnga sɔ.» 
Yeezuu k'a lee ku bɩ, a yaa dɩga a n Lazaar bir. A birle dɔmɩm si lɛɛm. 
Bɩ kʊ Betanii bɩ laatʊ Zerizalɛm ma bɩ, a ga kʊ a ba cilo kaakʊ. 
Zɩɩfʊrɔ wɔɔ bʊr gʊta k'a bɩ yaa da Martɩ m kan Maarii kɩ, ŋ dakumboor bɩ garɛ minto. 
Kʊ Martɩ a ma, n ʊ, Yeezuu ɩ zɛm bɩ, a ta, an aa a zɛ ʊ, Maarii n gɔɔta har ʊ, an nɩ nyɩntam. 
Martɩ n a hɩ Yeezuu m, a ʊ: «Zuuba, kʊ nyɩnta ɩbɩɩ ʊ naa ʊ, mɔɔ dakumboor bɩ bɩ garɛ y. 
Bɩ, an gɔɔta naa, mɔɔ dɔ mɔɔ ʊ, hɔ haay k'ɩbɩɩ n n'a yɛm Woso han bɩ, a y'a karɛ ɩbɩɩ ʊ.» 
Yeezuu n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «Ɩbɩɩ dakumboor bɩ y'a mɩm bɔŋ ʊ.» 
Martɩ n a hɩ, a ʊ: «Mɔɔ dɔ, a y'a mɩm bɔŋ ʊ gəəno mɩmbɔʊrɛ dɔmɩm ʊ durnya nyarɛ ma.» 
Yeezuu n a hɩ m, a ʊ: «Mɔɔ m mɩmbɔʊrɛ, mɔɔ m *mɩsɩrbaa. Gʊaa k'a sɩra ka mɔɔ ʊ bɩ, baa k'a zaa ga, a mɩsɩrbaa yɩrɛ. 
Bɩ gʊaa k'an mɩsɩr, an nɩ sɩra kam mɔɔ ʊ, a bɩ gam fɩɩga y. Ɩbɩɩ sɩra ka naa ʊ ra?» 
A n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «Ɩɩn… Zuuba, mɔɔ sɩra ka, mɔɔ ʊ, ɩbɩɩ m *Krista, Woso Nyɩ k'a a ga m an bʊr durnya ʊ bɩ.» 
K'a meer bɩ ba ncɩnaaʊ bɩ, a a hʊr, an aa a danzaa Maarii bɩ bir, an a hɩ m sɩnsɛɛrɛ m, a ʊ: «Karɩnsaamba bʊr, an n'ɩbɩɩ birm.» 
Maarii k'a naa ma bɩ, a wuti zɔɔ-zɔɔ, an ta zi. 
Bɩ ma bɩ, Yeezuu n gasʊ kʊ b'ʊ cɩna y. A gɔɔta an nɩ bɩncɛ kʊ Martɩ a zɛ ʊ b'ʊ. 
Zɩɩfʊrɔ wɔɔ nyɩnta kan Maarii kɩ har ʊ b'ʊ, ŋn'ɩ bra bam nɩ. Kʊ ŋ y'a yɩ an wuti zɔɔ-zɔɔ an bɔ bɩ, ŋ zu a jɛ ʊ, ŋn'a hɔɔn darɛ, ŋ ʊ, ta an nawʊm yaa ra b'ʊ k'a yaa nyi ka. 
Maarii k'a yaa lee ku bɩncɛ kʊ Yeezuu n ta ʊ b'ʊ bɩ, k'a a yɩ lɛɛm bɩ, a sʊnta a taa an n'a hɩm nɩ, a ʊ: «Zuuba, kʊ nyɩnta ɩbɩɩ ʊ naa ʊ, mɔɔ dakumboor bɩ bɩ garɛ y.» 
Yeezuu k'a yɩ, an nɩ nyi kam kan zɩɩfʊrɔ kʊ ŋ yaa a da zaa ʊ rɔ wɔɔ kɩ bɩ, a heer a zɛ kʊ ma. 
A n a hɩ, a ʊ: «A bir awɔɔ ʊ ka?» Ŋ n a nyɩ bɔ nʊ, ŋ ʊ: «Zuuba, bʊr ɩ dɩga a.» 
Yeezuu n nyi ka. 
Zɩɩfʊrɔ wɔɔ n'a hɩrɛ, ŋ ʊ: «K'a dɩga a, a a ŋʊa gʊta!» 
Gɔsɩnnɔ n'a hɩrɛ ŋ barla, ŋ ʊ: «Gʊaa k'a bʊʊ k'a yɩ kan bʊʊbaa kɩ mɩm gʊ bɩ, a b'a dam ma, an a ka Lazaar bɩ gam na?» 
Yeezuu heer n a ya, an a zɛ kʊ ma, an a hʊr an ta yaa ra b'ʊ. Nyɩnta a ʊ ci yaa, ŋ n jaa ta a lɛ bɩ rɛ. 
Yeezuu n a hɩ, a ʊ: «K'a jaa bɩ gʊ rɛ.» Gər bɩ danlʊ Martɩ bɩ n a nyɩ bɔ nʊ, a ʊ: «Zuuba, a ji nyɩntaŋ ʊ, a dɔmɩm si hɔ n naa.» 
Yeezuu n a nyɩ bɔ nʊ, a ʊ: «Mɔɔ n a hɩ ɩbɩɩ m, mɔɔ ʊ, k'ɩ sɩra ka mɔɔ ʊ, ɩ Woso lɛbɔʊrɛ yɩm na?» 
Ŋ n jaa bɩ gʊ rɛ. Yeezuu n a bɔɔta, an dɩga brama, an a hɩ, a ʊ: «M Zɩ, mɔɔ ɩ barka darɛ ɩbɩɩ m, ɩbɩɩ k'ɩ mɔɔ yaadarɛ si bɩ minto. 
Mɔɔ dɔ, mɔɔ ʊ, ɩbɩɩ ɩ mɔɔ yaadarɛ sim dɔmɩm haay, bɩ meer bɩ ba mɔɔ ʊ gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ bɩr mɔɔ ma naa ʊ rɔ wɔɔ minto, kʊ ŋ n'a da ma, ŋn'a si, ŋ ʊ, ɩbɩɩ ɩ mɔɔ nyɔɔ.» 
K'a meer ba ncɩnaaʊ bɩ, a həən si kan paŋŋa kɩ, a ʊ: «Lazaar, bɔ!» 
Gər bɩ n bɔ, a gannɔ kan a wɔrɔ kɩ nɩ kʊsɩm kan pɛɛrarɔ kɩ, pɛɛra nɩ burgure a meer ma. Yeezuu n a hɩ gʊɔɔ wɔɔ m, a ʊ: «K'a pɛɛrarɔ wɔɔ hʊnhʊr ma, k'a to k'an ta!» 
Zɩɩfʊrɔ wɔɔ duro gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ nyɩnta kan Maarii kɩ jɩr ʊ rɔ wɔɔ, ŋnɩ hɔ kʊ Yeezuu a ba bɩ yɩ rɔ wɔɔ, gʊta sɩra ka ʊ. 
Gʊɔɔ gɔsɩnnɔ ta, ŋn'aa *farizɛɛnrɔ yɩ, ŋnɩ hɔ kʊ Yeezuu a ba bɩ dɩnda ŋ nɩ. 
*Wosocɛmannɩbənno jɩtannɔ kan farizɛɛnrɔ wɔɔ kɩ dʊdɔkənno wɔɔ kɩ a so kʊ ma, ŋn'a hɩ ŋ nɩ, ŋ ʊ: «Bɔ wɔɔ n n'a barɛ? Gʊaa naa do ɩ hoserlo bam gʊta. 
Kʊ wɔɔ to an n'a barɛ ncɩnaaʊ gʊaa haay sɩra kaŋ ʊ, *Rɔm zannɔ zɛm, ŋnɩ bɩ wɔɔ *Wosocɛ bɩ da, kan wɔɔ do bɩ kɩ.» 
Ŋ gʊaa deem k'a tɔ m *Kayiifʊ b'ɩ nyɩnta wosocɛmannɩbər jɩtaarɛ dɔɔraa bɩ do ma, an a hɩ, a ʊ: «Awɔɔ bɩr hɔsɩ jɩ marɛ y. 
Awɔɔ n a dɔ a ʊ, a jɩra kʊ gʊaa deem n ga, gʊaa haay minto kʊ wɔɔ do bɩ b'a zar haay ra?» 
A mɩŋŋa hɔɔndarɛ bɛɛ m meerbaa naa do nɩ y, kʊ wosocɛmannɩbər jɩtaarɛ m dɔɔraa bɩ do ʊ bɩ, Woso lemim naa do da a lɛ ʊ, a ʊ: Pakra m kʊ Yeezuu m ga do bɩ minto, 
bɩ do bɩ minto bɛɛ m bala y, k'a Woso nyɩnɔ kʊ ŋ y'a hɩsɩga bɩ so kʊ ma lɛɛ deem ʊ. 
Hinni bɩ do ʊ, ŋ y'a cɩnta ŋ ʊ, k'a zɛ gər. 
Bɩ minto y'a ka, Yeezuu n a ya, an nɩ bɩmbɩrm paan… zɩɩfʊrɔ jɩtannɔ wɔɔ bire ʊ y. A ta bɩncɛ niŋŋə k'an jɩnjɛɛm poohɔɔn ma b'ʊ, kʊ niŋŋə kʊ n n'a birm Efrayim ʊ b'ʊ, an aa gɔɔta b'ʊ kan a karɩndanyɩnɔ kɩ. 
Dɔmɩm bɩ do ʊ bɩ, *pakkɩ cibsa a zɔ. Pakkɩ bɩ k'a gɔɔta booti m bɩ yirbəə m gʊɔɔ gʊta a to bɔlɛɛmarɔ ʊ, ŋnɩ ta Zerizalɛm ʊ mɩŋŋa *hubɔrɛ zibəə minto. 
Ŋ n'a kam Yeezuu ma, bɩ ma bɩ, ŋ yɩ Wosocɛ ʊ, ŋn'a hɩm kʊ m, ŋ ʊ: «Awɔɔ y'a hɔɔn darɛ, a ʊ lɔ? A bɩ zɛm cibsa bɩ ma ra!» 
*Wosocɛmannɩbənno jɩtannɔ kan *farizɛɛnrɔ wɔɔ kɩ lɛ ka, ŋ ʊ: Gʊaa k'a bɩncɛ dɔ kan a hɩ k'a ku a nyasʊ.
