Likki.
17.
Yeezuu n a hɩ karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ m, a ʊ: «Mim k'a a da ma an gʊaa do bɩ b'a dam ma, a n nyɩnta ʊ y. Bɩ, a pakra bam gʊaa k'a nɩ zɛm nɩ bɩ m. 
Kʊ n bar da jɛnlajaa ma, n n'a da a wer, n n'a da higʊta ʊ jɩra, nyɩnɔ poorero nɔɔn duro deem k'a n'a dam mim ʊ bɩ m. 
K'a dundo a zi mɩŋŋa! K'ɩ danyɩ a zar ɩ ma, k'ɩ dʊndɔ, k'a a bɔ a taam, k'ɩ sugur ka ʊ. 
Bɩ k'a zar ɩbɩɩ ma gallɛm saanhɩna hindeem ʊ, an tɩr a bɔ an bʊr ɩbɩɩ zi an b'a hɩ, a ʊ: Mɔɔ m bɔ m taam, sugur ka m ʊ, k'ɩ sugur ka ʊ.» 
Kristazibəəzannɔ wɔɔ n a hɩ Zuuba m, ŋ ʊ: «Wɔɔ sɩrakarɛ da ra.» 
Zuuba n a hɩ, a ʊ: «Awɔɔ sɩrakarɛ bɩ, kʊ mutardɩ yaa a ku, awɔɔ y'a hɩm gɔ naa do m, a ʊ: Ɩ bɔɔta, ɩ yaa ɩ sɔɔn higʊta ʊ, b'a y'awɔɔ lɛ sim.» 
«Gʊaa bɩ kara n'awɔɔ bire ʊ naa ʊ, a zibəənyɩ n ta ʊ, an nɩ dam wʊm, gɛɛ, an nɩ dɔm hɔbɩsɩ ma. K'a a to poo ʊ, an bʊr, an a hɩ m, a ʊ: A ba zɔɔ ɩ bʊr, ɩ nyɩnta ɩ hɔbɩrɛ bɩ? 
A hɩ bɛɛ an nawʊrɛ, a ʊ: Hɔbɩrɛ ba gum naa ʊ, k'ɩ huuro da ɩ ra, ɩ sa ɩ ba ka m ʊ kʊ m a bɩ, kʊ m a mi, k'ɩ bɔkarɛ ɩ bɩ, ɩ mi ra? 
A yaa dam zibəənyɩ bɩ m barka ra? K'a a lɛ si an a zi bɩ ba bɩ minto ra? Mɔɔ n a si a yaa dam nɩ barka y. 
A maam sɔ kan awɔɔ kɩ. K'awɔɔ hɔ haay kʊ Woso lɛ ka awɔɔ ʊ k'a ba bɩ ba, k'a hɩ, a ʊ: Zibəənyɩnɔ m wɔɔ m bala, wɔɔ ʊ zibəə k'a a ga m bɩ ba.» 
Kʊ Yeezuu zaa sa an nɩ tam Zerizalɛm zeezi bɩ, bɔ a ʊ Galilee kan Samarii kɩ bire ʊ. 
K'a nɩ gasʊm tara deem ʊ bɩ, kusiməro bu b'a zɛ ʊ, ŋnɩ jɩm laatʊ, 
ŋn'a leer busu, ŋ ʊ: «Yeezuu, karɩnsaamba! Wɔɔ cicir zɛ!» 
Yeezuu k'a ŋ yɩ bɩ, a a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «K'a yaa a hɩnka wosocɛmannɩbənno m.» Kʊ ŋ y'a hʊr ŋn'ɩ tam bɩ, ŋ yaa a waa zaa ra, dintim n gʊ ŋ ma. 
Ŋ gʊaa deem k'a yɩ a laafɩɩ yɩ bɩ, a a wusigə an bʊr, an a leer busu, an n'a lɛ bɔm Woso ʊ. 
A n sʊnta Yeezuu taa, an a meer zuutə tara ʊ, an nɩ barka dam nɩ. Gʊaa bɩ, Samarii gʊaa m. 
Yeezuu n a hɩ, a ʊ: «Gʊaa bu haay bɛɛ y'a waa dintim n gʊ ŋ ma ra? Bɩ nɛɛhʊ rɔ wɔɔ be? 
Gɔsɩ n a bɔ ŋ bire ʊ k'a b'a lɛ bɔ Woso ʊ, kʊ saana naa do bɛɛ y.» 
A n a hɩ m, a ʊ: «Wuti ɩ ta, ɩ sɩrakarɛ ɩ bʊmbɔ.» 
Farizɛɛnrɔ wɔɔ n Yeezuu lar, ŋ ʊ: «Woso cirbəə bɩ zɛ an nawʊm brɔ?» A n a nyɩ bɔ ŋ nʊ, a ʊ: «Woso cirbəə bɩ bɩr zɛrɛ, an nɩ yɩrɛ y. 
N b'a hɩrɛ, ŋ ʊ: Woso cirbəə bɩ ba n nɩ, gɛɛ, a ɩ naa ʊ y!» A n a hɩ ŋ nɩ kɛɛrɛ, a ʊ: «K'a dɩga a, Woso cirbəə bɩ y'awɔɔ bire ʊ.» 
A n bɔkarɛ, an a hɩ karɩndanyɩnɔ m, a ʊ: «Dɔmɩnnɔ ta ʊ ŋn'ɩ zɛm, awɔɔ n n'a ŋʊam k'a Gʊaanyɩ bɩ yɩ hindeem ʊ bala, b'awɔɔ ba yɩrɛ y. 
N y'a hɩrɛ awɔɔ m, ŋ ʊ: A ba n nɩ, gɛɛ, a ɩ naa ʊ! A bɩ ta y, a b'a si a ta y. 
Bala, Gʊaanyɩ bɩ zɛrɛ dɔmɩm bɩ, zɛ an nawʊm amba brama tɩr nyakɩ bɔ dɔjɩr ʊ an ta meer ʊ bɩ m. 
Bɩ, a lɛɛkarɛ an fɩr yɩ gʊta, her gʊɔɔ nɔɔn n kaanm m. 
Hɔ k'a nyɩnta an ba Nowee dɔmɩm ʊ bɩ, a zɛm bɩ nyɩnta maam Gʊaanyɩ bɩ dɔmɩm ʊ: 
Ŋ nyɩnta ŋn'ɩ hɔ bɩm, ŋn'ɩ hɔ mim, ŋn'ɩ lʊ sam, ŋn'ɩ zim sam an ta an dɔmɩm kʊ Nowee gasʊ ʊ kɔɔlʊʊ ʊ bɩ ku. Bɩ, Woso a ka la gʊta n ba an ŋ bɩ haay. 
Gɛɛ, am hɔ k'a nyɩnta an ba Lɔttɩ dɔmɩm ʊ bɩ hɔ bɩ m: Ŋ yɩ hɔ bɩm, ŋn'ɩ hɔ mim, ŋn'ɩ hɔ sim, ŋn'ɩ hɔ hɛɛnm, ŋn'ɩ hɔ duum, ŋn'ɩ cɛ dɔm. 
Bɩ, dɔmɩm kʊ Lɔttɩ bɔ ʊ Sodɔm ʊ bɩ, Woso a ka sɛ n a to brama, an ba am la hɔ bɩ m, an ŋ zɩnzɛ haay. 
Gʊaanyɩ bɩ hɩnkarɛ hinni ʊ, a nyɩntam maam sɔ. 
Hinni bɩ do ʊ bɩ, gʊaa k'a nɩ cɛ mim ʊ a mɔdrɔ nɩ cɛ ʊ bɩ, a bɩ zer an gasʊ an ŋ gʊr y. A maam sɔ kan gʊaa k'a ta poo ʊ bɩ kɩ, a b'a bɔ an bʊr har ʊ y. 
K'a Lɔttɩ lʊ bɩ mim tɔ da a nɔ ʊ. 
Gʊaa k'a lɛ n taa k'a a meeyaa bʊmbɔ bɩ, a b'a yɩrɛ y. Bɩ gʊaa k'a yɛ zɛ ma mɔɔ tɔ ma bɩ, a y'a meeyaa bʊmbɔrɛ yɩm. 
A hɩ mɔɔ nawʊrɛ awɔɔ m, gum bɩ do ʊ, gʊaa hɩɩya y'a zɛm kʊ ra, ŋnɩ wʊta gargʊ deem la. N deem gaarɛ, n n deem to. 
Lannɔ y'a zɛm kʊ ra hɩɩya, ŋn'ɩ bɩr jaa lɔm. N deem gaarɛ, n n deem to.»  
Kʊ gʊaa hɩɩya ɩ hɔsɩra ʊ: N deem gaam, n nɩ deem to. 
Karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ n a lar, ŋ ʊ: «Zuuba, a nyɩntam ka?» A n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «Bɩncɛ kʊ sim n nɩ nyɩntaŋ ʊ bɩ, bɩncɛ bɩ do gɔbɩrgʊrɔ n n'a som kʊ ma ʊ.»
