Matiyee.
15.
Farizɛɛnrɔ kan wosocikarɩnsaambɔɔ gɔsɩnnɔ kɩ a to Zerizalɛm ʊ, ŋnɩ bʊr Yeezuu zi, ŋnɩ b'a hɩ m, ŋ ʊ: 
«Bɔ m, ɩbɩɩ karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ bɩr zum wɔɔ yaabɔɔ yɩnnɩbayɩrɔ wɔɔ ʊ? Ŋ n'ɩ hɔ bɩm, bɩ ŋ n a wɔ hir y. A n a ga nɩ y.» 
Yeezuu n a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «B'awɔɔ, a lɔ awɔɔ bɩr zum Woso lɛrɔ wɔɔ ʊ, a bɔkarɛ, an nɩ zum a yɩnnɩbayɩrɔ wɔɔ ʊ? 
Woso a hɩ, a ʊ: ‹Ɩ zɩ kan ɩ da kɩ da ʊ. Bɩ gʊaa k'a zɩ bɔ ʊ gɛɛ a da a bɔ ʊ, pakra m, kʊ n n a zɛ gər.› 
B'awɔɔ ʊ, kʊ gʊaa a hɩ a zɩ m, gɛɛ a da m, a ʊ: ‹Daahan kʊ mɔɔ m ga m, m a ka ɩbɩɩ ʊ bɩ, Woso hɔ nɩ.› 
Pakra bɛɛ m kʊ gʊaa bɩ do m a da a zɩ kan a da kɩ han y. Woso lemim bɩ zar awɔɔ nawʊrɛ ncɩnaaʊ a yɩnnɩbayɩrɔ wɔɔ minto. 
Zambʊzannɔ, Ezayii gumə ta ʊ, an awɔɔ mim hɩ, a ʊ: 
Gʊɔɔ nɔɔn duro, mɔɔ da ŋ nawʊŋ ʊ a lɛ m, bɩ ŋ heer laatʊ mɔɔ ma. 
Daʊrɛ kʊ ŋ n'a kam mɔɔ ʊ bɩ, vam m. Bala, durnya mim ŋn'a dɩndam gʊɔɔ m, ɩ ʊ Woso hɔ m.» 
Bɩ jɛ ʊ bɩ, Yeezuu zamaa bɩ bir, an a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «K'a tʊr ka mɩŋŋa k'a jɩ ma! 
Hɔ k'a nɩ gasʊm gʊaa lɛ ʊ an ta a nɔ ʊ bɩ bɛɛ nɩ dintim kam ma y, bɩ hɔ k'a nɩ bɔm gʊaa lɛ ʊ bɩ nɩ dintim kam ma.» 
A m naa, karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ n a zɔ ma, ŋn'a hɩ m, ŋ ʊ: «Farizɛɛnrɔ wɔɔ kʊ ŋ naa ma bɩ, ɩbɩɩ dɔ ɩ ʊ, a busu ŋ ma ra?» 
A n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «Hɔ haay kʊ mɔɔ Zɩ k'a nɩ arzana ʊ bɩ n a sɔɔn bɩ, n y'a wʊrm. 
K'a ŋ to, bʊʊrɔ nɩ bʊʊrɔ gaam. Bɩ, kʊ bʊʊ bʊr, an nɩ bʊʊ gaarɛ, ŋ gʊaa hɩɩya haay y'a zarm yaa ʊ!» 
Pɩyɛɛr n a hɩ m, a ʊ: «Hɔbɔkʊrɛnoom naa do jɩ hɩ ʊ m.» 
Yeezuu n a hɩ, a ʊ: «Awɔɔ gɔɔta, awɔɔ n a jɩ dɔ ra? 
Awɔɔ n a dɔ, a ʊ, hɔ haay k'a nɩ gasʊm gʊaa lɛ ʊ, an nɩ tarɛ a nɔ ʊ bɩ, ta an nawʊm, an nɩ bɔm garga ra ra? 
Bɩ, hɔ k'a nɩ bɔm gʊaa lɛ ʊ bɩ, a to a nawʊm gʊaa heer ʊ, bɩ nɩ dintim kam ma. 
Bala, gʊaa heer hɔɔndarɛ bʊnyaarɔ wɔɔ nɩ bɔŋ ʊ: gərzɛrɛ, jaandarɛ, koorbarɛ, gʊɔɔ tɔ zarlɛ, kan sʊnsɔwʊrɛ kɩ. 
Nawɔnnɔ nɩ dintim kam gʊaa ma, bɩ hɔbɩrɛ bɩ, ɩ n ɩ wɔ hir bɩ bɩr dintim kam gʊaa ma y.» 
Yeezuu k'a cem b'ʊ bɩ, a ta Tiir kan Sidɔn kɩ kʊrɔ wɔɔ ʊ. 
Lʊ nɩ b'ʊ an ta Yeezuu zi, zɩɩfʊ bɛɛ nɩ y, Kanaan do nyɩ m, an a hɩ Yeezuu m, a ʊ: «M cicir zɛ Zuuba, *Davɩd Nyɩ, mɔɔ nyɩmbʊɛɛrɛ zinə n ta m, an nɩ fɩr kam ma gʊta.» 
Yeezuu n a nyɩ bɔ nʊ y. A karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ n a zɔ ma, ŋn'a yɛ han, ŋ ʊ: «La lʊ naa do ra, bala, a ɩ wɔɔ kɔm kan a zeedarɛ kɩ.» 
Yeezuu n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «Mɔɔ nyɔɔ n y'a ʊ Israyɛl gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ bɔ kʊ ʊ kan seero kʊ ŋ nyaam nɔ wɔɔ kɩ zi.» 
Bɩ, lʊ b'a gu an bɩ kukurə zɛ a taa, an a hɩ m, a ʊ: «Zuuba, bʊr ɩ da mɔɔ han!» 
Yeezuu n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «A n a ga m, n nɩ nyɩnɔ hɔbɩrɛ sa, n n a ka jinnyɩnɔ ʊ y.» 
Lʊ bɩ n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «Sɩra m, Zuuba, baa jinnyɩnɔ wɔɔ ɩ hɔbɩrɛ bʊtʊ k'a a lo ŋ zuubarɔ wɔɔ taa bɩ gʊrlɛ, ŋn'a bɩrɛ.» 
A m naa Yeezuu n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «Lʊ, ɩ sɩrakarɛ gandaa ʊ! Ɩ hɔ k'ɩ lɛ n taa bɩ yɩm!» Lɛɛm bɩ, a nyɩmbʊɛɛrɛ b'a waa wakatɩ bɩ do ʊ. 
Yeezuu a hʊr b'ʊ, an ta Galilee higʊta lɛ ra, an aa der ci ra, an nyɩnta. 
Gʊɔɔ cir gʊta bʊr zi, lʊgɔrɔ m, bʊʊrɔ m, gandeenzannɔ m, bəətinno kan yaabazannɔ vantɔɔ kɩ m, ŋnɩ bɩ ŋ nyɩnta Yeezuu taa, an ŋ waa. 
Gʊɔɔ wɔɔ n zɛ kədə, kʊ bəətinno nɩ meer barɛ, gandeenzannɔ n a gannɔ yɩ, lʊgɔrɔ nɩ tam mɩŋŋa, bʊʊrɔ nɩ lɛɛ yɩm bɩ minto. Ŋ y'a lɛ bɔ Israyɛl Woso ʊ. 
Yeezuu a karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ bir an a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «Zamaa bɩ cicir ɩ mɔɔ zɛm, bala, a dɔmɩm kaakʊ hɔ m naa, ŋ nɩ kan mɔɔ kɩ, hɔsɩ ba ŋ ʊ, ŋn'a bɩrɛ y. Mɔɔ lɛ baa kʊ m ŋ da zaa ra kan nɔ kɩ y. Kʊ n bɛɛ nɩ, ŋ y'a dundom zaa ra.» 
Karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ n a hɩ m, ŋ ʊ: «K'ʊ bur yɩ ka pooyaa ʊ naa ʊ, ʊ a ka gʊɔɔ cir naa ʊ, k'an ŋ ka?» 
Yeezuu n ŋ lar, a ʊ: «Bur karɔ n ta awɔɔ ʊ?» Ŋ n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, ŋ ʊ: «Saanhɩna kan zɔ poorenyɩnɔ kɩ.» 
A n lɛ ka kʊ zamaa bɩ n nyɩnta tara ʊ. 
A n bur saanhɩna bɩ sa kan zɔnɔ wɔɔ kɩ an yaa da Woso m barka, an n'a zɩnzɛm la, an n'a kam a karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ ʊ, ŋn'a kam, zamaa b'ʊ. 
Ŋ n a bɩ, an ŋ ka, an a yɔ, ŋn'a kuuro gʊr kasɩ lɛ ma saanhɩna. 
Zamaa bɩ gʊɔɔyaarɔ a zɔ tisi si ma, n n lannɔ kan nyɩnɔ kɩ lɛ sa y. 
Bɩ jɛ ʊ bɩ, Yeezuu ŋ da zaa ra, an da kɔɔlʊʊ b'ʊ an ta Magadaan ʊ.
