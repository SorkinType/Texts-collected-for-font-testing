Matiyee.
14.
Dɔmɩm bɩ do ʊ bɩ, cir Hɩrɔɔdɩ Yeezuu mim ma, bala a tɔ bɔ lɛɛ haay ʊ. 
A n n'a hɩrɛ a zibəənyɩnɔ m, a ʊ: «Gʊaa naa do, Zaan-Batiisi m! K'a a mɩm bɔ ʊ gəəno bire ʊ bɩ y'a ka, an paŋŋa yɩ, an nɩ hoserlo barɛ.» 
Kʊ sɩra nɩ, Hɩrɔɔdɩ a ka, ŋnɩ Zaan nyasʊ, ŋn'a sur, ŋn'a du lɔnnɔcɛ ʊ, a dam Filipi lʊ k'an ta m, a tɔ m Hɩrɔdɩyaadɩ bɩ minto. 
Bala, Zaan a hɩ Hɩrɔɔdɩ m, a ʊ: «A n a ga m k'ɩbɩɩ n a ba ɩ lʊ y!» 
Hɩrɔɔdɩ a ŋʊa k'a Zaan zɛ gər, b'a ɩ nyi bam gʊɔɔ wɔɔ m. Bala, brɔɔ haay ɩ dɩgam Zaan ma, ŋ ʊ: Wosolɛsinnɩsorazaa m. 
Bɩ, Hɩrɔɔdɩ yɩrɛ dɔmɩm cibsa hinni ʊ, Hɩrɔdɩyaadɩ nyɩmbʊɛɛrɛ sar ba gʊɔɔ kʊ n yɩ ŋ bir cibsa ma rɔ wɔɔ lɛɛ, an Hɩrɔɔdɩ nɔ yɩ. 
Hɩrɔɔdɩ n a wer si, a ʊ: hɔ k'a lɛ n taa haay bɩ, a y'a kaŋ ʊ. 
Nyɩmbʊɛɛrɛ bɩ da bɩ n a nyʊnnyɔɔta, a ʊ: «A n Zaan-Batiisi mim yɛ laaga burə ʊ.» Nyɩmbʊɛɛrɛ bɩ n a yɛ maam sɔ. 
Cir bɩ heer n a zar, b'a b'a dam ma, an a gu y, k'a wer si hɔbɩrɛbinno wɔɔ bire ʊ bɩ minto. A m bɩ, an lɛ ka kʊ n n'a ka ʊ. 
A n gʊaa deem nyɔɔ, an aa Zaan mim gʊ ma lɔnnɔcɛ ʊ b'ʊ, 
an bʊr m laaga ʊ, an b'a ka nyɩmbʊɛɛrɛ b'ʊ, an a si, an aa a ka a da ʊ. 
Zaan karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ n bʊr, ŋnɩ bɩ Zaan gər bɩ sa, ŋn'aa bir, ŋnɩ bɔkarɛ ŋn'aa hɔ k'a a ba bɩ hɩ Yeezuu m. 
Yeezuu k'a mim naa do ma bɩ, a a hʊr lɛɛ bɩ do ʊ, an da kɔɔlʊʊ ʊ k'a ta toore ma a deem. Gʊɔɔ cir kʊ ŋ naa yɩ bɩ, ŋ y'a hʊr a kʊrɔ ʊ a gam ma, k'a zu a jɛ ʊ. 
Yeezuu k'a bɔ kɔɔlʊʊ b'ʊ bɩ, a gʊaa cir yɩ gʊta, gʊɔɔ wɔɔ cicir n a nyasʊ, an a sɩŋŋɩda sa, an nɩ ŋ yaabazannɔ waarɛ. 
Yirbəə k'a da bɩ, a karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ a zɔ ma, ŋn'a hɩ m, ŋ ʊ: «Gum da, bɩ pooyaa m naa, gʊɔɔ wɔɔ da zaa ra kʊ ŋ nɩ ta kʊrɔ wɔɔ ʊ, ŋn'aa hɔbɩrɛ si, ŋn'a bɩ!» 
Bɩ, Yeezuu a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «A n a ga m, ŋn'a hʊr y. K'a hɔbɩrɛ ka ŋ ʊ a zim.» 
A m bɩ, ŋn'a hɩ m, ŋ ʊ: «Bur soor kan zɔnɔ kɩ hɩɩya wɔɔ n ta m naa ʊ.» 
Yeezuu n a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «K'a ŋ ka m ʊ naa ʊ». 
A n lɛ ka, kʊ gʊɔɔ wɔɔ n nyɩnta bur bɩ ra, an bur soorlɔ wɔɔ si kan zɔ hɩɩya bɩ kɩ a wɔ ʊ, an dɩga brama, an barka da Woso m. A n buuro wɔɔ zɩnzɛ ra, an a ka a karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ ʊ, kʊ ŋ n'a ka zamaa b'ʊ. 
Gʊɔɔ wɔɔ haay a bɩ, an ŋ ka. Karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ bur kan zɔ kɩ kuuro kʊ ŋ gɔɔta rɔ wɔɔ gʊr kasɩ lɛ ma bueerehɩɩya. 
Gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ hɔ bɩ bɩ rɔ wɔɔ, yaarɔ wɔɔ lɛ a zɔ tisi soor ma. N n lannɔ kan nyɩnɔ kɩ lɛ sa y. 
Lɛɛm bɩ, Yeezuu a ka karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ n da kɔɔlʊʊ b'ʊ, kʊ ŋ nɩ bra higʊta bɩ gəm deem la, k'a gɔɔta a zamaa bɩ da zaa ra. 
K'a zamaa bɩ da zaa ra an a nya bɩ, a a hʊr a deem, an ta ci ra, k'a yaa da. Yirbəə k'a da bɩ, a gɔɔta b'ʊ a deem. 
Kɔɔlʊʊ bɩ n a gʊ hi bɩ lɛ bɩ ma, an ta laatʊ hi bɩ bire ʊ. Bala, hɩnhɛr y'a nyam kɔɔlʊʊ bɩ zi, hikurero wɔɔ n'a monɩm kɔɔlʊʊ b'ʊ 
Dɔ k'a bʊr an nɩ mɛm bɩ, a wuti an tɔ hi bɩ ra, an nɩ tarɛ ŋ zi. 
Kʊ karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ a yɩ an nɩ tam hi bɩ ra bɩ, nyibəə gasʊ ŋ ʊ, ŋn'a hɩrɛ, ŋ ʊ: «Toor m!» Ŋ n'ɩ zee dam. 
Lɛɛm bɩ, Yeezuu meer ba kan ŋ kɩ, a ʊ: «K'a sɩra ka, mɔɔ m, a da bɩ bɔ ʊ y!» 
Pɩyɛɛr n a hɩ m, a ʊ: «Zuuba, k'ɩbɩɩ m paan… k'ɩ lɛ ka kʊ m bʊr ɩ zi hi bɩ burə ʊ.» 
Yeezuu n a hɩ m, a ʊ: «Bʊr.» Pɩyɛɛr n bɔ kɔɔlʊʊ b'ʊ, an nɩ tɔm hi bɩ ra k'a ta Yeezuu zi. 
K'a hɩnhɛr bɩ yɩ an nɩ hɔnm kan paŋŋa kɩ bɩ, a da bɔ ʊ, hi bɩ n'a monɩm, an zee da, a ʊ: «Zuuba, m bɔ lɛ m!» 
Lɛɛm bɩ, Yeezuu a wɔ nyɔɔ an a nyasʊ, an a hɩ m, a ʊ: «Ɩbɩɩ sɩrakarɛ poore! A lɔ ɩbɩɩ n sɩnsɩm ka?» 
Ŋ n'a da kʊ ʊ, ŋnɩ gasʊ kɔɔlʊʊ b'ʊ hɩnhɛr bɩ n jɩm. 
Gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ nɩ kɔɔlʊʊ b'ʊ rɔ wɔɔ kukurə zɛ a taa, ŋn'a hɩrɛ, ŋ ʊ: «Woso Nyɩ n'ɩbɩɩ m paan….» 
Kʊ ŋ higʊta bɩ zɛ ra bɩ, ŋ yaa lee ku Zenezarɛtɩ ʊ. 
Kʊ bɩ do gʊɔɔ wɔɔ kʊ ŋ Yeezuu yɩ, ŋn'a dɔ bɩ, ŋ yaa a hɩ kʊ b'ʊ haay kan kʊrɔ jɩnjɛɛnnɔ wɔɔ kɩ, ŋ ʊ, a bʊr. Ŋ n bʊr yaabazannɔ wɔɔ m haay Yeezuu zi. 
Ŋ n a yɛ han, k'a a wɔ da a huu lɛ tɔkkɛ ma. Bɩ, gʊɔɔ kʊ ŋ y'a wɔ da ma rɔ wɔɔ waarɛ yɩ haay.
