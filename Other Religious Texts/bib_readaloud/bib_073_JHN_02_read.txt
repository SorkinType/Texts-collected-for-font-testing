Zaan.
2.
A dɔmɩm kaakʊ hinni ʊ bɩ, lʊsarɛ cibsa ɩ nyɩnta ʊ Galilee kʊ Kanaa ʊ. Yeezuu da nyɩnta b'ʊ. 
N Yeezuu bir lʊsarɛ cibsa bɩ ma sɔ kan a *karɩndanyɩnɔ kɩ. 
Dɩvɛn bɩ k'a bɩ nya bɩ, Yeezuu da a hɩ m, a ʊ: «Dɩvɛn ba ŋ ʊ dɔ y.» 
Yeezuu n a nyɩ bɔ ʊ, a ʊ: «M naa, kara y'ɩbɩɩ ku? Mɔɔ wakatɩ n a ku cɩna y.» 
A da bɩ n a hɩ zibəənyɩnɔ wɔɔ m, a ʊ: «K'a hɔ k'a n'a hɩrɛ awɔɔ m haay bɩ ba.» 
Sipiro ɩ nyɩnta b'ʊ sɔrdɩ, ŋnɩ tɩr hi tʊntɔŋ ʊ, zɩɩfʊrɔ wɔɔ *hubɔrɛ minto, amba ŋ tɩr a ba a Woso zaa zuure nɔ ʊ bɩ m. Bɩ sipi deem a da ma, an gər si si. 
Yeezuu n a hɩ zibəənyɩnɔ wɔɔ m, a ʊ: «K'a hi tʊntɔ sipiro nɔɔn duro ʊ, k'an ŋ hɩnhan.» Ŋ n hi bɩ tʊntɔ ŋ ʊ, an ŋ hɩnhan kan a lɛ kɩ. 
Yeezuu n a hɩ ŋ nɩ, a ʊ: «K'a kusi kɛɛrɛ, a ka hɔjirlezaa b'ʊ.» Ŋ n a kusi, ŋn'aa a ka ʊ. 
K'a hi k'a lʊr dɩvɛn bɩ da a lɛ ma bɩ, a n a dɔ k'a to a ʊ ka y. Bɩ, zibəənyɩnɔ kʊ ŋ y'a kusi rɔ wɔɔ a dɔ. A lʊ bɩ zim bɩ bir, 
an a hɩ m, a ʊ: «Gʊaa biyəə naa, dɩvɛn nyɩnta a tɩr lɛɛ ka, an a ka gʊɔɔ ʊ. Kʊ ŋ b'a mi, an ŋ ka, an bɔkarɛ, an bɔ ɩ bɩ nyɩnta bɩ ka ŋ ʊ. Ɩbɩɩ n bɔkarɛ ɩ dɩvɛn nyɩnta bɩ to, an wakatɩ naa do ku!» 
Yeezuu lɛɛkarɛ hoser n naa, an a ba Kanaa ʊ, Galilee kʊ ʊ. A a cirbəə bɩ hɩnka, a karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ n sɩra ka ʊ. 
Bɩ jɛ ʊ bɩ, a ta Kapɛrnawum ʊ kan a da kɩ, a danyɩnɔ kɩ, a karɩndanyɩnɔ kɩ. Ŋ yaa nyɩnta b'ʊ, bɩ, ŋ n maasɩrɛ ba y. 
Zɩɩfʊrɔ *pakkɩ cibsa a zɔ, Yeezuu n ta Zerizalɛm ʊ. 
A n aa dɩga a, ŋ yɩ ŋʊannɔ hɛɛnm *Wosocɛ hallu ʊ b'ʊ, kan seero kɩ, cirpʊnnɔ kɩ, busoolʊrlɛzannɔ nyɩnta b'ʊ sɔ. 
A n bar sa, an a ba madɩr, an la ŋ la haay Wosocɛ hallu ʊ b'ʊ kan seero wɔɔ kɩ, dono wɔɔ kɩ. A busoolʊrlɛzannɔ wɔɔ bɔɔlʊgʊ bɩ hɩsɩga, an ŋ mɔdrɔkuntərehɔɔ wɔɔ hɩnhɩsɩga. 
A n a hɩ cirpʊnnɔ hɛɛrɛ zannɔ wɔɔ m, a ʊ: «K'a bɔ nawɔnnɔ haay m naa ʊ, a bɩ mɔɔ Zɩ cɛ bɩ sa a ba daasɩ y.» 
Karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ n hɔ kʊ n y'a gʊrsɩra ba bɩ tɔ da a nɔ ʊ: ‹Ɩbɩɩ har bɩ ŋʊarɛ a so mɔɔ ʊ haay.› 
Zɩɩfʊrɔ jɩtannɔ wɔɔ n meerbaa bɩ sa, ŋ ʊ: «K'ɩbɩɩ ba ncɩnaaʊ naa, bɔ *makra ɩbɩɩ n n'a hɩnkam wɔɔ nɩ?» 
Yeezuu n a nyɩ bɔ ŋ nʊ, a ʊ: «K'a Wosocɛ naa do wurgə, mɔɔ yɩ m bɔrɛ m a dɔ dɔmɩm kaakʊ nɔ ʊ.» 
Ŋ n a hɩ m, ŋ ʊ: «Dɔɔraa busi a yaa sɔrdɩ wɔɔ Wosocɛ naa do dɔ, ʊ a nya ma. Ɩbɩɩ n bɔkarɛ, ɩ ʊ, k'ɩ dɔ dɔmɩm kaakʊ nɔ ʊ?» 
Bala, Wosocɛ k'a n'a mim hɩm bɩ, a mɩŋŋa mɛ m. 
Bɩ minto y'a ka, Yeezuu k'a b'a mɩm bɔ ʊ gəəno bire ʊ bɩ, a karɩndanyɩnɔ wɔɔ a tɔ da a nɔ ʊ, ŋ ʊ, a nyɩnta an a hɩ ncɩnaaʊ, ŋnɩ sɩra ka *Wosoci bɩ lemim ʊ kan meerbaa k'a ba bɩ kɩ. 
Yeezuu k'a nɩ Zerizalɛm ʊ *pakkɩ cibsa dɔmɩm ʊ bɩ, gʊɔɔ gʊta sɩra ka Yeezuu tɔ ʊ makrarɔ k'a ŋ ba ŋn'a yɩ rɔ wɔɔ minto. 
Bɩ, Yeezuu n yɛ, an sɩra ka ŋ ʊ y. Bala, a ŋ dɔ haay, 
a lɛ baa kʊ n n gʊaa kasɛtɩ ka ʊ y. Bala, a hɔ k'a nɩ gʊaa heer ʊ bɩ dɔ.
