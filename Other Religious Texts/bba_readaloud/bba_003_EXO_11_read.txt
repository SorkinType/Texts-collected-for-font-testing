Yɑribu.
11.
N deemɑ Yinni Gusunɔ u rɑɑ Mɔwisi sɔ̃ɔwɑ u nɛɛ, n tie wɑhɑlɑ tiɑ ye kon Eɡibitiɡibu kɑ ben sunɔ doke. Yerɑ yɑ koo de u bɛɛ yɔ̃su. U koo mɑm bɛɛ ɡirɑwɑ minin di kɑ dɑm. 
A tɔn durɔ kɑ tɔn kurɔ bɑɑwure sɔ̃ɔwɔ u burɑ yɑ̃ɑ wurɑɡinu kɑ sii ɡeesuɡinu kɑnɑ win beruse Eɡibitiɡiin mi. 
N deemɑ Yinni Gusunɔ u derɑ Eɡibitiɡibu bɑ kɑ Isirelibɑ nɔnu ɡeu mɛɛrɑ. Mɔwisi u mɑɑ sɑ̃ɑ tɔn boko sunɔn bwɑ̃ɑbu kɑ Eɡibitiɡii be bɑ tien nɔni sɔɔ. 
Mɔwisi u sere doonɑ Eɡibiti sunɔn min di, u nùn sɔ̃ɔwɑ u nɛɛ, ɑmɛniwɑ Yinni Gusunɔ u ɡeruɑ. Wɔ̃kurun bɔnun sɑkɑwɑ u koo Eɡibitin tem bukiɑnɑ. 
Bii yerumɑbɑ kpuro bɑ koo ɡbi, sɑɑ wunɛn bii yerumɑn di, wi u koo wunɛn sinɑ ɡɔnɑ tubi di sere kɑ yoo tɔn kurɔn bii tɔn durɔ ɡbiikoowɔ. Yɑɑ sɑbenun bii ɡbiikii dwɑnu nu koo mɑɑ ɡbi. 
Wuri bɛkɛ yi ko n wɑ̃ɑ Eɡibitin tem kpuro sɔɔ dɔmɑ te, yìn bweseru yi ǹ koore. Mɛyɑ yi ǹ koo mɑɑ wure yi ko. 
Adɑmɑ bɛsɛ Isirelibɑn mi, bɑɑ bɔ̃ɔ ɡɑ ǹ sun worimɔ sere kɑ bɛsɛn yɑɑ sɑbenɔ, kpɑ i n kɑ yɛ̃ mɑ Yinni Gusunɔ u sun wunɑnɛ kɑ bɛɛ Eɡibitiɡibu. 
Mɑ Mɔwisi u kpɑm nɛɛ, wunɛn bwɑ̃ɑbu kpuro bɑ koo nɑ bu kpunɑ nɛn wuswɑɑɔ bu mɑn sɔ̃ bu nɛɛ, n yɑrio kɑ nɛn tɔmbu kpuro be bɑ mɑn swĩi. Yen biru nɑ kon den yɑri n doonɑ. Ye u ɡɑri yi ɡeruɑ u kpɑ, mɑ u yɑrɑ Eɡibiti sunɔn min di kɑ mɔru bɑkɑ. 
Yinni Gusunɔ u rɑɑ nùn sɔ̃ɔwɑ u nɛɛ, Eɡibiti sunɔ kun wunɛn ɡɑri swɑɑ dɑkimɔ. Yerɑ yɑ koo de n mɑɑ sɔm mɑɑmɑɑkiɡinu ɡɑnu ko Eɡibitin temɔ. 
Mɔwisi kɑ Aroni bɑ sɔm mɑɑmɑɑkiɡii ni kpuro kuɑ Eɡibiti sunɔn wuswɑɑɔ ɑdɑmɑ Yinni Gusunɔ derɑ win ɡɔ̃ru ɡɑ bɔbiɑ mɑ u ǹ wure Isirelibɑ bu doonɑ win tem di.
