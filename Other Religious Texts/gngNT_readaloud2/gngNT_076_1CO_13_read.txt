1 Korɛnt yaab.
13.
N lienh binib ya lenm nin Uwien ya tondb ya lenm kɛ, ka nín ji yíe nib la, n te tɛn lisɛnbuɔl bii ligangaal là muɔh ki ŋmɔbe kuwaaku nɛ. 
Nì yé n ŋmɔbe bupaabu ki sɔkndeh Uwien ya ñɔbon, ki bɛn tigbɛr tà kɛ buɔ, ki ŋmɔbe mibɛnm kɛ ya bol, ki ŋmɔbe mitekjim ki tì gben', mì li fre kí cère ijuɔn ń nɛbre iʼbùol ŋɔ ka yíe nib la, ma yé bonn. 
Nì yé n taa' nʼfaal kɛ ki gbigbiire' binib, ki tuo' bi wìn' nni ŋɔ ka yíe nib la, ma ŋmɔbe kpɛle uba nin utɔ. 
Unil ŋmɔbe miyíem la, u ŋmɔbe lifɛ̀sɔngl nɛ, ki ŋmɔbe tinimɔ̀nt, ka ŋmɔbe nunfɔke, ka wɔngeh uʼba, ka ŋmɔbe gbengt, 
ka teh fɛ ya bonn, ka nuunh uʼtiɛma mɔnm, ka yé benpiebdaan, ka ŋuuh nib bo. 
Uʼyɛnm ŋa sɔnge nà ŋa cuube bo, ama uʼyɛnm pɛ tigbɛmɔ̀nt bo nɛ. 
Unil ŋmɔbe miyíem la, u ŋmɔbe sukle tibont kɛ ni nɛ, ki du Uwien bo isɛn kɛ ni, ki ŋmɔbe lidɛndɛnl isɛn kɛ ni, ki ŋmɔbe kuminku nà kɛ li tu wɔ bo. 
Miyíem ŋa ń gben fiebu. Uwien ya ñɔbon ya sɔknm mɛn li gben nɛ. Milencɛnm mà binib lienh mɛ̀ nnɔ mɔ li gben. Mibɛnm mɔ li gben. 
Fɛnfɛnnɔ wuɔ ti bɛn jenjenku nɛ, nɛ ki sɔkndeh Uwien ya ñɔbon mɔ jenjenku. 
Ama nà mɔn ki tì gben' lá baa' uyo wà la, ti bɛn nà nin ti teh nà jenjenku nnɔ kɛ li gben nɛ. 
N là yé kibuk ma nnɔ, n là lienh mibuwaalenm nɛ, ki bɛn tibont ya tingi kibuk bɛn ma bo, ki maaleh kibuk maaleh ma bo. N tì tien' uciɛn, nɛ ki dàan' ibuwaatùon. 
Fɛnfɛnnɔ, ti lɛnh tibont dɔmudɔmu nɛ, nì te tɛn ti likeh kinunliik kà ŋa lɛnh mɔnmɔnm ni nɛ, nɛ ki lɛnh tijinjint. Ama uyo we ní tí lɛ tù nin tiʼnunbu. Fɛnfɛnnɔ ma laan bɛn tibont ki tì gben'. Ama uyo we ní ń bɛnde tù kí tì gben tɛn Uwien bɛn nni ma bo. 
Tɔ, mitekjim nin lidɛndɛnl nin miyíem tuu ki te nɛ, ama tibont tuta nnɔ ni miyíem nɛ cɛn' tuʼkɛ.
