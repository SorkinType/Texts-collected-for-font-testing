Bee gbɛa Egipiↄ kí Falaↄ̃ↄ vɛ̃ɛbↄlɛna ń a pɛ̃ɛkɛnao kɛ̀aànↄ ĩ́i. 
Aà pↄ pà a ìwa gbɛ̃ↄn plapiↄzi, aà vɛ̃ɛbↄlɛnaↄ kía ń aà pɛ̃ɛkɛnaↄ kíao. 
Ɔ̃ à ń kákpɛu a dↄaiↄ gbɛ̃zↄ̃ↄ bɛ, kpɛ́ pↄ́ wà Yosɛfu dàu. 
Dↄaiↄ gbɛ̃zↄ̃ↄpi Yosɛfu dìlɛ aàↄ laaidↄmá. Aa gↄↄplakɛ̀ kpɛ́u we. 
Gwãasĩn doũ Egipiↄ kía vɛ̃ɛbↄlɛna ń pɛ̃ɛkɛnapio nana'ò ḿpla ḿpii, baade ń a pↄ́o ń a àsio. 
Kɛ́ Yosɛfu mↄ̀ ń gwai kↄↄ, a è an kã́ gà. 
Ɔ̃ à ń lá à mɛ̀: Bↄ́ yã́ mɛ́ tò á oosisia gbãi? 
Aa wèwà aa mɛ̀: Nana ↄ̃ wá ò, gbɛ̃e ku la kɛ́ à a àsi'owɛ̃ɛo. Ɔ̃ a ònɛ́: Lua mɛ́ nana àsi bↄↄlɛ dↄ̃o lò? À á nanapiↄ dau à siumɛɛ. 
Ɔ̃ vɛ̃ɛbↄlɛnaↄ kía a pↄ́ òɛ̀ à mɛ̀: Ma nana guu ma vɛ̃ɛli è ma saɛ. 
Vɛ̃ɛlipi gↄ̃n àaↄ̃ɛ. À labↄ̀ à vukɛ̀, à nɛ'ì à mà. 
Má Falaↄ̃ↄ imibↄ kũa, ↄ̃ ma vɛ̃ɛbɛpiↄ sɛ̀ má fɛ̃̀ aà imibↄpiu, má kpàwà. 
Yosɛfu òɛ̀: Nanapi àsin kɛ: Ligↄ̃n àaↄ̃piↄá gↄↄ àaↄ̃ɛ. 
Gↄↄ àaↄ̃ gbɛa Falaↄ̃ↄ a n misi. A ɛa n da n zĩ guu, ní ɛa ǹyↄ̃ vɛ̃ɛbↄlɛɛ̀, lá niↄ kɛ yãawa. 
Tó yã́ bↄ̀nnↄ na, to ma yã́ àↄ dↄngu. Gbɛ̃kɛkɛmɛɛ, ní ma yã'o Falaↄ̃ↄɛ, kɛ́ ǹ e ǹ ma bↄ kpɛ́ɛ bee guu. 
Asa wà ma kũ kpã́i za Ɛbɛluↄ bùsuuɛ, mɛ́ baa Egipi la mi yãe kɛ à kà wà ma da ɛɛ bee guuo. 
Kɛ́ pɛ̃ɛkɛnaↄ kía è Yosɛfu nanapi àsi'oa bↄ̀ ń vɛ̃ɛbↄlɛnao na, ↄ̃ a òɛ̀: Ma nana guu sↄ̃ má è má pɛ̃ɛ sɛa gbí àaↄ̃ diakↄ̃a. 
Gbí pↄ́ kú musu, kàa bui dasi pↄ́ má kɛ̀ Falaↄ̃ↄɛ ka a guu, ↄ̃ bãↄ lɛ́ ble ma musu. 
Yosɛfu òɛ̀: A àsin kɛ: Gbí àaↄ̃piↄá gↄↄ àaↄ̃ɛ. 
Gↄↄ àaↄ̃ gbɛa Falaↄ̃ↄ a n mi zↄ̃ a n loo líwa, bãↄ i n ble. 
A gↄↄ àaↄ̃deá Falaↄ̃ↄ izĩ dikpɛɛ, ↄ̃ à blɛkɛ̀ a ìwaↄnɛ. À vɛ̃ɛbↄlɛnaↄ kía ń pɛ̃ɛkɛnaↄ kíao bↄ̀lɛ kpɛ́u a ìwaↄ wáa. 
Ɔ̃ à ɛ̀a vɛ̃ɛbↄlɛnaↄ kía dà a zĩu, aà ɛa aàↄ vɛ̃ɛbↄlɛɛ. 
Ɔ̃ à pɛ̃ɛkɛnaↄ kía lòo líwa, lá Yosɛfu ń nanaↄ àsi'ònɛ́wa. 
Kásile Yosɛfu yã́ i dↄ vɛ̃ɛbↄlɛnaↄ kíagu lↄo, aà yã́ sã̀aàguɛ.
