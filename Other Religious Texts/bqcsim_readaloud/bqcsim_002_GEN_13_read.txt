Ɔ̃ Ablaũ bↄ̀ Egipi à sù Nɛgɛvɛ ń a nao ń a àizɛɛↄ píi ń Lↄtuo. 
Ablaũá ↄdeɛ, a pↄtuoↄ vĩ dasi ń ã́nusuo ń vuao. 
Bↄa Nɛgɛvɛ à sↄ̃kĩikpàkpa e à gɛ̀ kàò Bɛtɛli, gu pↄ́ à bòokpàu káau Bɛtɛli zãnguo ń Aio, 
gu pↄ́ à sa'okĩi bòu yãa. Ɔ̃ à Dii sìsi we. 
Lↄtu ìↄ bɛ ń Ablaũo sãnu. A sãↄ ń zuↄ ń zwã̀akpɛↄ vĩ sↄ̃. 
An pↄtuoↄ dasi, gupi sɛ̃ i mↄ́má ḿpiio. A yã́i aa fↄ̃ wàↄ ku sãnu lↄo. 
Ɔ̃ lɛkpaa gɛ̃̀ Ablaũ pↄdãnaↄ ń Lↄtu pↄdãnaↄ guu. Zĩbeezĩ sↄ̃ Kanaaↄ ń Pɛliziↄ ku bùsupiu. 
Ɔ̃ Ablaũ ò Lↄtuɛ: A maa lɛkpaa àↄ kú wá zãnguoo, ge ma pↄdãnaↄ ń n pↄdãnaↄ zãnguoo, asa wá daɛ doũɛ. 
Bùsu gwa da n aɛ píi. Wà kɛ̃aa. Tó n gɛ ↄzɛɛi, mí gɛ́ ↄplaai. Tó n gɛ ↄplaaiɛ sↄ̃, mí gɛ́ ↄzɛɛi. 
Kɛ́ Lↄtu gugwà, a è Yuudɛ̃ guzulɛ píi mↄ̀sɛ vĩ maamaaɛ. E Dii àↄ gɛ́ Sↄdↄũ ń Gↄmↄↄo kaalɛi, guzulɛpi dɛ lán Dii luwa, lán Egipi bùsuwa, e à gɛ̀ pɛ̀ Zoaawa. 
Ɔ̃ Lↄtu Yuudɛ̃ guzulɛ sɛ̀ píi. Aa kɛ̃̀kↄ̃wa, ↄ̃ Lↄtu dàzɛu gukpɛ oi. 
Ablaũ gↄ̃̀ Kanaa bùsuu, mɛ́ Lↄtu ku Yuudɛ̃ guzulɛ wɛ̃́lɛↄ zãnguo. À bòokpà Sↄdↄũ saɛ. 
Sↄdↄũdeↄ sↄ̃ aa vãiɛ, duunkɛn saↄn ń ũ Diiɛ. 
Lↄtu taa gbɛa Dii ò Ablaũɛ: Gu pↄ́ ń zɛu, wɛsɛ ǹ gugbãntoo ń gɛↄmidↄkĩio ń gukpɛo ń bɛ'aɛo gwa. 
Tↄↄlɛ pↄ́ ń èɛ píi, má n gbaɛ, iↄ dɛ mpi ń n buiↄ pↄ́ ũ e gↄↄpii. 
Má tó n buiↄ dasikũ lán bùsu'ũfãawa. Tó gbɛ̃e a fↄ̃ bùsu'ũfãa nao, a fↄ̃ n buiↄ nao sↄ̃ɛ. 
Fɛlɛ bɛbɛbɛbɛ bùsupi guu, ní a gbã̀a ń a yàasao gwa, asa má n gbaɛ. 
Ablaũ fɛ̀lɛ gɛ̀ bòokpà Mamale gbɛ̃́nɛli pↄ́ kú Hɛblↄ̃ↄ kĩ́i, ↄ̃ à Dii gbagbakĩi bò we.
