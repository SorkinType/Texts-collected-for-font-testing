KARABARA.
6.
Ɔwi gɔ i marasuãdze ɔtsɛ isi ibua ne, kakpadzɛ̃ ɔba i ma Yudase ma nsɛ maɣɛ Griki siɖe gu mma nsɛ maɣɛ si Hebrise ndɛ̃ sɔ si maɣɛ ikpɛnɛ araɖea ne, aito aɣo makuɛdze ma nto maɣɛ Griki siɖe. 
Ne ɔso katɔ̃memasɛdze iweo-inyɔ ɔkpere marasuãdze tsɔra ɔɖuɖu maɣere ma sɔ, “Ikote sɔ boasu bo ɔwi botã Ɣaa itɔ̃me iɣɛ ne iiɖe araɖea kutsuɛ. 
Ne ɔso kiniɔ ne, manyii, minyɔ mi ndɛ̃ si miɖi marɛrɛ̃ ikɔdzɛ ma anɔ lokpa fiɛ Siwarã Bielea ana pia i ma ame. Mmamɔ boasu boɣedza i karabara gamɔ kanya, 
si bosu bo ɔwi botã kayi ikparama gu Itɔ̃me Bielea iɣɛ.” 
Itɔ̃me nɛgbe ɔkote ma ɔɖuɖu, ne ɔso maɖi Stefano gɔ nɖe ɔturi gɔ mba kafɔkaɖe, fiɛ Siwarã Bielea ana pia i ɔ̃ ame. Maɖi Filipo, Prokoro, Nikanor, Timon, Parmena gu ɔfɔ gɔ lokpese ɔ Yudase gɔ marɔ Nikolao fiɛ ɔbɔrɛ i Antiokia sɔ mabuai Stefano. 
Maturi ikɔdzɛ magbe maɖi mate katɔ̃memasɛdze ne. Makparama kayi masu ma nrɔɔ̃ masia ma iso itã karabara. 
Maturi gbodzoo ɔfɔ Itɔ̃me Bielea gɔ itã sɔ mma lofɔ maɖe ɔba maasi i Yerusalem. Ma Yudase masɔrɛdze gbodzoo ana ɔfiniki masiai kafɔkaɖe gagbe. 
Stefano gɔ loyi ku abualɛra gu ɔle ɔbara awawãra gbodzoo i maturi ndɛ̃. 
Ma Yudase masande ikuri nɛ i ma makɔse ɔnyua fiɛ mabɔrɛ i Kirene gu Aleksandria gu mma lobɔrɛ i Kilikia gu Asia ɔtsɛ atɔ̃me ikpadzɛ̃gu Stefano. 
Ɣɛɛ kuwɛ̃ i ma ndɛ̃ iiwo ɔya i Stefano kanya, alasɔ Siwarã ɔtã wũ iwola nɛ ɔɖe ala ika. 
Ne ɔso mabara kanya kawɛ̃ ku mawɛ̃ sɔ maaɖaɛ kanya masia wũ iso sɔ, “Bonɔ sɔ Stefano ɔɣɛ ara wa lota aya i Mose gu Ɣaa iso.” 
Kanya iɖaɛ nɛgbe ɔtã sikpã ɔmɔɛ̃ mmara matedze gu ma Yudase makpakpa gu maturi ɔɖuɖu. Ne ɔso mamɔɛ̃ wũ makɛlɛgu ma atɔ̃mekaɣɛkɔ̃. 
Mma makɔ mabɔ sɔ maɖaɛ wũ kanya ɔɣɛ sɔ, “Stefano ne, ɔsɛ ɔɖe ika ɔta ɔya i Isɔrɛyo Sekelea gu Mose mmara iso ɔwi biara. 
Bonɔ ɔɣɛ ana sɔ ɔrɛrɛ̃ gɔ lobɔrɛ i Nasaret fiɛ marɔ wũ Yesu ne, ɔto ɔ̃abiɛ Isɔrɛyo nɛgbe fiɛ ɔ̃afinikira Mose mmara mɛ ɔtã bo.” 
Mma ɔɖuɖu nsi i atɔ̃mekaɣɛkɔ̃ mmɔ ɔkarã anɔ i Stefano iso, ne manya sɔ ɔ̃ katɔ̃ to ka fiɛ lɛ Ɣaa kpabo kare.
