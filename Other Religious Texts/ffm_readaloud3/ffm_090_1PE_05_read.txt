1 Piyeer.
5.
Miɗo waajoo mawɓe wonduɓe e mooɗon ɓee ɗum ɗoo, sabi miin duu mi mawɗo, mi seedinoke torraaji Iisaa Almasiihu ɗii, mi tawdete e teddungal ɓannginoyteengal ngal. 
Tiiɗee e sewre nde Laamɗo halfini on ndee. Pati ndurdon nde tilsere, ndurdon nde ɓernde laaɓunde no Laamɗo yiɗiri nii. Pati ndurdon nde kammari kaalisi, ndurdon nde soobee. 
Pati kaƴƴinee sewre nde Laamɗo halfini on ndee, kaa ngaɗanon sewre ndee misaalu nde njokka e mon. 
Nden so Duroowo Mawɗo oo ɓanngoyii, on keɓan deesewal teddeengal ngal bontataa abada. 
Hono non onon sukaaɓe jokolɓe, njaɓee yamiroore mawɓe. On fuu, kollondiree leyɗinkinaare ley gollidal mon, sabi Binndi ɗii mbii: «Laamɗo salanto mawninkiniiɓe, hinnoto leyɗinkiniiɓe ɓee.» 
Ndennoo, leyɗinkinanee Laamɗo Jom Semmbe oo, yalla omo toowna on so wakkati cuɓaaɗo oo yottike. 
Tippee sugullaaji mooɗon ɗii fuu yeeso Laamɗo, sabi omo waɗani on hakkille. 
Kakkilee, mbaalon e jeerngo! Sabi gaɲo mooɗon Ibiliisa ana waɗa yaha-warta no laddeeru yolbundu nii, ana ndaara mo faɗɗa faa ɲaama. 
Kaɓito-ɗon, tiiɗon e goonɗinal mooɗon, sabi oɗon anndi sakiraaɓe mooɗon saakiiɓe e aduna oo ɓee, kaɲum'en duu ana ngondi e ɗiin torraaji. 
Torraaji mon ɓooyan seeɗa. Kaa Laamɗo cattuɗo hinnaade en oo noddii en faa naaten e teddeengal mum duumiingal e Iisaa Almasiihu. Nden, Laamɗo bilta on, ɲiiɓina on, hokka on semmbe, darna koyɗe mon. 
Yo baawɗe ngoodan mo faa abada! Aamiina. 
Silas mo anndu-mi yo sakiraaɗo koolniiɗo wallii kam winndande on oo ɓataaki daɓɓo, waajaade on e seedanaade on: kabaaru hinnee Laamɗo goongaraajo oo, ana worri no mbinndir-mi nii. Keddodee hen! 
Deental goonɗinɓe gonngal Baabila, ngal Laamɗo suɓii no suɓorii on nii, engal salmina on. Marka ɓiyam oo duu ana salmina on. 
Calmondiree salminaango wondungo e jilli. On fuu yo on keɓu jam onon yimɓe Iisaa Almasiihu ɓee!
