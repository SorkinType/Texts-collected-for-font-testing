Titire mbeɗe ká Za̰a ɗaa.
1.
Ɓuru ɗaa mbeɗe key na ha rì ɓay ɓáake ɓay se tul ɓe káʼa ɗo pola ɗɔɗ nda̰w rɔɔ feri ɗo ɓáy na. Ɓuru laa ɓay ɓe káʼa ɓaa nda̰w, ɓuru kɔ ni ɓáy nun ɓuru gbak gbak nda̰w, nun ɓuru hɔy kara hu ká tul-e sḭi sḭi, a ɓúru zaa ni ɓáy nduo ɓuru kḭ nda̰w pi. Ɓe na, ka ɓa Ɓay ká ha̰ nzoɓri kǎwke ɓáy kumnun, 
a ví ya̰a sùo ziŋ nzoɓ ha̰ ɓuru kɔ ni, a ɓúru ɓa nzoɓ ɓay nasike. Sa̰wke mini ze, ɓuru ka-káa ɓay ɓe nzoɓ haŋa nzoɓri káw ɓáy kumnun ɓaŋguɔ na ha rì. Ɓe na, ka kaw lakun Bǎa Ŋgɛrɛwṵru nda̰w, rɔɔ a ví ya̰a sùo ziŋ nzoɓ ha̰ ɓuru kɔ ni. 
Fe ká ɓuru kɔ a ɓúru laa ɓayke na, ɓuru ka-káake ha rì, ɓay haŋa ɓo ɓaarì kara, ì ɗaa law mbḭw ka ziŋ ɓuru ká ɓuru zuɔ ziŋ Bǎa ɓáy Vi-e Zezu *Krisi. 
Ɓuru ɗaa mbeɗe se tul fe niri key na ha rì, ɓay haŋa suoriya ka ya̰a ɓil law naari kpol kpol. 
Ì kɔ, ɓay ká ɓuru laa ká nzaa Zezu, a ɓúru ɓaa ha rì na ze ɗo key: Ŋgɛrɛwṵru ɓa zaɗ taŋa, fe pire ndḭi mini kara zaa sùo-ɛ ya. 
Zaɗka nzoɓ ɓaa mii, ɓeri zuɔ ziŋ Ŋgɛrɛwṵru, rɔɔ a ɗaa fe ka̰aya ɓaŋguɔ lɛ, nzoɓke ku na ɓa nzoɓ kúɗ ɓay, a fe ɗáa ɓe kara ɗo máa ɓáy tusuɛ ɓay ya. 
Roo lɛ, zaɗka náa séri simseɗ ká ɓil zaɗ taŋa munu ká Ŋgɛrɛwṵru kḭ sùo-ɛ ɗo ɓil zaɗ taŋa na lɛ, náa zuɔri ziŋ kḭ ɓa lie ɓa lew, a ha̰ sím Zezu Vu Ŋgɛrɛwṵru na wàa feya̰a naari kaɗ kaɗ riw bele. 
Zaɗka nzoɓ ɓaa mii, ɓeri ɗaa feya̰a ya lɛ, ɓe kḭ sùo-ɛ ze ula sùo-ɛ, a tusuɛ ɓay kara ti ɓil law-ɛ ya. 
A roo lɛ, zaɗka náa tinari feya̰a naari ha̰ Ŋgɛrɛwṵru lɛ, kaʼa ndáka bumake ziŋ naari, a wàa naari *taŋ kaɗ kaɗ ká tul fe ka̰aya naari na riw bele, ɓay ḭi lɛ, ka ɓa nzoɓ káʼa ɗo tul ɓay ɓe, a ɗaa fe ká ɗo ɓáy zaɗɛ nzɛɗɛm. 
Zaɗka náa ɓaari mii, náa ɗáari feya̰a ya lɛ, náa ɗiri Ŋgɛrɛwṵru ɓa nzoɓ kúɗ ɓay, a Ɓay ɓe kara ɗo ɓil law naari ya.
