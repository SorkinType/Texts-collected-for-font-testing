Korinti I.
13.
Bɑɑ nɑ̀ n kɑ bɑrum bwese bwesekɑ ɡɑri mɔ̀ kɑ mɑm wɔllun ɡɔrɑdobɑn bɑrum mɑ nɑ kun tɔnu kĩ, nɑ sɑ̃ɑwɑ nɡe yɑnkokoru ǹ kun mɛ yɑnɡireru te bɑ soomɔ. 
Bɑɑ nɑ̀ n Gusunɔn ɡɑri ɡerubun dɑm mɔ, mɑ nɑ ɑsiri kpuro tubɑ kɑ yɛ̃ru kpuro, mɑ nɑ Gusunɔ nɑɑnɛ doke sere kon kpĩ n ɡuunu sɔ̃ nu swɛnyɔ, nɑ̀ kun tɔnun kĩru mɔ nɑ ǹ sɑ̃ɑ ɡɑ̃ɑnu. 
Bɑɑ nɑ̀ n nɛn dukiɑ kpuro sɑ̃ɑrobu wɛ̃, bɑɑ nɑ̀ n derɑ bɑ nɛn wɑsi wɔ̃ɔwɑ, nɑ̀ kun tɔnun kĩru mɔ nɑ ǹ ɑre wɑsi. 
Wi u tɔnu kĩ u rɑ tɛmɑnɛ, u rɑ n mɑɑ tɔn ɡeeru mɔ̀, u ku rɑ n nisinu mɔ, u ku rɑ woo kɑnɛ, u ku rɑ mɑɑ tii sue. 
Wi u tɔnu kĩ u rɑ n tɔmbun bɛɛrɛ yɛ̃, u ku rɑ win tii tɔnɑn ɑrufɑɑni kɑsu, u ku rɑ mɔru ko fuuku, u ku rɑ mɑɑ torɑrun ɡɑri yi win nukurɔ. 
Wi u tɔnu kĩ u ku rɑ yɛ̃ɛri kɔ̃sɑn sɔ̃ mɑ n kun mɔ ɡem sɔ̃. 
Wi u tɔnu kĩ u rɑ kɑ kpuro tɛmɑnɛ. Win nɑɑnɛ dokebu kɑ win yĩiyɔbu kɑ win tɛmɑnɑbu bu ku rɑ kpe. 
Wi u tɔnu kĩ win kĩrɑ ku rɑ n nɔru mɔ. Gusunɔn ɡɑri ɡerubu bu nɔru mɔ, Hunden bɑrum bwese bwesekɑn ɡerubu koo kpe, yɛ̃ru tɑ koo mɑɑ kpe. 
Domi bɛsɛn yɛ̃ru kɑ bɛsɛn Gusunɔn ɡɑri ɡerubu, yen ɡɑɑ kun yibɑ. 
Adɑmɑ sɑnɑm mɛ ye yɑ yibɑ yɑ koo nɑ, sɑnɑm mɛyɑ ye yɑ kun yibɑ, yɑ koo doonɑ. 
Sɑnɑm mɛ nɑ sɑ̃ɑ bii, nɑ rɑ ɡɑri ɡere nɡe bii, nɑ rɑ lɑɑkɑri ko nɡe bii, nɑ rɑ mɑɑ bwisiku nɡe bii, ɑdɑmɑ tɛ̃ ye nɑ ɡuro ɡuro kuɑ, nɑ biin dɑɑ deri. 
Ye sɑ wɑɑmɔ tɛ̃ yɑ sɑ̃ɑre ye sɑ wɑɑmɔ bɑri bɑri tɑsɑ sɔɔ, ɑdɑmɑ sɔ̃ɔ teeru sɑ ko rɑ wɑ nɔni kɑ nɔni. Ye nɑ yɛ̃ tɛ̃ yɑ ǹ yibɑ, ɑdɑmɑ sɔ̃ɔ teeru kon dɑ tubu sɑ̃ɑ sɑ̃ɑ nɡe mɛ Gusunɔ u mɑn tubɑ. 
Tɛ̃ ɡɑ̃ɑnu itɑ yeniwɑ yɑ wɑ̃ɑ, yeyɑ nɑɑnɛ dokebu, kɑ yĩiyɔbu, kɑ kĩru. Adɑmɑ kĩrɑ tɑ kere.
