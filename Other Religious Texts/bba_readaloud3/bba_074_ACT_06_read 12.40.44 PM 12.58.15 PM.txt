Gɔrobun Kookoosu.
6.
Sɔ̃ɔ mɛɛ te sɔɔ, nɡe mɛ nɑɑnɛ dokeobɑ sosimɔ, Yuu be bɑ Gɛrɛkim tɔnɑ ɡerumɔ bɑ kɑ be bɑ tie wɔki yèn sɔ̃ bɑ ben ɡɔminibu duɑrimɔ tɔ̃ɔ bɑɑteren dĩɑnun bɔnu sɔɔ. 
Mɑ ɡɔrobu wɔkurɑ yiru ye, bɑ nɑɑnɛ dokeobu kpuro mɛnnɑ bɑ nɛɛ, n ǹ weenɛ su Gusunɔn ɡɑrin wɑɑsu deri kpɑ sɑ n dĩɑnun ɡɑri mɔ̀. 
Yen sɔ̃, bɛsɛɡibu i tɔn durɔbu nɔɔbɑ yiru ɡɔsio bɛɛn suunu sɔɔ be bɑ seedɑ ɡeɑ diiyɑmmɛ, be bɑ Hunde Dɛɛro yibɑ kpɑ bɑ n bwisi mɔ, kpɑ su bu sɔmbu te nɔmu sɔndiɑ. 
Kpɑ bɛsɛn tii sɑ n kɑnɑru kɑ Gusunɔn ɡɑrin wɑɑsu mɔ̀ sɑ n dɔɔ. 
Tɔn be kpuro bɑ ɡɑri yi wurɑ kɑ nuku dobu. Mɑ bɑ Etiɛni ɡɔsɑ wi u Hunde Dɛɛro yibɑ, win nɑɑnɛ dokebu bu mɑɑ kpɑ̃, kɑ mɑɑ Filipu kɑ Pokori kɑ Nikɑnɔri kɑ Timɔɔ kɑ Pɑɑmɛnɑsi kɑ sere Nikolɑ Antiɔsiɡii wi u ɡɔsirɑ Yuu. 
Bɑ kɑ bu dɑ ɡɔrobun mi, mɑ ɡɔrobɑ bu kɑnɑru kuɑ bɑ bu nɔmɑ sɔndi wirɔ. 
Nɡe mɛyɑ Gusunɔn ɡɑri yi kɑ kpɑrɑrɑmɔ bɑɑmɑ. Mɑ nɑɑnɛ dokeobun ɡeerɑ sosimɔ tɑ dɔɔ Yerusɑlɛmuɔ, mɑ yɑ̃ku kowo dɑbirɑ mɑɑ nɑɑnɛ doke. 
Etiɛni wi, Gusunɔ u nùn durom bɑkɑm kuɑ u nùn dɑm kɑ̃, mɑ u sɔm mɑɑmɑɑkiɡinu kɑ yĩrenu kuɑ tɔmbun suunu sɔɔ. 
Adɑmɑ durɔbu ɡɑbu sɑɑ Yuubɑn mɛnnɔ yerun di te bɑ rɑ soku tii mɔwɔbuɡiru, kɑ Sirɛnin Yuu ɡɑbu kɑ Alesɑndiriɡibu kɑ ɡɑbu sɑɑ Silisi kɑ Asin di bɑ seewɑ bɑ kɑ Etiɛni sikirinɑmɔ. 
Adɑmɑ bɑ kpɑnɑ bu nùn kɑmiɑ yèn sɔ̃ u ɡɑri ɡerumɔ kɑ Hunde Dɛɛron dɑm kɑ bwisi. 
Yerɑ bɑ durɔbu ɡɑbu ɡobi kɑ̃ ɑsiri sɔɔ bu kɑ ɡere mɑ bɑ nuɑ u Mɔwisi kɑ Gusunɔ ɡɑri kɑm ɡerusi. 
Mɛyɑ bɑ kɑ tɔmbun mɔru seesiɑ, kɑ ɡuro ɡurobu kɑ woodɑ yɛ̃robu. Bɑ dɑ Etiɛnin mi bɑ nùn mwɑ mɑ bɑ kɑ nùn dɑ Yuubɑn ɡuro ɡurobun mɛnnɔn wuswɑɑɔ. 
Yen biru bɑ kɑ durɔbu ɡɑbu nɑ be bɑ nùn seedɑ weesuɡiɑ diisi bɑ nɛɛ, durɔ wini u rɑ n bɛsɛn sɑ̃ɑ yee dɛɛrɑru kɑ Mɔwisin woodɑ sɑnkumɔwɑ. 
Domi sɑ nuɑ u ɡeruɑ mɑ Yesu Nɑsɑrɛtiɡii wi, u koo sɑ̃ɑ yee teni surɑ, kpɑ u bɛsɛn deemɑ kpuro kɔsi ye Mɔwisi u sun wɛ̃. 
Be kpuro be bɑ sɔ̃ mɛnnɔ ɡe sɔɔ, bɑ Etiɛni nɔni ɡirɑri, mɑ bɑ wɑ win wuswɑɑ bɑllimɔ nɡe Gusunɔn ɡɔrɑdoɡiɑ.
