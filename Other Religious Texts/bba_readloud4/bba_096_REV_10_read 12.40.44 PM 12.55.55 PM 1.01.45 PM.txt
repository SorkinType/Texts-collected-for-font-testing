Kɑ̃siru.
10.
Nɑ mɑɑ kpɑm wɔllun ɡɔrɑdo dɑmɡii ɡoo wɑ u sɑrɑm wee wɔllun di. Guru wirɑ nùn wukiri, ɡuru wɑɑ win wiru tɛ̃ke, win wuswɑɑ bɑllimɔ nɡe sɔ̃ɔ, mɑ win nɑɑsu sɑ̃ɑre dɔ̃ɔ yɑri. 
U tire kure piibu ɡɑɡu nɛni ɡɑ wukiɑre win nɔmuɔ. U win nɔm ɡeun nɔɔ sɔndi nim wɔ̃kun wɔllɔ, mɑ u mɑɑ nɔm dwɑruɡuu sɔndi temɔ, 
mɑ u kukurɑ nɡe ɡbee sunɔn swĩi. Ye u kukurɑ u kpɑ, yerɑ ɡuru kukiribu nɔɔbɑ yiru yɑ nɔɔrɑ. 
Ye ɡuru kukiri bi, bu nɔɔrɑ bu kpɑ, mii mii nɑ sɔɔru kpɑ n kɑ ɡɑri yi yore. Adɑmɑ nɑ nɔɔ ɡɑɡu nuɑ sɑɑ wɔllun di ɡɑ nɛɛ, ye ɡuru kukiribu nɔɔbɑ yiru ye, yɑ ɡeruɑ ɑ ku ye yore. A ɡɑri yi nɛnuɔ ɑsiri sɔɔ. 
Yerɑ ɡɔrɑdo wi nɑ rɑɑ wɑ u yɔ̃ tem sɔɔ kɑ nimɔ, u win nɔm ɡeu yiiyɑ wɔllɔ, 
mɑ u bɔ̃ruɑ kɑ Gusunɔn yĩsiru wi u wɑ̃ɑ sere kɑ bɑɑdommɑɔ, wi u wɔllu kɑ ten ɡɑ̃ɑnu tɑkɑ kuɑ, kɑ tem kɑ mɛn ɡɑ̃ɑnu kɑ mɑɑ nim wɔ̃ku kɑ ɡen ɡɑ̃ɑnu. Gɔrɑdo wi, u ɡeruɑ u nɛɛ, sɑɑ kun mɑɑ dɛnyɑsiɑrɔ. 
Adɑmɑ sɑnɑm mɛ ɡɔrɑdo nɔɔbɑ yiruse u koo win kɔbɑ so, sɑnɑm mɛyɑ Gusunɔ u koo win himbɑ ye ɡoo kun yɛ̃ yibiɑ nɡe mɛ u rɑɑ win sɔmɔbu sɔ̃ɔwɑ. 
Yen biruwɑ nɔɔ ɡe nɑ rɑɑ nuɑ wɔllun di ɡɑ kɑ kpɑm mɑn ɡɑri kuɑ ɡɑ nɛɛ, ɑ doo ɑ tire kure piibu ɡe ɡɑ wukiɑre mi mwɑ ɡɔrɑdo win nɔmun di wi u yɔ̃ tem kɑ nim wɔllɔ. 
Nɑ dɑ ɡɔrɑdo win mi, nɑ nùn kɑnɑ u mɑn tire kure piibu ɡe wɛ̃. Mɑ u mɑn sɔ̃ɔwɑ u nɛɛ, n ɡu mɔɔ n tem n mwɛ. Gɑ koo sosiɑ nɛn nukurɔ, ɑdɑmɑ nɛn nɔɔwɔ ɡɑ koo dorɑwɑ nɡe tim. 
Nɑ tire kure piibu ɡe mwɑ win nɔmun di, mɑ nɑ ɡu temɑ. Geemɑ, nɛn nɔɔwɔ ɡɑ dorɑwɑ nɡe tim, ɑdɑmɑ ye nɑ ɡu mwɛ nɑ kpɑ, ɡɑ sosiɑwɑ dɑkɑ dɑkɑ nɛn nukurɔ. 
Yerɑ bɑ mɑn sɔ̃ɔwɑ bɑ nɛɛ, kon kpɑm Gusunɔn ɡɑri ɡere tɔn dɑbiru kɑ tem dɑbinun sɔ̃, kɑ bwese dɑbiru kɑ mɑɑ sinɑm dɑbinun sɔ̃.
