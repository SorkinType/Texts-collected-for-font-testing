Gɑlɑti.
6.
Nɛɡibu, ì n ɡoo mwɑ torɑru ɡɑru sɔɔ, bɛɛ be Gusunɔn Hunde u kpɑrɑmɔ, i yɛ̃ro swɑɑ ɡeɑ sureo kpɑm kɑ tii kɑwɑbu, kpɑ i n tii yɛ̃ bu ku rɑɑ kɑ bɛɛn tii kɔkirin sɔ̃. 
I n sɔmunu dɛrɑnɑmɔ kpɑ i kɑ Kirisin woodɑ mɛm nɔɔwɑ. 
Goo ù n tii mɛɛrɑ ɡoo mɑ u ǹ sɑ̃ɑ ɡɑ̃ɑnu, u tii nɔni wɔ̃kumɔwɑ. 
I de bɑɑwure u win tiin kookoosu yĩiyɑ kpɑ u wɑ si sɔɔ, ye u koo kɑ woo kɑnɑ, ɑdɑmɑ u ku woo kɑnɑ mɑ u ɡoo keren sɔ̃. 
Domi bɑɑwurewɑ u koo win tii tiin sɔmunu sɔbe. 
Wi bɑ Gusunɔn ɡɑri sɔ̃ɔsimɔ, i de yɛ̃ro u win ɡɑ̃ɑ ɡee ni u mɔ bɔnu ko kɑ wi u nùn sɔ̃ɔsimɔ. 
I ku tii torɑsiɑ. Bɑ ku rɑ Gusunɔ yɑɑkoru ko. Domi ye tɔnu u duurumɔ, yeyɑ u koo ɡɛ̃. 
Ù n duurumɔ ye win tii tiin dɑɑ yɑ kĩ, kɑm kobɑ yɑ koo nùn mɑruɑ. Ù n mɑɑ duurumɔ ye Gusunɔn Hunde u kĩ, wɑ̃ɑru te tɑ ku rɑ kpewɑ Gusunɔn Hunde koo nùn mɑruɑ. 
Ǹ n mɛn nɑ, su ku wɑsirɑ ɡeɑn kobu sɔɔ, domi sɑ̀ kun kpɑnɛ, sɑ ko wɑ su ɡɛ̃ sɑnɑm mɛ sɑɑ yɑ turɑ. 
Nɡe mɛyɑ sɑɑ bɑɑyere ye sɑ ɑyeru mɔ, su bɑɑwure durom kuɑ, n mɑm nɛɛre be bɑ Yesu nɑɑnɛ doke. 
Tɛ̃, i nɛn yori bɑkɑ mɛɛrio ye nɑ bɛɛ yoruɑmmɛ kɑ nɛn tiin nɔmɑ. 
Be bɑ kĩ bu woo kɑnɑ wɑsin sɔ̃, beyɑ bɑ kĩ bu bɛɛ tilɑsi ko i kɑ bɑnɡo ko. Adɑmɑ bɑ ye mɔ̀ kpɑ bu ku kɑ nɔni swɑ̃ɑru wɑ Kirisin dɑ̃ɑ bunɑnɑrun ɡɑrin sɔ̃. 
Bɑɑ be bɑ bɑnɡo kuɑ mi, ben tii bɑ ǹ woodɑ mɛm nɔɔwɑmmɛ. Mɑ bɑ kĩ bu bɛɛ bɑnɡo ko bu kɑ woo kɑnɑ. 
Adɑmɑ nɛ nɑ kun woo kɑnɑmɔ mɑ n kun mɔ bɛsɛn Yinni Yesu Kirisin dɑ̃ɑ bunɑnɑrun ɡɑrin sɔ̃, domi win dɑ̃ɑ bunɑnɑrun sɔ̃nɑ hɑnduniɑn ɡɑ̃ɑnu kpuro kun mɑɑ mɑn sɑ̃ɑwɛ ɡɑ̃ɑnu, mɛyɑ nu ǹ mɑɑ mɑn ɡɑwɑmɔ. 
Bɑnɡo kɑ bɑnɡo sɑriru yen ɡɑɑ kun sɑ̃ɑ ɡɑ̃ɑnu. Ye n sɑ̃ɑ ɡɑ̃ɑnu, yeyɑ ɑ n sɑ̃ɑ tɑkɑ koorɑ kpɑɑ. 
Be bɑ tɑkɑ koorɑ kpɑɑn woodɑ yeni swĩi, ɑlɑfiɑ kɑ durom mu n kɑ bu wɑ̃ɑ kɑ Gusunɔn tɔmbu kpuro. 
Sɑɑ ɡisɔn di ɡoo u ku mɑɑ mɑn wɑhɑlɑ ko domi nɑ boo bori mɔ nɛn wɑsi sɔɔ, yi nɑ wɑ Yesun sɔ̃. 
Nɛɡibu, bɛsɛn Yinni Yesu Kirisin durom mu n wɑ̃ɑ kɑ bɛɛ. Ami.
