Tɛsɑlonikɑ II.
2.
Nɛɡibu, nɑ bɛɛ kɑnɑmɔ i ku de bɛɛn lɑɑkɑri yu burisinɑ, bɛsɛn Yinni Yesu Kirisin wurɑmɑrun ɡɑrin sɔ̃ kɑ mɑɑ bɛsɛn mɛnnɑbu win mi, i ku mɑɑ de sɔmɔbun ɡɑri ɡɛɛ ǹ kun mɛ wɑɑsu ɡɑɑ ǹ kun mɛ tire te bɑ nɛɛ nɑ yoruɑ, yen ɡɑɑ yu bɛɛ biti ko yɑ̀ n ɡeruɑ mɑ Yinnin tɔ̃rɑ tunumɑ kɔ. 
I ku de bu bɛɛ nɔni wɔ̃ke bɑɑ fiiko. Yinnin tɔ̃ɔ te, tu sere nɑ tɔn dɑbirɑ koo Gusunɔ seesi, yen biru kɔ̃sɑn kowo wi, u koo kurɑmɑ wi u sɑ̃ɑ kɑm kooren bii. 
U koo tii suɑ n kpuro kere kpɑ u ye kpuro seesi ye bɑ sokumɔ Yinni kɑ ye bɑ sɑ̃ɑmɔ. U koo mɑm dɑ u sinɑ Gusunɔn sɑ̃ɑ yerɔ kpɑ u tii soku Gusunɔ. 
I ǹ yɑɑye mɑ nɑ bɛɛ yeni sɔ̃ɔwɑ sɑnɑm mɛ nɑ wɑ̃ɑ bɛɛn mi? 
Tɛ̃, i yɛ̃ sɑ̃ɑ sɑ̃ɑ ye yɑ nùn nɛnusi sere win sɑɑ yu kɑ turi. 
Domi kɔ̃sɑ yɑ sɔmburu mɔ̀ kɔ ɑsiri sɔɔ. Adɑmɑ wi u ɡinɑ ye yinɑsiɑmɔ u ko n ye yinɑsiɑmɔwɑ sere bɑ̀ n kɑ nùn doonɑ. 
Sɑɑ yerɑ kɔ̃sɑn kowo wi, u koo kurɑmɑ kpɑ Yinni Yesu u nùn wom wure u ɡo, kpɑ u nùn kpeerɑsiɑ kɑ win nɑɑrun yiiko. 
Kɔ̃sɑn kowo wi, u koo nɑ kɑ Setɑm dɑm, kpɑ u sɔm mɑɑmɑɑkiɡii bwese bwesekɑ ko ye yɑ sɑ̃ɑ weesuɡiɑ. 
U koo mɑɑ bu nɔni wɔ̃ke bu kɑ kom kɔ̃sum ko, be bɑ koo kɑm ko yèn sɔ̃ bɑ yinɑ bu ɡem kĩɑ mɛ mu koo bu fɑɑbɑ ko. 
Yenin sɔ̃nɑ Gusunɔ u koo de ɡɑ̃ɑ dɑmɡinu ɡɑnu nu bu torɑsiɑ bu kɑ weesu nɑɑnɛ ko. 
Nɡe mɛyɑ, be bɑ ǹ ɡem nɑɑnɛ doke mɑ bɑ kom kɔ̃sum kuɑ kɑ nuku dobu, bɑ koo tɑɑrɛ suɑ. 
Nɛɡibu, bɛɛ be Yinni u kĩ, sɑ ǹ ko ko sɑ kun Gusunɔ siɑre bɛɛn sɔ̃ sɑɑ bɑɑyere. Domi Gusunɔ u bɛɛ ɡɔsɑ sɑɑ toren di i kɑ fɑɑbɑ wɑ kɑ Hunde Dɛɛron dɑm wi u bɛɛ kuɑ Gusunɔɡibu, kɑ mɑɑ bɛɛn nɑɑnɛ ye i mɔ ɡem sɔɔ. 
Yenin sɑɑbuwɑ Gusunɔ u bɛɛ sokɑ kɑ bɛsɛn Lɑbɑɑri ɡeɑn kpɑrɑbu, i n kɑ bɔnu mɔ bɛsɛn Yinni Yesu Kirisin yiiko sɔɔ. 
Ǹ n mɛn nɑ nɛɡibu, i yɔ̃ro dim dim i n sɔ̃ɔsi ni nɛni ni sɑ bɛɛ sɔ̃ɔsi nɔɔ kɑ nɔɔ kɑ mɑɑ tireru sɔɔ. 
Gusunɔ bɛsɛn Bɑɑbɑ wi u sun kĩɑ, mɑ win durom sɑɑbu u sun dɑm kɑ̃ mɛ mu kun nɔru mɔ kɑ mɑɑ yĩiyɔ ɡeebu, wi kɑ bɛsɛn Yinni Yesu Kirisin tii 
bu bɛɛn ɡɔ̃rusu dɑm kɛ̃, kpɑ bu bɛɛ tɑ̃sisiɑ i kɑ sɔm ɡeenu kɑ ɡɑri ɡee kpuro ko.
