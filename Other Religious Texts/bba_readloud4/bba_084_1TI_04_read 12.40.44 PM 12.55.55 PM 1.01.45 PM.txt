Timɔte I.
4.
Adɑmɑ Gusunɔn Hunde ɡerumɔ kpɑsɑsɑ mɑ sɑnɑm ɡɑm sisi mɛ̀ sɔɔ ɡɑbɑ koo nɑɑnɛ dokebun swɑɑ deri bu hunde koni weesuɡibu mɛm nɔɔwɑ, kpɑ bu mɑɑ wɛrɛkunun sɔ̃ɔsiru swĩi. 
Sɔ̃ɔsi te, tɑ weewɑ sɑɑ wee kowobun min di be bɑ tii mɛɛrɑ ɡɑbu. Tɔn ben ɡɔ̃ru ɡɑ ɡu nɡe ɡe bɑ sii sunsu mɑni. 
Tɔn ben bweserɑ bɑ ɡerumɔ mɑ n ǹ wɑ̃ tɔmbu bu suɑnɑ, yen biru n ǹ mɑɑ weenɛ bu dĩɑnu kpuro di. Adɑmɑ Gusunɔ u ye kpuro tɑkɑ kuɑwɑ nɑɑnɛ dokeobu be bɑ ɡem ɡiɑ bu kɑ di kɑ siɑrɑbu sɑnnu. 
Domi ɡɑ̃ɑnu bɑɑnire ni Gusunɔ u tɑkɑ kuɑ ɡɑ̃ɑ ɡeenɑ, ɡɑ̃ɑnu sɑri ni sɔɔ, ni bɑ koo kɔ̃, ɑdɑmɑ bu ɡesi ni kpuro mɔɔ kɑ siɑrɑbu. 
Domi Gusunɔn ɡɑri kɑ kɑnɑrɑ ni kpuro dɛɛrɑsiɑmɔ. 
Wee, ɑ̀ n nɑɑnɛ dokeobu bwisi yini kɛ̃mɔ kɑɑ n sɑ̃ɑwɑ Yesu Kirisin sɔm kowo ɡeo, kɑɑ n mɑɑ wunɛn tiin hunde diisiɑmɔ kɑ nɑɑnɛ dokebu kɑ sɔ̃ɔsi ɡeerun ɡɑri yi ɑ swĩi. 
Adɑmɑ ɑ wiirɑ ɡɑri yinɔ yi tɔmbɑ sekɑ kpɑ ɑ n wunɛn hunde dendimɔ Gusunɔn bɛɛrɛ wɛ̃ɛbu sɔɔ. 
Geemɑ, wɑsin dendibu ɑrufɑɑni ɡɑɑ mɔ, ɑdɑmɑ Gusunɔn bɛɛrɛ wɛ̃ɛbu bu ɑrufɑɑni kpuro mɔ yèn sɔ̃ bu ɡisɔn wɑ̃ɑru kɑ mɑɑ siɑɡirun nɔɔ mwɛɛru mɔ. 
Gɑri nɑɑnɛɡiiyɑ mi. N wɑ̃ bu yi nɔmɑ ɡɑ̃ri mɑm mɑm bu mwɑ. 
Yen sɔ̃nɑ sɑ sunɑmɔ sɑ sɔmburu mɔ̀, domi sɑ bɛsɛn yĩiyɔbu doke Gusunɔ Yinni wɑso sɔɔ wi u sɑ̃ɑ tɔmbu kpuron Fɑɑbɑ kowo, su mɑm nɛɛre be bɑ nɑɑnɛ doke. 
A n bu sɔ̃ɔsi te yiiremɔ kpɑ ɑ n bu tu sɔ̃ɔsimɔ. 
A ku de bɑ n wunɛn ɑluwɑɑsiru ɡɛmɑ, ɑdɑmɑ ɑ de ɑ n nɑɑnɛ dokeobu kom ɡen sɔ̃ɔsimɔ wunɛn ɡɑri ɡerubu sɔɔ, kɑ wunɛn wɑ̃ɑrun kookoosu sɔɔ, kɑ wunɛn kĩru sɔɔ, kpɑ ɑ n nɑɑnɛ mɔ kɑ mɑɑ ɡɔ̃ru kpiku. 
Sere n kɑ tunumɑ, ɑ n tɔmbu Gusunɔn ɡɑri ɡɑriɑmmɛ kpɑ ɑ n bu dɑm kɛ̃mɔ kpɑ ɑ n bu keu sɔ̃ɔsimɔ. 
A ku hunden kɛ̃ru ɑtɑfiiru ko te tɑ wɑ̃ɑ wunɛ sɔɔ, te bɑ nun nɔmu bɛriɑ Gusunɔn sɔmɔbun ɡɑri sɔɔ sɑnɑm mɛ yiɡbɛ ɡuro ɡurobɑ nun nɔmɑ sɔndi wirɔ. 
A ɡɑri yini nɔni dokeo, ɑ mɑɑ yi tii wɛ̃ɛyɔ mɑm mɑm, kpɑ tɔmbu kpuro bu wunɛn wuswɑɑ dɑɑbu wɑ. 
A n tii sɛ, ɑ mɑɑ wunɛn sɔ̃ɔsiru lɑɑkɑri dokeo kpɑ ɑ n tɛmɑnɛ ɡɑ̃ɑ nini sɔɔ. Domi ɑ̀ n mɔ̀ mɛ, kɑɑ tii fɑɑbɑ ko kɑ mɑɑ be bɑ nun swɑɑ dɑki.
