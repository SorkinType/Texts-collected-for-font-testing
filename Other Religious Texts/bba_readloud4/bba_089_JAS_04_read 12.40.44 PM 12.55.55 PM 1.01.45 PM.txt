Yɑkɔbu.
4.
Mɑn diyɑ sɑnnɔsu kɑ sikirinɔsu nɑɑmɔ bɛɛn suunu sɔɔ. Su nɑɑmɔwɑ ɡɑ̃ɑ dɑbinun binɛn di ye yɑ rɑ n tɑbu mɔ̀ bɛɛn wɑsi sɔɔ. 
I kĩ i n ɡɑ̃ɑnu mɔ ni i ǹ kpɛ̃ i wɑ, mɑ i sɔɔru sɑ̃ɑ i kɑ tɔnu ɡo. I ɡɑ̃ɑnun binɛ mɔ ni i ǹ kpɛ̃ i wɑ, mɑ i sikirinɑmɔ, i sɑnkinɑmɔ. Ye i kĩ, i ǹ ye mɔ domi i ku rɑ Gusunɔ bikie. 
Ì n mɑɑ bikiɑ i ku rɑ wɑ yèn sɔ̃ torɑrɑ i kɑ bikiɑmɔ kpɑ i kɑ ye dendi bɛɛn tii tiin kĩru sɔɔ. 
Bɛɛ be i ǹ kɑ tɔn turo yɔ̃, i ǹ yɛ̃ mɑ hɑnduniɑn bɔrɔ Gusunɔn yibɛrɛwɑ? Ǹ n mɛn nɑ wi u kĩ u ko hɑnduniɑn bɔrɔ, u tii kuɑ Gusunɔn yibɛrɛ. 
I kun tɑmɑɑ kɑm sɔɔrɑ Gusunɔn ɡɑri ɡeruɑ yi nɛɛ, “Gusunɔ u bɛsɛn hunde ye u sun wɛ̃n kĩru bikiɑmɔ kɑ nisinu.” 
Adɑmɑ u rɑ tɔnu durom kue mɛ n kpɑ̃ɑru kere, domi Gusunɔn ɡɑri nɛɛ, “Gusunɔ kun wɑ̃ɑ be bɑ tii suen biruɔ, ɑdɑmɑ u tii kɑwobu durom kuɑmmɛ.” 
Ǹ n mɛn nɑ, i Gusunɔ wiru kpĩiyɔ. I Setɑm yinɔ kpɑ u bɛɛ suuri. 
I Gusunɔ susio, u koo mɑɑ bɛɛ susi. I bɛɛn nɔmɑ dɛɛrɑsio, bɛɛ durumɡibu. I bɛɛn ɡɔ̃rusu sɑ̃rɑsio, bɛɛ ɡɔ̃rusu yiruɡibu. 
I de i n nuki sɑnkire kpɑ i n ɡɔɔ wooru sɔ̃ kpɑ i swĩ. I de bɛɛn yɛ̃ɛsu su ɡɔsiɑ swĩi kpɑ bɛɛn nuku dobu bu ɡɔsiɑ nuku sɑnkirɑnu. 
I bɛɛn tii kɑwo Yinni Gusunɔn wuswɑɑɔ kpɑ u bɛɛ suɑ wɔllɔ. 
Nɛɡibu, i ku kɔ̃sɑ mɑninɑ. Wi u win winsim kɔ̃sɑ ɡerumɔ, ǹ kun mɛ wi u nùn sirimɔ, woodɑn kɔ̃sɑ u ɡerumɔ mɑ u ye sirimɔ. Wee, ɑ̀ n woodɑ sirimɔ, ɑ ǹ mɑɑ sɑ̃ɑ wi u rɑ ye mɛm nɔɔwɛ mɑ n kun mɔ wi u ye sirimɔ. 
Gusunɔ turowɑ woodɑ wɛ̃ɛmɔ, wiyɑ mɑɑ sɑ̃ɑ siri kowo. Wi turowɑ koo kpĩ u fɑɑbɑ ko, wiyɑ koo mɑɑ kɑm koosiɑ. Ǹ n mɛn nɑ, wɑrɑ rɑ n wunɛ ɑ kɑ wunɛn winsim siri. 
I mɑn swɑɑ dɑkio tɛ̃, bɛɛ be i mɔ̀, ɡisɔ ǹ kun mɛ siɑ i ko dɑ wuu kɑsɑ i ko mi wɔ̃ɔ tiɑ kpɑ i tenkuru ko i ɑre di. 
Domi i ǹ yɛ̃ ye bɛɛn wɑ̃ɑrɑ ko n sɑ̃ɑ siɑ. I sɑ̃ɑwɑ nɡe bukɔ ɡe ɡɑ rɑ wɔrumɛ sɑɑ fiiko sɔɔ, yen biru kpɑ ɡu yɑrinɑ. 
Mɛ n weenɛ i ɡere wee. Yinni Gusunɔ ù n kĩ, mɑ i wɑ̃ɑ, i ko yeni ko, ǹ kun mɛ yeɔnɔ. 
Adɑmɑ tɛ̃ i woo kɑnɑmɔ i tii suɑmɔ. Woo kɑnɑ bin bweseru kpuro bu ǹ wɑ̃. 
Nɡe mɛyɑ bɑɑwure wi u ɡeɑn kobu yɛ̃ mɑ u kun kue, u torɑwɑ.
