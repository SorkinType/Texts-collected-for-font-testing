Romankooɓe.
13.
Yo gooto fuu ɗowtano laamiiɓe ɓee, sabi laamu walaa so wanaa iwruɗo to Laamɗo oo, kasen duu laamiiɓe ɓee Laamɗo lammini ɗum'en. 
Saabe ɗum, neɗɗo fuu calaniiɗo laamu saliima yamiroore Laamɗo, caliiɗo yamiroore Laamɗo nee jukkete. 
Gaɗoowo ko moƴƴi walaa ko huldata hooreeɓe ɓee, kuloowo ɓe dee, gaɗoowo ko boni. Aɗa yiɗi waasude hulde laamu naa? Ndennoo, waɗu ko moƴƴi, o yette. 
Sabi laamu yo gollanoowo Laamɗo faa nafe. Kaa so tawii ko boni ngaɗataa, hul, sabi wanaa meere laamu wakkorii kaafaawi, o gollanoowo Laamɗo faa o tabintina jukkungo mum faade e gaɗoowo ko boni fuu. 
Ndennoo, waajibi ɗowtano-ɗon laamiiɓe ɓee, wanaa saabe hulde jukkeede tan, pati hakkillaaji mon ɲiŋa on duu. 
Ɗum saabii so oɗon njoɓa lampo, sabi laamiiɓe ɓee e ley golle mum'en nanngugol lampo ngol, ɓe gollanooɓe Laamɗo. 
Ndokkee ɓe fuu hakkeeji maɓɓe. Ndokkee lampo mo kaan-ɗon hokkude lampo, ndokkon usuru mo kaan-ɗon hokkude usuru, kulon mo kaan-ɗon hulde, teddinon mo kaan-ɗon teddinde. 
Pati ɲamaande fuu ɓiloo hakkunde mon so wanaa ɲamaande jilli. Homo fuu rewa banndum jilli. Sabi jiɗɗo neɗɗo hono mum fuu, tabintinii Sariya Muusaa oo. 
E ley ɗum, ɗii ɗoo kaɗaaɗi: «Pati jeenu, pati ɲaaɗu hoore, pati wujju, pati tuuye-ɗaa», kanji e jamirooje goɗɗe ɗee fuu eɗe kawriti e ndee ɗoo yamiroore wootere: «Njiɗiraa tanaa maa no njiɗir-ɗaa hoore maa nii.» 
Jiɗɗo tanaa mum gollataa ɗum ko boni, ndennoo, jilli tabintinta Sariya oo. 
Oɗon anndi wakkati mo ngon-ɗen e mum. Wakkati mo kaan-ɗon finde oo yonii. Sabi jooni kisindam ɓuri ɓadaade en diina nde naatuno-ɗen e goonɗinal ndee. 
Jemma oo woɗɗiima, ɲalooma oo ɓattiima. Ndennoo, njoppen gollude golleeji nimre ɗii, ɓamen kaɓitorɗe fooyre, 
nguurden no haaniri, no wonɓe e fooyre ɲalooma nii, tawee wanaa e ley wanngiyaare e sigiro e ko soɓi e njaahilaaku e pooɗondiral e haasidaaku. 
Ɓoornoree Joomiraaɗo men Iisaa Almasiihu no kaddungal nii, pati kiinnee giɗaaɗe yonki mon, pati njokkon ɗe.
