2 TƐSALONIKA  Waragɛ Ween Ram o Ayeedek Pɔl Ɔl o Joowo Abaak Tɛsalonika.
1.
Aneeta Pɔl kibeen Silas been Timozi. Kayeedekuŋ waragɛ nico igeet ɔl o ceez o Joowo kuture o kazi Tɛsalonika o atuyu Jook ween baatina been Manyi Yesu Kiristo. 
Abon bai Jooi ween baatina been Manyi Yesu Kiristo anyuŋ igeet dɛtɛn ki ganɔn. 
Dɔl o yaai tuwento, kacina naaga abon kanyeka Jook zany tup nɔkɔ ŋaaten igeet, eeci tuwɛn unooŋ been rɛɛzɔnɛt unooŋ azaacɛ kizi maka. 
Gi o zin gɔɔn kabololuŋ naaga igeet ole o atu Jook o nɛɛn, eeci niiga amezu piryakzɛt, ma azuunu tuwɛn unooŋ. Mayo nuun agidenu, ma apirnanu ɔrɔɔt o, ŋaan azuunu nɔkɔ. 
Ma piryakzɛt nici akunakuŋ igeet ka keyeliza imɛɛn o payiin o Joowo, ka mã amɛnanu niiga amezu piryakzɛt, ka agantɔ ki baliin o Joowo. 
Acacakuŋ tiŋeere igeet Jooi piryakzɛt cunooŋ o gole ci abil tɔp, ma apirnanɛ niini ɔl o gɔɔn buk apirnanuŋ niigi igeet. 
Ŋaan tiŋeere anyuŋ Jooi igeet o apirnanu yubzɛn, igeet kibeen ageet buk. Agɔɔn niini gi nico katin iitene ci abadaan Manyi Yesu tammu tadena ɔrkɔran been toonnyak ogin adɔi e. 
Anyaa niini tiŋeere goo ci appe, ka kiiya kipirai ɔl o gɔɔn abor nɔɔnɔ, ma buk abor kaviyak o abon o Manyi ona Yesu Kiristo. 
Ajowa tiŋeere niigi piryakzɛt been targanyinɛt ci abil been nɛɛn. Ma aŋɛryai niigi ŋaaten Manyi Yesu been titinyonte o dɔyiz onin adikir. 
Ma iitene ci akuni niini neke ɔl o atuwe dook adiŋdiŋan nɔɔnɔ. Zin buk tiŋeere niiga aromoneku kibeen nɔɔnɔ, eeci utuyu niiga zɔɔz o Joowo abon baal kaduwakuŋ naaga igeet e. 
Gi o zin gɔɔn kajinekuŋ naaga igeet Jook tup o nɛɛn. Kajina Jook ona ka kanyuŋ niini igeet dɔyiz ci abaaku baayiz o abon baal laadun awakuŋ niini igeet e. Abon bai atirituŋ niini igeet dɔyize onin utuguz kaal o abon arɔɔŋ zinzeeti ugooc, been liŋliŋɔn unooŋ tuwento. 
Ma gole nico adoŋnyi zaara o Manyi onai Yesu ŋaatunooŋ, ma ajowanu buk niiga titinyɔn ŋaatin bonat o Jook onai been Manyi Yesu Kiristo.
