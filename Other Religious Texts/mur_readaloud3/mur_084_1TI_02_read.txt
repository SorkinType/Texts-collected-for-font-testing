1 TIMOZI.
2.
Inoko zin rak ɔɔwa karicanekuŋ igeet ijintek Jook ɔl dook. Ilaltek Jook ŋaaten nɔɔgɔ. Anycek buk Jook zany kaale o agoonek niini nɔɔgɔ. 
Ijintek Jook alaat o adikir ki ɔl dook o aliŋliŋ been nɔɔgɔ ka kabaayit naaga yaak ganɔnta, ma kagɔɔn kaal o arooŋeket Jooi ageet zinzeetine ci abon. 
Mã kagɔɔn nɔkɔ, abon. Ma buk koca atalnɛ Jooi ween ruguzoi onai. 
Arɔɔŋ niini ɔl dook kagaac zɔɔz ween didi ka kojokta niigi rogɛt o abil been nɛɛn. 
Eeci adoye Jooi ṯɔr nɔkɔ. Buk ɛɛti o arom Jook kibeen ɔl o deer adoye ṯɔr nɔkɔ. Zin ɛɛti nici Yesu Kiristo nɛɛn. 
Nɔɔnɔ nɛɛn o adaai ka kɔɔga ɔl daayize onin cabiinte o Loryento. Agooni gi nici iitene o arek Jooi, ma ayelza buk gi nici zɔɔz o arɔɔŋi Jooi ɔl dook korogti. 
Zin giye nico ɛŋɛrawan Jooi aneet kizi toonnyai ci kuuwayi ole ween modɛn, ka kedemezek naana nɔɔgɔ tuwɛn been gɔl ween didi. Ma zɔɔz ci kaduwakin noko alaŋ een vɔlɔŋ, bar zɔɔz ceen didi. 
Karooŋ zin naana ɔl o mac tuwento dook kagaac gɔl ci aŋaryin. Karooŋ naana ɔl o didi arek zinzeeti ugeec Jook. Mazi adoŋek niigi Jook azɛɛn ŋaryiinta, abon agɔɔn nɔkɔ ŋaati akɔmni daŋɔn been bornɛt nɔŋ zinzeetine ugeec. 
Karooŋ buk ŋaai kanyayit rumanɛ ci abon calaŋ een rumanɛ ci ayelzan eleeti irenɔnta. Ma alaŋ aluc ooti gole ci irenonto. Ma alaŋ aborcek warki ki kalbɛ ci ka kiirenti. 
Bar abon agɔɔn niigi kaal ci abon ci ayelza gi o eeginɛ niigi ŋaai ci atu Jook. 
Abon ŋaai azii demziin jayinta, kɔkɔm daŋɔn. 
Alaŋ naana karooŋ ŋaai kedemezit ɔl ci mac, karabɔŋ bar ka kanyayit niigi alaazɛt ci ajuki nɔɔgɔ, bar abon niigi ajaki. 
Kaduwa zɔɔz nico o, eeci baale ɛɛnyca Jooi ɔɔwa rak Adam. Ma vurta ɛtɛɛnya ni Eev. 
Ma buk baale Loryen alaŋ alaba Adam, bar alaba Eev, ma uulal niini lotinɛt o Joowo. 
Bar zin Jooi arogoz ŋaai gole o aritan niigi dɔl, matɛ amɛna niigi tuwɛnta ki reezinta been ŋaati agɔɔni niigi kaal ci abon arɔɔŋ Jooi calaŋ iiren.
