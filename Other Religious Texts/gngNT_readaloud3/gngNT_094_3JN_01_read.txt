Kugbɔntaaku kùa San kɛle' kù nnɔ.
1.
Nʼjɔ Gayus, min uciɛn fuondeh ŋɛ. N yíe ŋɛ imɔ̀nmɔ̀n nɛ. 
Nʼjɔ, n kàareh ki teh nɛ̀ ń li mɔn aʼbo isɛn kɛ ni nɛ; á li ŋmɔbe ugbɛnɛnsɔnge mɔnmɔnm, tɛn aʼfuobm mɔn Uwien saan ma bo. 
Tiʼtɔb Kristo yaab biba baa', ki lá tɔke' nni a pɛ tigbɛmɔ̀nt bo ma bo nin a fuobe tigbɛmɔ̀nt ni ma bo, nɛ nʼyɛnm sɔnge' ki gbien'. 
Nà li cère nʼyɛnm ń sɔnge kí tì kɛnde si: ń cii nʼbumu pɛ tigbɛmɔ̀nt ya sɛn bo nɛ. 
Nʼjɔ, a pɛ Kristo ya sɛn bo mɔnmɔnm ki teh uʼnib nà mɔn nin bi yé bicɛnb ma kɛ nnɔ. 
Biʼmɔ len' Kristo ya taanl yaab saan a yíe binib ma bo. N gbáanh ŋɛ nɛ, li toreh bɛ biʼsɛncenl ni Uwien yíe ma bo. 
Kimɛ bi cuonh ki sɔnh Kristo ya tuonl nɛ ka teknh niba bà ŋa pɛ Wien bo saan. 
Nɛn bo, nì kpɛ tinbi nɛ ń li toreh bi ya nib, ŋɔ tinbi nin bɛn ń taan kí li tɔkeh binib tigbɛmɔ̀nt. 
N là kɛle' tigbɛr tuba ki de' Kristo ya taanl yaab. Ama Diotrɛf wà yíe wɔ ń li yé niʼkɛ ya sɛnlier nnɔ ŋa tɔnge tiʼgbɛr. 
Nɛn bo nɛ n baa' ní la, n li tɔke u teh nà kɛ nnɔ. Nɛn si: u lienh tiʼbo tigbɛbiɛrt tà kɛ nnɔ. U tien' nɛn kɛ ki ye nà sɔnge' uʼyɛnm. Tiʼtɔb Kristo yaab baa' la, wa teknh bɛ, nɛ bà mɔ yíe bɛ ń teke bɛ la, u piendeh bɛ, ki ŋuɔh bɛ Kristo ya taanl ni. 
Nʼjɔ Gayus, la tòkre bà teh nà bre; ama bà teh nà mɔn, kimɛ wà kɛ teh nà mɔn yé Uwien yɔ nɛ. Wà mɔ kɛ teh nà bre ŋa bɛn Wien. 
Demetriyus wɔn, binib kɛ pɛ̀keh wɔ nɛ. U pɛ tigbɛmɔ̀nt tà bo nnɔ mɔ wɔngeh ki teh u yé unil nɛ. Tiʼmɔ pɛ̀keh wɔ nɛ, nɛ a bɛn ki ye ti pɛ̀keh wɔ ma nnɔ nì yé imɔ̀n nɛ. 
N ŋmɔbe tigbɛr tù yɛbe ń tɔke ŋɛ ama ma yíe ń kɛle tù gbɔnku ni. 
N daan ki ye nì pukn' waamu la, n li lɛ ŋɛ, tí tɔke tigbɛr. 
Uwien ń de ŋɛ uyɛnduɔn. Aʼjɔtieb fuondeh ŋɛ. Aʼmɔ ń fuonde tiʼyaab kɛ jɛjɛ kí de tɛ.
