Mikpiirm.
4.
Ni ya puoli bó nɛ n laa' tidɛknt yaam bunañɔbu buba yɛ paaki bó, nɛ n bi cii' udaan wà ya niɛke bó mikpiɛkm nnɔ giere' ufaa bo tɛn linatunl ki ye: «Dùo ní ń wuɔn ŋɛ nà we ní nɛ̀ ń tien.» 
I ya tàan bo nɛ *Mifuoñaanm taa' nni, nɛ n laa' kibɛrbiɛk kiba se paaki bó, uba kɛ kiʼbo. 
Wà kɛ kiʼbo nnɔ windeh tɛn itɛn yà bi yih yɛ̀ jaspi nin saduwann iʼdaaku faa nnɔ nɛ. Kugɔnguɔku kuba guɔn' ki linde' kibɛrbiɛk nnɔ ki windeh tɛn litɛnl là bi yih lɛ̀ emolod nnɔ. 
Mubɛrbiɛmu pile nin munan mɔ se ki guɔn' kɛ̀. Biciɛnb pile nin binan mɔ kɛ mubɛrbiɛmu nnɔ bo, ki guo tiwɛnpenpient, ki kpiɛ miñɔ̀km ya yukpiɛkpiɛ. 
Kibɛrbiɛk nnɔ ya bùol muɔreh piɛbpiɛb tɛn utɛmɛknde. Kuwaaku mɔ ñɛh kiʼsaan tɛn utaa nɛ tɛngeh. Timucincint tulole te kiʼnun bó tùoo. Timucincint nnɔ yé Uwien ya Fuoñaanm nɛ. 
Kibɛrbiɛk nnɔ ya nun bó ní naan miñunciɛnm nɛ, ki wien tein ki likeh ki càareh tɛn lidingl. Tiwɛnfuobt tunan nɛ guɔn' kibɛrbiɛk nnɔ siik ni. Tuʼnun bó nin tuʼpuoli kɛ yé inunbii nɛ ñɛin. 
Kiwɛnkpiɛk naan ucind nɛ, kiliek naan unajɛ, kitaak ya nun bó naan unil ya nun bó, nnan naan ukaab nɛ laanh. 
Tiwɛnfuobt tunan nnɔ kɛ ŋmɔbe ifiɛbe iloluob nɛ, ki ŋmɔbe inun ifiɛbe nnɔ bo ñɛin, paaki nin tingi kɛ. Ñɔnku nin wien ni tù tuu ki gɛh iyuon nɛ ki teh: «Uñaan, uñaan, uñaan, Yonbdaan Uwien mituɔm kɛ ya daan nɛ yé uñaan. Wɔn nɛ là te, ki biɛ ki te, ki we ní mɔ.» 
Uyo kɛ tiwɛnt nnɔ gɛh iyuon nɛ ki kpiɛkreh wà kɛ kibɛrbiɛk bo nnɔ, ki ñɛngeh wɔ, ki faareh wɔn wà fuobe uyo kɛ nnɔ. Tù gɛh uyo wà la, 
biciɛnb pile nin binan nnɔ gbaandeh wà kɛ kibɛrbiɛk bo nnɔ ya nun bó ki pukeh wɔ, ki kpiɛkreh wɔn wà fuobe uyo kɛ nnɔ, ki kpɛ̀reh biʼyukpɛkpiɛ ki blinh kibɛrbiɛk nnɔ ya nun bó ki teh: 
«Yonbdaan Uwien, sin nɛ kpɛ bɛ ń kpiɛke ŋɛ, kí ñɛnge ŋɛ, a ŋmɔbe mituɔm, kimɛ sin nɛ ñɛn' tibont kɛ, a yíe tù ń li te nɛ, ki ñɛn' tù.»
